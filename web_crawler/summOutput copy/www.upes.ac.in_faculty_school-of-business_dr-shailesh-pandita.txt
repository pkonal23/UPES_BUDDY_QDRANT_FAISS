Dr. Shailesh Pandita is an esteemed academic professional currently serving as an Assistant Professor - Senior Scale at the School of Business, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies. He holds a Ph.D. in Marketing Management, with a specialization in consumer adoption behavior. Dr. Pandita's teaching philosophy emphasizes the creation of an engaging and interactive learning environment, focusing on practical applications and fostering critical thinking skills to prepare students for real-world challenges. He integrates research insights into his curriculum to provide students with a thorough understanding of contemporary business issues.

In his academic role, Dr. Pandita teaches courses such as Sales & Distribution Management and Marketing Management. He is actively involved in research and has published numerous papers in respected journals indexed by ABDC, SSCI, and Scopus. His research interests focus on consumer behavior, particularly in the context of the ride-sharing economy and digital platforms, exploring psychological and economic factors that influence consumer decisions. He is also interested in the sharing economy, systematic literature reviews, and tourism management.

Dr. Pandita has made significant contributions to academia, organizing key events like Faculty Development Programmes and conferences. His dedication to advancing knowledge extends beyond the classroom, as he is passionate about creating engaging learning experiences for his students. His achievements include being a Gold Medalist in his MBA program and receiving the Infosys Foundation prize for excellence in 2019. He was also awarded the Best Paper Award at the 2nd International Conference on Contemporary Issues in Business Innovation, Technology & Social Science.

Before his current position, Dr. Pandita was associated with the Faculty of Management Studies at The ICFAI University in Baddi, Himachal Pradesh. For further inquiries, he can be contacted via email at shailesh.pandita@ddn.upes.ac.in.
