The B. Tech. Chemical Engineering (Refining & Petrochemicals) program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the dynamic petrochemical industry, which is currently valued at approximately USD 190 billion, making India the sixth-largest player globally. The curriculum spans a variety of essential topics, including refining technologies, petrochemical processing, specialty chemicals, data analytics, and bio refineries. This comprehensive approach ensures that graduates are not only well-versed in theoretical concepts but also gain practical experience through labs, industrial visits, and internships.

**Program Structure:**
- The program is structured over eight semesters, with courses including Leadership & Teamwork, Process Economics, Control Lab, Process Chemistry Lab, Basic Electrical and Electronics, and more.
- Key subjects such as Heat Transfer, Chemical Reaction Engineering, and Advanced Separation are included, alongside practical lab sessions and projects.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates can expect a wide array of career opportunities across various sectors such as Chemicals, Refining, Petrochemicals, Gas, Fertilizers, Energy solutions, and Pharmaceuticals. Potential job roles include:
- Production Engineer
- Design Engineer
- Petroleum Engineer
- Refining Engineer
- Process Engineer
- Research Engineer
- Process Automation Engineer
- Smart Manufacturer
- Data Analyst

**Industry Connections and Placements:**
The program emphasizes employability, with graduates securing positions in renowned companies like ExxonMobil, Cairn India, Reliance Industries Limited, consulting firms such as McKinsey & Company and Bain & Company, and tech firms like Samsung Heavy Industries. The curriculum is developed in collaboration with industry experts, ensuring students are well-prepared for the challenges of the global market.

**Admission Criteria:**
- Candidates must meet a minimum eligibility requirement of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, specifically in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- Selection is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.

**Future Scope:**
The program aligns with global trends towards sustainability, energy innovation, and digitization. Graduates are positioned to play a crucial role in the evolving landscape of the petrochemical industry, particularly in gas engineering and sustainable practices.
