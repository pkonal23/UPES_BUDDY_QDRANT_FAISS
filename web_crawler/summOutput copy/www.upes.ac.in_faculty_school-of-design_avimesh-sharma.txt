Avimesh Sharma is an accomplished faculty member with over 12 years of experience in the 3D Games and Animation industry. He specializes in producing both low and high-resolution environment models, specifically tailored for the stylized 3D video game sector and the Metaverse. His skills encompass crafting 3D assets, props, and levels suitable for various media, including film, gaming, virtual platforms, television, and print media. Avimesh is adept in multiple modeling and texturing techniques, and he integrates his artistic talents into art and craft projects.

Since joining UPES in 2016, Avimesh has effectively combined his academic pursuits with his passion for creativity, teaching as an Assistant Professor in Game Design. His teaching philosophy emphasizes practical applications and hands-on experiences, where he shares insights from his extensive industry background. He adopts a "Learn-Create-Share" approach, encouraging a dynamic learning environment that promotes engagement and exploration among students.

Avimesh actively encourages student participation in national and international competitions, research conferences, and mentoring initiatives. His professional development includes attending Erasmus staff mobility training focused on the Metaverse at Cag University, Istanbul, Turkey, in 2022.
