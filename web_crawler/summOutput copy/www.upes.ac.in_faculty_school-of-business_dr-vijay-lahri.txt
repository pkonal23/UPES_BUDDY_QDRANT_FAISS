Dr. Vijay Lahri is an Assistant Professor at the School of Business, UPES, specializing in Operations Research, Operations Management, Supply Chain, and Logistics Management. His teaching philosophy emphasizes creating a supportive and encouraging learning environment for students, utilizing various pedagogical tools such as concept mapping, classroom discussions, and problem-based learning. Dr. Lahri has a robust academic background, having been awarded the MHRD Fellowship (JRF/SRF) at IIT-ISM Dhanbad from 2016 to 2021, and the GATE fellowship from 2014 to 2016. His previous experience includes serving as an Assistant Professor in Operations and Supply Chain at the Uttaranchal Institute of Management, Uttaranchal University, and as a research assistant at NEOMA Business School in Rouen, France.

His research interests focus on Decision Modelling, Sustainable Supply Chain Optimization, and Operations Management. Dr. Lahri has made significant contributions to the field through published research indexed in the WOS/ABDC/Scopus databases and has presented his findings at various international conferences organized by prestigious institutions like IITs and IIMs. He has also served as a reviewer for several academic journals, including Expert Systems with Applications and the Jordan Journal of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering, as well as for the Inspire Awards-Manak 2019-20 of the National Innovation Foundation, under the Government of India.

Dr. Lahri has developed mathematical models addressing lot sizing and network design problems, integrating sustainability considerations in uncertain environments, which are beneficial for industries. His courses include Supply Chain Modelling, Logistics Management, Sustainability, Analytics, and Decision-making Modelling using MCDM Techniques.
