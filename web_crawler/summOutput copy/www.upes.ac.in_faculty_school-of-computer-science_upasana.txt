Upasana completed her Ph.D. thesis at the National Institute of Technology Patna (NITP) in June 2024, focusing on "Lung Disease Identification using Computer Vision and Deep Learning Techniques." She holds an M. Tech. degree from the National Institute of Technology Meghalaya, where she studied from 2017 to 2019, and a B. Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from DVSIET, Meerut, obtained from 2010 to 2014.

Her passion for teaching is evident as she aims to make learning engaging by connecting theoretical concepts to practical, real-world scenarios. Upasana qualified for the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) in 2017 and was awarded the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD) scholarship during her M. Tech. studies. Additionally, she received an Institute Scholarship for her Ph.D. at NIT Patna from 2019 to 2024.

Upasana has 1.5 years of professional experience as a Health Officer in Noida, where she was responsible for developing and maintaining websites, managing health records databases, and creating user-friendly interfaces for health applications. She is a member of IEEE and has published research articles in esteemed journals. During her Ph.D. program, she guided six interns in their summer internships at NIT Patna.

Her areas of expertise include Data Mining, Programming (C and Python), Machine Learning, and her research interests span medical image analysis, computer vision, machine learning, deep learning, and data mining.
