The MBA in Aviation Management (Airline Service Operations) program at UPES School of Business is designed to provide a comprehensive and practical education in the aviation sector over a two-year duration. The program emphasizes experiential learning through summer internships, live projects, industrial tours, and a dissertation, ensuring that students gain both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience.

### Program Structure and Curriculum
The curriculum covers various critical aspects of aviation management, including:
- **Fundamentals of Airline Operations**
- **Airport Planning & Management**
- **Aviation Security & Safety**
- **Airline Economics**
- **Aviation Marketing**
- **Data Analysis and Decision Making**
- **Financial Management**
- **Business Ethics and CSR**
- **Innovation and Entrepreneurship**

Students can specialize in areas such as airport operations, air cargo operations, airline revenue management, or consulting. The program also integrates advanced software training, including tools like R-studio and analytics software, preparing students for a technology-driven industry.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates of the program can pursue a variety of roles in the aviation industry, including:
- Airline Manager
- Airport Manager
- Aviation Consultant
- Aviation Analyst
- Aviation Sales and Marketing Manager
- Aviation Operations Manager
- Aviation Financial Manager
- Aviation Safety Manager
- Airline Revenue Manager
- Air Cargo Manager

The program has a strong placement record, with graduates securing positions at notable organizations such as GMR Group, Adani Group, Vistara Airlines, and others. Starting salaries for fresh graduates typically range from INR 6-10 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills and exposure.

### Admission Criteria
To be eligible for the MBA in Aviation Management, candidates must have:
- A minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university.
- Performance in UPESMET or other national level exams (MAT, CMAT, CAT, XAT, GMAT, NMAT) followed by a personal interview.

Students with a minimum eligible score of 70 percentile in MAT/CMAT will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test.
