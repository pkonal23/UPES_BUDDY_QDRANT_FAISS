The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is actively engaged in initiatives aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), aiming to foster a sustainable and resilient future for all. UPES emphasizes innovation, collaboration, and community engagement to inspire change and create a better tomorrow.

### Key Initiatives and Programs

1. **Social Internships**: All first-year students are required to participate in mandatory social internships with organizations in the social sector. This initiative is designed to raise awareness about social issues in India while developing empathy and leadership skills among students.

2. **Support Staff Education**: UPES is committed to uplifting its support staff through programs focused on computer literacy and English communication skills. This initiative aims to enhance their professional capabilities and personal growth.

3. **Community Development**: The university's location in Uttarakhand, characterized by its hilly terrain and rich biodiversity, provides a unique opportunity to address local and global sustainability challenges. UPES utilizes this geographical advantage to implement initiatives in clean energy, environmental conservation, and community development.

4. **Smart Village Transformation (Project Utthan)**: This project aims to establish Dhalani and Koti as the first 'Smart Model Villages' in Uttarakhand, focusing on healthcare, education, and livelihood to promote sustainable rural development.

5. **Educational Programs**: UPES integrates sustainability into its curriculum and research, preparing students to become future leaders capable of driving meaningful change. Programs such as LearnShaala, Lakhon Khwahishen, and the Smart Class Program illustrate UPES's commitment to community education and environmental conservation.

6. **Innovative Projects**: Initiatives like Aipan VR and Project Payal showcase UPES's creative approach to education and cultural preservation, utilizing virtual reality to enhance learning experiences and investing in girls' education for a brighter future.

### Commitment to the Sustainable Development Goals

UPES aligns its efforts with various SDGs, including:
- **SDG 1**: No Poverty
- **SDG 4**: Quality Education
- **SDG 5**: Gender Equality
- **SDG 6**: Clean Water and Sanitation
- **SDG 7**: Affordable and Clean Energy
- **SDG 8**: Decent Work and Economic Growth
- **SDG 10**: Reduced Inequalities
- **SDG 11**: Sustainable Cities and Communities
- **SDG 12**: Responsible Consumption and Production
- **SDG 13**: Climate Action
- **SDG 14**: Life Below Water
- **SDG 15**: Life on Land
- **SDG 16**: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions
- **SDG 17**: Partnerships for the Goals

Through partnerships with industry leaders and community engagement, UPES empowers individuals and communities to contribute to achieving these global goals. The university's dedication to sustainability is evident in its various programs aimed at leadership development, holistic education, and the promotion of gender equality.

### Conclusion

UPES invites students, faculty, partners, and the global community to participate in its sustainability journey. By collectively working towards the SDGs, UPES aims to create a world characterized by equality, environmental stewardship, and shared prosperity.
