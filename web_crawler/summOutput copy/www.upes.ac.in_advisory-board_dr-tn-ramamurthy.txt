Dr. Thandavarayan Ramamurthy is a prominent INSA-Senior Scientist at the National Institute of Cholera and Enteric Diseases (NICED) in Kolkata. He has a significant academic and research background, having supervised 12 Ph.D. students across various institutions, including the University of Calcutta, Jadavpur University, and Utkal University in Orissa. Dr. Ramamurthy has an extensive publication record, with over 300 articles in reputable national and international journals.

His educational qualifications include a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) from Presidency College, Madras University, a Master of Science (M.Sc.) from the Center of Advanced Study in Marine Biology at Annamalai University, and a Ph.D. in Marine Biology from the same institution. Dr. Ramamurthy's research focuses on Clinical Microbiology, Environmental and Food Microbiology, Immunology, and Molecular Biology, particularly in developing therapies for Cholera and Enteric Diseases.

In addition to his research contributions, Dr. Ramamurthy has received numerous national and international awards and serves as an editorial board member for several esteemed journals in his field. His expertise and leadership in microbiology make him a key figure in advancing research on cholera and related diseases.
