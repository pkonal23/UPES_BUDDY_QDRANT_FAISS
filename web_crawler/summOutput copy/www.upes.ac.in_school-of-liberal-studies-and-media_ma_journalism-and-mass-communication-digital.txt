The MA Journalism & Mass Communication (Digital) program at UPES School of Liberal Studies and Media is a two-year comprehensive course structured into four semesters, focusing on the growing digital media landscape. The curriculum is designed to equip students with essential skills in media writing, electronic production techniques, and digital communication, bridging theory with practical insights through engagement with industry experts.

**Key Features:**
- **Holistic Curriculum:** The program allows students to specialize in subject-focused areas and choose minor subjects from other disciplines, enhancing their educational experience.
- **Industry-Relevant Skills:** Students gain skills in multimedia storytelling, data journalism, digital marketing, and social media management, preparing them for various roles such as digital journalists, content creators, and communication specialists.
- **Practical Training:** The program emphasizes internships, on-the-job training, and real-world projects, ensuring graduates are well-prepared for the challenges of the media industry.
- **Modern Infrastructure:** The school boasts advanced studio and lab facilities for hands-on experience in digital production.

**Career Opportunities:** Graduates can pursue careers in news organizations, advertising agencies, PR firms, and corporate communications, among others. The program's strong industry connections facilitate internship opportunities, leading to high placement rates in reputable media and digital content firms.

**Eligibility Criteria:** Applicants must have a minimum of 50% in their 10th, 12th, and graduation in any stream. Selection is based on personal interviews.
