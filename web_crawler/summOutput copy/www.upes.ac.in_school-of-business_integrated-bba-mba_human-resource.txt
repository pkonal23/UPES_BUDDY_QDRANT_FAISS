The Integrated BBA MBA program with a specialization in Human Resource Management offered by UPES is designed to provide students with a comprehensive education that combines general business administration principles with specialized HR knowledge. The program spans multiple semesters and covers a wide range of subjects, ensuring graduates are well-equipped for careers in HR management.

### Program Structure
- **Semesters**: The program consists of eight semesters, including core subjects like Operations Management, Business Communication, and Strategic Management, alongside specialized HR courses such as Global HRM, Compensation Management, and HR Analytics.
- **Electives**: Students can choose elective courses in areas like talent management, employee engagement, and diversity and inclusion, allowing them to tailor their education to specific interests.
  
### Admission Criteria
- **Eligibility**: Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in both Classes X and XII. Admission is based on performance in the entrance exam (UG - UPESMET) or other qualifying tests (UGAT/CUET).
- **Selection Process**: Interested students are encouraged to fill out an enquiry form for detailed information about the fee structure and application process.

### Learning Opportunities
- **Practical Exposure**: The program emphasizes real-world experience through internships, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts. This hands-on approach helps students bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application.
- **Facilities**: Students have access to modern classrooms, an Analytics Lab, and a well-stocked library, enhancing their learning experience.

### Career Prospects
Graduates of this program have promising career opportunities across various sectors, including corporate organizations, consulting firms, and non-profits. Potential job roles include:
- HR Manager
- Recruiter
- Training Specialist
- HR Consultant
- Talent Manager
- Organizational Development Specialist
- HR Analyst

The starting salary for fresh graduates typically ranges from INR 6-30 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills and exposure.

### Industry Trends
The HR field is evolving, with a growing demand for skilled professionals who can integrate technology and data analysis into HR practices. The program prepares students to leverage tools like HRIS, ATS, and data analysis software, ensuring they are well-prepared for the future job market.

### Contact Information
For further inquiries or to apply, interested individuals can reach out via the UPES contact channels or fill out the enquiry form provided on their website.

Overall, the Integrated BBA MBA program with a specialization in Human Resource Management at UPES offers a robust educational foundation, practical experiences, and a pathway to a successful career in HR.
