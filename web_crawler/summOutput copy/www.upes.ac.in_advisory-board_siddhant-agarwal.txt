Siddhant Agarwal serves as the Developer Community Manager for the Asia-Pacific (APAC) region at Neo4j. He has been recognized for his significant contributions to the community-building sector, notably through his nominations as a finalist in the 2021 CMX Community Industry Awards. Specifically, he was nominated in two categories: "Community Professional of the Year for a Developer Relations Community" and "Community Professional of the Year." 

The CMX Community Industry Awards were established in 2019 by the CMX team to honor outstanding achievements within the community industry, providing a platform to celebrate the accomplishments of individuals and organizations in this field. 

In addition to his award nominations, Siddhant is a passionate public speaker, having delivered presentations at over 2,000 national and international forums. His efforts have positively impacted the lives of more than 500,000 individuals, including students and developers. 

Siddhant graduated in 2015 with a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) degree in Computer Science Engineering (CSE), specializing in Oil & Gas Informatics, from the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). His recognition as a finalist in the CMX Community Industry Awards 2021 underscores his dedication to community engagement and development within the tech industry.
