Dr. Abhinav Sharma is an Assistant Professor (SG) at the School of Advanced Engineering at UPES, with a robust academic background and extensive research experience. He completed his B. Tech. at H. N. B. Garhwal University in 2009, followed by an M. Tech. and Ph. D. at Govind Ballabh Pant University of Agriculture and Technology in 2011 and 2016, respectively. Before joining UPES, he served as an Assistant Professor at Bipin Tripathi Kumaon Institute of Technology for one and a half years.

Dr. Sharma has a diverse teaching portfolio, covering both undergraduate and postgraduate courses in areas such as Analog Electronics, Digital Electronics, Signal & Systems, Computer Networks, Analog and Digital Communication, Microprocessors, Embedded Systems, as well as advanced topics like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Soft Computing Techniques. His teaching philosophy emphasizes the integration of theoretical and practical knowledge, using real-world examples and software tools like MATLAB and Python to enhance student understanding and problem-solving skills.

In terms of research, Dr. Sharma actively supervises multidisciplinary projects at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, focusing on the application of artificial intelligence and machine learning in electrical and electronics engineering. His current research endeavors include developing novel hybrid swarm intelligence algorithms for antenna array pattern synthesis and solar parameter estimation, as well as exploring machine learning algorithms for non-cooperative target recognition using RCS data and computer vision applications in fields like biomedical engineering and precision agriculture.

Dr. Sharma has made significant contributions to academia, authoring 40 research papers in SCI/Scopus-indexed journals and conferences, publishing six books with notable international publishers, and holding five Indian patents. He has been recognized for his research excellence, receiving the Award of Excellence in Research from UPES in 2018 and being named a star researcher for two consecutive years (2021 and 2022). He also serves as Co-PI on the Seed Money Project titled "Precision Agriculture."

For further inquiries or collaboration, Dr. Abhinav Sharma can be contacted via email at abhinav.sharma@ddn.upes.ac.in. His research interests include Signal Processing, Communication, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Swarm Intelligence.
