Dr. Shaurya Gupta is an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science, where he specializes in subjects such as In-Memory Processing, Stream Processing, Applications of Machine Learning in Industries, and Data Communication and Networks. He emphasizes the importance of industry-relevant credentials and inducements in shaping student learning experiences.

Dr. Gupta has a solid academic background, having completed his PhD in Information Technology from Amity University, Rajasthan, and an M.Tech in Computer Science from Jagannath University, Jaipur. He has over 13 years of experience in various domains including teaching, administration, liaison and coordination, student management, and research and analysis. Prior to his current position at UPES, he served as an Assistant Professor at Amity University, Rajasthan.

His scholarly contributions include a total of 23 publications, which encompass papers in reputed research journals, conference proceedings indexed by Scopus, and book chapters. Notably, he holds one granted patent and has three additional patents published. He has also edited two academic books.

Dr. Gupta's teaching philosophy revolves around fostering a conducive learning environment that promotes knowledge integration and practical applicability of skills. He believes that an effective classroom should provide accurate knowledge and skills that prepare students for real-world challenges. He advocates for a learning-oriented education that focuses on enhancing student engagement and understanding.
