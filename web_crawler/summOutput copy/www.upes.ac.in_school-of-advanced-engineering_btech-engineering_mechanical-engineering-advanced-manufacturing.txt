The content outlines the comprehensive B.Tech. program in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Advanced Manufacturing offered by the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). The program is structured across eight semesters, emphasizing advanced manufacturing technologies, robotics, additive manufacturing, and automation, which are critical for modern industrial practices.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII to qualify for the program.

**Program Highlights:**
- The curriculum includes specialized courses such as Advanced Manufacturing Processes, Machine Controls, Microcontrollers, and Control of CNC Machine Tools.
- Students gain practical experience through laboratory courses and industry-focused R&D projects, ensuring they are well-prepared for the evolving manufacturing landscape.
- The program emphasizes robotics and additive manufacturing, which are pivotal in enhancing productivity and efficiency in manufacturing processes.
- Collaboration with industry leaders, notably BOSCH, provides students with exposure to real-world projects and innovative learning environments.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates can pursue careers in various sectors including manufacturing, automotive, aerospace, and technology. Potential job roles include manufacturing engineers, production managers, quality control specialists, design engineers, and research associates. The program also offers robust placement support facilitated by UPES's strong industry connections, ensuring students have access to lucrative job opportunities.

**Industry Trends:**
The program prepares students for emerging trends in advanced manufacturing, such as:
- Automation and intelligent factories
- Increased use of additive manufacturing (3D printing)
- The Internet of Things (IoT) for real-time data analysis
- Digital twin technology for process optimization
- Sustainability practices and circular economy principles
- Artificial Intelligence (AI) for supply chain optimization

**Contact Information:**
For more details regarding admissions, course structure, and placements, prospective students can fill out an inquiry form on the UPES website or contact the university directly. 

Overall, the B.Tech. program at UPES is designed to equip students with the latest skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in the rapidly advancing field of manufacturing engineering, particularly in the context of Industry 4.0.
