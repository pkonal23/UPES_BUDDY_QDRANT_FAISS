Chhotelal Kumar is an accomplished academic with a notable background in computer science, having qualified the Graduation Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) in the years 2016, 2018, and 2019. He is currently an Assistant Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), having joined in July 2023. Prior to this role, he gained teaching experience from June 2015 to July 2018, focusing on GATE Computer Science and placement preparation.

Kumar holds an M.Tech in Information Technology from the National Institute of Technology Patna (NITP), which he completed in 2020, and a B.Tech in Information Technology from Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, West Bengal, earned between 2011 and 2015. He submitted his Ph.D. thesis titled "Improved Item Embedding and Preferential Schemes for Session-based Recommendation Systems" to NITP in July 2023.

His teaching philosophy emphasizes creating an interactive and adaptable learning environment. He is dedicated to individualized instruction and promotes critical thinking, practical application, and real-world connections, aiming to instill a lasting passion for learning in his students. Kumar has taught a wide range of subjects essential for GATE preparation, including data structures and algorithms, computer networks, database management systems, automata theory, and formal languages. He employs interactive teaching methods to encourage deep understanding and enthusiasm for computer science.

In addition to his teaching responsibilities, Chhotelal Kumar is an active researcher and an IEEE member. He has published several research articles in esteemed journals and has served as a reviewer for numerous articles in reputed journals and conferences. His research interests include recommendation systems, data mining, machine learning, deep learning, information retrieval, and emotion recognition.
