Justice VK Maheshwari has had a distinguished legal career spanning 34 years within the Uttar Pradesh Judiciary. Born in Saharanpur, Uttar Pradesh, he completed his Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) before earning his Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) degree in 1972. He began his legal practice at the Bar in Saharanpur and subsequently excelled in the Uttar Pradesh Judicial Service, securing the second rank upon his selection in 1975.

Throughout his career, Justice Maheshwari held various judicial positions across multiple locations including Moradabad, Farukhabad, Mainpuri, Roorkee, Bareilly, Dehradun, Kanpur, and Nainital. He was promoted to District and Sessions Judge in 2003, serving in the District of Chamoli. In 2004, he was appointed as the Registrar General of the High Court of Uttarakhand in Nainital, a position he held until his retirement in 2009.

Post-retirement, in 2016, Justice Maheshwari was appointed as the Vice-Chairman of the Uttarakhand Public Services Tribunal in Dehradun. In addition to his judicial roles, he contributes to academia as an honorary faculty member for organizations such as ONGC, THDC, and NHPC.
