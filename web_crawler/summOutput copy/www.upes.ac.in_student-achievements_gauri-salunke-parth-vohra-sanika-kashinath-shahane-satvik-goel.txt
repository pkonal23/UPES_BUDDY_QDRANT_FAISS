In 2023, Gauri Salunke, Parth Vohra, Sanika Kashinath Shahane, and Satvik Goel received an Honorable Mention in the esteemed Design Intelligence Award for their project titled "Sensory Arcade." This innovative interactive mat is designed to enhance social skills among mildly autistic children in school environments. Their achievement highlights a significant commitment to addressing societal challenges through creative design solutions. The Design Intelligence Award is an international competition that fosters cultural connections and celebrates creativity aimed at resolving global issues. The students' project not only garnered recognition but also reflects their dedication to impactful design. Congratulations are extended to the winners for their remarkable accomplishment. The project was mentored by Manas Ranjan Mishra, a professor at the School of Design.
