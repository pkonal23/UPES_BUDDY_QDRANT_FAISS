The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a comprehensive 4-year Bachelor of Science (B.Sc. Hons.) in Chemistry by Research, designed under the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 framework. This program emphasizes a research-oriented curriculum, enabling students to gain in-depth knowledge and practical skills in chemistry while providing flexibility through multiple exit and entry options. 

### Academic Structure
- **Program Duration**: 4 years, divided into 8 semesters.
- **Credits**: Each semester includes courses totaling a maximum of 20 credits.
- **Exit Options**: 
  - After 40 credits: Certificate in chosen discipline.
  - After 80 credits (2 years): Diploma.
  - After 120 credits (3 years): Undergraduate degree.
  
Students can choose specializations in **Polymer Chemistry** or **Industrial Chemistry**, with the final year focusing on research and dissertation work. The program also supports lateral entry, allowing students to join at different stages. Graduates can pursue further studies such as a Master's or Ph.D. in chemistry or related fields.

### Eligibility Criteria
- Minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII with a focus on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology (PCM/B).

### Career Opportunities
Graduates of the B.Sc. (Hons.) in Chemistry by Research program have a wide range of career options, including:
- Research Scientists
- Laboratory Analysts
- Quality Control Specialists
- Chemical Engineers
- Opportunities in pharmaceutical, biotechnology, chemical manufacturing, and environmental sectors.

The program prepares students for competitive exams like JAM to enhance their higher education prospects.

### Admission Process
The selection process includes performance in a personal interview, ensuring that candidates are well-suited for the program.

### Program Highlights
- State-of-the-art research labs.
- Guidance from experienced faculty members from renowned universities.
- Emphasis on skill development and industry-relevant training.
- Strong placement track record, with graduates securing positions in leading research institutions and industries.

### Contact Information
For further details about the program, fee structure, and application process, interested candidates can reach out to the **School of Advanced Engineering** at UPES.

This program not only equips students with essential skills and knowledge in chemistry but also opens doors to advanced research opportunities, making it an excellent choice for aspiring chemists.
