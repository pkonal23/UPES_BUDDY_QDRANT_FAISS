**Profile Summary of Prof. Yamini Negi**

**Current Position:**
- **Assistant Professor, Psychology** at UPES (2021 - Present)
- **Mental Health Professional, Independent Consultant** (2017 - Present)

**Contact Information:**
- Email: yamini.negi@ddn.upes.ac.in

**Research Interests:**
Prof. Yamini Negi is a dedicated educator and researcher with over seven years of experience in mental health and well-being. Her primary research focuses on:
- Mental Health Research
- Depression
- Mindfulness-based interventions
- Promoting mental health among adolescents and young adults, especially in university settings.

**Research Projects and Grants:**
1. **Critically-timed sleep + light interventions (SALI)**: A project aimed at improving mood in young adults with depression, funded by the SEED Grant at UPES (INR 6,59,000) where she serves as Principal Investigator (PI).
2. **Yoga-based positive psychology intervention**: Exploring its efficacy on stress and well-being in adults, also funded by a SEED Grant at UPES (INR 80,000) [PI].
3. **Daily suicidal ideation study**: Co-PI for a project examining suicidal ideation and stressors in Northern India, funded by the Global Health Research Innovation Centre, Miami University, USA (USD 2,900).
4. **Promoting academic environment**: Co-PI for a project addressing campus interpersonal violence in Indian Higher Education Institutions, funded by the University of California, Berkeley (USD 50,000).

**Work Experience:**
- **Head, Research and Development** at Psych Rich Wellness, India (2017-2018).
- **Co-founder and CEO** of Psych Rich Wellness, focusing on mental health services (2018-2021).

**Teaching Experience:**
Prof. Negi has taught various courses, including:
- Introduction to Psychology I & II
- Psychological Counselling Skills
- Leadership and Teamwork
- Mindfulness-based Cognitive Therapy
- Mental Health of Young Adults
- Depression and Suicidal Ideation
- Cost-effective Psychological Interventions
- Managing Relationships and Being Happy

**Educational Background:**
- Graduated from the University of Delhi.
- MSc. in Mental Health Research from the University of Nottingham, U.K., awarded the ‘Developing Solutions Scholarship’.

**Contributions to UPES:**
Prof. Negi played a pivotal role in establishing UPES’ first lab dedicated to psychological research, equipped with industry-standard equipment. She has also been actively involved in fostering national and international research collaborations with institutions like Miami University, University of Louisville, University of California, San Diego, and University of Oxford.

**Teaching Philosophy:**
Prof. Negi believes in education as a means to develop both minds and hearts. She emphasizes the importance of making the learning process personally meaningful through action and practice. She integrates research into the teaching process, encouraging students to engage with real-life problems and seek ethical solutions that reflect in their personal and academic lives.

**Notable Achievements:**
- Instrumental in the development of mental health initiatives and research at UPES.
- Advocate for evidence-based practices in mental health.

Prof. Yamini Negi remains a significant figure in the field of psychology, contributing to both academic and practical aspects of mental health education and research.
