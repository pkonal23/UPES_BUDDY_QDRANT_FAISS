The MBA in Oil & Gas Management program at UPES is designed to prepare students for mid and senior management roles within the Oil & Gas industry, both nationally and globally. The curriculum emphasizes practical learning through internships and on-the-job training, allowing students to apply academic knowledge in real-world contexts. The program covers various industry practices and emerging trends across Upstream, Midstream, and Downstream sectors.

Key components of the program include training in essential software and technologies like Advanced Excel, Power-BI, SPSS, TABLU, SAP, and Python, which enhance employability in the competitive job market. The demand for skilled professionals in the Oil & Gas sector continues to grow, making this MBA a promising pathway for aspiring candidates.

**Curriculum Overview:**
- **Semester 1:** Courses include Petroleum Exploration, Business Communication, Accounting for Managers, and New Alternative Energy.
- **Semester 2:** Focuses on Financial Management, Advanced IT Applications for Oil & Gas, and Operations Management & Petrochemical Business.
- **Semester 3:** Covers Strategic Management, Business Ethics and CSR, and various specialization papers.
- **Semester 4:** Involves summer internships and major projects, providing real-world exposure.

The program also emphasizes industry insights through case studies, guest lectures, and industry visits. Students are encouraged to engage in research projects and seminars, which broaden their perspectives and enhance their understanding of the industry.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.
- Admission is based on performance in UPESMET or national level exams (MAT, CMAT, CAT, XAT, GMAT, NMAT) followed by a personal interview.
- Students with a minimum eligible score in these exams may be exempt from UPESMET.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates can explore various career paths including roles such as:
- Oil & Gas regulatory and financial consultant
- Energy Analyst
- ESG specialist
- Business Development Marketing
- Sustainability consultant
- Fuel procurement analyst

Starting salaries for fresh graduates typically range from INR 6 to 30 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills and market conditions.
