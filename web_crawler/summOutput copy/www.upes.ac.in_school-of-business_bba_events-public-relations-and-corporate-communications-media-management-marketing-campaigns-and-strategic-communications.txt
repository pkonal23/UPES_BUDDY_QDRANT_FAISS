The BBA program in Events, Public Relations, and Corporate Communications at UPES School of Business is designed to prepare students for a thriving career in the communications sector, particularly as the industry increasingly shifts towards digital platforms. This program emphasizes the importance of social media, marketing, and analytics, equipping graduates with essential skills for diverse career paths, including social media management, digital marketing, public relations, event management, and data analysis.

### Key Program Features:
- **Hands-On Experience**: Students gain practical knowledge through industry internships, on-the-job training, and major projects, making them career-ready for the media and entertainment industry or for pursuing doctoral research.
- **Skill Development**: The curriculum fosters a blend of creativity, communication, and analytical skills, crucial for success in events, public relations, and corporate communications.
- **Career Opportunities**: Graduates can find employment in leading media houses such as Times of India, Hindustan Times, Indian Express, TV Today, NDTV, and various content start-ups. Entry-level salaries range from INR 4 to 10 lakh per annum, depending on individual capabilities and industry exposure.

### Admission Process:
- **Eligibility Criteria**: Students must meet the minimum eligibility requirements, which include a score of 50% in 10th and 12th grades from any stream.
- **Entrance Exams**: Admission is based on the UPESMET-UG, UGAT, or CUET entrance exams.

### Program Specialization:
The program offers a specialization in Social Media, Marketing, and Analytics, covering key themes such as:
- Social media handling and campaigns
- Market research
- Data analytics
- PR strategies

This specialization prepares students to leverage social media tools and conduct high-quality digital market research, ensuring they are well-equipped for the demands of the industry.

### Facilities and Learning Environment:
Students will have access to state-of-the-art studio and lab facilities to enhance their learning experience in digital production and stay updated with the rapidly evolving media landscape. They will also learn from experienced media professionals who provide valuable insights and practical knowledge.

### Contact Information:
For more details or to apply, interested students can fill out the enquiry form or contact UPES directly. 

Overall, the BBA in Events, Public Relations, and Corporate Communications at UPES is a comprehensive program that prepares students for a successful career in the dynamic field of communications, making it an excellent choice for those looking to excel in this sector.
