Piyush Rawat is currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) since June 2022, within the School of Computer Science. He completed his Ph.D. in 2022 from the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Hamirpur, where he also earned his MTech in Computer Science and Technology in 2018. His research specialization lies in Wireless Sensor Networks and the Internet of Things (IoT), and he has a strong publication record, having authored research articles in SCI, Scopus, and ESCI-indexed journals.

Dr. Rawat has demonstrated a commitment to his professional development, qualifying for the Graduate Aptitude Test (GATE) in the years 2015, 2016, 2017, and 2019. He is passionate about teaching, viewing it as a mutually enriching experience that enhances both the instructor's and students' understanding of complex concepts. He believes that teaching is not just about delivering knowledge but involves engaging with learners to foster a deeper comprehension of the subject matter. This process of articulating and elucidating concepts not only aids in student learning but also reinforces his own grasp of the topics, ultimately contributing to his research efforts.

In addition to his teaching responsibilities, Dr. Rawat has actively participated in scholarly activities, presenting research papers at various international conferences and contributing to academic journals. His teaching philosophy emphasizes the importance of creating meaningful learning experiences that facilitate the exploration of knowledge boundaries.
