The **B. Tech. in VLSI Design and Technology** program at the UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to equip students with a robust foundation in microelectronics, focusing on the design and fabrication of next-generation integrated circuits and chips. The curriculum, spanning from 2023 to 2027, is strategically aligned with the **India Semiconductor Mission**, addressing global demands in semiconductor industries and core electronics chip manufacturing. 

### Key Program Features:
- **Industry-Relevant Curriculum**: The coursework covers a wide range of topics, including:
  - VLSI fabrication
  - ASIC (Application-Specific Integrated Circuit) and SoC (System-on-Chip) design
  - Low-power chip design
  - Advanced areas like quantum computing and AI-driven custom embedded chips.

- **Hands-on Experience**: Students gain practical exposure through:
  - Use of leading VLSI design tools such as Xilinx, AMD, Cadence, Synopsys, and Mentor Graphics.
  - Industrial internships, visits, and collaborations with global semiconductor companies.

- **Career Opportunities**: Graduates are prepared for various specialized roles in the semiconductor and VLSI industries, such as:
  - VLSI Interconnects
  - Analog Design Engineer
  - ASIC System Design Engineer
  - RF IC Design Engineer
  - IC Packaging and Testing Engineer
  - Mixed Signal Design Engineer
  - Memory Designer

- **Placement Success**: The program boasts a 100% placement rate, with graduates securing an average package of **5.1 LPA** and the highest package reaching **6.2 LPA**. This success is attributed to the program's strong industry connections and focus on producing industry-ready graduates.

### Eligibility Criteria:
- Minimum of **50% marks** in Classes X and XII, with at least **50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics** in Class XII.
- Admission is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.

### Program Structure:
- The program spans **eight semesters**, with courses including:
  - Digital System Design
  - Communication Skills
  - Network Engineering
  - Analog Electronics
  - Embedded Systems
  - AI and Machine Learning Applications
  - Capstone Projects
