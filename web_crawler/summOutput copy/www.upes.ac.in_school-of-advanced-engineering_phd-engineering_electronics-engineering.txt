The UPES School of Advanced Engineering is offering a Ph.D. program in Electronics Engineering designed to cultivate highly skilled researchers through a comprehensive academic journey. This program integrates coursework, teaching components, and original research, emphasizing the development of both theoretical and practical knowledge in various electronics engineering domains. The curriculum covers advanced topics such as semiconductor devices, VLSI, optical systems, signal processing, robotics, and next-generation networks like 5G/6G.

**Key Program Features:**
- **Research Focus**: Candidates will engage in original research leading to a dissertation, which includes formulating research questions, conducting experiments, and defending their findings before a committee.
- **Mentorship**: Ph.D. candidates will receive guidance from distinguished faculty members with backgrounds from prestigious institutions, ensuring a high-quality educational experience.
- **Financial Support**: Students holding NET-JRF or INSPIRE fellowships are eligible for an annual cash grant of ₹2,00,000. Additionally, participants may receive a monthly stipend of up to ₹30,000 during their studies.
- **Career Opportunities**: Graduates can pursue various career paths in academia, industry, and research, focusing on roles such as professors, electronics system designers, and specialists in VLSI, embedded systems, and robotics. The program also prepares students for advancements in AI and machine learning applications within electronics.
