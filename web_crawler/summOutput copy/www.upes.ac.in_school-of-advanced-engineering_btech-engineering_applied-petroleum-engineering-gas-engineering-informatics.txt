The B.Tech. Applied Petroleum Engineering program, specializing in Gas Engineering Informatics, is offered by the UPES School of Advanced Engineering. This program is designed to equip students with essential knowledge and practical skills needed in the rapidly evolving gas sector, which is experiencing a significant increase in global energy demand. 

**Program Overview:**
- **Specialization**: Gas Engineering Informatics
- **Key Focus Areas**: The curriculum emphasizes automation, smart grids, predictive maintenance systems, and energy optimization algorithms. It also highlights eco-friendly gas systems and the integration of renewable energy sources, aligning with the industry's sustainability goals.
- **Technological Integration**: Students will learn to leverage data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence to address real-time industry challenges.

**Curriculum Structure:**
- The program spans several semesters, covering core subjects such as:
  - Reservoir Engineering
  - Natural Gas Processing
  - Advanced Drilling Engineering
  - Data Analytics and AI in Petroleum
  - Pipeline Engineering and Flow
- Students will also engage in hands-on experiences through specialized labs (e.g., Petroleum Product Testing Lab, Drilling Fluid Lab) and industrial internships.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) in Class XII.
- Selection is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates have promising career prospects in various sectors, including:
- Exploration and Production
- Reservoir Engineering
- Gas Processing
- Data Analytics for oil and gas operations
- Roles in leading energy companies, consulting firms, research organizations, and government agencies.

**Program Highlights:**
- Experienced faculty with industry expertise.
- State-of-the-art laboratories and industry collaborations.
- Strong placement record with graduates securing lucrative opportunities.
