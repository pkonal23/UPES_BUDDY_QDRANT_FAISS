The B.Tech. in Chemical Engineering with a specialization in Energy Systems and Storage at UPES School of Advanced Engineering is a comprehensive undergraduate program designed to equip students with essential knowledge and skills in chemical engineering, focusing specifically on energy technologies. The four-year curriculum integrates theoretical learning, practical training, and research opportunities, preparing students for the evolving energy industry.

**Program Highlights:**
- **Hands-On Experience:** Students engage in extensive lab work, industrial visits, and internships, allowing them to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world challenges.
- **Industry Collaboration:** The curriculum is developed in partnership with industry experts, academia, and alumni, ensuring relevance to current industry demands.
- **Career Opportunities:** Graduates are well-prepared for roles such as Production Engineer, Design Engineer, Petroleum Engineer, and Data Analyst, among others, across sectors like Chemicals, Petrochemicals, Energy Solutions, and Research & Development.

**Curriculum Overview:**
The program spans eight semesters, covering core chemical engineering principles such as thermodynamics, process control, and transport phenomena. Specialized courses include renewable energy sources, fuel cells, and energy storage technologies, reflecting the latest advancements in sustainable energy. 

**Future Scope:**
The energy sector is rapidly evolving, with a growing demand for professionals skilled in clean energy technologies. The program emphasizes environmental sustainability and aligns with UN Sustainable Development Goals, preparing students to contribute to climate change mitigation efforts.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII. Admission is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.

**Contact Information:**
For inquiries or further information, students can fill out an enquiry form or contact the UPES School of Advanced Engineering directly. The program encourages prospective students to apply now to secure their future in the dynamic field of energy systems and storage.

Overall, the B.Tech. program at UPES is a robust platform for aspiring chemical engineers, providing them with the tools and experiences needed to succeed in a critical and rapidly changing industry.
