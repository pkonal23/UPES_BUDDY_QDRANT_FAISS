Dr. Kumar Shreshtha's teaching philosophy emphasizes the creation of an engaging and inclusive learning environment aimed at fostering critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a passion for lifelong learning among students. He adopts a student-centric approach that recognizes the diverse backgrounds and learning styles of his students. To promote active learning, he incorporates real-world examples and practical applications through interactive discussions, hands-on activities, and projects. 

Currently, Dr. Kumar teaches electives in Consumer Behaviour, Digital Marketing, and Marketing Analytics, alongside the core subject of Marketing Management. He has accumulated a total of 11.5 years of professional experience, with 9 years in the industry and 2.5 years in academia. His academic journey began after earning a Mechanical Engineering degree from Malviya National Institute of Technology, Jaipur, followed by an MBA from XLRI, Jamshedpur, where he focused on marketing. He has held senior management positions for 3 years post-MBA, specifically in business development and sales.

Before joining the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), he served as an assistant professor at the Vijay Patil School of Management (DY Patil University, Navi Mumbai). Dr. Kumar also worked at Tega Industries as a Senior Manager and spent 6 years at Essar Steel Ltd, where he was involved in various projects, operations, and sales functions. 

He completed his PhD at IIM Calcutta, focusing on “informal markets” within the marketing domain, after which he began his academic career in June 2022. Dr. Kumar is actively involved in research, having attended three international marketing conferences and published a case study and a book chapter with Springer. His research interests lie in macromarketing and informal markets, with plans to submit papers to top journals in these areas soon.
