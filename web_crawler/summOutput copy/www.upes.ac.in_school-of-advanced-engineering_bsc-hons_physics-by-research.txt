The program offered by the UPES School of Advanced Engineering is a comprehensive 3-year Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Physics, designed to equip students with a robust understanding of both theoretical and experimental physics, while also fostering critical thinking and research skills. The curriculum is structured into eight semesters, allowing for a maximum of 20 credits each semester, with multiple entry and exit options in accordance with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 guidelines.

### Program Structure:
- **Credit System**: Students can exit the program at various stages based on credits earned:
  - **40 Credits**: Certificate in the relevant discipline.
  - **80 Credits**: Diploma.
  - **120 Credits**: Full-fledged undergraduate degree (B.Sc.).
  - **160 Credits**: B.Sc. (Hons) or B.Sc. (Hons) with Research.

- **Specializations**: Students can choose from elective specializations such as Astronomy & Astrophysics, Computational Physics, and Materials Science & Nanotechnology in their final year. 

- **Research Opportunities**: The program emphasizes research, especially in the fourth year, where students can either focus on advanced domain-specific courses or engage in extensive research leading to a thesis defense.

### Curriculum Highlights:
- **Core Courses**: Include subjects like Classical Mechanics, Quantum Mechanics, Statistical Mechanics, Electromagnetic Theory, and more.
- **Skill Enhancement**: Courses such as Design Thinking, Environment Sustainability & Climate Change, and Scientific Communication are included to enhance students' practical skills.
- **Internships**: Opportunities for summer and social internships are provided to gain hands-on experience in real-world applications.

### Career Prospects:
Graduates from this program have promising career opportunities in:
- **Research & Development**: Positions as research associates or scientists in laboratories.
- **Industry Applications**: Roles in technology-driven sectors such as aerospace, renewable energy, and telecommunications.
- **Data Analysis & Consulting**: Utilizing their analytical skills in financial services and consulting.

### Admission Criteria:
- **Eligibility**: Minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII with a focus on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- **Selection Process**: Includes a personal interview or CUET (Common University Entrance Test).

### Contact Information:
For more details regarding the program, fee structure, or admission process, interested candidates are encouraged to reach out directly to the UPES School of Advanced Engineering.

This comprehensive program not only prepares students for immediate career opportunities but also lays a solid foundation for those wishing to pursue further studies, including M.Sc. or Ph.D. programs, both domestically and internationally, making it an excellent choice for aspiring physicists.
