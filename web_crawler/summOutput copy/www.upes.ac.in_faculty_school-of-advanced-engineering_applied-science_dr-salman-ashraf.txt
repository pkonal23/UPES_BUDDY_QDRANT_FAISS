Dr. Salman Ashraf is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), specifically within the School of Advanced Engineering. He completed his Ph.D. at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Mandi, where his research focused on the boundedness of linear operators on function spaces over local fields. Prior to this, he earned an M.Sc. in Mathematics from IIT Madras and a B.Sc. (Honours) in Mathematics from Jamia Millia Islamia in New Delhi. 

Dr. Ashraf's research interests encompass Harmonic Analysis, Operator Theory, and Function Spaces. He has also qualified the GATE examination in Mathematics in 2018. His teaching philosophy centers on a student-focused approach, where he aims to foster a strong understanding of mathematics and cultivate genuine interest among students. He emphasizes creating an inclusive and engaging learning environment that encourages critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and active participation.

In his role, Dr. Ashraf teaches courses such as Advanced Engineering Mathematics-1 and is dedicated to enhancing the educational experience of his students. He has contributed to the academic community through various publications in esteemed international journals.
