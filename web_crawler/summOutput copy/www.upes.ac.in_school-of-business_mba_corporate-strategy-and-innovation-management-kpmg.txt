The MBA Strategy & Consulting program at UPES, in collaboration with KPMG, is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in corporate strategy and innovation management. This program targets individuals interested in pursuing careers in business or consulting, providing flexibility in career paths based on personal strengths and interests.

### Course Structure and Details:
- **Program Focus**: The curriculum includes core courses such as Strategic Management, Innovation Management, Corporate Strategy, Business Ethics, and Corporate Social Responsibility. Students will also engage in practical components like case studies, major projects (dissertations), and summer internships to gain hands-on experience.
- **Technology Skills**: Students will develop proficiency in various software and analytical tools, including SAP, Six Sigma, advanced Excel, and R-studio, which are essential for modern business environments.
- **Eligibility Criteria**: Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and graduation from a recognized university. They must also meet specific entrance exam criteria, including scores from MAT, CMAT, CAT, XAT, GMAT, or NMAT.

### Career Prospects:
Graduates can expect to pursue diverse roles across various sectors, including:
- Strategic Planner
- Operations Manager
- Supply Chain Consultant
- Business Development Manager
- Change Management Consultant
- Entrepreneur/Start-up Founder
- Digital Transformation Manager
- Sustainability Manager

The program's focus on innovation management and change management prepares graduates to lead in a rapidly evolving business landscape. 

### Industry Trends:
The program aligns with current industry trends, emphasizing the importance of digital transformation, sustainability, and corporate social responsibility. Graduates are well-prepared for strategic leadership roles that drive growth and competitiveness.

### Placement Opportunities:
The UPES School of Business has seen significant improvements in placements, with notable companies such as KPMG, Amazon, HDFC Bank, Adani, and IBM actively recruiting graduates. The highest salary for MBA freshers has reached INR 30 Lac per annum, with the top 10% earning an average CTC of INR 18.06 Lac per annum.
