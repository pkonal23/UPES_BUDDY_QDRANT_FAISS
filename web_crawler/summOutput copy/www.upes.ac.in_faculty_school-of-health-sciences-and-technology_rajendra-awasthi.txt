Dr. Rajendra Awasthi is a distinguished associate professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) with over 18 years of experience in the field of pharmaceutical sciences. He holds a Ph.D. in Pharmaceutical Sciences from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Hyderabad, India. His expertise spans several areas, including novel drug delivery systems, nanomedicine, excipient development, and pharmaceutical product development, particularly focusing on micro and nano delivery systems.

Dr. Awasthi has made significant contributions to pharmaceutical research, evidenced by his inclusion in the top 2% of scientists globally, as recognized by Stanford University, California, and his biography being featured in Who’s Who in the World®. He has authored over 180 scientific papers published in high-impact journals, accumulating a cumulative impact factor exceeding 350 according to Clarivate Analytics. His innovative work includes the development of targeted drug delivery systems for neurodegenerative disorders and skin diseases such as psoriasis. He has also isolated and tested various natural polymers for applications in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and food.

In terms of academic roles, Dr. Awasthi has taught a variety of undergraduate and graduate courses, including novel drug delivery systems, biopharmaceutics, pharmacokinetics, advanced product development, and research methodology, with a particular emphasis on integrating AI into healthcare. His teaching philosophy emphasizes creating a dynamic and engaging learning environment that promotes collaboration, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. He aims to inspire students by incorporating real-world examples and research into his curriculum, fostering an atmosphere that values diverse perspectives and ethical conduct.

Dr. Awasthi has also supervised two Ph.D. scholars who have successfully completed their degrees and is currently mentoring five additional Ph.D. candidates, alongside more than 16 postgraduate students. His previous positions include roles at Amity University, the Laureate Institute of Pharmacy, and MIET, Meerut, as well as serving as a Professor and Principal at NKBR College of Pharmacy and Research Centre in Meerut. He began his professional career in 2006 with Torrent Pharmaceuticals in Ahmedabad.
