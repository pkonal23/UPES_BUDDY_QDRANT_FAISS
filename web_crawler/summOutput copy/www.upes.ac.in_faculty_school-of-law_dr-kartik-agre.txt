Dr. Kartik Agre is an Assistant Professor - Senior Scale at the School of Law, specializing in Family Law, Constitutional Law, Law of Contracts, and Law of Torts. His teaching philosophy emphasizes the importance of bridging theoretical knowledge with practical application, particularly in complex legal subjects like Family Law. Dr. Agre believes that students can better understand legal concepts when they engage with real-world scenarios and their implications. To facilitate this, he incorporates practical case studies, simulations, and interactive discussions into his teaching. He also enhances the educational experience by organizing special lectures with legal experts, providing students with insights from seasoned professionals in various areas of law.

Prior to his current position at UPES, Dr. Agre served as an Assistant Professor at Presidency University in Bengaluru and has also held positions at Arni University in Kangra, ICFAI University in Dehradun, and Geeta Institute of Law in Panipat. He has over 5.5 years of experience in legal education and has made significant contributions to the field through his research, authoring several reputable papers on key issues and advancements in Family Law.

Dr. Agre has delivered a distinguished lecture on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UNSDG), further demonstrating his commitment to integrating global perspectives into legal education. His academic achievements include being recognized as a merit scholar during his undergraduate studies.

In terms of recognition, Dr. Agre has excelled in various scholarly activities. He secured first place in a paper presentation at Mohanlal Sukhadia University, Udaipur, on June 15, 2021, and also achieved first position at a National Conference at ICFAI University, Dehradun, on June 8, 2019. Additionally, he has participated in quiz competitions, securing fourth place in the Inter College Constitutional Quiz organized by UPES on November 21, 2014, and first place in the First ICFAI Intra Constitutional Quiz held on November 26, 2014. In 2011, he achieved second place in Ad Zap (Advertisement Guru) at an event organized by IIPM Dehradun.
