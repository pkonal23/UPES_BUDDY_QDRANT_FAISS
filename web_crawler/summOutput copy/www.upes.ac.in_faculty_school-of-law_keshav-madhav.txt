Dr. Keshav Madhav is an Assistant Professor at UPES University in the School of Law, specializing in Constitutional Law and Family Law. He holds a BBA LLB (Hons) from ICFAI University, Dehradun, and an LLM from Amity Institute of Advanced Legal Studies, Noida, where he was awarded the Academic Excellence Medal. Additionally, he earned his Ph.D. from Radha Govind University in Jharkhand.

Before his tenure at UPES, Dr. Madhav served as an Assistant Professor at Amity University and Starex University. He has published several articles in reputable legal journals and has been recognized with an Academic Excellence Award during his LLM studies.

Dr. Madhav teaches a variety of courses, including Constitutional Law, Family Law, Law of Torts, Law of Contracts, Comparative Constitution, and Indian Penal Code.
