The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) has achieved specialized accreditation for two of its programs offered by the School of Business: the Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Oil and Gas Management and the Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Logistics and Supply Chain Management. This accreditation was granted in 2017 by the International Accreditation Council for Business Education (IACBE), based in the USA. Notably, UPES holds the distinction of being the only university in India to have received IACBE accreditation for these programs.
