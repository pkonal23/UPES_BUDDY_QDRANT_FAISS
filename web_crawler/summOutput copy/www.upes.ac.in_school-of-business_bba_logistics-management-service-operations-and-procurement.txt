The BBA in Logistics Management (Service Operations & Procurement) at the School of Business, UPES, provides a robust educational framework focused on service operations management. The program emphasizes critical areas such as service design, quality management, customer relationship management, and service innovation, equipping students with the necessary skills to optimize service processes and enhance customer experiences. The curriculum also covers strategies for improving service quality, managing projects, and mitigating supply chain risks within a service-oriented framework.

### Curriculum Overview:
- **Core Subjects** include:
  - Semester 1: Business Communication I, Business Mathematics, Operations Management, Meta 101
  - Semester 2: Financial Management, Business Accounting, Introduction to Multimodal Transportation
  - Semester 3: Organizational Behavior, Business Statistics, Climate Change (Theory)
  - Semester 4: Introduction to Logistics, Service Operations, Business Ethics and CSR
  - Semester 5: Logistics Planning & Strategy, Logistics Information System, Summer Internship, International Logistics
  - Semester 6: Customer Relationship Management, Dissertation I, Retail Logistics, Environmental Sustainability & Social Responsibility

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates of this program are well-prepared for various roles in the service sector, including positions in third-party logistics (3PL), airlines, hotels, banks, insurance, and EdTech firms. Potential job titles include:
- Supply Chain Analyst
- Key Account Manager
- Data Analyst
- Operations Executive
- Logistics Coordinator
- Project Manager
- Warehouse Manager
- Customer Service Manager

The program also opens pathways for advanced studies in management, with graduates often pursuing leadership courses.

### Industry Exposure:
The BBA in Logistics Management program emphasizes experiential learning through internships and live projects, providing students with hands-on experience in esteemed organizations. Graduates typically enjoy promising placement prospects, with starting salaries ranging from INR 4-10 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills and exposure.

### Admission Criteria:
To be eligible for the program, candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII. Selection is based on performance in entrance exams such as UPESMET-UG, UGAT, or CUET.

### Facilities and Global Exposure:
UPES offers state-of-the-art facilities, including smart classrooms and an Analytics Lab. Students have opportunities for global exposure through exchange programs with leading management institutes.
