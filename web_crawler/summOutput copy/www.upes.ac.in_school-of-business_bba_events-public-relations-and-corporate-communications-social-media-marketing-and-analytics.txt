The BBA program in Events, Public Relations, and Corporate Communications with a specialization in Social Media, Marketing, and Analytics at UPES is designed to prepare students for the evolving digital landscape of the communications industry. As businesses increasingly shift towards digital platforms, there is a growing demand for skilled professionals in areas such as social media management, digital marketing, public relations, event management, and data analysis. Graduates of this program can expect to have diverse career opportunities in notable media houses like Times of India, Hindustan Times, Indian Express, and various content start-ups. 

### Academic and Industry Insights:
The curriculum emphasizes hands-on experience through internships, on-the-job training, and major projects, which are crucial for success in the media and entertainment sectors. Students will develop a well-rounded skill set combining creativity, communication, and analytical abilities, essential for thriving in corporate communications and event management. The program aims to equip students with the latest tools and techniques in digital marketing, focusing on strategic communications and event management. 

### Career Prospects:
Graduates can pursue roles such as:
- Social Media Manager
- Digital Marketing Specialist
- PR Executive
- Event Coordinator
- Corporate Communications Officer
- Marketing Analyst

The expected starting salary for freshers ranges from INR 4 to 10 lakh per annum, depending on the candidate's skills and industry exposure.

### Admission Process:
Candidates interested in the program must meet specific eligibility criteria and undergo a selection process based on entrance exams such as UPESMET-UG, UGAT, or CUET. The program requires a minimum of 50% in 10th and 12th grades, regardless of the stream.

### Program Highlights:
- Access to state-of-the-art studio and lab facilities for comprehensive digital production learning.
- Learning from experienced media professionals who provide valuable insights and practical knowledge.
- Focus on emerging trends in social media, marketing, and analytics.

### Contact Information:
For further inquiries or to apply, interested candidates can fill out an enquiry form, including their first name, last name, email address, and mobile number. 

This program is an excellent choice for those looking to build a rewarding career in the communications sector, aligning with the industry's demand for skilled professionals equipped with a comprehensive understanding of digital marketing and social media strategies.
