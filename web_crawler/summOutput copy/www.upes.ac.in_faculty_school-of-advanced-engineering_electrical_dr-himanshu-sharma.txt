Dr. Himanshu Sharma is an Assistant Professor at the School of Advanced Engineering, specializing in the field of Electrical Engineering with a focus on polynomials and their iterations. His research primarily investigates the irreducibility and monogenity of polynomial iterates, contributing to the field through publications in reputable journals.

Dr. Sharma holds a Ph.D. in Algebraic Number Theory from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi, where he also served as a teaching assistant for various pure mathematics courses during his doctoral studies. Since joining the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), he has taught Advanced Engineering Mathematics I to first-semester B.Tech Computer Science students and Basic Mathematics to first-semester BCA students during the July to December 2024 term.

His teaching philosophy is rooted in the belief that education should inspire critical thinking and creativity among students, equipping them with the necessary skills and values to address real-world challenges. Dr. Sharma views education as a transformative journey that shapes both the intellect and character of students, aiming to cultivate ethical and innovative contributors to society.

In recognition of his academic contributions, Dr. Sharma received funding of 150,000 INR from IIT Delhi to attend a conference in France during his Ph.D. Additionally, he was awarded an early-doc fellowship from IIT Delhi from May 1st to July 31st. He has actively participated in numerous international conferences and workshops, both in India and abroad, and has delivered talks at various seminars.
