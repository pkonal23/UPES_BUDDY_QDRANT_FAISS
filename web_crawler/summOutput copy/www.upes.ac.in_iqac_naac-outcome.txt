The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) has conducted multiple accreditation assessments for the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). 

- The first peer team visit for the 1st Cycle occurred from November 10 to 15, 2008, resulting in UPES receiving a Grade B accreditation with a Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) of 2.72.
  
- A re-assessment for the 1st Cycle took place from September 4 to 7, 2013, where UPES improved its accreditation status, achieving a CGPA of 2.79.

- The 2nd Cycle of NAAC accreditation was conducted from February 25 to 27, 2021. In this assessment, UPES was awarded a Grade A accreditation with a CGPA of 3.02, which is valid from March 1, 2021, to February 28, 2026.

This information highlights UPES's progress in accreditation over the years, showcasing its commitment to maintaining and improving educational standards.
