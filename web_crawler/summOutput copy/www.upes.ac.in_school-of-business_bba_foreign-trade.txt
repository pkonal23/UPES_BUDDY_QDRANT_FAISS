The UPES School of Business offers a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Foreign Trade program designed to equip students with comprehensive knowledge and skills in international business and trade. This program emphasizes practical learning through major projects, summer internships, and live projects with companies, thereby providing hands-on experience in the field of foreign trade.

### Program Structure:
- **Semesters Breakdown**:
  - **Semester 1**: Introduction to key business disciplines including Microeconomics, Business Accounting, and Financial Management.
  - **Semester 2**: Focuses on Business Statistics, Governance, and critical thinking skills.
  - **Semester 3**: Covers Organizational Behavior, Business Mathematics, and Environmental Sustainability.
  - **Semester 4**: Includes Technologies of Business Communication and Business Ethics.
  - **Semester 5**: Concentrates on International Trade, CSR, and Trade Negotiations.
  - **Semester 6**: Involves advanced topics like Trade Analytics, Dissertation, and specialized courses.

### Specializations:
Students can choose from various specializations including:
- International Trade
- Analytics and Market Research

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates can pursue diverse roles such as:
- International Trade Specialist
- Export/Import Coordinator
- Customs Compliance Officer
- Global Logistics Manager
- Trade Analyst or Consultant

The program prepares students for employment in multinational corporations, government agencies, logistics firms, and international organizations. Starting salaries for fresh graduates in this field typically range from INR 6-10 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills and exposure.

### Industry Trends:
The field of foreign trade is evolving due to factors such as globalization, digital transformation, changing trade policies, and a growing focus on sustainability. Professionals are encouraged to stay updated on these trends and engage in continuous professional development.

### Learning Environment:
UPES provides state-of-the-art facilities, including smart classrooms and an Analytics Lab, alongside opportunities for student exchange and study abroad programs with global partner institutes. The curriculum integrates real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts to enhance practical understanding.
