Professor Nilav Mazumdar is a seasoned expert in the field of Creative and Communication, specializing in visual strategy, content planning, branding, and advertising. With over a decade of industry experience, he has provided creative and communication solutions to prominent brands including Mondelez (formerly Cadbury), FICCI, Hewlett Packard, and Tata Motors. 

His teaching philosophy emphasizes the importance of creating a learning environment that fosters personal growth, practical knowledge, and strategic thinking. Prof. Mazumdar aims to develop not just creative professionals but well-rounded creative individuals. He has over five years of teaching experience and is currently an Assistant Professor at the School of Design.

His areas of expertise include advertising, visual communications, motion graphics, design management, elements of video and filmmaking, storytelling, and creative writing. 

For further inquiries, he can be contacted via email at nmazumdar@ddn.upes.ac.in.
