Prof. Dr. G. G. Gangadharan is a prominent figure in the field of Ayurveda, recognized for his contributions to establishing centers of excellence for this traditional practice in Bangalore and other locations. He has been a pioneer in conducting rigorous scientific analyses, developing innovative policies, and advocating for effective citizen engagement to promote a cleaner, safer, and healthier environment.

Dr. Gangadharan is a Fellow of Ashoka Innovators for the Public and the Ashoka Trust in the USA. He holds a PhD from Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth in Pune and a Master of Management degree from McGill University in Canada. Additionally, he is a Fellow of the Rashtriya Ayurveda Vidyapeeth.

Currently, he serves as the Director of the Tekri Ayurveda Center. He has also made significant contributions to the field as the editor of the well-known Indian journal, ANCIENT SCIENCE OF LIFE, and has coordinated an all-India program focused on Local Health Traditions and Ayurveda, which played a crucial role in reviving Indian Systems of Medicine and Local Health Traditions.

Prof. Dr. Gangadharan began his career with Arya Vaidya Pharmacy in Coimbatore, where he was instrumental in developing new dosage forms and traditional Ayurveda formulations, encompassing both classical and proprietary methods. He is recognized as an expert in authentic Ayurvedic diagnostic methods, particularly pulse diagnosis, and has successfully treated numerous lifestyle disorders.

His dedication to propagating Ayurvedic knowledge and promoting Local Health Traditions is evident through his advocacy for integrating Ayurveda and traditional healthcare systems into contemporary medical practices. He has initiated both basic and applied research in traditional medicine, expanding the horizons of healthcare research and drug development.
