Manisha Solanki is an Assistant Professor at the School of Business, UPES, Dehradun, where she has been serving since July 2017. She holds a Bachelor's degree (B.Sc. Hons.) from CCS Haryana Agriculture University, Haryana, a Master's degree (M.Sc.) in Advertising Management & Public Relations from GJ University of Science and Technology, Haryana, and is currently pursuing a Doctorate (D.Phil.) at Martin Luther University, Halle-Wittenberg, Germany.

She has been involved in significant research projects, including serving as Co-Principal Investigator (Co-PI) on the project titled "Improving Teaching Effectiveness in E-Learning Environment," which is supported by an international competitive research grant, the David A. Wilson Award for Excellence and Innovation in Higher Education Teaching & Learning (2017-2018). This project received funding of INR 17,00,000 for a duration of two years (2018-20). Additionally, she is also a Co-PI on a project for the development of a blended module aimed at enhancing critical thinking skills in law graduate programs, funded by UPES SEED Grant with an amount of 1.5 Lakh.

Her research interests include Social/Digital Media Marketing, E-Learning, and Sustainability. She has contributed to various publications, such as the article "Bio-Vitrimers for Sustainable Circular Bio-Economy," published in the journal Polymers (2022), and has co-authored papers on innovative online teaching practices and time investment in online teaching. 

Solanki has received recognition for her work, notably the David A. Wilson Award, which underscores her commitment to enhancing teaching effectiveness in the e-learning space. Her academic contributions reflect a strong focus on improving educational methodologies and integrating sustainability into her research.
