Dr. Anil Kumar, a distinguished educator and a prominent member of the "WInSAR (Western North America Interferometric Synthetic Aperture Radar Consortium)" Executive Committee as Adjunct II, is affiliated with UNAVCO. He also serves as a guest faculty member at the Indian Institute of Remote Sensing (IIRS), ISRO, located in Dehradun, India. Dr. Kumar was selected as the Lead Speaker for the NISAR (NASA ISRO Synthetic Aperture Radar) Science Workshop held in 2015 at the Space Applications Centre (SAC) of ISRO in Ahmedabad, India. Additionally, he has held the position of Principal Investigator at the Space Application Center, ISRO.
