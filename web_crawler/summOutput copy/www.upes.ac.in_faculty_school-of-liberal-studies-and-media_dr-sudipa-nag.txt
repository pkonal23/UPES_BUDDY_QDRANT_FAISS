Dr. Sudipa Nag is an Assistant Professor (SG) at the School of Liberal Studies and Media, where she emphasizes student-centric learning that is heavily focused on problem-solving. She recognizes the significance of classroom learning in connecting students with real-world applications, aiming to facilitate their understanding of concepts and enhance engagement in classroom activities. Dr. Nag is dedicated to helping students appreciate the relevance of their learning in real-life situations, ultimately contributing to their overall happiness.

**Academic Background:**
- **Master’s Degree:** Specialization in Developmental Psychology and Guidance and Counselling Psychology from Gauhati University.
- **Ph.D.:** Focused on Organizational Behavior from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati, with a thesis titled “Influence Strategies and Gender Differences.”

**Work Experience:**
Before her tenure at UPES, Dr. Nag served as a Research Assistant in the Organizational Behavior (OB) and Human Resource Management (HRM) area at the Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Ranchi. Her notable project involved “Classifying human emotions using thermal imaging.” She has also collaborated with various IT companies in Bangalore, developing questionnaires for targeted studies and data analysis.

**Research Interests:**
Dr. Nag's research spans several areas, including:
- Organizational Behavior
- Gender Differences
- Positive Psychology
- Neuropsychology
- Mental Health
- Social Psychology

She has published her research in esteemed journals such as the IIMB Management Review, Psychological Studies, and the International Journal of Psychology. Additionally, she has presented her work at multiple national and international conferences.

**Courses Taught:**
Currently, Dr. Nag teaches Applied Social Psychology to undergraduate students at UPES.
