Mr. Suvojit Dhara is an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science, UPES Dehradun, specializing in Data Science and data-driven multi-criteria decision-making. He holds a B.Sc. (Hons) in Mathematics from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, and both an M.Sc. and a Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur, where he achieved top honors in his master's program. His academic journey is marked by notable achievements, including receiving the prestigious Prime Minister Research Fellowship during his Ph.D. and the Institute Silver Medal for academic excellence.

His research interests encompass a wide range of topics, including Multi-Attribute Decision-Making, Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Student Performance Assessment, and various programming languages such as Java and Python. He has authored several scholarly articles and book chapters in reputable journals and publishers, including Elsevier, Springer Nature, and Taylor & Francis. His commitment to continuous learning and improvement reflects his dedication to adapting to the evolving educational landscape and inspiring a lifelong passion for learning among his students.
