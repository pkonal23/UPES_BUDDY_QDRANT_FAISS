Avni Joshi is an Assistant Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), specializing in animation and design education. Prior to her current role, she served as an Assistant Professor at Avantika University in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, where she taught digital tools in the B. Design program. Additionally, she managed the Animation & Multimedia department at Apeejay Stya University in Gurugram, Haryana.

### Professional Experience
Avni has nearly eight years of experience in the design process, disruptive innovation research, new design development, and higher education. Her expertise encompasses:
- 3D Animation Design
- 3D Rigging
- Game Design
- Creating animations for various media platforms including film, television, advertising, and video games.

She employs a mix of design software, hand-drawn techniques, and stop-motion animation to produce compelling animations that effectively convey complex emotions and narratives.

### Teaching Philosophy
Avni's teaching philosophy emphasizes the importance of providing students with the freedom to explore their passions while equipping them with essential skills for success in the animation industry. She believes in nurturing each learner's unique personality and perspective on design innovation. Her approach includes:
- Encouraging experimentation and risk-taking.
- Integrating real-world experiences and industry contacts into the curriculum.
- Motivating students to challenge limitations and pursue their creative potential.
