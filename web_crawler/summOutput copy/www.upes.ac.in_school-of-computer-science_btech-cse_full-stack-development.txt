The **B.Tech. Computer Science & Engineering (Full Stack Development)** program, offered by the **UPES School of Computer Science** in collaboration with **Xebia** since 2022, is designed to equip students with a comprehensive skill set essential for success in the rapidly evolving field of Full Stack Development. This four-year curriculum emphasizes both front-end and back-end development while integrating advanced artificial intelligence technologies.

### Program Overview
- **Curriculum Structure**: The program spans eight semesters, covering a wide array of subjects, including foundational courses in computer science, programming, web development, AI fundamentals, natural language processing, deep learning, and cloud computing. Students will engage in hands-on projects and gain industry exposure to ensure they are prepared for real-world challenges.
- **Key Subjects**: 
  - Data Structures and Algorithms
  - Object-Oriented Programming
  - Python Programming
  - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  - Cloud Computing
  - Frontend Development (React, Angular)
  - Backend Development (Node.js, Java)
  - Database Management Systems

### Career Prospects
Graduates of this program will possess unique qualifications that blend full-stack development skills with AI expertise, opening doors to various career paths such as:
- AI Software Engineer
- Machine Learning Engineer
- Full Stack Developer with AI Integration
- AI Product Manager
- Data Scientist

The program is poised to meet the growing demand for developers who can seamlessly integrate AI technologies into applications, enhancing user experiences and operational efficiencies.

### Admission Criteria
To be eligible for admission, candidates must meet the following requirements:
- Minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII
- A minimum of 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) in Class XII
- Selection will be based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.
