Dr. Rajesh Maithani is a Senior Associate Professor in the School of Advanced Engineering at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), with a robust academic and research background in mechanical engineering, particularly in heat transfer and renewable energy systems. He can be contacted via email at rmaithani@ddn.upes.ac.in.

**Educational Background and Experience:**
Dr. Maithani earned his Ph.D. from Uttarakhand Technical University, Dehradun, in 2017. With over 17 years of teaching and research experience, he has dedicated approximately 13 years to DIT University as an Assistant Professor in the Mechanical Engineering Department before transitioning to UPES. He also has one year of industrial experience.

**Courses Taught:**
Dr. Maithani has taught a diverse array of courses, including but not limited to:
- Engineering Thermodynamics
- Heat and Mass Transfer
- Convective Heat Transfer
- Power Plant Engineering
- Internal Combustion Engines
- Advanced Internal Combustion Engines

**Research Interests:**
His research interests are primarily centered around:
- Heat transfer and analysis of heat exchangers
- Renewable energy, with a specific focus on solar thermal systems
- Experimental analysis of thermal systems
- Various optimization techniques

Dr. Maithani has an impressive publication record, with over 70 research papers in prestigious national and international journals, contributions to several book chapters, and more than 10 patents.

**Awards and Grants:**
He has received the Excellence in Research award from DIT University in 2019 and from UPES in both 2021 and 2022. Additionally, he secured SEED funding for his project titled "Design and Optimization of Solar Thermal Systems."

**Scholarly Activities:**
Dr. Maithani is actively involved in collaborative research, both nationally and internationally, focusing on innovative heat transfer techniques and renewable energy solutions. He is currently guiding two Ph.D. scholars and has successfully supervised 14 M.Tech dissertations. His scholarly contributions include approximately 70 research articles, numerous conference presentations, and a significant number of patents.

**Teaching Philosophy:**
Dr. Maithani's teaching philosophy emphasizes creating a student-centered, practical, and inclusive learning environment. He aims to foster critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and ethical professionalism among his students, equipping them with the necessary knowledge and skills to excel in mechanical engineering and contribute positively to society.

In summary, Dr. Rajesh Maithani is a dedicated educator and researcher with significant contributions to the fields of heat transfer and renewable energy, supported by a solid teaching philosophy aimed at student development and engagement.
