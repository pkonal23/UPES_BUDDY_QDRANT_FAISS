The BBA program in Events, Public Relations, and Corporate Communications with a specialization in Social Media, Marketing, and Analytics at UPES School of Business is designed to prepare students for a dynamic career in the rapidly evolving communications industry. As businesses increasingly shift towards digital platforms, there is a growing demand for skilled professionals proficient in social media, marketing, and analytics. Graduates can pursue various career paths, including roles such as social media manager, digital marketing specialist, public relations executive, event coordinator, corporate communications officer, and marketing analyst.

**Program Overview:**
- The curriculum emphasizes hands-on experience through industry internships, on-the-job training, and major projects, equipping students with practical skills for the media and entertainment industry or for further academic pursuits such as doctoral research.
- The program focuses on developing a well-rounded skill set that combines creativity, communication, and analytical abilities, essential for success in events, public relations, and corporate communications.
- Key themes include social media handling, campaign development, market research, data analytics, and PR strategies, ensuring graduates are adept at utilizing various social media tools and conducting high-quality digital market research.

**Career Prospects:**
- Graduates have excellent employment prospects, with opportunities available at renowned media houses such as Times of India, Hindustan Times, Indian Express, TV Today, NDTV, and various medium-sized content start-ups.
- Starting salaries for freshers range from INR 4 to 10 lakh per annum, depending on individual capabilities and industry exposure.

**Admission Process:**
- The selection process for this program requires candidates to meet minimum eligibility criteria, including a combination of 50% in 10th and 12th grades from any stream.
- Interested students must apply through the UPESMET-UG entrance exam, UGAT, or CUET.

**Contact Information:**
For more details or inquiries, prospective students can fill out an enquiry form or contact the UPES School of Business directly.

This comprehensive program not only prepares students for immediate employment but also empowers them to strategically navigate and contribute to the success of modern businesses and organizations in a digital-first world.
