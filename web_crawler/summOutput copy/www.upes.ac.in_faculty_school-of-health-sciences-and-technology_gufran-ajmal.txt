**Profile Summary of Dr. Gufran Ajmal**

**Current Position:**
Dr. Gufran Ajmal serves as an Assistant Professor (Selection Grade) at the School of Health Sciences and Technology (SoHST), University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun. His email contact is gufran.ajmal@ddn.upes.ac.in.

**Educational Background:**
- **B. Pharm** from Jamia Hamdard, New Delhi.
- **M. Pharm** and **Ph.D.** in Pharmaceutics from the Department of Pharmaceutical Engineering & Technology, IIT (BHU), Varanasi. He qualified the Graduate Pharmacy Aptitude Test (GPAT) in 2010, achieving an All India Rank (AIR) of 30.

**Research Interests:**
Dr. Ajmal's research focuses on:
- Novel drug delivery systems.
- Enhancement of drug solubility.
- Rate-controlled 3D structure-oriented delivery systems for wound healing.

**Publications:**
Dr. Ajmal has contributed significantly to the field through various research articles and book chapters. Key publications include:
1. "Metronidazole Loaded Polycaprolactone-Carbopol Blends Based Biodegradable Intrapocket Dental Film for Local Treatment of Periodontitis," published in Drug Delivery Letter (2021).
2. "Application of Electrospun Nanofiber in Wound Healing: Recent Trends and Patents Analysis," published in the International Journal of Life Science and Pharma Research (2023).
3. "Formulation, optimization, hemocompatibility and pharmacokinetic evaluation of PLGA nanoparticles containing paclitaxel," published in Drug Development and Industrial Pharmacy (2019).

He has also edited several books, including contributions to "Drug Delivery Systems for Metabolic Disorders" (2022) and "Novel Drug Delivery System" (2022).

**Teaching Experience:**
Dr. Ajmal has taught various subjects to B. Pharm students, such as:
- Physical Pharmaceutics I & II (Theory & Practical)
- Pharmaceutical Microbiology (Theory & Practical)
- Biopharmaceutics and Pharmacokinetics (Theory)
- Industrial Pharmacy II (Theory)
- Novel Drug Delivery System (Theory)

**Teaching Philosophy:**
Dr. Ajmal emphasizes creating a dynamic learning environment that fosters inquiry, critical thinking, and collaboration. He values diverse perspectives and aims to inspire students to engage actively in their learning journey, promoting a lifelong commitment to professional growth in pharmacy.

**Work Experience:**
Before his current role at UPES, Dr. Ajmal worked at:
- Apeejay Stya University, Gurugram, Haryana (August 2020 – January 2024).
- Bharat Institute of Technology, Meerut, U.P. (August 2019 – August 2020).
- Cognizant Technology Solutions India Pvt. Ltd., Kolkata (December 2012 – September 2014).

**Scholarly Activities:**
Dr. Ajmal is actively involved in the academic community as a reviewer for several prestigious journals, contributing to the advancement of pharmaceutical sciences.
