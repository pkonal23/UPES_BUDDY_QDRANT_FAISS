Dr. Anand Kumar is a faculty member at the School of Computer Science, with a strong focus on research and education in the fields of underwater acoustic communication and networking, channel estimation, underwater target detection and localization, machine learning, and analog signal processing. His work aims to enhance data transmission and signal reliability in underwater environments, contributing to advancements in technology and exploration.

**Work Experience:**
Dr. Kumar has held significant positions, including:
- **Project Associate**: Worked on underwater sensor networks from October 2021 to January 2023, supported by the Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB) under the Department of Science & Technology (DST), Government of India. His contributions during this period were notable in advancing research in this specialized field.
- **Assistant Professor**: Served at the School of Information and Communication Technology, Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida, from October 2023 to May 2024. He was actively involved in curriculum development and student mentorship, demonstrating a commitment to academic excellence.
