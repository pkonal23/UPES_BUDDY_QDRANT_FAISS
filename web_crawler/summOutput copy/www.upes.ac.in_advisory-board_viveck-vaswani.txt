Viveck Vaswani is a prominent film producer and an alumnus of Campion School and Cathedral and John Connon. He began his career in acting, featuring in India's first television soap opera, 'Khandaan'. Vaswani transitioned into production with his work on the Marathi film 'Kis Bai Kiss' and a significant television series titled 'Nai Dishayen', where he starred alongside notable actors such as Navin Nischol, Anju Mahendroo, Girish Karnad, Parikshat Sahni, Sharon Prabhakar, and Jayant Kripalani.

He produced the film 'Gawaahi', marking the directorial debut of Anant Balani, and the film featured renowned actors including Zeenat Aman, Tanuja, and Vikram Gokhale, along with India's Oscar-nominated directors Shekhar Kapur and Ashutosh Gowariker. Throughout his career, Vaswani has acted in over 100 films.

In collaboration with GP Sippy, he produced three films under Sippy Films: 'Patthar Ke Phool', 'Raju Ban Gaya Gentleman', and 'Aatish', where he also took on minor roles. Notably, he played a significant role in launching the careers of prominent actors such as Raveena Tandon and Shah Rukh Khan, and provided opportunities for directors Sanjay Gupta and Sanjay Gadhvi, who both assisted on 'Patthar Ke Phool'. 

Vaswani's contributions to the film industry have been substantial, both in front of and behind the camera, solidifying his legacy in Indian cinema.
