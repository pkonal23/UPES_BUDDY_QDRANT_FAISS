The International Accreditation Council for Business Education (IACBE) is headquartered in Olathe, Kansas, USA, and boasts member institutions across more than 20 countries globally. The IACBE employs a set of accrediting principles that focus on eight critical areas: outcomes evaluation, strategic planning, curriculum, faculty, scholarly and professional activities, resources, internal and external partnerships, and educational innovation. These principles serve as the foundation for evaluating business programs.

The IACBE is responsible for accrediting a wide range of academic programs, including associate's, bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees, specifically in business and related fields. This accreditation is applicable to institutions offering undergraduate and/or graduate degree programs worldwide.
