The program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a specialized B.Tech. in Aerospace Engineering with a focus on Defence Technology. This curriculum is designed to prepare graduates for promising careers in the defense and aerospace sectors, emphasizing industry-relevant training, internships, and hands-on projects that provide exposure to cutting-edge technologies and real-world challenges. Graduates are highly sought after by leading companies in the defense sector and related industries due to the comprehensive and rigorous nature of the program.

### Key Program Details:
- **Specialization**: Defence Technology
- **Curriculum Focus**: 
  - Weapon systems
  - Military communication systems
  - Radar technology
  - Cybersecurity
  - Advanced defense technologies
- **Objective**: To produce skilled engineers capable of driving technological advancements in the defense industry and supporting the modernization of armed forces.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates can pursue various roles, including:
- Space Mission Design Engineers
- Propulsion Engineers
- Aircraft Design & Analysis Engineers
- Aircraft Production Engineers
- Aerospace Modeling & Simulation Engineers
- Air Defense Systems Engineers
- Aircraft Maintenance Engineers
- Scientists in Defense Research and Development Organizations
- Technology Specialists and Research and Development Associates
- Positions in specialized fields like Space Warfare

### Program Structure:
The program is divided into eight semesters, covering a range of subjects and practical labs, including:
- Aerodynamics
- Propulsion Systems
- Aircraft Systems and Instruments
- Computational Mechanics
- Flight Mechanics
- Engineering Mathematics
- Various specialized labs such as Aircraft Structures Lab and Propulsion Lab

### Eligibility Criteria:
- Minimum 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII.
- Admission is based on UPESEAT / JEE Mains / Board Merit / SAT / CUET performance.

### Placement and Industry Connections:
UPES boasts an impressive placement record, offering excellent placement assistance and industry connections to enhance career prospects. The program is aligned with the Indian government's initiatives like "Make in India," promoting indigenous defense manufacturing, thereby ensuring that graduates can find rewarding careers in defense manufacturing and related fields.
