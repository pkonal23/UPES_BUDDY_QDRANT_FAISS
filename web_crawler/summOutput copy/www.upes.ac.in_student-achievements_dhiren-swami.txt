Dhiren Swami, a recent graduate from the B.Des. Transportation Mobility Design program (2018-22), was awarded 1st Prize for his exceptional design of the B22 electric bike at the ADI Pune Design Festival 2023. His mentor, Associate Professor Nitin Sharma from the School of Design, guided him through the project, which he described as an enriching journey filled with valuable learning experiences. Swami emphasized that the project aimed to address the mobility needs of the new generation, starting with the development of user scenarios and sketches, and culminating in the detailed rendering of the innovative bike model. This achievement highlights both Swami's talent and the educational support he received during his studies.
