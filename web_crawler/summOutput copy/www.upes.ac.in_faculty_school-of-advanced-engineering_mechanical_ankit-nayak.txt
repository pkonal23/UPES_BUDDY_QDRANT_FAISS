Dr. Ankit Nayak is an Assistant Professor (SG) at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun, India, where he specializes in robotics, automation, CAD, and additive manufacturing. His academic journey includes a PhD from IIITDM, Jabalpur, where he achieved top rank in his coursework and received the D&M Proficiency Gold Medal Award for the best cross-disciplinary project. Following his PhD, he served as an Assistant Professor at the School of Automation, Banasthali Vidyapith in Rajasthan from 2020 to 2022, and then as a Postdoctoral Fellow at the University of Hong Kong in 2022.

Dr. Nayak's teaching philosophy emphasizes making complex engineering concepts intuitive and accessible. He teaches a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate courses, including Introduction to Robotics, Industrial and Service Robots, Micro and Nano Robots, Advanced Robotics, Additive Manufacturing, and Manufacturing Science. His approach integrates theoretical knowledge with practical skills, utilizing simulations, working models, mathematical frameworks, and programming to help students connect new material to real-world applications. He is committed to fostering a passion for lifelong learning and innovation among his students by introducing the latest advancements in the field.

In terms of scholarly activities, Dr. Nayak has received multiple awards for his research contributions, including a best paper award at the CIMS 2020 conference for his work on 3D printing and the Best Presentation Award at the PHENMA 2019 conference in Vietnam. He has also filed two U.S. patents related to implementing automation in surgery and collaborates with international startups and academic institutions to address research gaps. Dr. Nayak is in the process of establishing a laboratory focused on computer-assisted surgery, which will explore applications of artificial intelligence in CAD modeling.
