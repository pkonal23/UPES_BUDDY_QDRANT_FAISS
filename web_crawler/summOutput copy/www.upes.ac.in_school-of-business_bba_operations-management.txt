The BBA in Operations Management program at UPES School of Business offers a comprehensive education designed to prepare students for successful careers in various operational roles across industries. The program spans three years, with a curriculum that covers essential topics such as operations management, supply chain management, inventory management, quality management, lean manufacturing, six sigma, project management, business analytics, and data science. This diverse coursework equips students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary for managing and optimizing operations within organizations.

**Program Highlights:**
- The curriculum includes real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts, enhancing students' understanding of operations management.
- Students are required to complete two mandatory internships, providing hands-on experience in both the social and corporate sectors.
- The program encourages participation in major projects, summer internships, and live projects, ensuring that students gain practical exposure to real-world challenges.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates of the BBA in Operations Management can pursue a wide range of career paths, including:
- Operations Manager
- Supply Chain Manager
- Inventory Manager
- Quality Manager
- Lean Manufacturing Manager
- Six Sigma Manager
- Project Manager
- Business Analyst
- Data Scientist

The demand for operations professionals is expected to grow, driven by trends such as the expansion of e-commerce, automation, data analytics, and sustainability initiatives. Starting salaries for fresh graduates range from INR 6 to 30 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills and experience.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
To apply for the program, candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII. Admission is based on performance in entrance exams such as UPESMET-UG, UGAT’25, or CUET.

**Global Opportunities:**
Students have the chance to participate in global programs, including a semester abroad at the University of California, Berkeley, and student exchange programs at top universities worldwide. There are also progression opportunities for master’s programs with Nottingham Trent University and ULaw Business School.
