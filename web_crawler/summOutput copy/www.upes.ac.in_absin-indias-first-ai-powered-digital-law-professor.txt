ABSIN is India's first AI-powered digital law professor, developed as a digital twin of Abhishek Sinha, who serves as the Associate Dean and Professor at the School of Law, UPES (University of Petroleum and Energy Studies). This innovative tool aims to revolutionize legal education by integrating extensive legal expertise and providing students with personalized and interactive tutorials.

ABSIN is positioned to enhance the learning experience for law students by leveraging advanced AI technology to offer tailored educational content. This initiative marks a significant advancement in the field of legal education in India, promising to pave the way for a more efficient and engaging learning environment.

For further details regarding ABSIN and its offerings, including eligibility criteria and other relevant information, please refer to the official UPES website or contact the School of Law directly.
