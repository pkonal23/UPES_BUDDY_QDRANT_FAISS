The MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) program at UPES School of Business is a comprehensive two-year course aimed at preparing graduates for leadership roles in the power and renewable energy sectors. The program combines core business disciplines with specialized knowledge in power and renewable energy, focusing on essential skills such as leadership, strategic thinking, and decision-making.

### Program Highlights:
- **Learning Environment**: The management school features modern smart classrooms, an analytics lab, a well-equipped library, and advanced analytical tools, fostering a technology-driven educational experience.
- **Hands-On Learning**: The curriculum emphasizes practical experiences through major projects, summer internships, and live projects with industry partners, ensuring students gain real-world insights into power business and regulations.
- **Specialization**: The program offers a specialization in Power Business and Regulation, covering efficient power management, regulations, project financing, energy conservation, and policy challenges in the energy industry, including climate change and rising energy demands.
- **Curriculum Integration**: Students will learn data-driven decision-making skills, including data mining, predictive analytics, machine learning, and data visualization, using cutting-edge software and tools like Excel, SPSS, Python, and R-studio.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates can pursue diverse roles such as:
- Power and Green Energy Regulatory and Financial Consultant
- Energy Efficiency Consultant
- Business Development Professional
- ESG Specialist
- Sustainability Consultant
- Electricity Network Designer

The program also prepares students for mid and senior management roles in power consulting, financing, and business development both in India and internationally, addressing a significant shortage of skilled professionals in the power sector.

### Admission Criteria:
- A minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.
- Eligible scores from national level exams such as MAT, CMAT, CAT, XAT, GMAT, or NMAT can exempt students from the UPES Management Entrance Test, leading to direct Group Discussion and Interview rounds.

### Placement Outcomes:
The MBA program has recorded impressive placement results, with graduates securing positions in renowned companies like Accenture, Infosys, Wipro, CRISIL, and Deloitte. Starting salaries range from INR 7 to 30 lakhs per annum, depending on individual skills and exposure.
