Dr. Amit Kumar Chawla is a distinguished material scientist with a specialization in nanotechnology, boasting 15 years of experience in teaching and research. He has a strong commitment to achieving exemplary results while fostering a culture of lifelong learning among his students. Dr. Chawla has successfully established a state-of-the-art research laboratory equipped with advanced synthesis and characterization tools, demonstrating his exceptional skills in developing cutting-edge research facilities.

His research interests include thin film-based hydrogen sensors, hard coatings, wear-resistant coatings, and resistive random-access memory. Dr. Chawla is an active member of the academic community, holding life memberships in both the Indian Association of Physics Teachers and the Indian Physics Association. He has an impressive publication record, with over 75 articles published in reputable journals, and he regularly shares his knowledge through informative talks about advanced equipment and its practical applications, benefiting research scholars and faculty from various institutions.

Dr. Chawla has taught a wide array of subjects to undergraduate and postgraduate students, including Solid State Physics, Elements of Modern Physics, Engineering Physics, Solar Cell Technology, Basic Electronics Engineering, Digital Electronics, and Introduction to Nanotechnology. His teaching philosophy emphasizes placing learners at the center of the educational experience, creating a supportive environment that embraces innovative teaching methodologies. He believes in the integration of advanced technologies to enhance learning without compromising the foundational educational process.

Before his tenure at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dr. Chawla served as a Research Associate at IIT Roorkee, where he significantly contributed to the establishment of the thin film laboratory and various projects in nanoscience. At UPES, he has led the development of the Centre of Nanoscience and Nanotechnology, showcasing his leadership in the field.
