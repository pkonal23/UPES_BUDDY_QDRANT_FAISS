The MBA in Aviation Management program at UPES School of Business is designed to prepare students for leadership roles in various sectors of the aviation industry, including airlines, airports, and aviation services. The curriculum spans several key areas, including airline operations, airport economics, aviation marketing, and safety, providing a comprehensive understanding of the industry's dynamics and challenges.

### Program Structure and Curriculum
The program is divided into four semesters, each focusing on different aspects of aviation management:
- **Semester 1**: Fundamentals of Airline Operations, Accounting for Managers, Business Communication, and more.
- **Semester 2**: Aviation Management, Airline Economics, and IT Applications in the aviation sector.
- **Semester 3**: Strategic Management, Aviation Security & Safety, and Aviation Marketing.
- **Semester 4**: Aviation Enterprise Management, Airport Planning & Management, and a dissertation.

Students can choose from various specializations, including Airline Revenue Management, Air Cargo Management, and Airport Operations Management, among others. The program also emphasizes practical experience through summer internships, live projects, and industrial tours.

### Admission Requirements
To be eligible for the program, applicants must have:
- A minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.
- A qualifying score in national entrance exams such as UPESMET, CAT, XAT, GMAT, or NMAT, with specific percentile requirements outlined for exemptions from the UPES Management Entrance Test.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates of the program can pursue various roles, including:
- Airline Revenue Analyst
- Aviation Sales and Marketing Professional
- Air Cargo Operations Manager
- Aviation Analyst
- Business Analyst

Starting salaries for graduates typically range from INR 6 to 10 lakh per annum, depending on their skills and exposure. The program also prepares students for mid and senior management roles, addressing the industry's demand for skilled professionals.

### Facilities and Learning Environment
The UPES School of Business offers modern amenities, including smart classrooms, an analytics lab, and a well-stocked library. The curriculum incorporates advanced software and technology training, equipping students with essential skills for data-driven decision-making.

### Placement and Industry Connections
The program boasts strong placement opportunities with prominent companies such as GMR Group, Adani Group, Vistara Airlines, and more. Students have the chance to engage in internships and projects that connect them with industry leaders, enhancing their employability.

### Additional Information
Students are encouraged to participate in exchange programs, seminars, and research projects to broaden their professional networks and academic experiences. The program also includes exposure to real-world case studies and guest lectures from industry experts.
