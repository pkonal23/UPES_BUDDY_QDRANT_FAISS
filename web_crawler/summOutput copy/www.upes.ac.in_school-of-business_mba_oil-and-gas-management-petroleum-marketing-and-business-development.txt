The MBA in Oil & Gas Management (Petroleum Marketing & Business Development) program at the UPES School of Business is designed to provide students with a comprehensive education that combines general management principles with specialized knowledge of the oil and gas industry. The program emphasizes hands-on experience through major projects, summer internships, and live projects with leading industry companies, ensuring that students gain practical insights into the sector.

**Program Highlights:**
- The curriculum includes a variety of domain-specific subjects such as Petroleum Exploration, Fundamentals of Oil & Gas Business, Refinery Management, Financial Management, Marketing Research, and more.
- Students benefit from a conducive learning environment featuring modern smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, and a comprehensive library equipped with advanced analytical tools.
- The program integrates real-world applications through industry visits, guest lectures from experts, and case study analyses, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge to practical situations.
- Emphasizes the importance of hands-on learning, providing students with practical experience through internships and on-the-job training.

**Specialization Focus:**
The Petroleum Marketing & Business Development specialization addresses the retail marketing of petroleum products, consumer behaviors, product branding, and the unique challenges associated with marketing in this sector. Students engage in industrial assignments that enhance their leadership skills and provide practical experience.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- A minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.
- Candidates with a MAT/CMAT score of 70 percentile or above are exempt from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will be called for Group Discussion and Interview if qualified. Other accepted scores include valid CAT, XAT, GMAT, and NMAT scores.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates can pursue a variety of roles in the oil and gas sector, including:
- Oil & Gas regulatory and financial consultant
- Energy Analyst/SME in IT and consultancy
- ESG specialist
- Power and Carbon Trading expert
- Energy Efficiency Consultant
- Fuel procurement analyst
- CGD Project Manager
- Sustainability consultant
- Business Analyst
- Energy Storage and E-vehicle expert
- Oil & Gas Transportation and distribution roles

The program has a strong placement record, with graduates being recruited by esteemed organizations such as ONGC, Accenture, Infosys, Wipro, Deloitte, Shell, and Exxon Mobil. Starting salaries range from INR 6-30 lakh per annum, reflecting the diverse skill sets and practical exposure that students gain.
