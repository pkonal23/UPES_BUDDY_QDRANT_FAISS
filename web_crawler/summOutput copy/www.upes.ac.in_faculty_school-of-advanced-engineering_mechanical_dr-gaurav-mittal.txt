Dr. Gaurav Mittal is an esteemed academic and researcher with a robust background in Mechanical Engineering, specializing in various domains including Experimental and Computational Combustion, Chemical Kinetics of Hydrocarbon Fuels, and Alternative Fuels. His extensive research interests encompass Autoignition and Flame phenomena at elevated pressures, Computational Fluid Dynamics, Gas Turbine Combustion, and Solar Energy.

### Research and Professional Experience
Dr. Mittal has held significant positions in academia, including serving as a Principal Investigator on multiple projects funded by prominent US federal agencies such as NASA, the National Science Foundation (NSF), and the Department of Energy (DOE). He has also contributed his expertise to various research panels associated with these organizations and the Petroleum Research Fund.

His prior experience includes working as a Research Associate at Case Western Reserve University (2005-2008), followed by a tenure at The University of Akron, where he began as an Assistant Professor in 2008 and was promoted to Associate Professor in 2014. He later served as a Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Graphic Era University from 2017 to 2022.

Dr. Mittal's contributions to the field include the development of advanced engines and combustors, utilizing innovative experimental facilities to investigate the chemical kinetics of fuels under conditions relevant to modern engines. His research output is substantial, with numerous publications in high-impact journals, indicating a significant citation record.

### Teaching Philosophy
Dr. Mittal is committed to fostering a deep understanding of core engineering principles among his students. He emphasizes the importance of mastering basic concepts as a foundation for tackling more complex engineering challenges. He actively promotes project-based learning, integrating contemporary computational tools to enhance student engagement and learning experiences. His teaching repertoire includes a range of core and specialized engineering courses, particularly in Thermodynamics, Fluid Mechanics, Mechanics of Materials, Heat Transfer, Kinematics of Machines, and advanced Fluid-Thermal Sciences topics such as Combustion and Computational Fluid Dynamics.

### Academic Credentials
Dr. Mittal earned his B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Kharagpur, followed by a Master's in Computer Applications from IIT Delhi. He completed his Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering with a focus on Combustion at Case Western Reserve University.
