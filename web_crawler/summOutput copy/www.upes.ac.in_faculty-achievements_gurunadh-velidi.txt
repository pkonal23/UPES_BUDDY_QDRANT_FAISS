Dr. Gurunadh Velidi has been awarded the SERB SIRE Fellowship, which is an initiative by the Science and Engineering Research Board (SERB) aimed at promoting international research experiences. The research will take place at UNIST (Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology), a prestigious institution located in South Korea, known for its commitment to innovation and research excellence.
