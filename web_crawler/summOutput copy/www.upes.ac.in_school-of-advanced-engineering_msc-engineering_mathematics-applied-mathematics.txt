The M.Sc. Mathematics (Applied Mathematics) program offered by the UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to equip students with advanced mathematical skills applicable across various industries. The curriculum emphasizes practical applications of mathematical principles, preparing graduates to tackle complex challenges in fields such as logistics, supply chain management, transportation, production planning, fluid dynamics, materials science, electromagnetics, and computational physics.

### Key Features of the Program:
- **Specialization Courses**: The program includes a range of specialization courses such as Differential Equations, Calculus of Variations, Theory of Operators, Classical Mechanics, Operations Research, and more, allowing students to tailor their education to their interests.
- **Hands-on Learning**: A rigorous curriculum blends theoretical knowledge with practical applications through hands-on projects and research opportunities, fostering a dynamic learning environment.
- **Expert Faculty**: Students benefit from mentorship by faculty members who are experts in their respective fields, ensuring a high-quality educational experience.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates of the program can explore diverse career paths, including:
- **Financial Services and Risk Management**: Utilizing quantitative skills for analysis and strategy development.
- **Data Science and Analytics**: Meeting the growing demand for data-driven insights in various sectors.
- **Academia and Research**: Engaging in teaching and statistical research, contributing to the field of mathematics.
- **Software Quality Assurance**: Opportunities in manual testing for those interested in technology.

### Future Scope:
The future scope of the program is promising, with graduates expected to make significant contributions in research, teaching, and various applied mathematics roles. The program's interdisciplinary approach and focus on optimization techniques prepare students for the competitive job market.

### Admission Process:
- **Eligibility**: Candidates must have a minimum of 50% in 10th and 12th grades (PCM/B) and 50% in graduation in a relevant subject.
- **Selection Criteria**: Admission is based on individual performance in a personal interview.
