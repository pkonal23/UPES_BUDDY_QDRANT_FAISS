The Marketing Management program offered by UPES is meticulously crafted to provide students with the essential knowledge and skills required for success in operations management. Spanning two years, the curriculum encompasses a broad spectrum of topics, including supply chain management, inventory management, quality management, lean manufacturing, six sigma, project management, business analytics, and data science. This comprehensive approach ensures that graduates possess a well-rounded understanding of operations management.

### Program Structure and Practical Exposure

Students engage in practical learning through real-world case studies, industry visits, and internships, facilitating a deeper comprehension of operations management. The program is divided into four semesters, with key courses including:

- **Semester 1**: Accounting for Managers, Business Analysis and Communication I
- **Semester 2**: Financial Management, Production Planning & Control, Innovation and Entrepreneurship
- **Semester 3**: Service Operations, Total Quality Management
- **Semester 4**: Business Ethics and CSR, Project Management

### Career Opportunities and Industry Demand

Graduates of the MBA in Operations Management program are well-prepared for various roles such as operations manager, supply chain manager, inventory planner, quality manager, and more. The job market is promising, with renowned companies like Flipkart, Reliance Industries, HUL, GATI KWE, Adani Wilmar, Accenture, Deloitte, Kuehne Nagel, Xiaomi, TATA Croma, Odex, Transystem, and Amazon actively seeking skilled professionals. Fresh graduates can expect starting salaries ranging from INR 6-30 lakh per annum, influenced by their skills and experience.

### Admission Criteria

To apply for the program, candidates must meet the following eligibility requirements:

- Minimum 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.
- Exemption from the UPES Management Entrance Test for candidates with a minimum eligible score:
  - MAT/CMAT 2025: 70 percentile
  - CAT 2024/XAT 2025: 50 percentile or above
  - GMAT: Minimum score of 400
  - NMAT 2024/25: Minimum score of 120

The selection process also includes a Group Discussion and Personal Interview for qualified candidates.
