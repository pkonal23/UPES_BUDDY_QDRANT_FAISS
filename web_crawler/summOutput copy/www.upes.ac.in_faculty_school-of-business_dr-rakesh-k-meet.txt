Dr. Rakesh K. Meet is a seasoned educator and practitioner with an extensive background in both industry and academia, boasting 28 years of experience in leadership roles across various Indian and foreign multinational companies. His last position in the industry was as Head of Retail Operations at Vodafone India Limited. Dr. Meet holds a triple Master's degree in Business Management, International Business, and Psychology, and he completed his Ph.D. in Management from UPES, Dehradun. He is also a certified trainer in Neuro Linguistic Programming and an assessor of Multiple Intelligences Developmental Scales, certified by Dr. Branton Shearer from Ohio, USA.

In his academic career, Dr. Rakesh has taken on both teaching and administrative roles, serving as an Associate Professor of Marketing at the School of Business (SOB) at UPES since August 2023. He previously held the position of Dean of Management at DBUU from 2018 to 2020 and was an Area Chair for Marketing at DBS from 2020 to 2023. He has received accolades such as the Best Area Chair Award at DBS for 2021-22 and several awards during his industry tenure, including Best KAM Head and Best Cluster Head.

Dr. Rakesh's teaching focuses on various marketing and general management courses, including B2B Marketing, Sales and Distribution Management, Marketing Management, Services Marketing, Consumer Behavior, Digital Marketing, and Business Environment. His teaching philosophy emphasizes the Socratic method, fostering interactive discussions and critical thinking among students. He utilizes case studies and project-based learning to provide practical context and hands-on experience, preparing students for real-world business challenges.

His research interests are diverse, encompassing educational technology, technology adoption, Gen Z, sales leadership, and sustainable marketing. Dr. Rakesh has published three papers in Q1 journals indexed in Scopus, ABDC, Web of Science, and SSCI, focusing on consumer adoption of technological innovations. His research profile can be found at [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=z6FVQq8AAAAJ).
