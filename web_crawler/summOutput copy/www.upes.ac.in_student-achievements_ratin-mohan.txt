Lexus India has announced the finalists for its prestigious Nature Electrified Design Contest, which commenced in April 2023 in celebration of World Earth Day. This competition aimed to encourage innovative design concepts that fuse nature with electrified themes. A distinguished jury panel meticulously evaluated the submissions, ultimately selecting a few exceptional finalists based on their design expertise, representation of the electrified natural theme, and the creativity demonstrated in their concepts.

Among the notable finalists is Ratin Mohan, an Assistant Professor (Selection Grade) at the School of Design. His achievement in this contest highlights his mentorship role and contribution to fostering creativity in design.
