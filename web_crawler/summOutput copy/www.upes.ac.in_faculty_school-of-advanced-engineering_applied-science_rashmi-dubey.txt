Dr. Rashmi Dubey is an Assistant Professor - Senior Scale at the School of Advanced Engineering. Her research interests are primarily in the areas of Convective Heat and Mass Transfer, Stability Analysis, and Double-diffusive Convective Phenomena. She emphasizes the importance of connecting theoretical concepts to real-life applications, ensuring that her students gain a profound and clear understanding of the subject matter.

She is recognized as a rising figure in applied mathematics, having published numerous papers in high-quality international journals (SCI) and presented her research at various international conferences. Her teaching portfolio includes courses such as Calculus for Engineers, Applications of Differential and Difference Equations, Applied Linear Algebra, Engineering Mathematics, and Discrete Mathematics.

Throughout her academic career, Dr. Dubey has received several accolades, including the Best Graduate Award (Gold Medal) in B.Sc. (Hons.) from Jamshedpur Women's College (Autonomous) and a post-graduate merit scholarship from the University Grants Commission, Government of India, awarded to university rank holders. Recently, she was granted an International Travel Grant from the National Board for Higher Mathematics, Department of Atomic Energy, Government of India, to support her research endeavors.

Before her tenure at UPES, Dr. Dubey served as an Assistant Professor at VIT - AP University for two years. Her scholarly contributions extend beyond research; she is actively involved in the academic community through various roles, such as reviewing papers for prestigious journals like Physics of Fluids, Applied Mathematics and Computations, and Materials Today: Proceedings. Additionally, she has organized international workshops and conferences, serving as a core organizing committee member and convener. Dr. Dubey is also a life member of the Indian Society for Theoretical and Applied Mechanics.
