Puja Tiwari is an Assistant Professor at the School of Business, with a strong focus on behavioral finance, securities analysis, and portfolio management. Her research interests are centered on understanding how psychological factors influence the investment decisions of individual investors, particularly examining the impacts of personality traits, psychological biases, gender, and financial literacy.

Before her tenure at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Puja was a research scholar at Banaras Hindu University. She has contributed to the academic community by publishing papers in peer-reviewed journals and presenting her research at various national and international conferences. 

Her expertise encompasses areas such as Investors' Psychology, Behavioral Asset Pricing, and Behavioral Finance.
