Dr. Nabin Sen is an Assistant Professor - Selection Grade at the School of Business, with a focus on supply chain management, inventory modeling, and operations research. He teaches various courses including Business Mathematics, Business Statistics, Quantitative Techniques for Managers, and Business Research Methodology to both undergraduate and postgraduate students.

Dr. Sen's teaching philosophy emphasizes creating an engaging and inclusive learning environment that encourages critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a passion for lifelong learning. He adopts a student-centric approach that recognizes the diverse backgrounds and learning styles of his students. His teaching methods include the use of real-world examples, interactive discussions, hands-on activities, and projects to promote active learning.

He holds a doctorate from Jadavpur University in Kolkata and a Post Graduate Diploma in Applied Statistics from the Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata. With over 14 years of teaching experience and more than 7 years of research experience, Dr. Sen has taught mathematics and statistics to engineering and management students in various institutions. His research primarily focuses on supply chain management, particularly inventory management issues. He has qualified the CSIR-UGC-NET in Mathematical Science in June 2011 and the GATE in Mathematics in 2015.

Dr. Sen has received recognition for his work, including the Best Research Paper Award at the International Conference on “Standardizing Management Practices: Challenges & Opportunities” (ICSMP-2021), held from August 12 to 14, 2021, at the Faculty of Management Studies, MLSU in Udaipur, Rajasthan, India. He has published numerous articles in esteemed national and international journals such as the International Journal of Production Research, Journal of Management Analytics, Rairo-Operations Research, and Sadhana. He is also involved in supervising undergraduate students' dissertations and serves as a course coordinator for undergraduate programs at UPES, Kandoli campus.

Prior to his current role at UPES, Dr. Sen was an Assistant Professor in Mathematics at K L University, Andhra Pradesh, and has worked in various educational organizations since 2008, where he progressed from lecturer to Assistant Professor (Senior Scale). He has been engaged in Master’s degree courses since 2019.
