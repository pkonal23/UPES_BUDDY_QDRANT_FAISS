The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) has formed a partnership with Amazon Web Services (AWS) Academy to enhance the educational offerings in cloud computing for its students. This collaboration aims to prepare students for industry-recognized certifications and careers in the cloud sector. AWS Academy experts will be responsible for training the faculty, ensuring that they are well-equipped to deliver high-quality education in this field. Additionally, students enrolled in the program will receive certificates upon completion, validating their skills and knowledge in cloud technologies. 

For more information or inquiries, interested individuals should check the UPES School of Computer Science and School of Business sections related to this initiative.
