Mariyam Ilyas Siddiqui achieved recognition by presenting her research paper titled "Ms. Marvel: A Study of the Portrayal of American Muslims in Today's World of Science Fiction" at the Northeast Modern Language Association's 54th Annual Convention. This event took place in Niagara Falls, NY, from March 23 to March 26, 2023. Her presentation was part of the 'Muslims in America' session, where she explored the representation of American Muslims within the realm of science fiction, focusing on the significant theme of resilience. The convention aimed to examine how marginalized bodies are represented in literature, particularly those that demonstrate persistence and perseverance through their narratives.
