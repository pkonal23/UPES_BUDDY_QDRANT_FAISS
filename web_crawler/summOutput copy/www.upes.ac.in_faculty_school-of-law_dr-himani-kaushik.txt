Dr. Himani Kaushik is an accomplished academic currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the School of Law at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). She holds a PhD, a Master’s in Philosophy, and a Master’s in Arts, all from the School of International Studies at Jawaharlal Nehru University in Delhi. Additionally, she completed her undergraduate studies in Political Science honors at S.G.T.B. Khalsa College, University of Delhi.

Throughout her career, Dr. Kaushik has garnered several accolades, including a seed grant project for one year in 2021-2022 from G.D. Goenka University and recognition for securing second place in her undergraduate program at Delhi University.

Her professional experience includes significant roles prior to joining UPES, such as working at G.D. Goenka University and Delhi University. She has also served as a Research Officer at Niti Aayog on two projects for a year and as a Research Assistant at the Indian Institute of Public Administration, where she was involved in report writing and research for various Government of India projects. Additionally, she worked as a Research Associate at the Foundation for National Security Research, under the Ministry of Defence.

Dr. Kaushik’s teaching philosophy emphasizes guiding students to enhance their understanding of key political concepts and develop essential skills. She aims to connect political studies with real-world issues, encouraging students to critically evaluate their political beliefs and become reflective, responsible democratic citizens. Her courses cover a wide range of topics, including Feminism and Feminist Politics, Public Administration, India’s Foreign Policy, Comparative Government and Politics, International Relations, Local Governance and Development Politics, Public Policy, Political Process in India, Global Politics, and Political Theory.

To facilitate learning, Dr. Kaushik employs various assessment methods, including short-answer exams and quizzes, to help students articulate complex political concepts clearly and effectively.
