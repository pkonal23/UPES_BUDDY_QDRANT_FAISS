Dr. Dabashish Majumdar has achieved a significant milestone by securing the first Ph.D. in Transportation Design, a rare accomplishment in both India and internationally. His doctoral research focused on the design and Human-Machine Interaction (HMI) of an Amphibious Vehicle aimed at amusement purposes in India. This work not only contributes to the field of Transportation Design but also symbolizes the culmination of his extensive 30-year career journey in the industry. 

For further details or inquiries, please refer to the School of Design where Dr. Majumdar is affiliated.
