Dr. Shalini Singh is an accomplished academic with approximately 12 years of experience in teaching, research, and administration. Currently, she serves as an Assistant Professor of International Business and Marketing at the School of Business, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun, India. Previously, she held positions as a Visiting Faculty at the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT) in New Delhi and as an Assistant Professor (Selection Grade) at IMS Unison University in Dehradun.

Dr. Singh holds a Doctorate in Marketing from DIT University, Dehradun, and her primary teaching subjects include International Business and Marketing. She has a keen research interest in e-impulse buying, focusing on its antecedents, structural modeling, and cross-cultural implications. Her scholarly contributions include publications in reputable journals indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, and the ABDC list. Additionally, she has presented her research at various prestigious conferences, including those held at IIM Ahmedabad, IIT Delhi, UTM Malaysia, and the MTMI Conference in Mauritius.

In her role at UPES, Dr. Singh teaches several courses, including International Marketing, International Business, Marketing Management, and Brand Management. Her teaching philosophy emphasizes the importance of ethics in education, aiming to foster critical and empathetic thinking among students to promote social and environmental upliftment.
