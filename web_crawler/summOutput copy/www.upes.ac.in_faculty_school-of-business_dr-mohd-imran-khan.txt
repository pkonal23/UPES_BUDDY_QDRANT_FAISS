Dr. Mohd Imran Khan serves as an Assistant Professor (Senior Scale) in the School of Business at UPES, bringing a wealth of experience and expertise in various fields including Sustainable Supply Chain Management, Halal Supply Chain Management, Circular Economy, and Industry 4.0. He holds a Doctorate Degree in Industrial Engineering from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi, and has over four years of prior experience as an Assistant Professor at Lovely Professional University, as well as a Teaching Personnel role at GB Pant University of Agriculture and Technology.

Dr. Khan is recognized for his strong commitment to research and teaching excellence, with numerous collaborative publications in top-tier academic journals. His mentorship has led to the successful guidance of three PhD scholars, showcasing his dedication to fostering academic growth. His research primarily focuses on sustainable performance measurement within Halal supply chains, utilizing modern ICT technologies like Blockchain to enhance transparency.

In the classroom, Dr. Khan emphasizes the practical application of theoretical concepts through case studies, simulations, and experiential learning, aiming to bridge the gap between theory and real-world business practices. He creates an interactive and engaging learning environment that encourages student participation, critical thinking, and problem-solving. His teaching portfolio includes a variety of subjects such as Operations Management, Operations Research, Business Analytics, Data Visualization, and Total Quality Management at both postgraduate and undergraduate levels.

Additionally, Dr. Khan contributes to the academic community as a reviewer for various journals, playing a significant role in shaping research directions within his field.
