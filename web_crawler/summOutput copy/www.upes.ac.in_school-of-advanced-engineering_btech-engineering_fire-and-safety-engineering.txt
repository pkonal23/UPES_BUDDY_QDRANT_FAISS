The B.Tech. in Fire & Safety Engineering program at the UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to equip students with extensive knowledge and practical skills necessary for success in the field of fire and safety. The curriculum is aligned with industry standards, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for careers in fire prevention, risk assessment, disaster management, and the implementation of safety protocols. The program emphasizes hands-on learning, facilitated by experienced faculty and state-of-the-art facilities, allowing students to engage with real-world challenges.

### Key Features of the Program:
- **Industry-Relevant Curriculum**: The program covers essential topics such as fire hazards and forensics, structural fire protection design, fire risk assessment, and emergency response strategies.
- **Practical Experience**: Students gain practical skills through labs, workshops, and internships, which are integral to their learning experience.
- **Global Exposure**: UPES collaborates with prestigious international universities, providing opportunities for exchange programs and exposure to global best practices.
- **Placement Opportunities**: The program boasts a 100% placement record, with graduates securing positions in diverse sectors such as construction, oil & gas, manufacturing, and banking, with the highest salary package reaching 13.5 lakhs.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates can pursue various roles including:
- Safety Officers
- Fire and Safety Officers
- Risk Assessment Consultants
- Disaster Management Specialists
- Health & Safety Managers

### Eligibility Criteria:
Prospective students must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII.

### Application Process:
- Interested candidates can apply online through the UPES website.
- The last date to apply for the program is February 25, 2025.

### Contact Information:
For more information, prospective students can reach out via the inquiry form available on the UPES website or contact the admissions office directly.

This program is aimed at producing industry-ready professionals who can effectively address fire and safety challenges, ensuring the protection of lives and properties while advancing their careers in this vital field. The emphasis on sustainability and environmental safety further prepares graduates to implement innovative solutions in their future roles.
