Dr. Ajit Yadav is an Assistant Professor of Finance at the School of Business, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun, India. He holds a Doctorate in Finance from the Institute of Management Studies at Banaras Hindu University in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, and has qualified for the UGC-NET JRF. His primary teaching areas include Financial Management, Financial Analytics, International Financial Management, and Derivatives.

Dr. Yadav's research interests encompass Standard Finance, Asset Pricing, and Behavioural Finance. He has published research papers in various reputable journals listed in Scopus, Web of Science, and ABDC, and has also edited books. He is active in the academic community, having presented his research at numerous national and international conferences.

In terms of teaching philosophy, Dr. Yadav aims to instill ethics in his students through quality education, encouraging them to become responsible citizens. He emphasizes interactive and engaging teaching methods to promote active participation and critical thinking. Dr. Yadav is committed to creating a nurturing and inclusive learning environment, motivating students to explore new concepts, develop practical skills, and foster a passion for lifelong learning. He focuses on the holistic development of students, ensuring their all-round growth.

Dr. Yadav has approximately two years of academic, research, and administrative experience. Prior to his role at UPES, he was an Assistant Professor at the School of Management Sciences in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, where he taught subjects such as Financial Management, Security Analysis and Portfolio Management, Behavioural Finance, and Financial Analytics.
