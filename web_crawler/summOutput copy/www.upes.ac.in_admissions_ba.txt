The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a diverse range of Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) programs designed to equip students with essential skills for various professional fields. To be eligible for admission, candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII and can apply online or offline by paying an application fee of Rs. 1750. The admission process includes filling out an application form, submitting required documents, and attending a personal interview.

UPES provides five B.A. programs, which are:

1. B.A. (Hons.) English
2. B.A. (Hons.) History
3. B.A. (Hons.) Political Science
4. B.A. Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital)
5. B.A. Digital and Mass Media (Broadcasting)

The curriculum is designed to foster critical thinking, research, and communication skills, preparing students for careers in journalism, digital marketing, content creation, public relations, and more. Graduates can pursue roles such as content writers, editors, digital marketers, and media managers, with opportunities in both private and public sectors.

UPES emphasizes practical learning and industry exposure, partnering with leading companies to enhance students' employability. The university has a successful placement record, with prominent recruiters like Airbus, Deloitte, and KPMG participating in campus placements. Placement assistance is provided, although it does not guarantee job placements.

The university boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including three world-class auditoriums, advanced labs, and a secure campus environment, ensuring students have a conducive learning atmosphere. Additionally, UPES provides hostel facilities with limited on-campus accommodation available on a first-come, first-served basis.

For further inquiries, students can contact UPES through their official website or admission office. The last date to apply for the B.A. programs for the academic year 2025 is February 28, 2025.
