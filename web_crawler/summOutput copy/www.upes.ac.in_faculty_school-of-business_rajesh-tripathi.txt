Dr. Rajesh Tripathi is an accomplished academic and media professional with over 18 years of experience in the fields of academics, media, and journalism. He holds a Doctorate in Strategic Management from the Motilal Nehru National Institute of Technology (MNNIT), Allahabad, and a Masterâ€™s degree in Business Administration (MBA) from Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapeeth (MGKV), Varanasi. Additionally, he has a Master's degree in English Literature from the University of Allahabad and a Post Graduate Diploma in Mass Communication and Journalism from Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU), New Delhi. He also completed a Certificate course in Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) from the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO) in Geneva, Switzerland, and is UGC-NET qualified.

Currently, Dr. Tripathi serves as a Program Lead and Assistant Professor (Selection Grade) at the School of Business, UPES Dehradun, where he has been employed since 2012. His teaching philosophy focuses on transforming grounded knowledge into practical applications, utilizing indigenously designed case studies and introducing innovative initiatives such as Industry Reporting to keep students informed about the latest business developments. He teaches various subjects, including Strategic Management, Business Ethics, Corporate Governance, IPR, Sustainability, and media-related courses.

Dr. Tripathi is supervising six doctoral scholars on research topics such as technology transfer, sustainable leadership, sustainable development, and safety and operational issues in the aviation industry. He has published numerous research papers on strategic management, business models, and other significant topics in both national and international journals, and he frequently presents at conferences held at prestigious institutions like IIMs and NITs. His cases have gained recognition and are taught at IIM Kolkata and Concordia University in Canada.

In addition to his academic contributions, Dr. Tripathi has received several accolades, including induction into the Circle of Achievement at the Reynolds Centre for Business Journalism at Arizona State University, USA, and he was nominated as an Honorary Communication Scientist at the Institute of Applied Science (IASc.) in Allahabad, which is funded by the Department of Science and Technology. He has also been honored with a Scroll of Honour from the International Council of Jurists.
