Surana & Surana International Attorneys collaborates with the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) to enhance educational opportunities for law students through various moot court competitions and allied events. This initiative aims to foster platforms for discussion while equipping students with essential skills in research, writing, and advocacy.

The partnership specifically focuses on the B.B.A., LL.B. (H) - Corporate Laws and B.Com. (LL.B) Taxation Law programs. Surana & Surana plays a crucial role in developing the curriculum, delivering courses, facilitating faculty internships, organizing joint seminars and conferences, and providing student internships.

Founded in 1971 and headquartered in Chennai, India, Surana & Surana International Attorneys is recognized as one of the largest law firms in South India. The firm specializes in several practice areas, including Arbitration, Corporate Law, Corporate Compliance, Civil and Criminal Litigation, Infrastructure, Intellectual Property, Mergers & Acquisitions, and Real Estate.
