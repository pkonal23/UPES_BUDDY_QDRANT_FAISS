Neha Verma is a certified trainer specializing in fact-checking and media literacy. She notably represented the School of Modern Media at the APAC Trusted Media Summit 2022, which was organized by Google and took place on September 20-21, 2022. This summit is the fifth annual event of its kind, gathering a diverse group of participants including journalists, fact-checkers, educators, academics, activists, and politicians from across the Asia-Pacific region. The primary objective of the summit is to address the issue of disinformation by providing a platform for the counter-misinformation community to connect, share knowledge, and discuss best practices related to fact-checking, verification, media literacy, and research.
