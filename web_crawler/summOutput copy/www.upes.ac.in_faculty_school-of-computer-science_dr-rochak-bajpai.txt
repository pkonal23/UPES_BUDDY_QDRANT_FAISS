Dr. Rochak Bajpai is an accomplished academic with a Ph.D. from Delhi University, New Delhi, where he conducted research titled “Some Studies on Mixed Line Rate (MLR) Optical Networks.” He also holds an M.Tech in Digital Communication from G.B.T.U. and a B.Tech from C.S.J.M. University. With approximately twenty-two years of experience in higher education, Dr. Bajpai has mentored numerous students in areas such as internships, projects, higher education, and placements. His previous roles include serving as an Internship Coordinator, Project Coordinator, a member of the departmental NBA Team, and Assistant Head of International Relations at his former institution.

Currently, Dr. Bajpai is an Assistant Professor - Selection Grade at the School of Computer Science. His research interests encompass a range of topics including fiber optics, nonlinearity in fiber optics, optical and digital communication, stochastic modeling, blockchain technology, and the application of machine learning in communication. He has been recognized with a Teaching Research Fellowship for his Ph.D. work and received the Best Paper Award at CCTES-18.

Before his tenure at UPES, Dr. Bajpai was an Assistant Professor at KIET Group of Institutions in Delhi-NCR and at GLA University in Mathura. His teaching philosophy emphasizes active learning, utilizing various strategies such as quizzes, discussions, historical perspectives, and case studies to engage students. This approach aims to foster curiosity and help students connect theoretical concepts with practical applications.

Dr. Bajpai has taught several courses, including Optical Communication, Analog Electronics, Digital System Design, Linear Integrated Circuits, Advanced Engineering Mathematics, and MATLAB programming for various applications in Signals and Systems, Digital Signal Processing, and Control Systems. He also acts as a reviewer for numerous conferences and coordinates internship programs for B.Tech. students.
