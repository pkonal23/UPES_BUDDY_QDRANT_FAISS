The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), established in 2003 by the UPES Act passed by the Uttarakhand State Legislature, is a recognized institution under the University Grants Commission (UGC) of India. It is listed under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956, and has expanded its charter through the UPES (Amendment) Act, 2018. UPES is a member of several prestigious organizations, including the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and the International Association of Universities (IAU).

UPES has achieved notable rankings in various national and international assessments. In the QS World University Rankings: Asia 2025, UPES is ranked 148th, marking a significant improvement of 70 positions, making it the second most improved university in Asia. It is also recognized as the top private university in academic reputation in India by the QS World University Rankings 2025, placing it in the 801-850 band globally. In the Times Higher Education (THE) World University Rankings 2025, UPES is positioned in the 501-600 band globally and ranks 7th in India, while in the Young University Rankings 2024, it is in the 201-250 band globally.

The institution has received specialized accreditation for two of its programs – the Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Oil and Gas Management and MBA in Logistics and Supply Chain Management – from the International Accreditation Council for Business Education (IACBE), USA. Additionally, it has been accredited with an ‘A’ grade and a CGPA of 3.02 by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) for the period from March 1, 2021, to February 28, 2026.

In terms of specific program rankings, UPES is recognized among the top 150 institutions globally for Telecommunication Engineering and ranks in the top 400 for Electrical and Electronic Engineering, as per the Shanghai Ranking 2023. The National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2024 places UPES 46th among universities in India, with specific rankings of 28th in Law, 41st in Management, and 42nd in Engineering.

The university's law programs are recognized by the Bar Council of India (BCI), and its School of Design has been a member of the World Design Organization (WDO) since 2019. Furthermore, the Bachelor’s and Diploma programs from the UPES School of Health Sciences and Technology are recognized by the Pharmacy Council of India.

UPES also emphasizes distance education, with programs approved by the Distance Education Council (DEC). The university has received recognition as a Scientific and Research Organization by the Department of Industrial and Scientific Research (DISR) under the Ministry of Science and Technology, Government of India.
