The B.Tech program in Computer Science & Engineering with a specialization in Internet of Things (IoT) at UPES School of Computer Science offers a comprehensive and dynamic education designed to equip students with essential knowledge and skills for the rapidly evolving IoT landscape. The curriculum covers foundational concepts to advanced applications, including IoT security, cloud platforms, programming, and sensor technologies.

### Practical Engagement
Students engage in practical projects through thesis or capstone projects, where they gain hands-on experience in embedded coding, sensor deployment, data analysis, and IoT applications. The program emphasizes real-world projects and industry collaborations, preparing graduates for various roles in sectors like healthcare, manufacturing, agriculture, and smart cities.

### Eligibility and Admission Process
To be eligible for the program, applicants must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII, along with 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII. Admission is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET. The last date to apply is February 25, 2025.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates are well-prepared for roles such as IoT Solution Architect, Embedded Systems Engineer, Data Analyst, and IoT Software Developer. The program boasts impressive placements, with an average annual package of 11.46 LPA and a highest package reaching 26.41 LPA. Notable recruiters include Accenture, Amazon India Limited, Barclays, and Capgemini.

### Industry Trends and Future Scope
The IoT sector is anticipated to grow significantly due to advancements in 5G and edge computing, enhancing device capabilities and security. The integration of AI and machine learning into IoT applications is expected to drive innovation further. Graduates will have opportunities in smart cities initiatives, R&D centers, and IoT-focused startups, positioning them to contribute to the digital transformation across various industries.
