Dr. Gunjan Vasant Bonde is an accomplished academic and researcher with a strong background in pharmaceutical sciences, particularly in the areas of Pharmaceutics, Biopharmaceutics, and drug delivery systems. He qualified for the Graduate Pharmacy Aptitude Test (GPAT) in 2010 and has received numerous prestigious awards and grants throughout his career. Notable recognitions include the "Bharat Shiksha Gaurav Puraskar-2023" from KTK Outstanding Achievers and Education Foundation, the "SPER Best Student (Ph.D.) Award, 2020," and various scholarships such as the "Sir Ratan Tata Scholarship" and "Maharashtra Talent Search Scholarship."

Dr. Bonde completed his Bachelor of Pharmacy (B. Pharm) from the University of Mumbai and a Master of Pharmacy (M. Pharm) with Distinction from the University of Pune. He earned his Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Technology - Banaras Hindu University (IIT-BHU), focusing on the design and development of nano-drug delivery systems for breast cancer treatment. He has also worked as a Teaching Assistant at IIT-BHU and has participated in research roles at Abbott Healthcare Pvt. Ltd. and the National AIDS Research Institute.

Currently, Dr. Bonde serves as an Assistant Professor - Senior Scale at the School of Health Sciences and Technology at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. He has authored 18 publications and contributed to four book chapters, with a strong emphasis on developing nanoparticles, nanofibers, and other innovative drug delivery systems. His research interests include enhancing drug solubility and bioavailability, pharmacokinetics, and biodistribution.

Dr. Bonde's teaching philosophy centers on the responsibility of educators to guide students effectively, utilizing various teaching aids and fostering an environment of lifelong learning. He values authenticity, sincerity, and ethical practice in his role as an educator, aiming to contribute to his students' success.

His recent publications include articles in esteemed journals, and he has an h-index of 13 with 520 citations. He has also received significant funding for his research, including an Extra-mural Grant from Oil India Limited and a Seed Grant from UPES.
