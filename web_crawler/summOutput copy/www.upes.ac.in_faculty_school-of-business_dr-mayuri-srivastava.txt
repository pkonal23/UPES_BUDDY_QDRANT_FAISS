Dr. Mayuri Srivastava is an Assistant Professor - Senior Scale at the School of Business, UPES. She holds a PhD in Management from the Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, Ranchi, where her doctoral research concentrated on the individual-level antecedents that influence sustainability-oriented entrepreneurial intentions and behaviors among youth in emerging economies. 

Her research interests encompass Sustainable Entrepreneurship, Tribal Entrepreneurship, the Entrepreneurship Ecosystem, and Sustainable Development. Currently, Dr. Srivastava is investigating the macroeconomic factors that affect the sustainable entrepreneurship ecosystem in India. She has a strong academic background, having been a Silver Medalist during her postgraduate studies at the Xavier Institute of Social Service, Ranchi, for the batch of 2016-18.

Dr. Srivastava's teaching philosophy emphasizes the empowerment of students to become innovative, sustainable, and socially responsible leaders. She advocates for an educational approach that promotes critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration, equipping students to address complex business challenges and make a positive impact.

In terms of scholarly contributions, Dr. Srivastava has published her research in reputable journals and edited book series, and she has presented her work at numerous national and international conferences. She is also involved in the academic community as a reviewer for prestigious conferences such as EURAM and INDAM, as well as for various SCOPUS and WoS indexed journals.

She currently teaches courses on Principles and Practices of Management and Mergers and Acquisitions and Corporate Restructuring to undergraduate students.
