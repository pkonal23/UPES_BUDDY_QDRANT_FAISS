Dr. Nilanjana Banerjee is an Associate Professor and Cluster Head at the School of Advanced Engineering, known for her dedication to equity in education and diverse teaching methodologies. Her approach includes various assessment techniques aimed at fostering holistic learning, such as project-based learning, flipped classrooms, and a combination of synchronous and asynchronous lectures. Dr. Banerjee promotes application-oriented assessments through open book and open resource examinations, allowing students to learn at their own pace and excel academically.

Her professional journey includes significant roles prior to her current position. Dr. Banerjee was a Lecturer in the Department of Chemical Engineering at Maharaja Sayajirao University, Vadodara (2010-2012), where she focused on teaching and mentoring students. She then worked as a post-doctoral fellow (2016-2017) at Missouri University of Science and Technology, specializing in high-shear polymer rheology. Following this, she served as a Senior Consultant at Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA), where she collaborated with state universities in Maharashtra to optimize fund utilization and enhance academic quality.

Her research interests include Alternate Energy Resources, Biomass and Biofuels, Decarbonization, Household Air Pollution, Reactor Design and Engineering, and Modeling and Simulation. She teaches Chemical Reaction Engineering to pre-final year students, integrating theoretical concepts with practical applications using tools like MS Excel.

Throughout her 10-year academic career, Dr. Banerjee has taught various courses such as mass transfer, heat transfer, hydrodynamics, fluid mechanics, and risk management in energy industries. She has received several accolades, including gold medals for academic excellence during her undergraduate studies and a nomination for the ISTE-IPCL best master thesis award. She also holds a post-graduate diploma in Leadership and Organizational Management (LEAP) from UPES and a Six Sigma White Belt certificate.

In her current role, Dr. Banerjee manages a team of 40 faculty and staff members and oversees the academic progress of 800 students across multiple undergraduate and postgraduate programs in chemical and petroleum engineering and earth sciences. She has played a pivotal role in strategic planning for the School of Advanced Engineering and has been involved in accreditation processes like NAAC and NBA. Dr. Banerjee is a member of the American Institute of Chemical Technology and the Institute of Engineering and Technology in the UK, maintaining research collaborations with prestigious institutions such as MIT, ICT Mumbai, and the Max Planck Institute of Multidisciplinary Sciences.
