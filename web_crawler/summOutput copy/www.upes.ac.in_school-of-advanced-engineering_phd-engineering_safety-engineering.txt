The Ph.D. in Safety Engineering program at the UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to produce highly skilled researchers equipped to address complex safety challenges across various sectors. The program emphasizes practical applications, interdisciplinary collaboration, and rigorous coursework, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for the evolving demands of the safety engineering field.

### Key Program Details:
- **Duration**: 3 to 5 years.
- **Financial Support**: Ph.D. scholars receive a monthly stipend of up to ₹30,000 and exceptional students with NET-JRF or INSPIRE fellowships can receive an annual cash grant of ₹2,00,000.
- **Mentorship**: Students benefit from guidance by esteemed faculty from IITs, NITs, and top QS ranking universities, as well as collaboration with 6 international faculty members.
- **Curriculum**: The coursework includes core topics such as hazard identification, risk assessment, safety management systems, and regulatory compliance, along with advanced statistics, research methods, and specialized topics in system safety engineering and environmental safety.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates of the program can pursue careers in various fields, including:
- Research and Development (R&D) in environmental, health, fire, and occupational safety.
- Academic positions as professors or lecturers.
- Roles in environmental monitoring, fire safety engineering, and occupational health safety.

### Future Scope and Industry Trends:
The future of Safety Engineering is promising, focusing on areas like wastewater-based surveillance, environmental monitoring, and sustainable workplace practices. Graduates will be well-positioned to lead in the development of eco-friendly and resilient structures, addressing the growing demand for safety and sustainability in industries.
