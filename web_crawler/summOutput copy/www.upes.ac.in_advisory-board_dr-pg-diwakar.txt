Dr. P. G. Diwakar has had a distinguished 36-year career at the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), where he held several key positions, including:

- Associate Director of the Earth Observation System
- Programme Director for Earth Observation & Disaster Management System
- Deputy Director at the National Remote Sensing Centre (NRSC) in Hyderabad
- Director of Earth Observation & Disaster Management
- Scientific Secretary of ISRO in Bengaluru
- Honorary Distinguished Professor at ISRO

Currently, he is the ISRO Chair Professor at the National Institute of Advanced Studies (NIAS), located on the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Campus in Bangalore, India. Dr. Diwakar has authored over 100 publications in both national and international journals and symposiums, showcasing his expertise in various domains, including Space Technology & Applications, Digital Image Processing, Application Software Design & Development, and Geospatial Technology and Solutions. 

For further details, he is based at the IISc Campus in Bangalore, India.
