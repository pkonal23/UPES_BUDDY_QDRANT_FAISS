**Profile Summary of Silky Goel**

Silky Goel is an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science, UPES, with over 7 years of experience in both teaching and the industry. She joined UPES in 2019 after working at Graphic Era Hill University from 2016 to 2019. Her academic background includes a B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering from Uttarakhand Technical University, Dehradun, and an M.Tech in Computer Engineering from IIIT Bhubaneswar. Currently, she is pursuing a PhD at UPES, Dehradun.

**Research Interests**
Silky Goel's research focuses on Medical Imaging Analysis, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning within the healthcare sector. She has a strong publication record, contributing to various journals, conferences, and book chapters, particularly in the fields of computer vision and image processing.

**Teaching Philosophy**
Silky believes in fostering a supportive and encouraging learning environment that integrates group activities, real-world applications, and continuous assessment. Her approach empowers students to engage deeply with the material.
