The UPES School of Design offers a comprehensive Bachelor of Design (B.Des) program with a focus on Mobility and Vehicle Design, equipping students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the evolving transportation industry. This undergraduate program emphasizes the integration of cutting-edge technology and innovation, utilizing state-of-the-art facilities such as the High-Performance Visualization Lab and the Rapid Product Development Lab. Students are trained to design mobility solutions that are not only functional but also socially significant, addressing both technical and socio-economic considerations.

### Future Scope and Industry Trends
The program prepares students for various career paths including Project Associate, Design Strategist, Transportation Designer, Entrepreneur, and Digital Sculptor. It highlights the growing demand for skilled professionals in the transportation sector, driven by advancements in technology such as cloud-based systems, mobility-as-a-service (MaaS), and integrated travel solutions. Industry collaborations with companies like Tata Elxsi, Eicher Motors, and Ola Motors enhance students' practical knowledge and exposure to real-world design challenges.

### Selection Criteria and Eligibility
To be eligible for the B.Des program, students must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII (any stream) and must go through a selection process based on performance in National Level Exams (NID/NIFT/UCEED/JEE-Mains), UPESDAT, Board Merit, or CUET, followed by a Portfolio Presentation and Interview Round.

### Curriculum and Facilities
The curriculum for 2023-2027 is designed to provide students with essential design knowledge through lectures, discussions, and hands-on assignments. The program covers specialized areas such as vehicle exteriors and interiors, user interface and experience design, and alternative transportation modes. Students benefit from access to advanced labs equipped with tools like Cintiq, Mac computers, CAD software, clay modeling, 3D printing, and VR technology, fostering a conducive learning environment.

### Career Opportunities and Placement
Graduates can expect to enter a dynamic job market, with opportunities in various sectors including heavy vehicle manufacturing and automotive design. The program has strong ties with prominent recruiters like LTIMindtree and TCS Design Studio, and graduates can anticipate an average annual salary of approximately Rs. 6.50 Lakhs.
