The UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a B. Tech. program in Aerospace Engineering with specializations in Space Technology, Avionics, and Defence Technology. This program is designed to equip students with both practical skills and theoretical knowledge essential for careers in the aerospace sector, which is characterized by a strong demand for skilled engineers driven by advancements in space exploration, satellite technology, and defense systems.

### Curriculum Overview
The curriculum is comprehensive and includes various subjects across eight semesters, integrating core engineering principles with specialized knowledge. Key subjects include:
- **Aerodynamics**
- **Flight Mechanics**
- **Propulsion Systems**
- **Orbital Mechanics**
- **Avionics System Design**

Students also engage in hands-on learning through modern labs such as the Aircraft Avionics System Lab and the Propulsion Lab, as well as through industrial internships and major projects.

### Specializations
1. **Space Technology**: Focuses on spacecraft design, satellite dynamics, and space environment studies.
2. **Avionics Technology**: Covers navigation systems, radar engineering, and aircraft communication technologies.
3. **Defence Technology**: Emphasizes warfare simulations, missile fundamentals, and defense navigation systems.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates from this program can pursue careers in various domains, including:
- Space research organizations
- Aircraft design and maintenance companies
- Defense research and development agencies
- Satellite technology firms

The program boasts strong industry connections, facilitating placement opportunities with top aerospace companies, ensuring graduates are well-prepared to enter the workforce.

### Admission Process
To apply for the B. Tech. Aerospace Engineering program, candidates must meet the eligibility criteria of a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII. Selection is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Marks, SAT, or CUET.

### Contact Information
For further inquiries or to apply, potential students can reach out to the UPES School of Advanced Engineering. The last date to apply for the program is February 25, 2025.

This detailed program structure and the emphasis on practical experience and industry relevance position UPES graduates as valuable assets in the rapidly evolving aerospace sector.
