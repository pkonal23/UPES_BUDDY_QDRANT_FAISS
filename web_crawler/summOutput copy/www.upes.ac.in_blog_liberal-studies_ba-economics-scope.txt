The UPES (University of Petroleum and Energy Studies) offers comprehensive Liberal Studies programs designed to prepare students for future career opportunities across various sectors. These programs emphasize a cutting-edge curriculum and industry-relevant skills, enabling students to tailor their career paths according to their interests and ambitions.

### Key Highlights of the Programs:
- **Salary Potential**: Entry-level positions typically offer salaries ranging from ₹4-6 lakhs per annum, with experienced professionals potentially earning between ₹10-12 lakhs. For self-employment or startups, starting salaries can range from ₹5-7 lakhs, with growth potential of ₹12-15 lakhs for senior roles.
- **Career Opportunities**: Graduates can pursue roles such as financial analysts, investment bankers, corporate consultants, and economic advisors. The need for professionals in public relations, corporate communications, and data analytics is also increasing, especially for those skilled in social media.
- **Economics Degree**: A B.A. in Economics equips students with analytical, quantitative, and problem-solving skills, opening doors to careers in finance, data science, and public policy. Specific roles include:
  - **Financial Analyst**: Evaluates investment performance and advises clients.
  - **Data Scientist**: Analyzes large datasets using statistical tools and machine learning.
  - **Market Research Analyst**: Studies market conditions to guide marketing strategies.
  - **Corporate Consultant**: Advises organizations on economic trends and strategic planning.
  - **Policy Advisor**: Works with government bodies to formulate economic policies.

### Program Structure:
- **Practical Exposure**: Students will engage in projects, internships, and industry interactions that provide hands-on experience in financial modeling, portfolio management, and economic forecasting.
- **Curriculum Focus**: The programs include courses on financial markets, investment strategies, risk management, and corporate finance, integrating economics with data science.

### Salary Overview:
- **Entry-Level Salaries**: 
  - Economics graduates: ₹4-6 lakhs per annum.
  - Data scientists: ₹8-10 lakhs per annum.
  - Corporate consultants: ₹8-10 lakhs per annum.
- **Experienced Professionals**: 
  - Economics graduates: ₹8-12 lakhs.
  - Data scientists: ₹15-25 lakhs.
  - Corporate consultants: Upwards of ₹20 lakhs.
