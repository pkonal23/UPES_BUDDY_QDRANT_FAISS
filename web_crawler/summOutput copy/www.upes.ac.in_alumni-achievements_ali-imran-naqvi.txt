Mr. Ali Imran Naqvi, the current Chief Executive Officer of Gensol Engineering Ltd., has been recognized as one of the outstanding figures in the Indian solar industry by being included in the SQ India 40 Under 40 program. This initiative highlights the achievements of exceptional individuals who play crucial roles as catalysts in their companies amidst the evolving solar landscape.

Naqvi, an alumnus of the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), graduated in 2007 with an MBA in Power Management. His recognition in the 40 Under 40 club underscores his significant contributions and leadership within the solar sector in India.

For further details, you may refer to the School of Business at UPES or Gensol Engineering Ltd. for inquiries regarding Naqvi's achievements and the broader impact of his work in the industry.
