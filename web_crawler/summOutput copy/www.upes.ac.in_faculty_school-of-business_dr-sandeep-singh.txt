Dr. Sandeep Singh is an Assistant Professor at the School of Business, UPES, specializing in Business Statistics, Quantitative Techniques for Managers, Optimization Techniques, and Business Research Methodology. He holds a Ph.D. from Banaras Hindu University and is recognized as a scholar in statistics, with a strong focus on statistical modelling, biostatistics, and Statistical and Mathematical Demography.

Dr. Singh has made significant contributions to the field through his extensive research, having published 20 papers in reputable national and international journals. His research emphasizes both applied and theoretical statistical methods, particularly in developing probability models and applying them to real-life problems.

In his role as an educator, Dr. Singh is committed to mentoring students at various academic levels, enhancing their analytical and quantitative skills essential for effective business leadership. He teaches core and advanced statistics courses, integrating real-world applications and the latest advancements to improve students' decision-making abilities.

Before his tenure at UPES, Dr. Singh served as an Assistant Professor in Statistics at Sharda University, Greater Noida, within the Department of Mathematics at the School of Basic Sciences and Research. He has also qualified for the National Eligibility Test (NET) with Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) from the University Grants Commission (UGC).

Courses taught by Dr. Singh include:
- Mathematical Statistics I
- Statistical Inference I
- Data Science I
- Population Mathematics I
- Statistical and Mathematical Demography I
- Business Analytics I
- Optimization Techniques I
- Quantitative Techniques
