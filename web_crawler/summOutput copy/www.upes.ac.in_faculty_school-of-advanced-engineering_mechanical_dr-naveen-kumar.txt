Dr. Naveen Kumar is an Assistant Professor in the Mechanical Cluster at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), specializing in Manufacturing Technologies. His primary research interests encompass a wide range of areas, including Additive Manufacturing, Joining Technologies specifically for Electric Vehicle (EV) applications, Advanced Welding Technologies, Welding Automation, Process Monitoring, Material Characterization Techniques, Simulation of various joining processes, Surface Engineering, and Erosive Wear.

In addition to his research, Dr. Naveen is actively involved in the academic community as a reviewer for several prestigious national and international journals. These include the *Proceedings of the iMeche, Part E: Journal of Process Mechanical Engineering*, *Welding International*, *Frontier Robotics and AI*, *Journal of Manufacturing Processes*, *Metallurgical and Materials Transactions A*, *International Journal of Advanced Manufacturing Technology*, and *Journal of Materials Engineering and Performance*.

Before his tenure at UPES, Dr. Naveen served as an Institute Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at IIT Delhi from May 2022 to May 2024. He also held a position as an Assistant Professor at the Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET) Lucknow from September 2021 to May 2022.
