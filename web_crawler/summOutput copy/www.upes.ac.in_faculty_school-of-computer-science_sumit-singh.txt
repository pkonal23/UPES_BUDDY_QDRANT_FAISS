**Profile Summary of Sumit Singh**

**Current Position:**  
Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science, UPES.

**Education:**

**Teaching Philosophy:**  
Sumit Singh emphasizes inclusivity, engagement, and the promotion of curiosity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning among students. His teaching approach focuses on active learning, collaboration, and practical applications, integrating innovative strategies and technology. He is committed to diversity and equity in education, aiming to equip students with the necessary skills and mindsets to succeed in a dynamic environment. He views teaching as a collaborative and evolving process, valuing feedback and reflection to support continuous learning.

**Work Experience:**  
Before joining UPES, he served as a Senior Research Fellow (SRF) at the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad.

- International committee member for the **14th International Conference on Intelligent Human-Computer Interaction (IHCI)** (2022).
