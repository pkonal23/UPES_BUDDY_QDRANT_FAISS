The BBA in Oil & Gas Management (Petroleum Business) program at UPES School of Business is designed to prepare students for diverse and dynamic career opportunities within the energy sector. Graduates can pursue roles such as Oil & Gas regulatory and financial consultants, Energy Analysts, ESG specialists, CGD Business Development Managers, and positions in transportation, distribution, business development, and marketing related to Oil & Gas. 

The program emphasizes practical exposure through summer internships, live projects, and placements, ensuring students gain a robust foundation for success in the corporate world. Students also have the opportunity for global exposure through student exchange and study abroad programs with leading international partner management institutes.

Key components of the curriculum include:
- Major projects and hands-on learning experiences.
- Engagement in research projects, seminars, and industry networking.
- A focus on retail marketing of petroleum products, consumer behavior, and leadership through industrial assignments.
- Training in relevant software and technology such as Advanced Excel and Power-BI.

The program addresses the industry's significant shortage of skilled professionals by providing practical experience that enhances students' competitiveness in the job market. The curriculum covers the Upstream, Midstream, and Downstream sectors, emerging trends in energy, and includes subjects like Natural Gas Business, Refinery Management, and Marketing Research.

UPES School of Business features modern facilities including smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, and an extensive library, creating an effective learning environment. The program has seen an increase in placement opportunities, with companies like Ernst and Young, Deloitte, KPMG, and SHELL actively recruiting graduates. Starting salaries for fresh graduates typically range between INR 3-9 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills and industry exposure.

To be eligible for the program, students must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII. The admission process involves performance evaluation through entrance exams such as UPESMET-UG, UGAT, or CUET.
