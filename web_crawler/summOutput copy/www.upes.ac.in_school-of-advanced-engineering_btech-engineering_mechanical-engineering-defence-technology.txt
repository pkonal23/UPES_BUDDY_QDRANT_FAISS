The B. Tech. Mechanical Engineering (Defence Technology) program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to excel in the defence sector, which is increasingly critical in today's evolving global landscape. The program emphasizes hands-on learning, industry partnerships, and a curriculum tailored to meet the demands of defence technology.

### Program Overview:
- **Infrastructure and Partnerships**: The program benefits from robust infrastructure and global partnerships, providing students with exceptional learning experiences and exposure to diverse career opportunities both locally and internationally.
- **Focus Areas**: The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including weapon systems, military communication, radar technology, aerospace engineering, and cybersecurity. This multidisciplinary approach prepares students for various roles within the defence industry.

### Curriculum Structure:
- **Semesters**: The program spans eight semesters, with courses including:
  - Engineering Mathematics
  - Fluid Mechanics
  - Mechanics of Materials
  - Manufacturing Processes
  - Advanced Manufacturing
  - Specializations in areas such as Machine Design, Aerospace Modelling, and Propulsion Engineering.
- **Hands-On Experience**: Students engage in projects related to missile systems, UAVs, and cybersecurity, fostering practical skills essential for their future careers.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates are well-prepared for diverse roles, including:
- Space Mission Design Engineer
- Propulsion Engineer
- Aircraft Design & Analysis Engineer
- Scientists in Defence Research and Development Organizations
- Air Defence Systems Engineers
- Opportunities for entrepreneurial ventures in the defence sector.

### Industry Connections:
The program boasts strong ties with leading companies in the aerospace and defence sectors, such as:
- Safran Aerospace India
- Airbus
- Boeing
- Tata Advanced Systems
- Honeywell Technologies
- Bombardier India Engineering Centre
- Numerous startups and tech firms focusing on aerospace innovations.

### Admission and Eligibility:
- **Eligibility Criteria**: Applicants must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class XII with a focus on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Selection is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.
- **Application Process**: Interested candidates can apply through the UPES website, where they can also find detailed information regarding the fee structure and course specifics.

### Contact Information:
For inquiries or further information, prospective students can fill out an enquiry form or contact the admissions office through the UPES website.

### Conclusion:
The B. Tech. Mechanical Engineering (Defence Technology) program at UPES is a gateway to a promising career in the defence sector, preparing students to meet India's security challenges and contribute to national advancement. With a strong emphasis on practical experience and industry engagement, graduates are poised to become leaders in the defence technology field.
