Bhumika Ray is an Assistant Professor in the Senior Scale at the School of Business. She is currently engaged in research focusing on several key areas, including the Future of Work, Gig Employment, Employer-Employee Relationships, Psychological Contracts, and overall Well-Being.

Bhumika is in the process of completing her Ph.D. in Organisational Behaviour and Human Resource Management at the Indian Institute of Management (IIM), Sambalpur, India. She has an academic background that includes a Master of Business Administration and a Bachelor of Arts in Foundations of Management from the Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Indore, where she participated in the Integrated Programme in Management (IPM). Furthermore, she holds a Bachelor of Arts with Honors in Sociology from Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU). 

Her professional career includes experience at Tata Trusts, which she undertook after completing her postgraduate studies.
