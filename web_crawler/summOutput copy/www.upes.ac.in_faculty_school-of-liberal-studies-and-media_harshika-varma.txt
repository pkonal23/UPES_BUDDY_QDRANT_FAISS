Dr. Harshika Varma is a Senior Assistant Professor at the School of Liberal Studies and Media, with a strong focus on Advertising, Political Communication, and Digital Marketing. Her extensive research background and industry experience enable her to deliver practical education in Corporate Communications, Public Relations, Advertising Research, Media Planning, Event Management, Theories of Advertising and Public Relations, and Brand Management.

**Contact Information:**
- Email: harshika.varma@ddn.upes.ac.in

**Work Experience:**
- Amity University (February 2022 – June 2023)
- Jaipur National University (January 2014 – May 2018)
- Chandigarh Group of Colleges (January 2013 – September 2013)

**Educational Background:**
Dr. Varma holds a doctorate in Journalism and Mass Communication, a Certificate Course in Business Management from IIT-Delhi, and a master’s degree from the School of Communication Studies at Panjab University, Chandigarh.

**Scholarly Activities and International Engagements:**
Her commitment to academia is reflected in her role as the Organizing Secretary for the International Conference on "Gender and Politics in Media and Literature" in 2023. She has also been an invited speaker for discussions on the substantive representation of women in Asian Parliaments, hosted by the Konrad Adenauer Foundation. Additionally, she represented India at the Europe-Asia Dialogue Program in Brussels, Belgium.

**Awards and Recognition:**
Dr. Varma has received several prestigious awards, including:
- Indian Army Women's Day Achiever Award (2021) from HQ 10 Corps, Bathinda Cantt.
- Nari Shakti Samman Award (2023) for her significant contributions to education.

**Teaching Philosophy:**
Dr. Varma's teaching approach emphasizes experiential learning and research-driven methodologies, fostering a dynamic and student-centered learning environment. She encourages active engagement through hands-on projects and practical exercises, emphasizing the importance of research and critical analysis. Her global engagements and focus on women's empowerment highlight her dedication to providing a diverse and inclusive educational experience, nurturing students' talents and empowering them to succeed in their careers.

Overall, Dr. Harshika Varma's extensive experience, academic credentials, and commitment to education and empowerment position her as a leading figure in her field.
