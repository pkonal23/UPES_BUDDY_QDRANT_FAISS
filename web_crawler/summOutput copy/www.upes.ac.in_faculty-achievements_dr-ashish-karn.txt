Dr. Ashish Karn has recently achieved a significant milestone by securing a highly competitive research grant for the project titled "Early Science Technology Engineering Education and Mathematics (ESTEEM) of India Research Centre." This grant will facilitate research into various critical areas, including mathematics, astronomy, sustainable agriculture, and food preservation systems in India. 

In addition to this achievement, Dr. Karn has also been recognized as an invited speaker at the International Conference on Indian Knowledge Systems, highlighting his expertise and contributions to the field of mechanical engineering.

For further information, Dr. Ashish Karn is associated with the School of Advanced Engineering.
