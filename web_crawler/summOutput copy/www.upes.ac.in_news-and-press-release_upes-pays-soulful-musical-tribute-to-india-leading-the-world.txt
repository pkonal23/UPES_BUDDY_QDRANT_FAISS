**Summary of UPES Initiatives and Achievements**

UPES (University of Petroleum and Energy Studies), located in Dehradun, India, recently celebrated the spirit of the nation by launching a music video titled "India Leads the World." This initiative highlights India's emergence as a global superpower, emphasizing its rich cultural heritage alongside modern advancements. The video features various art forms, including Bharatnatyam and contemporary dance, and showcases performances by students, faculty, and staff members, including senior leaders like Sharad Mehra (Chairman of Hydrocarbons Education Research Society) and Dr. Ram K Sharma (Vice Chancellor of UPES), reflecting the university's commitment to inclusivity and community spirit.

The music for the video was composed by Nishant Ramteke, a talented musician from Maharashtra, and the project was produced in collaboration with Silvercord Films. The video pays homage to the significant contributions of India on the global stage, particularly during challenging times, such as the COVID-19 pandemic. It serves as a reminder of India's potential and aspirations as a leader in various sectors.

UPES was established in 2003 through the UPES Act by the State Legislature of Uttarakhand and is recognized as one of the top private universities in India. According to the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2023, UPES ranks 52 overall, 54 in engineering, and 39 in management. It has also been recognized by QS World Rankings 2023 as the 3rd best private university in India and ranked 243 in Asia. The university boasts an ‘A’ grade accreditation from NAAC and has achieved over 90% placements in recent years, earning a 5-Star rating for employability.

UPES offers a diverse range of graduate and postgraduate programs across eight schools, including:
- School of Advanced Engineering
- School of Computer Science
- School of Design
- School of Law
- School of Business
- School of Health Sciences & Technology
- School of Modern Media
- School of Liberal Studies

With approximately 13,000 students and over 1,500 faculty and staff members, UPES embodies values of respect, inclusivity, and passion in education.

For further information, UPES can be contacted through their official website or by visiting their campus in Dehradun, Uttarakhand.
