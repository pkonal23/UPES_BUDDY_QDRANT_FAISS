The B. Tech. Civil Engineering program at UPES offers a comprehensive four-year undergraduate curriculum designed to produce industry-ready professionals. The program focuses on advanced construction technologies, infrastructure development, and sustainable practices, preparing students for the dynamic job market. 

### Program Structure and Specializations
Students can choose from three specializations:
1. **Transportation Engineering**
2. **Environmental Engineering**
3. **Construction Management**

The curriculum includes practical labs such as Physics Lab, Concrete Technology Lab, and Engineering Design Lab, alongside theoretical courses in subjects like Fluid Mechanics, Structural Design, and Engineering Economics. The program is structured across eight semesters, with opportunities for research projects, industrial internships, and explorations in emerging technologies like AI, Robotics, and 3D Printing.

### Faculty and Collaborations
The program is supported by esteemed faculty from globally recognized universities and industry experts. It features collaborations with major industry players like L&T and consultants such as Ascela and TraNXT Advisory, ensuring that course syllabi are relevant to real-world applications. Students also benefit from global exposure through partnerships with institutions like the University of Exeter, University of Aberdeen, and Indiana University Purdue University Indianapolis (IUPUI).

### Career Opportunities
Graduates can pursue a variety of roles in both private and government sectors, including positions as Design Engineers, Project Managers, and Site Engineers. Opportunities also exist in academia, research, and consultancy. The program boasts a high placement rate of 90%, with the highest salary package reaching 8 lakhs. Graduates are equipped to work in organizations such as PWD, NHAI, and various multinational corporations.

### Admission and Eligibility
To apply, candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII. The last date to apply for the program is February 25, 2025. Selection criteria include performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.

### Contact Information
For further details, prospective students can reach out to the admissions office through the UPES website or contact their support team for inquiries related to the program.

This program is particularly beneficial for students looking to tailor their education toward specific interests in civil engineering while gaining practical experience and global exposure.
