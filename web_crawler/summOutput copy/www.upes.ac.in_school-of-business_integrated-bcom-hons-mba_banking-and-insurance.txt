The Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA program offered by UPES School of Business focuses on providing students with a comprehensive education in business management, particularly in the banking and insurance sectors. The curriculum is designed to equip students with both foundational knowledge and specialized skills necessary for successful careers in these fields.

### Program Structure
- **Duration**: The program spans multiple semesters, covering essential subjects such as Accounting, Finance, Marketing, Human Resource Management, and Strategic Management, alongside specialized banking and insurance courses.
- **Key Courses**: Important subjects include Financial Modeling, Risk Management, Business Law, Corporate Restructuring, and Digital Marketing, among others.
- **Soft Skills Development**: The program emphasizes the development of soft skills such as communication, leadership, teamwork, and critical thinking through co-curricular activities, presentations, and group discussions.

### Industry Collaboration
The program collaborates with various banks, insurance companies, and financial institutions to enhance learning through guest lectures, workshops, and seminars. These partnerships provide students with industry-specific training and valuable networking opportunities.

### Internships and Practical Experience
Students are encouraged to undertake internships with banks and insurance companies, which provide real-world experience and the opportunity to apply theoretical knowledge in practical settings.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates of the program are well-prepared for diverse roles in the financial sector, including:
- Banking Managers
- Investment Analysts
- Risk Managers
- Insurance Underwriters
- Financial Consultants
- Compliance Officers

The program aims to prepare students for the evolving landscape of the banking and insurance industries, driven by technological advancements and regulatory changes.

### Future Scope and Industry Trends
The program highlights emerging trends in the financial sector, including:
- Integration of technology in banking (fintech) and insurance (insurtech)
- Growing demand for professionals with skills in digital tools, analytics, and risk management
- Increasing focus on sustainability and ESG factors in finance
- Opportunities in multinational banks and international finance institutions

### Admission Process and Eligibility
- **Eligibility Criteria**: Students must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII.
- **Selection Process**: Admission is based on the candidate's performance in a personal interview.
- **Contact Information**: For further inquiries, students can reach out via the provided contact methods, including phone numbers and email addresses.

### Conclusion
The Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance program at UPES is designed to provide a robust education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills, preparing students for successful careers in a dynamic and competitive industry. The focus on industry collaboration, soft skills development, and practical experience ensures that graduates are well-equipped to meet the demands of the banking and insurance sectors.
