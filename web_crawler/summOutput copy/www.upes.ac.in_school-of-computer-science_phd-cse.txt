The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is currently accepting applications for its PhD program in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE). The program is designed for candidates with a Master's degree or a professional degree equivalent to a Master's degree in Computer Science or allied sciences. Applicants must have a minimum of 55% marks in aggregate or an equivalent grade 'B' on the UGC 7-point scale. A relaxation of 5% is available for candidates belonging to SC/ST/OBC (non-creamy layer) and other specified categories.

Key deadlines include the last date to apply on August 9, 2024, and the last date to pay the fee on August 4, 2024. The program also provides five years of placement assistance, with graduates securing attractive packages of up to INR 50 lakh annually. Graduates can pursue careers as researchers or research scientists in R&D organizations, or they may choose to start their own companies.

The PhD program has a rigorous structure, emphasizing research methodology, computer architecture, and advanced topics such as Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Cybersecurity. Candidates are expected to submit a research proposal and work closely with faculty advisors throughout their studies. 

UPES offers a supportive environment with excellent research facilities, including state-of-the-art labs and libraries. The university also provides various scholarships for deserving students, including academic fellowships and freeships for economically weaker sections.

For further inquiries or assistance, prospective students can reach out via email at research@ddn.upes.ac.in or phd@upes.ac.in. Additionally, more information regarding scholarships can be found at https://www.upes.ac.in/admissions/scholarships.

Important dates for the admission process include:
- Application Deadline: August 9, 2024
- Last Date to Pay Fee: August 4, 2024
- Registration Date: June 10, 2024
- Commencement of Session: June 21, 2024
