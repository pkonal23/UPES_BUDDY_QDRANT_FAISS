**Profile Summary of Dr. Gagandeep Kaur**

Dr. Gagandeep Kaur is an Associate Professor at the School of Law, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun, with over 14 years of teaching and research experience in prestigious universities. She holds an LL.B. and LL.M., both with Gold Medals, and a Ph.D. in Law, specializing in Cyber Law, Information Technology Law, Cyber Crimes, and Data Security Law. 

**Achievements and Awards:**
- She received the ‘University Scholarship Holder’ during her LL.B. and LL.M. studies at Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar.
- In 2016, she was honored as the ‘Best Faculty Coordinator’ during the University Fest at Law College Dehradun, Uttaranchal University.
- In 2017, she was awarded the ‘Best Faculty Award’ at the College of Legal Studies, UPES.
- Most recently, in 2023, she received the ‘Research Excellence Award’ at UPES.

**Courses Taught:**
Dr. Kaur teaches various subjects, including Information Technology Law, Cyber Crimes, e-Commerce, Digital Copyrights Law, and IT Governance & Regulation. She emphasizes the importance of integrating theoretical knowledge with natural law philosophies and values to cultivate empathetic legal professionals. Her teaching approach includes elements of "flipping the classroom," where students engage in pre-reading assignments and quizzes to enhance their understanding of legal provisions.

**Research Contributions:**
Dr. Kaur has made significant contributions to the field of Cyber Law, authoring four books:
1. *Jurisprudence of e-Commerce and Consumer Protection*
2. *Cyber Laws & IT Protection*
3. *Sports Law: Issues & Challenges*
4. *Laws on Cloud Computing: Demystifying Laws, Policies & Privacy Compliances*

Additionally, she serves as the editor of another book titled *Emerging Dimensions of Cyber Law & IPR: Issues & Challenges in 21st Century*. She has published over 95 articles in peer-reviewed, UGC, SCOPUS Indexed, and Web of Science Journals, along with several book chapters with esteemed publishers such as Springer, Wiley, Palgrave Macmillan, Apple Academic Press, and CRC Press. Dr. Kaur has presented more than 100 research papers at national and international conferences and has served as a judge in various competitions, including Moot Court and Trial Advocacy.

**Professional Experience:**
Before her tenure at UPES, Dr. Kaur was associated with:
- Rajiv Gandhi National Law University, Patiala
- Guru Nanak Dev University, Amritsar
- Law College Dehradun, Uttaranchal University

She is also recognized as a mentor and has been invited as a resource person for workshops and faculty development programs.
