The UPES (University of Petroleum and Energy Studies) is committed to supporting students in making informed career choices, especially in light of current uncertainties. The university offers a comprehensive MBA admissions process for the academic year 2025-26, including the UPES Management Entrance Test (UPESMET) and various non-examination pathways.

### Key Features of the MBA Admissions Process:

1. **Eligibility Criteria**:
   - Candidates must graduate from a recognized university.
   - A minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation is required.
   - Students with a MAT/CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile or higher are exempt from the UPESMET and will proceed directly to an online interview if qualified.
   - Acceptable scores for CAT 2024/XAT 2025 (minimum 50 percentile), GMAT (minimum score of 400), and NMAT 2024/25 (minimum score of 120) are also recognized.

2. **Application Process**:
   - Students are encouraged to start the application process early.
   - Applications can be submitted online.
   - A demo test is recommended for practice and to resolve any queries prior to the examination.
   - Personal interviews will be conducted after the examination, with specific slot bookings available.

3. **Examination Details**:
   - The UPESMET will be conducted online, lasting 2 hours.
   - The exam will cover sections including General English, Analytical and Logical Reasoning, and Quantitative Data Interpretation and Sufficiency.
   - Students will receive their login credentials for the online exam via email and SMS a day before the test date.

4. **Refund Policy**:
   - The university adheres to UGC guidelines regarding refunds.

5. **Contact Information**:
   - For inquiries, students can reach out via the toll-free number: **18001028737**.
   - Email correspondence can be directed to **enrollments@upes.ac.in**.

6. **Additional Resources**:
   - A Candidate Guide Document is available for download, providing detailed information about the admission process.
   - Students can also take a demo test to familiarize themselves with the exam format.
