The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a comprehensive B.Tech program in Chemical Engineering that uniquely integrates Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), and digitalization into its curriculum, making it the only institution in India to do so. This innovative approach equips students with a skill set that aligns with current industry trends, preparing them for diverse roles in various sectors including chemicals, refining, petrochemicals, gas, fertilizers, energy solutions, pharmaceuticals, and specialty chemicals.

**Program Structure and Curriculum:**
The curriculum spans eight semesters and includes foundational courses such as Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics, Mass Transfer, Fluid Mechanics, Reaction Engineering, and Heat Transfer, supplemented by practical laboratory experiences. Advanced topics include Carbon Capture Sequestration, Circular Economy, and Digitalization of Process Technology. The program also features specializations in Energy Systems and Storage, Refining and Petrochemicals, and Digitalization of Process Technology, catering to the growing demand for clean energy professionals and technological advancements in the industry.

**Hands-on Experience and Research Opportunities:**
Students benefit from state-of-the-art facilities at the Centre of Excellence for Energy Systems, engaging in hands-on projects and research. The faculty consists of experts from prestigious global institutions, providing students with access to cutting-edge knowledge and potential pathways to postgraduate and Ph.D. programs.

**Career Prospects and Industry Collaborations:**
The program boasts a remarkable placement record, with 100% of graduates securing positions at leading companies such as ExxonMobil, Shell, and Reliance Industries, among others. Graduates are prepared for roles including Production Engineer, Design Engineer, and Data Analyst. UPES's collaboration with the University of Queensland enriches the program with international perspectives, further enhancing students' readiness for global careers.

**Eligibility and Admission Process:**
To apply, candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII. The selection criteria include performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET. The last date to apply for the program is February 25, 2025.

**Contact Information:**
For more details, prospective students can reach out through the UPES official website or contact the admissions office directly.

Overall, the B.Tech in Chemical Engineering at UPES offers a robust education that aligns with global trends in sustainability, innovation, and technology, positioning graduates for successful careers in a rapidly evolving field.
