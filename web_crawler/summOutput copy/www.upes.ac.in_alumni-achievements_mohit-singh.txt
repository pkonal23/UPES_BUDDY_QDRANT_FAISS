Mohit Singh has achieved notable recognition in the renewable energy sector, specifically in solar power. He was recently awarded the prestigious "40 Under 40 - Young Indian Solar Pioneer" award, which highlights individuals under the age of 40 who have significantly contributed to solar energy advancements in India. Out of a competitive pool, he was selected as one of 40 individuals from across the country, making him the youngest recipient on the list.

The selection process was conducted online, with a physical event scheduled for October 2023 to honor the awardees. Mohit Singh currently holds the position of Assistant Vice President at Climate Connect Digital. He is an alumnus of the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), where he graduated in 2017 with an MBA in Power Management. His achievement underscores his impactful role in the Indian renewable energy landscape, particularly in solar power initiatives.
