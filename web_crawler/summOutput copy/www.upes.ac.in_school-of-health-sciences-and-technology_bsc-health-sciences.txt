The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) program at its School of Health Sciences and Technology, which includes a specialization in Microbiology. This specialization delves into the study of microorganisms and their effects on human health and the environment. The curriculum covers essential topics such as microbial physiology, medical microbiology, immunology, and microbial genetics, equipping students with laboratory skills in microbial identification and disease diagnosis. Graduates can pursue careers in various sectors, including research institutions, healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and environmental agencies.

### Curriculum Overview
The B.Sc. program emphasizes a comprehensive educational experience, offering a cutting-edge curriculum and state-of-the-art facilities. Students engage in hands-on training across disciplines such as medical laboratory technology, radiology, optometry, and healthcare informatics. The program is designed to ensure that graduates are well-prepared for the healthcare sector through industry-relevant training, internships, and projects.

### Specializations Offered
In addition to Microbiology, the B.Sc. program allows students to specialize in:
- **Food Nutrition & Dietetics:** This specialization focuses on nutritional needs and includes practical sessions on designing personalized diets and managing nutrition-related disorders.
- **Clinical Research:** This area covers clinical trial design, data analysis, and regulatory requirements, preparing students for roles in clinical research organizations and pharmaceutical companies.

### Admission Criteria
To be eligible for the B.Sc. program, students must have:
- A minimum of 50% marks in both X and XII exams, with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics, and English as major subjects in XII.
- The last date to apply for the program is February 28, 2025.

### Facilities and Support
UPES provides hostel facilities for students, with accommodations available on a first-come, first-served basis for both boys and girls. Students interested in on-campus housing must pay the fee in advance. 

### Career Opportunities
Graduates of the B.Sc. program can explore various career paths, including:
- Clinical Research Associates
- Healthcare Administrators
- Public Health Specialists
- Medical Coders
- Healthcare Consultants
- Pharmaceutical Sales Representatives

The program's strong focus on practical training and industry collaborations enhances graduates' employability in leading healthcare companies, hospitals, and research organizations.

### Additional Information
UPES emphasizes the importance of student feedback and evaluations, which provide insights into the quality of education, faculty, and career opportunities. The program also includes a SHODH research grant for students pursuing research projects.

For more details on the program, including fee structures and hostel information, students are encouraged to contact UPES directly or visit their official site.
