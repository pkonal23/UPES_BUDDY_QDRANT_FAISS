Dr. Varun Sapra is an accomplished academic and computer science professional with 18 years of experience in both academia and industry. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and currently serves as an Associate Professor at the School of Computer Science at UPES. Dr. Varun is dedicated to creating a positive and stimulating learning environment that promotes critical thinking and intellectual development among students. He believes in education as a collaborative process that emphasizes active engagement, inquiry, and exploration, rather than merely the transmission of knowledge from teacher to student.

Before his tenure at UPES, Dr. Varun was associated with Jagannath International Management School in New Delhi, which is affiliated with Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University. Throughout his career, he has not only fulfilled his teaching responsibilities but also taken pride in guiding students in their academic and professional development. He supervises research projects and actively encourages student involvement in extracurricular activities, hackathons, and competitions.

Dr. Varun is also a prolific researcher, having contributed significantly to the field of computer science through over 40 research papers, 6 patents, and the editing of three books. His research interests primarily focus on the applications of machine learning and deep learning. He is committed to lifelong learning and frequently participates in conferences, workshops, and seminars to remain updated with the latest advancements in computer science.

In his teaching, Dr. Varun covers a blend of core and specialized subjects, including programming languages such as C and C++, Object Oriented Analysis and Design, Python, Software Engineering, Database Management Systems (DBMS), Web Technologies, and Machine Learning. He employs innovative teaching methods to engage students and enhance their critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
