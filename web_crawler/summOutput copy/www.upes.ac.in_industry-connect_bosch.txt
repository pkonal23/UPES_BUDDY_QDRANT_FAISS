Bosch is a prominent global supplier of technology and services across various sectors, including Mobility Solutions, Industrial Technology, Consumer Goods, and Energy and Building Technology. In India, Bosch operates the largest development center outside of Germany, focusing on comprehensive engineering and technology solutions.

In collaboration with the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Bosch aims to enhance knowledge transfer, research, and development in Automation Technologies. This partnership is designed to bolster the technical skills, employability, and entrepreneurial capabilities of both undergraduate and postgraduate students enrolled in programs such as Applied Petroleum Engineering, Geo-Science Engineering, Geo-Informatics Engineering, Mechatronics, and Electronics.

The initiative emphasizes the importance of equipping students with the necessary competencies to thrive in the evolving technological landscape, thereby fostering a more skilled workforce in the field of Automation Technologies.
