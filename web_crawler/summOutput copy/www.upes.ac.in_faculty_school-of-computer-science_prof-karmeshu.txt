Professor Karmeshu is a distinguished academic in the field of mathematical modeling and simulation, with a rich history of leadership and contributions to various educational and research institutions. He served as the elected President of the 85th Session of the Indian Science Congress Association (ISCA) and held significant positions on the Senate of several Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs), including IIT Delhi and IIT Roorkee. He has also been involved in curriculum development and advisory roles, particularly in the areas of Bioinformatics and Mathematical Sciences.

Throughout his career, Prof. Karmeshu has contributed to numerous committees and groups, such as the Expert Group on Informatics Technology under the New Millennium Indian Technology Leadership Initiative (NMIITLI) of CSIR and the Standing Committee on National Mission on Education through ICT, MHRD. He has been a guest editor for special issues on Mathematical Modeling during CSIR's Golden Jubilee Year and has served multiple terms on the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Committee in the Mathematical Sciences.

His teaching philosophy emphasizes the cultivation of analytical capabilities among students, preparing them for diverse careers, including research. He focuses on the importance of understanding basic concepts and their relevance, fostering interdisciplinary discourse, and encouraging the use of mathematical and computational methods to solve real-world challenges. Prof. Karmeshu promotes interactive learning and collaborative projects, aiming to create an engaging educational environment.

Over the last four decades, he has taught various courses related to deterministic and stochastic systems across disciplines such as Computer Science, Engineering, Biological Sciences, and Management Science. His research interests include non-linear stochastic modeling, simulation of multidisciplinary systems, and the application of mathematical modeling in understanding complex phenomena like urbanization and environmental issues.

Before his tenure at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Prof. Karmeshu was a Professor and Dean at Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) from 1986 to 2017 and served as a Distinguished Professor at Shiv Nadar University (SNU) from 2017 to 2022. He has also held visiting positions in Europe, Canada, and Mexico and has delivered numerous research seminars internationally.

In recognition of his significant contributions, he has received several prestigious awards, including the Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Prize for Science and Technology, the CM Jacob Medal, and the Distinguished Service Award from the Vijnana Parishad of India. He was honored with a D.Sc. (Honoris Causa) by Dayalbagh Educational Institute and is a fellow of various scientific societies.

Currently, Prof. Karmeshu is the Director of the Centre for Stochastic Modelling and Simulation at UPES and serves as the Chairman of the Scientific Computing Panel, Naval Research Board, DRDO. He is also an Adjunct Professor at Atal Bihari Vajpayee Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management (IIITM-Gwalior).
