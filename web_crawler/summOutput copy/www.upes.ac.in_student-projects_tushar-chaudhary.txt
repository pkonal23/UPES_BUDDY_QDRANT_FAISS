Tushar Chaudhary is a student enrolled in the Bachelor of Design (B.Des) program, specializing in Transportation & Mobility Design, as part of the 2019-2023 academic batch. He has completed a project focused on designing an electric version of the TVS XL, which is intended for both personal and commercial use. 

The project emphasizes creating an electric iteration that features significant storage capacity and customizable options to suit various cargo needs and extensions. The primary objective of this design study is to identify the preferences and requirements of potential customers, enabling the development of electric variants of the TVS XL that meet their specifications.

Tushar's work is supervised by Senior Associate Professor Kishor Tikale from the School of Design. The project falls under the category of student projects, highlighting its academic context and mentorship.
