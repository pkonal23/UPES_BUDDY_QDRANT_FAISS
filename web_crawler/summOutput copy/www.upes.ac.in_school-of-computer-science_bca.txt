The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is a comprehensive three-year undergraduate degree focused on computer science and its applications. The course is structured into eight semesters, covering essential topics such as programming, data structures, databases, and specialized areas like Artificial Intelligence (AI), Cyber Security, and Internet of Things (IoT). 

**Program Highlights:**
- **Accreditation:** UPES is ranked 46th by NIRF in the Engineering category for 2024 and is accredited by the University Grants Commission of India.
- **Placement Success:** The BCA program boasts a 100% placement rate, with over 630 students placed by more than 190 national and international recruiters. The average starting salary for graduates is between ₹5-7 LPA, which can rise to ₹4-10 LPA within 2-4 years.
- **Specializations:** Students can choose from specializations such as AI, Cyber Security, and IoT, preparing them for careers in diverse fields like finance, healthcare, e-commerce, and more.
- **Industry Collaboration:** UPES maintains alliances with major organizations like IBM and Microsoft, enhancing the practical learning experience for students.
- **Scholarship Opportunities:** Scholarships are available for eligible students, and the university offers lifetime placement assistance.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
To enroll in the BCA program, candidates must have:
- 50% marks in Class X and XII.
- Mathematics, Computer Science, or Information Technology as a major subject in Class XII.

**Additional Opportunities:**
- Students have the option for credit transfer programs with institutions like the University of Queensland, Australia, and can participate in semester abroad programs at the University of California, Berkeley.
- Hostel facilities are available on-campus for both boys and girls, with limited accommodation on a first-come, first-served basis.

**Career Prospects:**
Graduates can pursue various roles including:
- Software Developer
- Business Analyst
- Data Scientist
- Cyber Security Analyst
- Digital Marketer
- Educator

The BCA curriculum is designed to equip students with the skills necessary for entry-level positions or further studies in Master of Computer Applications (MCA) or Master of Business Administration (MBA).
