Dr. Sushil Kumar Rai is a distinguished faculty member at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), specializing in macroeconomics and applied econometrics. He holds a Doctor of Economics degree focused on Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs), which equips him with a profound understanding of the intricate dynamics surrounding MSMEs, contemporary macroeconomic challenges, and environmental economics.

**Research Interests:**
Dr. Rai's primary research interests include energy and environmental economics, highlighting his commitment to addressing critical issues within these fields.

**Work Experience:**
Before his tenure at UPES, Dr. Rai was an Assistant Professor and Head of the Department of Economics at Mody University of Science & Technology in Sikar, Rajasthan. His extensive teaching experience enables him to adopt a holistic approach to education, emphasizing the fundamentals of economics within the broader socio-economic context.

**Teaching Approach:**
Dr. Rai teaches a range of courses, including managerial economics, econometrics, macroeconomics, and international trade, at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. He is known for creating an engaging learning environment that promotes intellectual growth through interactive methods such as lectures, class participation, brainstorming sessions, problem-solving activities, and case studies.

**Academic Contributions:**
Dr. Rai has made notable contributions to the academic community through his research publications, focusing on MSMEs, macroeconomic issues, and energy and environmental economics. His work has been featured in reputable peer-reviewed journals, underscoring his expertise in these areas. He is also actively involved in the peer-review process for several esteemed journals, including:
- Environmental Science and Pollution Research (Springer)
- International Journal of Sustainable Economy (Inderscience)
- South Asia Economic Journal (SAGE)
- International Journal of Entrepreneurship and Small Business (Inderscience)

**Awards and Grants:**
During his PhD studies, Dr. Rai was awarded a research fellowship from Banaras Hindu University (BHU), reflecting his dedication to academic excellence.
