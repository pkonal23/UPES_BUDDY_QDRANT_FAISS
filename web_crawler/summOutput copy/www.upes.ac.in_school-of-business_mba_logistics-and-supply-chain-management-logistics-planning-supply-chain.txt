The MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Logistics Planning/Supply Chain) program offered by the School of Business at UPES is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge for mid and senior management roles across various industries, both domestically and internationally. The program emphasizes practical, hands-on learning through summer internships, live projects, and a major dissertation, allowing students to gain real-world experience and insights into logistics planning and supply chain management.

### Key Features of the Program:
- **Curriculum**: The curriculum is tailored for specialization in logistics and supply chain management, covering essential topics such as demand planning, forecasting, procurement, inventory management, transportation, warehousing, and distribution. It integrates real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from experts, providing a comprehensive understanding of the field.
- **Industry Engagement**: Students are encouraged to engage with the industry through live projects, research initiatives, seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering a dynamic learning environment connected to current industry trends and challenges.
- **Global Perspective**: Opportunities for exchange programs, study abroad initiatives, and semester exchanges with leading global partner institutes enhance students' global perspectives and networking opportunities.
- **State-of-the-Art Facilities**: The UPES management school is equipped with modern smart classrooms, an analytics lab, a well-stocked library, and advanced analytical tools, ensuring a conducive learning environment.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates of the program can pursue diverse roles such as:
- Demand Planner
- Supply Planner
- Sourcing Specialist
- Transport Planner
- Purchasing Specialist
- Production Planner
- Positions in e-commerce, warehouse management, procurement, freight transportation, and inventory management.

The program has seen a commendable surge in placement opportunities, with leading companies like Flipkart, Reliance Industries, HUL, GATI KWE, Adani Wilmar, Accenture, Deloitte, Kuehne Nagel, Xiaomi, TATA Croma, Odex, Transystem, and PI Industries actively recruiting graduates. Starting salaries for successful candidates typically range from INR 6-30 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills, knowledge, and industry exposure.

### Admission Criteria:
- **Eligibility**: Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream. 
- **Entrance Exams**: Candidates with a minimum eligible MAT/CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempt from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Other accepted scores include a valid 50 percentile and above for CAT 2024/XAT 2025, a score of minimum 400 or above for GMAT, and a score of 120 and above in NMAT 2024/25.
- **Application Deadline**: The last date to apply is February 25, 2025.
