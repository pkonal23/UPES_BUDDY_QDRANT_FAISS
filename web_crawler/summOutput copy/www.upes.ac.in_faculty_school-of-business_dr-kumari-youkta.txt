Dr. Kumari Youkta is an Assistant Professor at the School of Business, specializing in health economics, healthcare systems, health inequalities, and development economics. She holds a PhD in Economics from the Indian Institute of Technology Patna and has extensive teaching experience at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. 

Dr. Youkta is dedicated to creating a dynamic and inclusive learning environment that effectively bridges academic theory with practical application. Her teaching philosophy emphasizes fostering critical thinking, analytical reasoning, and problem-solving skills in her students, preparing them for real-world challenges. She employs interactive discussions, case studies, and experiential learning in her courses, which include Microeconomics and International Economics, to ensure a comprehensive understanding of economic principles.

In addition to her teaching responsibilities, Dr. Youkta actively engages in scholarly activities, having presented her research at prestigious platforms such as the Population Association of America, the Australian Health Economics Society Conference, and the Indira Gandhi Institute of Development Research. She has authored numerous publications in reputable journals indexed in SCOPUS, Web of Science, and the Australian Business Deans Council (ABDC).

For further inquiries or to connect with Dr. Youkta, she can be reached via email at kumari.youkta@ddn.upes.ac.in.
