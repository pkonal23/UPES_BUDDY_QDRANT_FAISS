The VII Surana & Surana and UPES School of Law National Insolvency Law Moot Court Competition 2024 is scheduled to take place from September 5 to September 7, 2024, at the UPES Kandoli Campus. This event aims to enhance legal education by providing a platform for law students to apply their knowledge practically, focusing specifically on advancements in Insolvency Law.

The competition will feature multiple phases, beginning with virtual rounds on August 9-10, 2024, followed by offline rounds at the School of Law. It will include registration and memorial exchanges, preliminary rounds, quarter-finals, semi-finals, and culminate in a final round judged by a panel of esteemed legal professionals and judges from top law firms and legal institutions.

The guest of honour for the event includes Adv. Anand Chhibber, Advocate at the High Court of Punjab and Haryana, and notable chief guests such as Mr. Ankit Jain, Partner Designate at Luthra and Luthra Law Offices, and Hon'ble Mr. Justice Aniruddha P. Mayee from the High Court of Gujarat. The competition encourages participation from students across various universities in Uttarakhand, specifically targeting students from the UPES School of Law.

The primary objective of the competition is to bridge the gap between theoretical law education and real-world legal practice, allowing students to engage in rigorous research, writing, and advocacy within a competitive environment. It serves as a valuable learning experience, helping participants develop their legal skills and understanding of insolvency law.

For more information, participants can refer to the UPES School of Law's official communication channels.
