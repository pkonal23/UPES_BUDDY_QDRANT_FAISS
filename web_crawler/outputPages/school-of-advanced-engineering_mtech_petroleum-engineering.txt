The curriculum of the program covers a wide range of subjects related to petroleum engineering. Students will delve into areas such as petroleum geology, drilling, formation evaluation, production, reservoir engineering, reservoir geomechanics, reservoir modeling and simulation, economics and risk management, enhanced oil recovery, HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment) issues in the oil and gas industry, and programming in petroleum engineering. This comprehensive approach ensures that graduates are well-rounded professionals capable of tackling various aspects of the petroleum industry.
