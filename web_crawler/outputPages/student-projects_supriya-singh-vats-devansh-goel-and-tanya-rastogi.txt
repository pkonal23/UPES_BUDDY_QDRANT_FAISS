Afrikiko is an innovative project to bring the vibrant essence of African culture to life through a unique cafe experience. Our design incorporates traditional African motifs, textiles, and art to create a warm and inviting atmosphere. The cafe also features a menu inspired by various African cuisines, offering a taste of the continent's diverse culinary heritage. The project emphasizes sustainability and using local materials and promote African cuisine.
