Profile Summary
School of Advanced Engineering
Assistant Professor
Dr. Susmay Nandi earned his Ph.D. from the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Meghalaya, where his research focused on the analysis of Magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) flows with heat and mass transfer, particularly studying MHD fluid flow past different geometries with various physical aspects. He has done M.Sc. in Mathematics & Computing from the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati and a B.Sc. (Honours) in Mathematics from the University of North Bengal. He also pursued a B.Ed. degree from the University of North Bengal.
As an Assistant Professor, Dr. Nandi imparts his expertise in subjects like Differential Equations, Engineering Mathematics II, Mathematics III, and Discrete Mathematics to undergraduate students. His research interests encompass Boundary Layer Theory, Magnetohydrodynamics (MHD), Nanofluids, Hybrid Nanofluids, Heat and Mass Transfer, and Flow through different geometries.
Dr. Nandi's teaching philosophy centres around creating an engaging and inclusive learning environment that fosters critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a passion for lifelong learning. He adopts a student-centric approach that acknowledges the diverse backgrounds and learning styles of his students. By incorporating real-world examples and practical applications, he strives to promote active learning through interactive discussions, hands-on activities, and projects.
