Work Experience
Dr. Sahinur Rahman Laskar brings 3.9 years of experience. Prior to joining UPES, he worked with Assam University, Silchar (CSE, Department) as a Guest Faculty position.
His research interests include natural language processing, machine translation, question answering, information retrieval, and machine learning. His PhD research lies in Machine Translation, where Dr. Laskar has demonstrated a strong commitment to advancing knowledge through original and impactful research. Throughout his academic journey, Dr. Laskar has published his work in reputable journals and actively participated in conferences, wherein he has presented and shared his findings with the wider academic community. Dr. Laskar has been working on low-resource neural machine translation for Indian northeastern languages (Assamese, Mizo, Khasi, Nyishi) and has achieved benchmark results in numerous machine translation competitions like WMT, WAT, and LoResMT in the past several years.

School of Computer Science
Dr. Sahinur Rahman Laskar was awarded a Ph.D. in 2023 by NIT Silchar, preceded by M.Tech. (IT) in the year 2015. He received his B.E. (CSE) from Assam Engineering College, Guwahati, India in 2011. His research work mainly revolves around improving the quality of low-resource language translation, along with exploring its multi-modal counterparts. Dr. Laskar has at least 39 published papers in reputed conferences/workshops (27), journals (8), book chapters (2), and patents (2).
