The partnership between UPES and Bosch in India seeks to promote knowledge transfer, research and development in the field of Automation Technologies. The tie-up will improve the technical competence, employability, and entrepreneurship skill of undergraduate and postgraduate students for programs such as Applied Petroleum Engineering, Geo-Science Engineering, Geo-Informatics Engineering, Mechatronics and Electronics.
