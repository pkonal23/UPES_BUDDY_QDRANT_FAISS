As technology advances and the healthcare sector evolves, biotechnologists will play a pivotal role in developing innovative solutions in genetic engineering, pharmaceuticals, and bioinformatics. Microbiologists will continue to play a crucial role in various industries, particularly in pharmaceuticals, healthcare, and environmental sciences. The emphasis on infectious diseases, biotechnology, and environmental sustainability will drive demand for microbiologists in research, diagnostics, and product development. The growing awareness of healthy living and preventive healthcare will drive demand for nutritionists and dietitians. Opportunities in hospitals, fitness centers, food industries, and research institutions will expand as individuals prioritise their health and well-being.
