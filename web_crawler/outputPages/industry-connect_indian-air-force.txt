Under this partnership, UPES will reserve seats and offer scholarships of up to 30% for the wards and spouses of Indian Air Force personnel enrolling in full-time undergraduate programs. Eligible programs include BBA, B.Tech., LL.B., B.Sc. (Hons), BA, and BA (Hons). Scholarships will be awarded based on academic performance in class 12 exams, CUET, or JEE scores.
