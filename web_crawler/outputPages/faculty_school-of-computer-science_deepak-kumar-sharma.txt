Assistant Professor (SS)
Deepak Kumar Sharma
Prior to joining UPES, Deepak served as an Assistant Professor at Ganpat University in Gujarat, India from 2013 to 2015. Over the course of the last 10 years, he has held various administrative positions, including Course Coordinator, Internship Coordinator, and roles within Examination Teams and Project Committees. In addition to his administrative responsibilities, Deepak has demonstrated his organizational skills by coordinating academic events such as UHackathon (1.0, 2.0, 3.0, 4.0) and international conferences like ICIIP, ICPDGC, NGCT and ICMLDE. He has also facilitated several workshops and Faculty Development Programs (FDPs) for various teachers and students during his academic career. He has trained various students in Python programming under the Infosys Foundation Program.
Deepak's teaching philosophy centres on creating a student-centred environment that promotes active learning and intellectual curiosity. He strives to empower students by providing the tools and guidance they need to succeed. Utilizing innovative teaching methods and practical applications, Deepak fosters critical thinking skills and cultivates a supportive and inclusive classroom. Through collaboration, respect, and open dialogue, he aims to instil a lifelong love for learning and prepare students for future success.
