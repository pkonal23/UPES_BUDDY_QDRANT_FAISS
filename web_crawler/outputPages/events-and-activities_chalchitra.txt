Launched in 2022, the Chalchitra Film Festival has emerged as a leading platform for smartphone filmmaking in India and globally. Chalchitra is South Asia's only competitive mobile film festival, organized annually by the School of Liberal Studies and Media at UPES, Dehradun. The festival champions the democratization of creative expression, empowering filmmakers to craft compelling fiction and nonfiction narratives using smartphones. It provides a dynamic space for emerging and established storytellers to push creative boundaries. Through workshops, screenings, and discussions, Chalchitra fosters a vibrant community of digital-first filmmakers.
