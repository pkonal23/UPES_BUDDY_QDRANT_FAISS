UPES School of Health Sciences and Technology and Oil India Limited (OIL) – a govt. of India enterprise – have collaborated to conduct joint research in petroleum exploration and bio-surfactant producing microbes-based enhanced oil recovery. As part of the agreement, OIL experts will also provide guest lectures to SoHST students.
