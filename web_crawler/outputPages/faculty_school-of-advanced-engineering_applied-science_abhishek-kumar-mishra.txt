His expertise, publication record, and funded projects demonstrate his commitment to advancing scientific understanding and finding solutions to real-world problems. Dr. Mishra's teaching philosophy emphasizes education as a transformative process that goes beyond mere knowledge transfer. He believes that practical applications and real-world examples bridge theory to tangible outcomes in materials science. Along with encouraging interdisciplinary collaboration and exploring intersections with physics and related fields, he aims to equip students with the ability to apply their knowledge to contemporary challenges. This fosters critical thinking, adaptability, and meaningful contributions to the scientific community.
Dr. Abhishek Kumar Mishra is a distinguished academician honoured with an award from the American Chemical Society (ACS) for his remarkable contributions, recognizing him as one of the most cited Indian authors in the year 2019. Dr. Mishra's research primarily focuses on utilising DFT-based calculations to explore various applications, such as gas sensing, catalysts for CO2 conversion, and 2D nanomaterials for electronics. He has authored over 60 papers in renowned journals, including Advanced Functional Materials and Journal of Material Chemistry C.
Profile Summary
School of Advanced Engineering
Dr. Abhishek Mishra has made significant contributions to the field of materials science and computational physics. His scholarly activities and achievements highlight his expertise and recognition in the scientific community. He has secured notable research grants, including the DST SERB-SURE Grant on Engineering Materials for Integrated CO2 capture and conversion, and the Indo-Belarus DST-SERB grant. Dr. Mishra's research interests extend to various areas, such as engineering 2D materials for carbon capture and conversion, designing vdW-heterostructures for nanoelectronics applications, and studying 2D carbon-based heterostructures. These projects have been supported by prestigious grants like the CV Raman Indo-African Research Grants. Additionally, Dr. Mishra received a startup grant from UPES, Dehradun, for his work on novel catalysts for CO2 capture and conversion. His research efforts have been further recognized through the DST Fast Track Grant (2010), which supported his study on the conformation and dynamics of conducting polymers. Along with being an affiliate member of the Royal Society of Chemistry, he is also the guest editor of renowned journals like Molecules and Symmetry which further exemplifies his standing in the field.

Dr. Abhishek Kumar Mishra
Before joining UPES, Dr. Mishra served as a senior postdoc research associate at University College London from 2013 to 2016 and was a DST Young Scientist fellow at Jawaharlal Nehru Centre for Advanced Scientific Research (JNCASR), Bangalore from 2010 to 2013. He also worked as a postdoc fellow at Texas A&M University and Banaras Hindu University from 2009 to 2010. In addition to his role at UPES, Dr. Mishra is also serving as a research visitor at the Southern University of Denmark. In the past, he has taught in esteemed institutes like Amity University and the University of Lucknow.
Dr. Abhishek Mishra has received several prestigious awards and recognitions throughout his career: SERB Sure research grant for three years (PI), Year 2023. Indo-Belarus research grant for three years (Co-PI), Year 2023. Top Cited Indian Author, American Chemical Society (ACS), Year 2020. Young Scientist Award, Department of Science and Technology (DST), New Delhi, Year 2009. EPSRC, UK Postdoctoral Fellowship, Year 2013. Robert A. Welch Foundation Postdoc Fellowship, USA, Year 2010. Gold Medalist, University of Lucknow, Lucknow, Year 2003: This accolade signifies Dr. Mishra's exceptional academic performance during his undergraduate studies, as he achieved the highest marks among his peers in the BSc examinations.
