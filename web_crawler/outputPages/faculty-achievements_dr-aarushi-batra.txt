Faculty Achievements
Dr. Aarushi Batra received the 'Best Presenter' award for her paper ""Integrating Gender Perspective in Judicial Decisions in the Light of Low Representation of Women in Judiciary"" at the National Conference on Working of the Indian Judicial System. The conference was organized by the Indian Law Institute, Delhi, on 22nd-23rd April 2023.
Dr. Aarushi represented UPES at the National Conference on Transforming India: Risk to Resilience at JNU Campus on 16th March 2023 in Delhi. During the conference, she chaired the technical session on ""Disaster Policy and Governance.
