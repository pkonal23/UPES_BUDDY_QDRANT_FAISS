The Ph.D. program in Communication at UPES School of Liberal Studies is designed to cultivate scholars with a comprehensive understanding of media and communication studies. Throughout the program, scholars will engage deeply with relevant literature, theories, and concepts, fostering a solid theoretical foundation. Emphasizing practical application, they will leverage acquired knowledge from coursework, academic engagement, and thesis writing to make meaningful contributions to the evolving academic landscape of media studies. The program equips scholars with the ability to translate their learnings into real-world scenarios, honing skills in presenting theoretically sound arguments and actively participating in academic debates, seminars, and conferences. Additionally, scholars will develop the capacity to deliver well-prepared lectures, conduct seminars and workshops, and produce research-driven, peer-reviewed articles, thereby enhancing the research output of both the school and the university. Rigorous literature review activities will enable scholars to articulate key arguments within existing, new, and developing literature in the dynamic field of media and communication studies.
