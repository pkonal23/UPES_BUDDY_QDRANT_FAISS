This project features a smart drone that farmers can use to observe the farmlands and give back information recorded through cameras.
Eligibility Criteria
The drone pilot operates the drone, relaying information in real-time. This provides the ability to analyse lands from high above with more details.
The drone has cameras to observe the ground and take pictures.
