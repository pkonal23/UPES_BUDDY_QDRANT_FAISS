Before joining UPES, Goutam had worked in various universities and industries and has more than 20 years of teaching and industry experience.
Goutam Datta is pursuing his Ph.D. from Banasthali Vidyapith, Banasthali, Rajasthan. He completed his M.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering, B.E. in Computer Science and Engineering, and Diploma in Industrial Electronics from M.D. University, Rohtak, Amravati University, Maharashtra, and Board of Technical Examination, Mumbai, Maharashtra, respectively. His areas of interest lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Machine learning, in specific areas relating to language translation and Deep learning. He has undergone various short-term training programs on Image and Video Processing, Data Analytics, and Natural Language Processing at IITs, IIITs, and IIMs. He has also delivered hands-on training on various tools at different universities. He has more than 20 years of teaching and industry experience. He authored around 20 research papers for international conferences, international journals, etc. He has also filed and published many patents. He has reviewed several conference papers and journal articles. Goutam has professional memberships with ISTE and EAI. Goutam joined UPES in October 2018.
