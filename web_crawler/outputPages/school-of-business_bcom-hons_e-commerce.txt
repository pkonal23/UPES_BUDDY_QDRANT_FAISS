program offered by UPES School of Business provides students with an excellent foundation for a successful career in the rapidly growing field of e-commerce. The program focuses on equipping students with essential skills in online marketing, digital retailing, supply chain management, and data analytics, among others, to thrive in the digital marketplace. UPES School of Business ensures that students receive comprehensive industry exposure through internships, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts, enabling them to understand the practical aspects of e-commerce. With its strong industry connections and a curriculum tailored to meet the demands of the digital economy, the program prepares students for a wide range of job opportunities in e-commerce companies, digital marketing agencies, retail organizations, and start-ups. Graduates of the program are well-equipped to contribute effectively to the dynamic and evolving world of e-commerce.
