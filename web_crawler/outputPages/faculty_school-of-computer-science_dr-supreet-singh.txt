Faculty
Dr. Supreet Singh has over 8 years of experience in academia, with a focus on teaching and research in computer science. He has served as an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science Engineering at SGT University, where he taught a variety of graduate and postgraduate courses and supervised numerous projects by students. He has also served as a Research Associate at IIIT Delhi, working on government-aided projects. He has served as an assistant professor at Chandigarh University, where he was the academic coordinator and was also in charge of admissions.
