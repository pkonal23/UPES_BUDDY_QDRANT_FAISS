Shahina Anwarul is a dedicated, research-oriented and reliable professional with M.Tech degree in Information Security from NIT Allahabad in 2016 and B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow in 2013. In addition to this, Shahina has qualified NET & JRF in 2022. She has completed an esteemed women empowerment program ‘Shakti’ conducted by UPES. She is a Quick Heal Certified Cyber Security Professional (QCSP). She is also a certified Ethical Hacker from EC Council. Presently, she is working as an Assistant Professor-Selection Grade in Systemics Cluster, School of Computer Science, UPES, Dehradun. She is currently pursuing her PhD Degree from UPES, Dehradun. She has more than 7 years of experience in academics and research. Her research interest lies in Deep Learning, Image Processing, Computer Vision and Information Security.
