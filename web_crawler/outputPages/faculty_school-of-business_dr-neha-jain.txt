Assistant Professor - Senior Scale
Prof. Jain received the fellowship for doctoral program from Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani, Pilani Campus. She has also received the ‘Best paper’ award in the conferences.
Prof. Jain’s scholarly activities are marked by her active engagement in advancing economic research and education. She has published extensively in peer-reviewed journals on topics like international trade, sustainability, and environmental quality. Her research is frequently presented at national and international conferences, reflecting her commitment to contributing to global economic discussions. Her involvement in organizing and participating in workshops and seminars highlights her dedication to fostering academic dialogue. Dr Jain’s commitment to professional development and public engagement enriches her teaching and supports the broader academic community. Through these activities, she continuously strives to enhance understanding and drive progress in her field.
Profile Summary
Prof. Jain’s teaching philosophy is rooted in the belief that education should be both engaging and transformative. She views teaching as a collaborative process that fosters critical thinking, encourages active participation, and connects theoretical concepts with real-world applications. Her approach integrates diverse teaching methods, including case studies, interactive discussions, and hands-on projects, to cater to various learning styles and promote deep understanding. Jain is committed to helping students develop not only their academic knowledge but also their analytical and problem-solving skills, preparing them to navigate the complexities of the global economy.
School of Business
Before joining UPES, Dr. Jain was at the Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani, Pilani campus, held a teaching assistant position at the Department of Economics and Finance while pursuing PhD. She also worked as a guest faculty in the Department of Management, Banasthali Vidyapith.
Contact
Dr. Neha Jain is an Assistant Professor of Economics with extensive expertise in international economics and sustainable development. Her research encompasses a broad spectrum of topics, including international trade, trade openness, sustainability, and environmental quality. In her teaching role, she offers courses on business environment, global context for business, and various aspects of economics, reflecting her diverse academic interests. Dr. Jain is dedicated to exploring the intersections between economic policies and global challenges, striving to enhance understanding and contribute solutions in her fields of specialization.
