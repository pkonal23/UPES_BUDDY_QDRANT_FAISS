Profile Summary
Deepika Kamboj has over nine years of experience in academia, currently serving as an Assistant Professor-Senior Scale at UPES, Dehradun, since September 2024. Previously, she held the position of Assistant Professor at the KIET Group of Institutions from September 2022 to September 2024, where she specialized in Machine Learning and Data Analytics. Prior to that, she worked under the TEQIP III Project (A Project of World Bank in association with Ministry of Education, India)  at MBM Engineering College, Jodhpur, from 2018 to 2022, focusing on enhancing technical education. Deepika also taught at KIET Group of Institutions from January 2016 to December 2017 and served as an Assistant Professor at the Noida Institute of Engineering & Technology for six months in 2015, where she emphasized Data Mining and related subjects. Her diverse teaching portfolio highlights her commitment to integrating research and practical applications in computer science education.
Deepika Kamboj believes in fostering an inclusive and engaging learning environment where students are encouraged to think critically and collaborate actively. She strives to create a classroom where every student feels valued and inspired to contribute, ensuring they leave not only with knowledge but with the confidence to apply it creatively.
