He had 36 years of extremely successful professional career at Dept. of Space, ISRO and developed expertise in the fields of Space Technology & Applications, Digital Image Processing, Application Software Design & Development and Geospatial Technology and Solutions.
Dr. P. G. Diwakar is currently serving as ISRO Chair Professor at National Institute of Advanced Studies (NIAS), IISc Campus, Bangalore, India. He has previously served as Distinguished Scientist at ISRO.
