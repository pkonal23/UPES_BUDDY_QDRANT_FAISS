MA Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital)
What you can expect to earn after completing a master's degree program in digital or broadcast journalism and mass communication depends on a number of factors, including your specific job title, industry, geographic area, and level of expertise. Depending on employment type and expertise, journalism and mass communication (digital) salaries range from INR 3-15 lakhs per year. Digital and mass media (broadcasting) salaries can range from INR 3-20 lakhs per year. These values are averages and might vary greatly depending on qualifications, work function, and other factors.
Career Opportunities
Completing an MA Program in Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital) or Digital and Mass Media (Broadcasting) opens up a wide range of career opportunities in the field of media and communication.
Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital) graduates have careers as digital content creators, social media managers, digital marketers, online journalists, multimedia editors, and digital media analysts at media organisations, digital marketing agencies, and online publications. Digital and Mass Media (Broadcasting) graduates may find employment in radio and television broadcasting, production, post-production, content creation, and editing. They can be radio or television producers, news reporters, video editors, production managers, or marketers and advertisers.
