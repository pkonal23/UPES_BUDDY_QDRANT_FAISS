The Travel & Ground Handling Operations specialization delves into the intricacies of managing travel agency operations and coordinating ground handling services. Students acquire practical skills to ensure a smooth experience for passengers, emphasizing customer service excellence. The program explores the dynamics of airline partnerships and alliances, shedding light on codeshare agreements, interline partnerships, and their impact on travel agencies and ground-handling services. Ethical considerations, privacy protection, and compliance obligations are also covered, instilling in students a commitment to ethical behavior and customer trust. Overall, the BBA in Aviation Management with a focus on Travel & Ground Handling Operations equips graduates with the knowledge and skills needed for successful careers in this rapidly expanding industry.
