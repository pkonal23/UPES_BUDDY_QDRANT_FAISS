The BBA Global program equips students with a worldwide perspective and international exposure, enabling them to explore global prospects while contributing to their home country. A BBA Global or a similar global-focused specialisation lays a strong groundwork for diverse opportunities in the global business arena. Potential career paths include roles such as International Business Analyst, Global Supply Chain Coordinator, International Marketing Assistant, Cross-Cultural Consultant, Global Human Resources Assistant, International Trade Assistant, Global Operations Coordinator, Business Development Associate, International Finance Assistant, and Entrepreneur.
