Dr. Anjali Sonkariya is an accomplished academician and researcher specializing in mathematics, optimization, and data science. She has a deep passion for leveraging mathematical principles to solve real-world business challenges. Dr. Sonkariya’s expertise lies in data envelopment analysis, Operations research and mathematical modelling. She has published research papers in reputed peer-reviewed journals and presented her work at international conferences. Additionally, her professional experience as a data scientist at Siemens Energy enabled her to deliver impactful data-driven insights, further enriching her academic endeavours. Currently, she teaches Business Mathematics, Research Methodology & Report Writing and Business Analytics integrating practical applications with conceptual learning to inspire students.

Before joining UPES, Dr. Anjali Sonkariya was at Galgotias University, where she worked as an Assistant Professor, contributing to undergraduate education and academic development. During her Ph.D. journey, she also served as a Teaching Assistant for UG students at IIT Roorkee. She worked as a data scientist at Siemens Energy, where she contributed to key projects such as anomaly detection in salary data, inflation rate analysis, and total remuneration dashboards using Python and Tableau. Her blend of academic and professional expertise ensures her students benefit from a holistic understanding of theoretical and applied concepts.
