Dr. Monika Yadav has been recognized as an IEEE Senior Member in 2022. She has also received distinctions in her post-graduation and graduation degrees.
Dr. Monika Yadav completed her Ph.D. on "Optimal allocation of DG for resilient electrical distribution grid" and M.Tech. in Electrical Engineering with a specialization in Power System Engineering in 2015 from IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, Jharkhand. Her research work has resulted in the publication of numerous articles in SCI/Scopus-indexed journals and conferences. She possesses expertise in Distribution Grid Expansion Planning, optimization and control problems, and energy alternatives.

Dr. Monika Yadav's research interests encompass various areas, including Distribution Grid Expansion Planning, Resiliency enhancement of distribution grid against disturbances, Sustainable planning of distribution systems, Grid code compliance for renewable energy (RE) integration, and Microgrid Reliability.
Throughout her academic career, Dr. Yadav has taught various core electrical subjects and multidisciplinary subjects like Power Systems, Switchgear and Protection, Electrical Machines, Microprocessors and Microcontrollers, Embedded Systems, and Electromagnetic Field Theory, among others. Apart from teaching and research, she provides mentorship to B.Tech and M.Tech students as needed and conducts GATE classes for students.

Dr. Yadav firmly believes in prioritizing societal impact over mere technical and economic aspects in education. She strives to design and deliver technical curricula that align with current social needs.
