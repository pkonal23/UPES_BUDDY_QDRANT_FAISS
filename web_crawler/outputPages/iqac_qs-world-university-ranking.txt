Outcome
The QS world university rankings is an annual publication of university rankings that includes our independent regional tables in addition to the global overall and subject rankings, which identify the top universities worldwide for the study of 51 different subjects (such as Asia, Latin America, emerging Europe and central Asia and the Arab region). The most popular university rankings in the world are those published by QS The QS rankings currently lead the higher education industry with consistent innovation and growth, progressively affecting the way students and institutions engage. Now in its 17th year, the QS rankings continue to produce authoritative, independent global rankings and evaluation of institutions worldwide.
