The field of Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) is experiencing significant growth due to advancements in technology, globalization, and increased awareness of intellectual property protection. The digital platforms, e-commerce, and knowledge-based economy have fueled the demand for IPR experts. Cases related to copyright infringement, patent disputes, trademark violations, and trade secret misappropriation are on the rise. Additionally, emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, and biotechnology bring new complexities to intellectual property. Consequently, there is an increasing demand for skilled lawyers who can navigate these evolving landscapes, provide legal advice, protect intellectual property rights, and represent clients in both domestic and international contexts.
