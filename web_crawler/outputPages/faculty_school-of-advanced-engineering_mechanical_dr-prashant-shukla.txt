Dr. Prashant Shukla
Dr. Prashant Shukla has gained diverse work experience in various roles and organizations throughout his career. He began his career as a Design Engineer at HEW of Jaypee Associates, where he contributed to industrial design projects. He worked as an Assistant Professor at Uttaranchal University, Dehradun, and later at UPES, Dehradun, where he shared his expertise with students in the field of machine design and related subjects. Additionally, he served as an Online Instructor of Design Thinking at the New School of Design and Architecture in California, expanding his reach and impact in the field of design education.
