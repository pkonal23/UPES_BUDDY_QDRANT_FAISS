The MBA in Oil & Gas Management (Petroleum Marketing & Business Development) program at UPES School of Business is a flagship course offered by the Energy Department. This specialised program is designed to provide students with a unique blend of general management education along with a focused understanding of the oil and gas industry. The curriculum covers a range of domain-specific subjects, including Understanding of Natural Gas Business, Fundamentals of Oil & Gas Business, Refinery Management, Business Analysis, Financial Management, Marketing Research, and more. Taught by experienced instructors from around the globe, students gain comprehensive knowledge and skills crucial for success in the oil and gas sector. UPES distinguishes itself not only through academic excellence but also by integrating curricular activities. Students have the opportunity to become members of the student chapter affiliated with the Federation of Indian Petroleum Industry, allowing them to actively engage in industry-related initiatives. This practical exposure enables students to apply their managerial skills, fostering organizational growth and preparing them for the competitive global market.

The MBA in Oil & Gas Management (Petroleum Marketing & Business Development) program equips students for mid and senior management positions in consulting, financing, business development, analysis, research, and other areas within the global Oil & Gas industries. The industry faces a significant shortage of skilled professionals capable of effectively managing the complexities of the Oil & Gas business. Emphasizing hands-on learning, the program provides practical experience through internships and on-the-job training, enabling students to apply classroom knowledge to real-world scenarios and gain a competitive edge in the job market. The curriculum ensures a comprehensive understanding of the industry, covering practices, challenges, and emerging trends in Upstream, Midstream, and Downstream sectors. Additionally, students gain exposure to various software and technologies, including Advanced Excel, Power-BI, SPSS, TABLU, SAP, and Python, enhancing their employability and market relevance. This program not only addresses the industry's demand for skilled professionals but also positions students for success in the dynamic field of Oil & Gas management.
