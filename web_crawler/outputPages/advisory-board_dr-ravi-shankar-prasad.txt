Dr. Ravi Shankar Singh, Ph.D, is the Senior Director of Clinical Pharmacology at Pfizer Research and Development. Currently, he leads development of antivirals.  Before joining Pfizer, he worked at Abbvie (Chicago, IL, USA), University of Florida (Gainesville, FL, USA), Central Drug Research Institute (Lucknow, UP, India) and Dr. Reddy’s lab (Hyderabad, AP, India).
