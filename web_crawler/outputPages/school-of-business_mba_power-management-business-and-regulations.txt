The future scope of the MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) program is promising and expansive. Geared towards preparing students for mid and senior management roles in power consulting, financing, business development, and research within India and globally, the program addresses a significant shortage of skilled power professionals in the industry. Emphasizing hands-on learning, the curriculum includes internships and on-the-job training, providing practical experience to apply classroom knowledge to real-world scenarios. Covering industry practices, challenges, and emerging trends such as Hydrogen, Energy storage, electric vehicles, carbon trading, and digitalization, the program equips students with a deep understanding of the sector. Exposure to software and technology like Python, R-Studio, TEMOA, and PyPSA enhances their employability. With India's ambitious targets in renewable energy and green hydrogen production, coupled with global growth in the green energy segment and digital innovations in the power sector, the demand for Power Management graduates, especially Energy Analysts, is on the rise, promising a bright and prospective future for them.
