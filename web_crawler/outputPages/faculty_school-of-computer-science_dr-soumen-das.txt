Soumen Das is an Assistant Professor and skilled researcher specializing in Computer Vision, Image Processing, and Gesture recognition. He has a Ph.D. from the National Institute of Technology Silchar. His  work focuses on improving human computer interaction and development of  sign language recognition system for deaf community. He has published several papers in reputed conferences and journals. Dr. Das is passionate about collaborating with others and advancing computer science in these exciting areas.
