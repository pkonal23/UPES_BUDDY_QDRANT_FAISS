The program offers two specializations: Logistics Planning/Supply Chain and Business Process in Supply Chain. The Logistics Planning/Supply Chain specialization focuses on managing products from creation to distribution, refining processes to optimize systems, equipment, and storage facilities. This specialization opens up career opportunities in mid or senior management roles in logistics, e-commerce, manufacturing, warehousing, aviation, shipping, and related industries, both in India and globally. On the other hand, the Business Process in Supply Chain specialization covers various aspects such as strategic planning, demand planning, supply planning, procurement, manufacturing, warehousing, order fulfillment, and transportation business processes.
