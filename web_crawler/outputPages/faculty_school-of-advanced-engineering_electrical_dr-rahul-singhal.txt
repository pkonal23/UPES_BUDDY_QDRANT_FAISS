School of Advanced Engineering
Assistant Professor
My philosophy centers on cultivating a dynamic learning environment where students actively engage with ideas, challenge preconceptions, and develop their own informed perspectives. I believe in fostering critical thinking skills, encouraging open dialogue, and promoting a collaborative community of learners.
Dr. Rahul Singhal has been honored with the esteemed Visvesvaraya PhD Scheme and has received travel grants from IEEE Indian Control Conference and IIT Delhi to attend conferences in India.
Dr. Rahul Singhal completed his B.Tech in Electrical Engineering from GB Pant University. He did his M.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IIIT Delhi. He has awarded his Ph.D in Electrical Engineering at MNIT Jaipur. He was awarded full-time Ph.D fellowship under Visvesvaraya PhD Scheme for Electronics and IT. He has attended 5 faculty development programme on artificial intelligence, optimization techniques, robotics and control theory.
