The BBA (Analytics and Big Data) program offered by UPES, Dehradun is a comprehensive three-year course that focuses on providing students with the necessary skills and knowledge to work with data and perform analytics tasks using programming languages. The program emphasizes practical skills that can be directly applied to real-world data problems. Students learn programming languages commonly used in data analytics, such as Python and R, which are known for their extensive libraries and frameworks supporting data manipulation, analysis, and visualization.
Various job roles, including BBA Data Analyst, Business analyst, Business Intelligence Analyst, Data Scientist, Data Consultant, Market Research Analyst, E-commerce Analyst, Risk Analyst, and Supply Chain Analyst, as well as Data Governance Specialist, are available for these graduates. Freshers in this field typically receive starting salaries ranging from INR 6-30 lakh per annum, depending on their skills, knowledge, and exposure.
