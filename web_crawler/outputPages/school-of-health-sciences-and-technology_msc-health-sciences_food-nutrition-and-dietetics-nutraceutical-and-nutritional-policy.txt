The nutraceutical industry offers products combining nutritional and pharmaceutical properties, including dietary supplements and functional foods. There is a growing demand for natural and functional ingredients, as well as a trend towards personalized nutrition using technology and data analysis. Consumers are also increasingly interested in sustainable and ethically sourced products. Collaboration between industry and research institutions is driving innovation, while digital health technologies are being utilized for personalized nutritional advice. Governments and industry players are focusing on fortification and enrichment to address micronutrient deficiencies and improve public health.
