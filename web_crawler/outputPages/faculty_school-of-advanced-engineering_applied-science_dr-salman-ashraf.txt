Scholarly Activities
Dr. Salman Ashraf earned his Ph.D. from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Mandi, where his thesis explored the boundedness of linear operators on function spaces over local fields. He holds M.Sc. in Mathematics from Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras and B.Sc. (Honours) in Mathematics from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi. His research interests include Harmonic Analysis, Operator Theory, and Function Spaces.
