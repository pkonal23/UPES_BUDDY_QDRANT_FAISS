The BBA in Oil & Gas Management (Oil & Gas Operations) program at UPES School of Business is a flagship course offered by the Energy Department, designed to produce dedicated managers with a comprehensive understanding of the oil and gas industry. This program integrates general management education with specialised knowledge in oil and gas management, ensuring that students are well-equipped to contribute effectively to the industry. The curriculum covers a range of domain-specific subjects, including Natural Gas Business, Oil & Gas Business Fundamentals, Refining, Petroleum Exploration, Petrochemical Business, Refinery Management, Business Analysis, Financial Management, and Marketing Research. Renowned instructors from around the world deliver these courses, providing students with a global perspective.

UPES distinguishes itself by not only focusing on academics but also connecting it with practical experiences. Students have the opportunity to join the student chapter of the Federation of Indian Petroleum Industry, allowing them to engage with the industry under the guidance of faculty advisors. This hands-on approach enables students to apply their managerial skills, fostering organizational growth and preparing them for the competitive global market. The program acknowledges the changing landscape of the oil and gas industry, emphasizing the transition towards a "Gas Based Economy" in India. This shift opens up job opportunities in areas such as City Gas Distribution, CNG as Automotive Fuel, and Pipeline Integration and Management System.
•    The BBA in Oil & Gas Management (Oil & Gas Operations) program emphasizes hands-on learning with a major project (dissertation), summer internships, and live projects with industry partners, ensuring students gain real-world experience in Oil & Gas operations.
The BBA in Oil & Gas Management (Oil & Gas Operations) program equips students for mid and senior management roles in various facets of the Oil & Gas industry, both nationally and internationally. With a substantial shortage of skilled professionals in the field, the program addresses this gap by emphasizing hands-on learning, offering practical experience through internships and on-the-job training. This practical approach not only allows students to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios but also enhances their competitiveness in the job market. The curriculum is comprehensive, covering industry practices and challenges while also incorporating the latest trends in energy sectors such as Upstream, Midstream, and Downstream. Exposure to software and technology, including Advanced Excel and Power-BI, further distinguishes students, providing them with a valuable edge in the job market and increasing their employability. Overall, the BBA in Oil & Gas Management program is tailored to produce industry-ready professionals equipped with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the dynamic and demanding Oil & Gas sector.
