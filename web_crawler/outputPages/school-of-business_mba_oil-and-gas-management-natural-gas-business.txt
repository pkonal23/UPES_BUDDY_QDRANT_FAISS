The MBA in Oil & Gas Management (Natural Gas & Trading) program offered by UPES School of Business presents an array of lucrative career opportunities for its graduates. The MBA in Oil & Gas Management (Natural Gas & Trading) program equips students for mid and senior management positions in various facets of the Oil & Gas industry, both nationally and internationally.
