Dr. Anil Kumar - Achievement
Apart from being a remarkable educator, he is a member of the "WInSAR (Western North America Interferometric Synthetic Aperture Radar Consortium) Executive Committee as Adjunct II, part of UNAVCO". He is also a guest faculty member of IIRS, ISRO, Dehradun, India, and was selected as the Lead Speaker of the NISAR (NASA ISRO Synthetic Aperture Radar) Science Workshop - 2015, conducted in SAC, ISRO Ahmedabad, India. Dr Kumar has also served as the Principal Investigator for the Space Application Center, ISRO.
