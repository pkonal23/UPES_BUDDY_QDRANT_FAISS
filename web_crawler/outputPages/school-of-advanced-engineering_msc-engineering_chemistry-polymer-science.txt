The curriculum begins with a solid foundation in the Basics of Polymers, giving students a deep understanding of the fundamental principles that govern polymer chemistry and behavior. As students progress, they delve into advanced topics such as Structure-property relationships, which explores how the molecular structure of polymers influences their properties and performance. Polymer processing techniques are also covered, enabling students to grasp the intricacies of turning raw polymer materials into useful products. Moreover, students gain expertise in functional and industrial polymers, allowing them to explore the practical applications of polymers in various industries.
