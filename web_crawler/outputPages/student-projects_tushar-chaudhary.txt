Tushar Chaudhary, a student pursuing the degree of B.Des. with the specialisation in Transportation & Mobility Design from the batch of 2019-23 completed the project of designing an Electric version of TVS XL.
The project focuses on creating an electric iteration of the TVS XL, catering to personal and commercial usage. The design entails ample storage and customizable features to accommodate varied cargo and extensions. The aim of this design study for the TVS XL EV version is to discern the preferences and needs of potential customers and subsequently develop electric variants of the TVS XL that align with their specifications and requirements.
