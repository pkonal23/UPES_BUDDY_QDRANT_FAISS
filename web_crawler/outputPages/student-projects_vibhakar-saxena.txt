A project rooted in design research and development, focusing on the renowned Boat Headphone brand. The objective was to extract insights from research data and conceptualize innovative headphone designs. The Boat Friskyz headphone concept addresses the issue of headphone wire stretching shock. This innovative design incorporates stretchability to a certain extent, effectively mitigating the impact of shocks on the headphone wires.
