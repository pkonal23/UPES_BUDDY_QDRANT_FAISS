Its headquarters are in Olathe, Kansas, in the United States, and it has member institutions in more than 20 other nations. The IACBE's accrediting principles, which look at eight key areas including outcomes evaluation, strategic planning, curriculum, faculty, scholarly and professional activities, resources, internal and external partnerships, and educational innovation, are used to evaluate business programmes.
