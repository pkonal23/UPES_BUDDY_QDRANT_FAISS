School of Computer Science
Dr. Kumar is currently working as an Assistant Professor-Selection Grade in the School of Computer Science. Prior to joining computer science, he was an Assistant Professor- Senior Scale (2015-2018), and Assistant Professor- Selection Grade (2018-2023) in the Department of Mathematics at the UPES.  Before joining UPES, Dr. Kumar was a postdoctoral scholar and researcher at the University of Udine in Italy. During his time there, he made substantial contributions to the field of image processing. Dr. Kumar is able to give outstanding instruction of his extensive knowledge in the fields of mathematics and computer science.
Faculty
With a strong background in applied mathematics, computer science, and engineering, Dr. Sanoj Kumar has made significant contributions to the fields of numerical analysis, optimization techniques, mathematical statistics, image and video processing, and computer vision. He holds a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee. Dr. Kumar's research has been widely recognized through his publications in reputable journals and conferences. Dr. Kumar has an impressive publication record, with 23 publications and an H-index of 6. His work encompasses topics such as optical flow, disparity, object detection and tracking, orthogonal image moments, image encryption algorithms, image watermarking, digital signal processing, and more. Profile Summary
Dr. Kumar has extensive experience instructing a wide variety of maths courses.
