Devaleena Kundu presented the paper 'Graves Matter: Photographing Cemeteries and their Imperial Legacy' at the University of Edinburgh's Symposium on 'Photographs and Afterlives.' Her presentation discussed how colonial-era cemeteries in India reveal changing aesthetics of mourning rituals and commemoration.
