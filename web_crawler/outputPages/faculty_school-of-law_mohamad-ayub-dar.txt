Dr. Mohamad Ayub Dar holds the position of Assistant Professor within the School of Law at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). Throughout his academic career, he has demonstrated a robust commitment to scholarly endeavors, evidenced by his authorship of numerous research articles disseminated in esteemed legal publications. His scholarly contributions have consistently underscored his proficiency in analytical methodologies, critical inquiry, and adeptness in legal research. Notably, Dr. Dar's scholarly output encompasses a collection of research papers indexed in prestigious databases such as Web of Science and Scopus (Q1), attesting to the high caliber of his work. Particularly noteworthy is his paper titled "Destination Imaging, Compatibilities with Intellectual Property Rights-Based Tourism Industry," which has garnered recognition through its inclusion in the WIPO Knowledge Repository.
