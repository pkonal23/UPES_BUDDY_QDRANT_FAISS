The Ph.D. in Physics program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering aims to equip students with the necessary skills to engage in independent and collaborative original research within the field of physical sciences and related areas. The program places a strong emphasis on fostering critical thinking abilities among students, enabling them to analyze complex problems effectively. Furthermore, it emphasizes effective communication skills, both written and oral, ensuring that graduates can articulate their research findings with clarity and precision. The program is designed to instill a commitment to continuous professional development, encouraging scholars to stay abreast of advancements in their field, thereby contributing to the ongoing progress of scientific knowledge.

Admission to the Ph.D. program typically requires candidates to hold a master's degree in physics or a related field, accompanied by a robust academic record.
