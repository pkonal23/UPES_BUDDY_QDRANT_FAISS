Kanakshi Nema serves as an Assistant Professor (Law) at UPES, Dehradun, specializing in taxation law. She completed her B.Com from Sri Venkateswara College, University of Delhi and then went on to pursue her LL.B from Campus Law Centre, University of Delhi. She completed her LL.M., with specialization in technology and law from Hidayatullah National Law University, Raipur.
