The B.Tech. program offered by UPES School of Health Sciences and Technology is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the intersection between healthcare and technology. The curriculum combines core engineering subjects with specialised healthcare management, biotechnology, biomedical instrumentation, and healthcare IT courses. Students gain practical skills through laboratory sessions, industrial training, and hands-on projects on healthcare innovation and technology integration. The program also emphasises interdisciplinary learning, enabling students to develop a holistic approach to problem-solving in the healthcare industry. With a strong focus on research and industry collaboration, the curriculum equips students with the knowledge and skills necessary to contribute to advancing healthcare technology and positively impacting society.
