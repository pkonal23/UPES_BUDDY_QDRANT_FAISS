Ravi Yadav has completed his Ph. D from Department of Computer Science, Banaras Hindu University. He received his Post graduation i.e., M. Tech from KNIT, Sultanpur and Graduation i.e., B. Tech from Galgotia’s College of Engineering and Technology, respectively.  His research area includes Fog Computing, Trust management and Blockchain.
The course taught by him includes Operating system, Design Analysis and Algorithm, Data structures. Besides that, he taught and took labs on Solidity language.
Mr. Ravi Yadav has work with multidisciplinary teams and involved in collaborative works that includes research areas blockchain, trust management and fog computing.  He has good research publications in IEEE, Elsevier, and Springer publisher journals.  He is also active member of IEEE and ComSoc society.
