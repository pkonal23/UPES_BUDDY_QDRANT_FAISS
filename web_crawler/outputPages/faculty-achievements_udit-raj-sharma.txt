Faculty Achievements
Udit Raj Sharma received the Best Researcher Award at the International Conference on Sustainability and Subsidence. He was honored by Justice Surya Kant (Judge, Supreme Court of India) for his research paper titled "FROM HANIF QURESHI TO NAGARAJA: ANALYZING THE TWIST IN THE TAIL JURISPRUDENCE BY INDIAN SUPREME COURT TOWARDS ECOCENTRISM AND ANIMAL WELFARE." The paper was presented during the "WORLD ENVIRONMENT DAY OBSERVANCE 2022 and INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON SUSTAINABILITY & SUBSISTENCE: HUMAN RIGHTS & ENVIRONMENTAL ISSUES" from 5th June to 6th June 2022. The conference was organized with the CLEA‐MILATHUMAN RIGHTS & SOCIAL JUSTICE SUMMER SCHOOL-2022.
