Ananya Goel
Eligibility Criteria
Ananya Goel, a student of B.Tech. CSE in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning received the prestigious Best Research Paper Award at the AICTE-sponsored National Conference VSIIT'21. Her outstanding research paper, titled "Exploring the Application of IoT Technology in the Development of Smart Cities," showcased her profound understanding and valuable contributions to the dynamic realm of IoT technology and its pivotal role in shaping future cities. Furthermore, her research paper has been recognized and published as chapter 12 of the book "Visualizing and Sensitizing Innovations," solidifying her impact on the academic and research community.
