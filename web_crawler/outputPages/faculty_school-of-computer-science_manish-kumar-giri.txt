Profile Summary
Manish Kumar Giri received his Master of Engineering and Bachelor of Engineering degrees from Government Engineering College, Ujjain, and SSCET Junwani Bhilai, respectively. He has submitted his Ph.D. thesis in Electronics and Communication Engineering from National Institute of Technology Raipur. He is proficient in delivering course content, fostering engaging classroom discussions, and providing personalized guidance. He has proven expertise in application of machine learning in wireless communication, contributing to cutting-edge research and publications.
