Courses Taught
Dr. Amrita Chaskar completed her Ph.D. in the area of crisis communication from BITS Pilani K K Birla Goa Campus in April 2024. She has joined UPES as an Assistant Professor in School of Business, marking the start of her teaching career.
Eligibility Criteria
Dr. Chaskar has published research articles in reputed Scopus-indexed international journals. Her doctoral research focused on the crisis communication strategies of Goa's hospitality industry during the COVID-19 pandemic. She plans to build on her research findings and apply these insights to delve deeper into the area of organizational crisis communication and the related outcomes.
Dr. Chaskar received the Best TA Award during her doctoral programme from the Teaching Learning Centre- BITS Goa, demonstrating her dedication and commitment to foster a positive learning environment for the students.
