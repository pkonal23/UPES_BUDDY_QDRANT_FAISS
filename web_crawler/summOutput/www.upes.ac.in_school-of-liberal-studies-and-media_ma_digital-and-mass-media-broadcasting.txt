The MA Digital & Mass Media (Broadcasting) program at UPES School of Liberal Studies and Media is a comprehensive two-year course structured over four semesters. It is designed to equip students with a deep understanding of broadcast media, covering various essential topics such as media writing, communication skills, electronic and digital production, and more. The curriculum emphasizes practical learning, enabling students to gain real-world experience and cultivate their creativity in the digital and mass media landscape.

### Key Program Features:
- **Curriculum Highlights**: Courses include Advanced Filmmaking for Digital, Advanced Motion Graphics, Introduction to Creative Writing, Global Media and Digital Networks, Digital Video Production and Post-production, and more. The program also covers data and visual storytelling, social media brand building, and immersive technologies like AR and VR.
- **Practical Experience**: Students benefit from hands-on training through internships, on-the-job training, and major projects, which prepare them for diverse career roles in the media and entertainment sector.
- **Industry-Relevant Training**: The program is led by experienced media professionals, ensuring that students acquire industry-relevant knowledge and expertise.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates can pursue various roles within the broadcasting industry, including:
- Television Producer
- Radio Host
- Broadcast Journalist
- Video Editor
- Content Strategist
- Social Media Manager

The program also prepares students for positions in online content creation, podcasting, and multimedia production, with attractive opportunities available in leading media houses such as NDTV, ABP, NEWS18, and TV Today, as well as numerous mid-sized content startups. Fresh graduates can expect competitive starting salaries ranging from INR 4-10 lakh per annum, depending on their skills and industry exposure.

### Admission Criteria:
To apply for the program, students must meet the eligibility criteria of securing at least 50% in their X, XII, and Graduation in any stream. The selection process includes a personal interview.
