Deepanshu Singh Satwaliya is an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science at UPES, where he has been associated since March 2024. His teaching philosophy centers on the belief that every student is unique, with distinct talents, experiences, and learning styles. He perceives education as a lifelong journey, both for himself and his students, and is committed to inspiring them to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens.

Mr. Satwaliya teaches a variety of courses, including Computer Organization and Architecture, Digital Logic Design, Operating Systems, Data Structures and Algorithms, programming languages such as C and Python, as well as Data Warehousing, Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Networking. He previously served as an Assistant Professor at Jain (Deemed-to-be University) in Bangalore, where he was recognized as a high-end faculty member in a prestigious course.

He holds a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from F.G.I.E.T, Raebareli (a government college) under A.K.T.U., and an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from NIT Hamirpur. Mr. Satwaliya has qualified the Graduate Aptitude Test (GATE) in 2017, 2018, 2020, and 2024, and boasts over three years of teaching experience. He is also an accomplished scholar, having published research papers, patents, and a book on national and international platforms.

For further inquiries or to contact him, Mr. Satwaliya can be reached via email at deepanshus.satwaliya@ddn.upes.ac.in.
