**Dr. Harshika Varma**

**Profile Summary**

Dr. Harshika Varma holds the esteemed position of Senior Assistant Professor at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). Dr. Harshika Varma's exceptional career in higher education is marked by Dr. Harshika Varma's profound expertise in Journalism and Mass Communication, particularly in the realm of Indian Advertising.

Dr. Harshika Varma possesses an impressive educational background, boasting a doctorate in Journalism and Mass Communication, alongside a Certificate Course in Business Management from the renowned Department of Management Studies at Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT-Delhi). Dr. Harshika Varma also holds a master's degree from the prestigious School of Communication Studies at Panjab University, Chandigarh.

Dr. Harshika Varma's international presence is equally notable. Dr. Harshika Varma represented India at the Europe-Asia Dialogue Program in Brussels, Belgium, where Dr. Harshika Varma showcased Dr. Harshika Varma's proficiency in fostering meaningful cross-cultural exchanges and contributing to the international dialogue. Dr. Harshika Varma's academic excellence, dynamic speaking engagements, and distinguished awards solidify Dr. Harshika Varma's reputation as a respected educator and scholar.
