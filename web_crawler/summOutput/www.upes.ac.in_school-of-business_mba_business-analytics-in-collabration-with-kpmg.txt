The MBA in Business Analytics program at UPES School of Business, conducted in collaboration with KPMG, is designed to equip students with essential skills and knowledge in data-driven decision-making and analytics. The program covers a comprehensive curriculum that includes core business courses and specialized training in analytics, ensuring graduates are well-prepared for the evolving demands of the industry.

### Program Structure and Specializations
The MBA program spans four semesters and includes a variety of subjects such as:
- **Semester 1**: Accounting for Managers, Financial Management, Business Communication.
- **Semester 2**: Managerial Economics, Human Resource Management, Business Research Methods.
- **Semester 3**: Business Ethics and CSR, Operations Management, Strategic Management.
- **Semester 4**: Business Analytics, Big Data Analytics, and specialized courses in Cognitive Analytics and Data Mining.

Students can choose between two specializations: **Energy Analytics** and **Data Mining**. Energy Analytics focuses on the energy sector's unique requirements, while Data Mining teaches techniques for extracting insights from large datasets.

### Admission Criteria
To be eligible for the program, candidates must meet the following criteria:
- A minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.
- A qualifying score in national-level entrance exams such as MAT, CMAT, CAT, XAT, GMAT, or NMAT.

Students with a minimum MAT/CMAT score of 70 percentile, or equivalent scores in other exams, may be exempt from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will proceed directly to the Group Discussion and Interview stages.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates of the program are well-positioned for various roles in the analytics field, including:
- Data Analyst
- Business Intelligence Manager
- Financial Analyst
- Marketing Strategist
- Supply Chain Analyst

The program has seen significant improvements in placements, with students securing positions in renowned companies such as Flipkart, Reliance Industries, HUL, Accenture, and Deloitte. Starting salaries for fresh graduates specializing in data mining can range from INR 6 to 30 lakh per annum, depending on their skills and experience.

### Practical Experience
The curriculum emphasizes practical learning through major projects, summer internships, industry visits, and guest lectures by industry experts. Students gain hands-on experience in analytics and data-driven decision-making, preparing them for real-world challenges.

### Facilities and Resources
UPES School of Business offers state-of-the-art facilities, including smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, and a well-stocked library, along with advanced analytical tools to enhance the learning experience.

### Global Exposure
The program encourages international exposure through student exchange and study abroad opportunities with partner institutions, broadening students' perspectives and networking capabilities.

### Conclusion
The MBA in Business Analytics at UPES School of Business is a robust program that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, preparing students for successful careers in the rapidly growing field of business analytics. The collaboration with KPMG enhances the curriculum's relevance and provides students with valuable industry connections.
