**Dr. Arti Awasthi's Academic and Professional Profile**

Dr. Arti Awasthi is an esteemed Assistant Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), School of Liberal Studies. Dr. Arti Awasthi's academic journey began with a Ph.D. in Mathematics from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee.

Throughout Dr. Arti Awasthi's career, Dr. Arti Awasthi has dedicated herself to unraveling the intricacies of infectious disease dynamics. Dr. Arti Awasthi's expertise lies in developing mathematical models that aid in understanding the behavior of infectious diseases, paving the way for effective strategies to control and prevent their spread.

Dr. Arti Awasthi's research endeavors have garnered international recognition, with publications in renowned journals and presentations at international conferences. Dr. Arti Awasthi's work has been instrumental in advancing mathematical epidemiology, shedding light on complex vector-borne disease models.

Prior to joining UPES, Dr. Arti Awasthi held a position as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra. Dr. Arti Awasthi also served as a Visiting Researcher at the University of Alabama, USA, where Dr. Arti Awasthi delved into advanced mathematical models of vector-borne diseases.

Dr. Arti Awasthi's research pursuits have been bolstered by national fellowships, including the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR)-National Eligibility Test (NET) and the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE). Additionally, Dr. Arti Awasthi has played a pivotal role in an Indo-French Centre of Applied Mathematics (IFCAM)-funded project on infectious disease modeling.

**Teaching Philosophy and Interests**

Dr. Arti Awasthi's teaching approach centers around student-centric learning, emphasizing foundational clarity and interactive engagement. Dr. Arti Awasthi values student inquiry and fosters meaningful discussions, empowering them to apply mathematical concepts to real-world scenarios. Dr. Arti Awasthi's teaching portfolio encompasses courses in Calculus, Engineering Mathematics, Linear Algebra, Differential Equations, Dynamical Systems, and Abstract Algebra, catering to the diverse needs of undergraduate and postgraduate students.

**Awards and Scholarly Contributions**

Dr. Arti Awasthi's scholarly contributions have significantly advanced the field of mathematical epidemiology.
