The B.Tech Applied Petroleum Engineering (Intelligent Petroleum Systems) program offered by the UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to equip students with essential knowledge and skills for a successful career in the petroleum industry. This program emphasizes the integration of intelligent systems, advanced technologies, and practical applications, preparing graduates to meet the evolving demands of the sector.

### Program Overview
- **Course Structure**: The curriculum covers fundamental engineering principles alongside petroleum-specific subjects, including reservoir engineering, drilling engineering, production engineering, and well testing. It incorporates modern technologies such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics to optimize exploration and production processes.
- **Hands-On Experience**: Students engage with advanced simulators and industrial software, gaining practical experience through industry visits, internships, and lab work, which enhances their understanding of real-world scenarios.
- **Innovative Approach**: The program encourages students to develop novel technologies to optimize asset performance and enhance operational capabilities, fostering a culture of innovation within petroleum engineering.

### Specialization and Courses
The program includes various specialization courses throughout its semesters, such as:
- Advanced Drilling Engineering
- Natural Gas Engineering
- Oil and Gas Storage
- Enhanced Oil Recovery
- Data Analytics and AI in Petroleum
- Offshore Drilling and Reservoir Monitoring

### Eligibility Criteria
Candidates must meet the following requirements:
- Minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII.
- At least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII.
- Admission is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates can pursue various roles including:
- Reservoir Engineer
- Drilling Engineer
- Production Engineer
- Process Engineer
- Pipeline Engineer

### Industry Connections and Placements
The program boasts a robust placement record, with a 100% success rate for the latest batch. Graduates have received multiple job offers from prestigious companies such as:
- Cairn Oil & Gas-Vedanta Ltd.
