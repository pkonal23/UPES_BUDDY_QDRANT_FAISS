The Purkal Youth Development Society (PYDS) and the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) have played a pivotal role in transforming the futures of underprivileged students, enabling them to excel academically and transition into skilled professionals. A notable success story includes a student who secured a position at Microsoft with an impressive salary of 50 LPA, sharing insights on how he successfully navigated the job interview process. 

UPES has made significant strides in research, boasting a research output that triples the global average and featuring faculty members who are recognized as highly cited researchers worldwide. This commitment to academic excellence is further reflected in the university's initiatives to enhance corporate responsibility amidst rising temperatures and growing inequalities.

In alignment with University Grants Commission guidelines, UPES School of Law has appointed esteemed legal practitioners as Professors of Practice. This initiative aims to bridge the gap between industry and academia by integrating practical skills into the curriculum, leveraging the extensive industry experience of these professionals.

Students like Harshit Joshi, who specialized in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology, have successfully secured placements at leading companies like Samsung. He attributes his achievements to UPES's advanced curriculum, supportive faculty, research and development labs, and a robust placement cell.

The university also emphasizes its commitment to sustainability and leadership development, offering Executive Education Programs aimed at cultivating future leaders in Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) sectors. Additionally, students are encouraged to pursue entrepreneurial ventures, with one law student noted for founding two non-profits.

Bikram Pratim Bhuyan, an Assistant Professor at UPES School of Computer Science, received the prestigious Cotutelle Doctoral Grant to complete his PhD at the University of Paris-Saclay, France, providing insights into the rigorous preparations for doctoral studies.
