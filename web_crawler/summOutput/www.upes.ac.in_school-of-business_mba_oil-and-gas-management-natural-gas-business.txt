The MBA in Oil & Gas Management (Natural Gas & Trading) program at UPES School of Business has seen significant success in placements, with graduates securing positions in prominent companies such as ONGC, Accenture, Infosys, Wipro, Ernst and Young, Deloitte, EXXON MOBIL, SHELL, GAIL GAS, TOTAL, Adani Gas, and JIO-BP, among others. Start-ups are also actively recruiting these graduates, expanding their career opportunities. The initial salary for fresh graduates typically ranges from INR 6-30 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills and experiences.

**Program Overview:**
- The MBA program is designed to address the evolving needs of the oil and gas industry, particularly in light of India's transition towards a "Gas Based Economy." 
- The curriculum includes specialized subjects such as Understanding of Natural Gas Business, Fundamentals of Oil & Gas Business, Refining, and Petrochemical Business, all taught by experienced instructors.
- Students are encouraged to engage in practical experiences through summer internships, live projects, and industry visits, which are integral to the program.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and graduation from a recognized university in any stream.
- Admission is based on performance in UPESMET or other national level exams, followed by a personal interview.

**Special Features:**
- The program emphasizes technological proficiency, equipping students with skills in software like SAP, SCADA, and advanced analytics tools (Excel, SPSS, Python, R-studio).
- It promotes a global perspective through student and faculty exchanges, research projects, and seminars, ensuring a collaborative learning environment.
- As part of the Federation of Indian Petroleum Industry, students can join a student chapter that enhances industry connections and practical applications of their managerial skills.

**Career Paths:**
Graduates are prepared for a wide array of roles, including:
- Oil & Gas regulatory and financial consultant
- Energy Analyst/SME in IT and consultancy
- ESG specialist
- Power and Carbon Trading expert
- Energy Efficiency Consultant
- Business Development roles
- Fuel Procurement Analyst
- CGD Project Manager
- Sustainability Consultant
- Business Analyst in Energy Storage and E-vehicles
