   - **KhelUtsav**: The UPES Sports Festival that promotes physical fitness and sports participation among students.
   - **Freshers Party**: Organized by second-year students to welcome first-year students, providing a platform for social interaction and cultural display.

3. **National Service Scheme (NSS)**:
