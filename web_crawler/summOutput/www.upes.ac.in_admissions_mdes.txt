The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers Master of Design (M.Des.) programs through its nationally recognized School of Design. The focus of these programs is to nurture design entrepreneurs, emphasizing the integration of modern technology, design thinking, and digital transformation. The curriculum promotes multidisciplinary learning through a mix of customized coursework and practical projects, and students benefit from live classes conducted by leading industry specialists and designers in India.

### Eligibility Criteria
To be eligible for the M.Des. program, candidates must meet the following requirements:
- A minimum of 50% marks in their bachelor’s degree in any stream (10+2+3 years). Preference is given to graduates from B.Des., B.Arch., B.Tech., or BFA programs.
- Candidates must also have secured at least 50% marks in their classes X and XII.

### Admission Process
The admission process includes:
1. **Exam Pathway**: Candidates must take the UPES Design Aptitude Test (UPESDAT), followed by an interview and portfolio presentation.
2. **Non-Exam Pathway**: Candidates can also apply with valid scores from national-level exams such as CEED, NID, or NIFT, followed by a portfolio presentation and interview.

Candidates are required to submit their Aadhaar card number, PAN card number (including their parents’ PAN), and original academic records during the admission process. The application can be submitted online by clicking "Apply Now" or offline at the admission office, with a fee of Rs. 1750/-.

### Specializations Offered
UPES provides various specializations within the M.Des. program, including:
- Communication Design
- Interaction Design
- Mobility and Vehicle Design
- Product Design

### Career Prospects
Graduates of the M.Des. program can pursue diverse career opportunities, including:
- Interior Designer
- Visual Merchandise Designer
- Product Designer
- Graphic Designer
- UI/UX Specialist
- Brand Strategist
- Creative Director

The program also equips students with transferable skills such as logical thinking and analytical abilities, which are beneficial across different career domains.

### Scholarships and Facilities
UPES offers scholarships for M.Des. students, and the university provides a range of facilities, including:
- State-of-the-art laboratories and auditoriums
- Hostel accommodations on a first-come, first-served basis, with separate blocks for boys and girls
- 24x7 medical facilities, including a team of doctors
- Transport services and multi-cuisine food options
