Dr. Harvinder Pal Singh is a prominent academic and researcher with a specialization in energy economics, firm-level energy efficiency, and sustainable development. He boasts over eight years of experience in both academia and industry, having held significant positions at UPES School of Business and ICFAI Business School. His research interests prominently feature innovation, firm internationalization, and the pursuit of sustainable development goals (SDGs), with a robust portfolio of publications in high-impact journals.

### Work Experience
Dr. Singh has a strong commitment to active and collaborative learning in his teaching approach. He employs innovative methods such as case studies, data-driven discussions, and practical assignments to cultivate critical thinking among students. His teaching philosophy includes integrating real-world problems into the curriculum, enabling students to gain expertise in economic analysis and decision-making. He also utilizes the "flipped classroom" model to enhance student engagement.

### Academic Contributions
Dr. Singh has authored numerous research papers in esteemed ABDC-ranked journals, concentrating on themes of energy efficiency, innovation, and sustainable practices within emerging economies. His current research projects investigate the implementation of environmentally sustainable measures in manufacturing firms. He has presented his findings at notable conferences, including the PAN IIM World Management Conference in 2021 and the ISDSI Global Conference in 2023. He is a life member of the Indian Economic Association and actively engages in various academic workshops and collaborative efforts.

### Courses Taught
Dr. Singh has taught a diverse range of courses at ICFAI Business School and LPU, including:
- International Economics
- Macroeconomics
- Business Economics
- Managerial Economics
- Business Statistics
- Econometrics
- Economics for Engineers

### Awards and Certifications
Dr. Singh has received various accolades for his scholarly activities and has participated in workshops like the Data Visualization with Power BI through Great Learning Academy (2024) and a Power BI Workshop by Techtip24 (2024).

### Current Position
As of July 2024, Dr. Singh serves as an Assistant Professor Senior Scale at UPES School of Business. His previous roles include Assistant Professor of Economics at ICFAI Business School (August 2022 – June 2024), Research Scholar (July 2016 – August 2022), and Lecturer at Mittal School of Business, LPU (January 2014 – May 2016). He began his career as an Executive in New Client Acquisition at India MART (June 2012 – December 2013).
