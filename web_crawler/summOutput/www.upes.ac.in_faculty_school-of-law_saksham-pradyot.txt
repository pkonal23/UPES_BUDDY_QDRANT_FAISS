**Profile Summary: Saksham Pradyot**

**Contact Information:**
- Email: saksham.pradyot@ddn.upes.ac.in

**Academic Background:**
- Integrated BBA LL.B. degree with a specialization in Corporate Laws (2015)
- LL.M. degree with a specialization in Constitutional & Administrative Laws (2018)
- Currently pursuing a PhD focusing on Media Laws

**Professional Experience:**
- Assistant Professor at the School of Law, UPES
- Legal Practitioner with 7 years of experience in Judicial and Quasi-Judicial Bodies in Uttarakhand
- Enrolled in the State Bar Council of Uttarakhand in 2015, with a Certificate of Commencement of Practice from the Bar Council of India
- Visiting Faculty of Law at various Law Schools and Business Schools in Uttarakhand since 2018

**Research Interests:**
Saksham's research interests encompass:
- Media Laws
- Casteism in India
- Constitutional Philosophy
- Sports Laws

**Publications:**
Saksham has contributed to various national and international journals and books. Notable publications include:
- "Medical Negligence in India" presented at the 2nd International Conference by Galgotias University, published in *Lex Et Societatis* (ISBN 978-93-87910-06-5)
- "The Impact of IT Rules, 2021 on Social Media in India" and "The Impact of Carbon Effect on Climate Change in India: A Need for Energy-Efficient Fuels," both in *Global Thoughts and Opinions* (ISBN 978-81-947778-0-9), edited by Hon'ble Justice A.K. Sikri
- "Social Media and Hijab Row" published in the *International Journal of Research in Social Sciences*

**Teaching Philosophy:**
Saksham emphasizes a teaching approach that motivates students to focus on long-term victories rather than short-term challenges. He aims to unlock each student's potential, recognizing that not all students possess the same skills. His fulfillment comes from positively impacting his students' lives and helping them evolve into better versions of themselves.
