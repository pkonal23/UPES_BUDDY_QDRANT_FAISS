Monica Pradyot is a dedicated and resourceful educator specializing in Taxation Law, currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the School of Law, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) since June 2022. She is committed to fostering the academic growth of her students by emphasizing hard work and the importance of consistent effort in achieving success. Monica draws upon her industry experience to guide students in their career paths and to help them develop essential soft skills.

Her academic contributions are notable, having published various articles, papers, and book chapters in both national and international journals. Some of her significant publications include:

1. **"The Impact of Carbon Effect on Climate Change in India: A Need for Energy-Efficient Fuels"** - Chapter in the book *Global Thoughts and Opinions*, edited by Honorable Justice A.K. Sikri.
2. **"RBI as Regulator and Changing Role"** - Research paper published in the *Centre for Banking Investment & Taxation, GNLU Law Journal*.
3. **"The Impact of IT Rules, 2021, on Social Media in India"** - Chapter in *Global Thoughts and Opinions*, edited by Honorable Justice A.K. Sikri.
4. **"Medical Negligence in India"** - Presented at the 2nd International Conference by Galgotias University and published in *Lex Et Societatis*.
5. **"Exploring the Role of Carbon Taxation Policies on CO2 Emissions: A Comparative Analysis with Japan"** - Published in the *Scopus Journal of European Chemical Bulletin*.
6. **"Sustainable Development: An Indian Case Study"** - Published in the *International Journal of Research in Economics and Social Sciences*.
7. **"Is India Truly on the Path of a Sustainable Future? A Study on the Legal Approach to Energy-Efficient Fuels in India towards Reducing Carbon Emissions"** - Published in the *International Journal of Research in Social Sciences*.
8. **"Wrongful Termination of Employees during Covid-19: An Analysis of Labor Codes"** - Published in the *International Journal of Research in Social Sciences*.

Monica's research interests are centered on developing sustainable policies and legal frameworks that promote economic growth, with specific focuses on taxation laws, energy law, and company law. She is currently pursuing her Ph.D. with a concentration on Energy and Environment Laws, having completed her BBA LL.B. (Corporate Laws) in 2015 and her LL.M. (Energy Laws) in 2017.

Before her tenure at UPES, Monica gained valuable experience as an Associate at D H Law Associates (now Dentons Link Legal) in Mumbai, where she worked in the Banking and Finance team for 1.5 years. She also served as a visiting faculty member at UPES for five years.

In addition to her teaching role, Monica is actively involved in various academic and extracurricular initiatives at UPES. She is the Faculty Convenor of the Jigyasa Quiz Club, Faculty Co-Convenor of the Youth Parliament, and a member of the Student Training and Placement Committee and Industrial Collaborations.

Monica has further enhanced her qualifications by completing online courses from prestigious institutions, including a course on 'Energy Within Environmental Constraints' from Harvard University and 'Renewable Energy & Green Building Entrepreneurship' from Duke University, North Carolina, USA.
