Dr. Akhilesh Mohan Srivastava is an accomplished academic in the field of Computer Science and Engineering, holding a Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IIT Indore), India. He also earned his Master of Technology (M.Tech) degree from Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, India. Dr. Srivastava has a robust publication record, with numerous papers featured in SCI-indexed journals and presentations at various national and international conferences. He is the author of three notable books: "Compiler Design," "Programming in C++," and "Object Oriented Programming using C++."

His research interests are diverse and include image processing, computer vision, pattern recognition, with a specific focus on 2D and 3D face recognition, deep learning, and biometric security. Dr. Srivastava has over eight years of teaching experience across multiple institutions and four years of industry experience. He has notably contributed to a research project titled "Development of an Efficient Ear Biometric System and Investigation of Age Invariant Nature of Human Ear," which was funded by the Science and Engineering Board (SERB) under the Department of Science & Technology (DST), Government of India, from February 2016 to July 2017.

In his teaching, Dr. Srivastava emphasizes a hands-on learning approach, believing that every student has the potential to succeed with the right guidance. He employs a methodology that includes revisiting previous lessons to connect students to current topics, encouraging questions, and utilizing real-life examples for better understanding. Student assessments are comprehensive, involving assignments, quizzes, examinations, and surprise tests.

Dr. Srivastava's areas of expertise include Data Structures, Operating Systems, Data Communications and Networking, Deep Learning, Design and Analysis of Algorithms, and Parallel Algorithms. His scholarly activities reflect his commitment to advancing knowledge in biometrics, machine learning, deep learning, pattern recognition, computer vision, image processing, and 3D point cloud processing. He has proposed innovative 3D data augmentation techniques and the fusion of 3D deep learning architectures, enhancing classification and recognition of 3D data.
