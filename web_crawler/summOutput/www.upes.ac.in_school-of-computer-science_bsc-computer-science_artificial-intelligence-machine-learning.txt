The content outlines the B.Sc. program in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) offered by the UPES School of Computer Science. The program is structured into eight semesters, encompassing a comprehensive curriculum that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills essential for the AI and ML fields.

### Program Overview:
- **Specialization**: Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
- **Total Credits**: 24 for major electives, with various labs and courses distributed across semesters.
- **Eligibility Criteria**: Students must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII, with Mathematics, Computer Science, or Information Technology as a major subject in Class XII.

### Curriculum Highlights:
- The curriculum includes courses such as:
  - Data Structures and Algorithms
  - Deep Learning
  - Natural Language Processing
  - Computational Linguistics
  - Statistics for Data Analysis
  - Programming in C, C++, and Python
  - Database Management
  - Cyber Security
  - Digital Electronics
- Students engage in hands-on labs (e.g., AI/ML labs, OOPs using C++ Lab, and more) and projects throughout the program to enhance their practical skills.
