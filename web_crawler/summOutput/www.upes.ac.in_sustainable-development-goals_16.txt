The Legal Aid Cell at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is dedicated to raising awareness among students about critical social issues and encouraging their active participation in addressing these challenges. This initiative aligns with Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 16, which emphasizes the promotion of peace, justice, and strong institutions as a cornerstone for sustainable development.

SDG 16 aims to establish peaceful and inclusive societies, ensure justice for all, and create effective, accountable, and inclusive institutions across all levels. Key targets associated with this goal include:

- **Reduction of Illicit Activities**: By 2030, there is a target to significantly diminish illicit financial and arms flows, enhance the recovery and return of stolen assets, and combat various forms of organized crime.
- **Promotion of the Rule of Law**: It seeks to promote the rule of law both nationally and internationally while ensuring equal access to justice for all individuals.
- **Corruption Mitigation**: A substantial reduction in corruption and bribery in all its forms is a critical target.
- **Inclusive Governance**: The goal includes broadening and strengthening the participation of developing countries in global governance institutions.
- **Effective Decision-Making**: There is an emphasis on ensuring responsive, inclusive, participatory, and representative decision-making processes at all levels.
- **Institutional Development**: Development of effective, accountable, and transparent institutions is essential.
- **Violence Reduction**: A significant reduction in all forms of violence and related death rates is targeted.
- **Child Protection**: Ending abuse, exploitation, trafficking, and all forms of violence against children, including torture, is a priority.
- **Access to Information**: Ensuring public access to information and protecting fundamental freedoms in accordance with national legislation and international agreements.
- **Legal Identity**: Providing legal identity for all, including birth registration, is also a focus area.

The Legal Aid Cell at UPES plays a vital role in assisting individuals in need by offering legal advice and support to navigate legal challenges. The cell prioritizes the best interests of those it serves, aiming to protect their rights and promote collective welfare within the community. Through these efforts, UPES is contributing to the broader objectives of SDG 16, fostering a more inclusive legal aid society.
