The B.Tech. program in Applied Petroleum Engineering at the UPES School of Advanced Engineering is a comprehensive four-year course designed to equip students with a robust understanding of the oil and gas industry. The curriculum integrates foundational engineering subjects in the initial semesters, transitioning into specialized petroleum engineering topics. Key features of the program include:

1. **Industry-Relevant Curriculum**: The curriculum emphasizes both theoretical and practical knowledge, incorporating subjects such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Drilling Engineering, Reservoir Engineering, and Production Engineering. This prepares students to address challenges in both conventional and unconventional energy resources.

2. **Specializations**: Students can tailor their studies by choosing from three specializations: Energy Systems and Storage, Intelligent Petroleum Systems, or Gas Engineering Informatics. This allows for a focused educational experience in areas of personal interest.

3. **Hands-On Experience**: The program offers access to modern laboratories and equipment used in the petroleum industry, along with opportunities for internships and field visits to oilfields and refineries. This practical exposure is crucial for real-world application of learned concepts.

4. **Placement Success**: The program boasts a 100% placement record, with graduates receiving attractive salary packages, some up to 33 lakhs. Notable recruiters include Cairn Oil & Gas, ExxonMobil, Reliance Industries, and consulting firms like McKinsey & Company.

5. **Global Exposure**: The program includes international academic collaborations with universities such as the University of New South Wales and the University of Aberdeen, enriching the educational experience and broadening students' perspectives on global energy practices.

6. **Research and Innovation**: Students are encouraged to engage in research projects, publish papers, and participate in conferences, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for advancing knowledge in petroleum engineering.

7. **Career Opportunities**: Graduates can pursue diverse roles, including petroleum engineers, energy consultants, and reservoir engineers, across various sectors such as oil and gas companies, renewable energy firms, and government agencies.

8. **Eligibility Criteria**: To apply, students must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, along with 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII.

9. **Application Deadline**: Interested candidates should note that the last date to apply is February 25, 2025.
