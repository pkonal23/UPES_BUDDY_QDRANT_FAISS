Dr. Siddharth Jain is an accomplished academic and researcher currently teaching at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). He offers a diverse range of courses, including Sustainable Energy System for Mobility and Advanced Propulsion Systems for M.Tech students, Machine Design for B.Tech students, and Contemporary Research Challenges and Trends for PhD students.

Before his tenure at UPES, Dr. Jain served as a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the National University of Singapore, where he collaborated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) as part of the Campus for Research Excellence and Technological Enterprise (CREATE) initiative. This program was dedicated to addressing sustainable solutions for challenges faced by megacities, strategic policy-making, and real-time environmental monitoring and response. Dr. Jain has also held positions as an Associate Professor at the University of Alberta in Canada and at the College of Engineering Roorkee in India.

Dr. Jain's academic journey includes both a master's and a doctoral degree from the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee, focusing on energy-related research, alongside a strong foundation in mechanical engineering from his undergraduate studies. His research interests encompass Biomass & Bioenergy, waste-to-energy technologies, Life Cycle Assessment, and Thermochemical conversion processes. He has actively engaged in various projects that have strengthened his commitment to sustainable energy, which he believes is crucial for global development and interdisciplinary applications.

His teaching philosophy emphasizes creating an engaging learning environment that encourages student participation and motivation. He integrates Project-Based Learning (PBL) into his classes, utilizing projects and case studies that allow students to connect theoretical knowledge with practical applications. Additionally, he employs a hybrid teaching model that combines traditional classroom instruction, PBL, and flipped classroom methodologies to enhance student learning experiences.

Dr. Jain has received multiple accolades for his contributions to education and research, including the Best Teacher award in 2015 and recognition as one of the world's top 2% researchers by Stanford University in both 2021 and 2022. Furthermore, he was honored with the Researcher of the Year Award by the Chief Minister of Uttarakhand, Shri Pushkar Singh Dhami, in 2021, and has secured three sponsored projects from TEQIP-3, UTU, in 2019.
