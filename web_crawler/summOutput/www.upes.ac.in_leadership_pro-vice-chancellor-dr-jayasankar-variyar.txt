Dr. Jayasankar Variyar serves as the Pro Vice Chancellor at UPES (University of Petroleum and Energy Studies), where he emphasizes the importance of community, collaboration, and innovation in shaping the future. He addresses students, parents, alumni, industry recruiters, and academic partners as the foundational elements of UPES, highlighting their role in driving the institution's ambitions and success. 

Under Dr. Variyar's leadership, UPES focuses on diversity as a core value, fostering an inclusive environment that enhances creativity and collaboration among students, faculty, and staff. This diverse community is essential for preparing students to thrive in a globalized world. The university is committed not only to academic excellence but also to developing compassionate and socially responsible individuals. Graduates are trained to be empathetic leaders who contribute positively to society, equipped with both technical skills and ethical values.

As technology continues to transform various aspects of life, UPES views this change as an opportunity for growth and innovation. The institution encourages students to master emerging technologies and transition from traditional roles to more creative and impactful positions, fostering a culture of curiosity and innovation aimed at benefiting society.

Dr. Variyar emphasizes a comprehensive educational approach that combines depth and breadth in learning. This prepares students to navigate future complexities with a strong knowledge base and interdisciplinary skills. Recognizing the necessity of adaptability, UPES nurtures a lifelong learning mindset among its students, ensuring they are well-equipped to face the challenges of a dynamic global landscape.

The university is dedicated to empowering individuals through rigorous academic programs, social internships, and strong industry partnerships, ultimately preparing graduates to excel confidently in their careers. Dr. Variyar envisions UPES as a transformative space that cultivates academic excellence and holistic development, aiming to produce changemakers ready to lead in an ever-evolving world.
