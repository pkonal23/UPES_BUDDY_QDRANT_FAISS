Dr. Kapil Gupta is an accomplished academic and researcher currently serving as an Assistant Professor (Senior Scale) at the School of Computer Science, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun. He holds a Ph.D. obtained in 2023 from the PDPM - Indian Institute of Information Technology, Design & Manufacturing, Jabalpur. His academic journey includes a B.E. with Honors from the Oriental Institute of Science and Technology and an M. Tech. from the School of Nano Technology at UTD RGPV, Bhopal.

Dr. Gupta has extensive teaching experience exceeding seven years and over three years in research. Prior to his current position, he worked as an Assistant Professor at the Oriental College of Technology in Bhopal from 2013 to 2020. He also served as a Senior Research Fellow and Post-doctoral Fellow at the Indian Knowledge System Lab at the International Institute of Information Technology in Bangalore, Karnataka.

His research interests encompass a broad range of areas including Time-Frequency Analysis, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Heart-Machine Interface, Brain-Computer Interface, and Data Analysis for Signal & Image Processing Applications in Biomedicine. Dr. Gupta's commitment to education is reflected in his teaching philosophy, which emphasizes a student-centered learning environment that incorporates real-world applications and collaborative learning. He aims to equip students with not only theoretical knowledge but also practical skills necessary for success in the evolving field of computer science.

Dr. Gupta has received accolades for his research contributions, including a best paper award for his work on knee joint VAG signals at ICCWC 2022 and a best paper presenter award for his research on EEG channel estimation at EASCT 2023. He has also been recognized through various certifications, including an Elite NPTEL online certificate for "Teaching & Learning in General Programs" and another for "Effective Engineering Teaching in Practice."

He has served on the technical program committee for notable conferences such as the 29th International Conference on Neural Information Processing (ICONIP 2022) and has contributed to reputable journals including IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics and IEEE Sensors Journal.
