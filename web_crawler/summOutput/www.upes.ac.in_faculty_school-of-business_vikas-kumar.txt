Vikas Kumar is an accomplished academic with over 20 years of teaching experience, currently serving as an Assistant Professor (SG) at the School of Business, UPES, Dehradun, where he has been since 2007. His teaching career began in 2003 at the Department of Management Studies, GLAITM, Mathura, which is affiliated with UPTU. In addition to classroom instruction, Kumar has facilitated Management Development and ISPE Training Programs for major organizations such as Oil India Limited (OIL) and Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL).

Kumar has actively participated in numerous faculty development programs at prestigious institutions, including IIM Bangalore, NIT Jalandhar, IIM Lucknow, IIT Roorkee, and IIM Indore. He also engaged in an Erasmus+ Faculty Exchange Program at the University of Maribor, Slovenia, where he delivered sessions aimed at international students. His teaching methodology integrates theoretical concepts with practical exercises and hands-on training.

His scholarly contributions include published papers on diverse topics such as Project Feasibility, Project Discounting Rate, the Environmental Impact of River Valley Projects, the Impact of COVID-19 on Tourism, and various Tourism projects. He has presented his research at both national and international conferences, fostering meaningful academic exchanges within the management education community.

Kumar is dedicated to mentoring students, guiding them through Summer Internships, Industrial Projects, and Dissertation Projects. He conducts preparatory sessions for student placements, group discussions, and NET exam preparation, promoting active participation and experiential learning through live projects. His administrative roles have included serving as course coordinator, timetable coordinator, and participating in activities related to NAAC, NBA, and IQAC.

His teaching repertoire encompasses subjects such as Project Management & Contract Administration, Production & Operations Management, Managerial/Business Economics, Introduction to Supply Chain Management, and Service Operations, catering to both undergraduate and postgraduate students across various schools within the university.

Kumar holds an MBA from the Faculty of Management Studies & Research at AMU, Aligarh, and a B.Tech. in Chemical Engineering from Zakir Husain College of Engineering & Technology, AMU, Aligarh. He has qualified for the UGC-NET in Management. His research interests focus on the environmental, social, and economic impacts of energy and infrastructure projects, as well as project costing and feasibility studies.
