The B.Sc. Computer Science in Data Science program at UPES School of Computer Science is meticulously designed to equip graduates with the necessary skills to analyze real-life problems scientifically, work collaboratively in multidisciplinary teams, and approach challenges with an ethical mindset. The program emphasizes a strong foundation in data science principles, including statistical analysis, predictive modeling, and forecasting, alongside practical applications using industry-standard tools.

**Program Structure and Curriculum:**
The curriculum is divided into eight semesters, covering a range of subjects such as:
- Programming in C and C++
- Data Structures and Algorithms
- Operating Systems
- Machine Learning and Deep Learning
- Data Visualization
- Cyber Security
- Environmental Sustainability
- Elements of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Hands-on experience is emphasized through labs and projects, including:
- Programming Labs (C, C++, Python)
- Data Visualization Labs
- Research Seminars
- Summer Internships
- Capstone Projects

Each semester includes major electives and practical labs, ensuring students gain interdisciplinary exposure and practical skills relevant to the data-driven job market.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
To enroll in the program, candidates must meet the following criteria:
- A minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII
- Mathematics, Computer Science, or Information Technology must be one of the major subjects in Class XII
- Admission is based on performance in a Personal Interview or CUET.
