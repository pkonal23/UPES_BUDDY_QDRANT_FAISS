The M.Sc. in Chemistry program with a specialization in Instrumental Chemical Analysis, offered by the UPES School of Advanced Engineering, prepares graduates for a promising career in various sectors, including pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and forensic investigations. The program emphasizes advanced analytical techniques and instrumental methods, covering essential topics such as Spectroscopy, Quality Management, Data Processing, and Analytical Separation Techniques.

**Program Structure and Curriculum:**
- The curriculum is designed to enhance analytical skills through rigorous coursework, including:
  - Fundamentals of Analytical Chemistry
  - Organic Reaction Mechanism
  - Polymer Chemistry
  - Electrochemical Thermodynamics
  - Molecular Spectroscopy & Diffraction Methods
  - Introduction to Computational Chemistry
- Students will engage in practical labs, including Inorganic Chemistry Lab and Physical Chemistry Lab, to gain hands-on experience.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates can pursue various roles such as:
- Research Scientists in pharmaceuticals and materials
- Scientific Officers ensuring compliance with regulations
- Consultant Chemists improving product quality
- Research Analysts interpreting complex data
- Quality Managers maintaining process precision
- Educators as Science Teachers
- Academic Research Editors and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs)

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- Applicants must have a minimum of 50% in 10th and 12th grades (with a focus on Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology) and also 50% in their graduation in a relevant subject.

**Application Process:**
- Interested candidates can apply through a structured admission process that includes a personal interview.

**Support Services:**
- The program offers dedicated placement support, assisting students in transitioning into industry roles or further academic pursuits. Graduates benefit from UPES's strong industry connections, which facilitate networking and job placement in renowned organizations.

**Contact Information:**
- For further inquiries, students can reach out via the provided contact options, including email and mobile number sections on the application platform.

In summary, the M.Sc. in Chemistry (Instrumental Chemical Analysis) at UPES equips students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in a variety of scientific fields, contributing to advancements in technology and public health.
