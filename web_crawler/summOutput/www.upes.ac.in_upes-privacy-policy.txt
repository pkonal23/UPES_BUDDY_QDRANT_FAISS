The document outlines the privacy policy of the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), detailing how the institution collects, uses, and manages personal information from users of its services. 

### Key Sections of the Policy:

1. **Information Collection**:
   - UPES gathers personal information such as names, contact details, educational background, and financial information (including bank and passport details) primarily to support its study abroad programs and other services like application processing and student profiles.
   - Non-personal information is also collected, which includes data that does not identify individuals, such as browser type and usage patterns.

2. **Use of Information**:
   - UPES uses collected information to manage its website, fulfill orders, improve services, administer contests, and communicate with users about college offerings and other relevant updates.
   - Users may receive communications via email, phone, SMS, or social media regarding products and services, with the understanding that they consent to such contacts.

3. **User Rights**:
   - Users have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal information stored by UPES. Requests for changes should be sent via email.
   - UPES retains user information as long as accounts are active or as necessary for legal compliance and internal operations.

4. **Disclosure of Information**:
   - UPES may disclose personal information to service providers who assist in operations but does not sell or trade personal information for promotional purposes.
   - Information may also be shared in compliance with legal obligations or to protect UPES's rights and safety.

5. **Data Security**:
   - UPES implements reasonable security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure, although it cannot guarantee complete security due to the nature of internet transmissions.

6. **Children's Privacy**:
   - UPES does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 16, and if such information is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

7. **Changes to the Privacy Policy**:
   - UPES reserves the right to update its privacy policy and will notify users of changes via electronic communication or through updates on its website.

8. **Contact Information**:
   - For questions or concerns regarding the privacy policy, users can contact UPES at **admissions@upes.ac.in**. Additionally, users can opt-out of mailing lists by emailing the same address or using the unsubscribe option in communications.

Overall, UPES emphasizes its commitment to user privacy and encourages users to review its policies regularly to stay informed about how their information is handled.
