Sugandha Sharma, a representative from the School of Computer Science, was honored with the award titled "Iconic Women Creating a Better Place for All" at the Annual Women Economic Forum 2022, which marked its 84th Global Edition. In addition to receiving this accolade, she was also invited to serve as a guest speaker at the event, highlighting her significant contributions and achievements in her field. This recognition underscores her commitment to making a positive impact in society through her work and influence.
