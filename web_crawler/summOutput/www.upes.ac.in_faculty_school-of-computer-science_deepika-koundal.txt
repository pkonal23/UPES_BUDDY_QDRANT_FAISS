Dr. Deepika Koundal is an accomplished academic with over 13 years of teaching and research experience, currently affiliated with the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). She has held significant positions, including Associate Professor at Chitkara University and Assistant Professor at NIT Hamirpur, and has contributed to UIET at Panjab University, Chandigarh. Her teaching career began at Baddi University of Emerging Sciences & Technology and included a role as a Lecturer at NCCE in Israna, Panipat.

### Research Interests and Achievements
Dr. Deepika has an impressive publication record, having authored numerous articles in highly regarded SCI-indexed journals and has been awarded a patent. She has edited seven books published by notable publishers like Elsevier, Springer, IGI, and Wiley. Furthermore, she serves as an Associate Editor for esteemed journals published by IEEE, Elsevier, and Taylor Francis and has held guest editor positions for publications such as *Computers & Electrical Engineering* and *Internet of Things*. Dr. Deepika is active in technical program committees and organizing committees, and she frequently shares her expertise through guest lectures and tutorials at various academic events.

### Teaching and Expertise
Dr. Deepika specializes in subjects including Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing, Digital Forensics, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. Her teaching philosophy emphasizes practical application, critical thinking, and problem-solving, aiming to equip students with the skills needed to thrive in rapidly evolving technological fields. She fosters a participative classroom environment that encourages student engagement through real-life case studies and discussions, focusing on the holistic development of her students.

### Awards and Recognition
Dr. Deepika has received numerous accolades for her contributions to academia, including:
- Honorary membership from the Neutrosophic International Science Association at the University of New Mexico in September 2016.
- IEEE Senior Member status.
- Research Excellence awards from UPES in February 2023 and April 2022.
- Recognition as a BRICS young scientist from NIAS-DST in September 2021.
- Research Excellence Award from Chitkara University in April 2018.
- TEQIP-II Fellowship during her Ph.D. studies from 2012 to 2014.
- Scholarship from the KC Mahindra Trust from 1998 to 2001.

### Academic Background
Dr. Deepika earned her Master's and Ph.D. degrees in Computer Science & Engineering from Panjab University, Chandigarh, in 2015, and holds a B.Tech degree in Computer Science & Engineering from Kurukshetra University, India.

### Scholarly Activities
Her scholarly contributions include over 100 research articles in SCI and Scopus-indexed journals and conferences, along with three authored books. She is involved in editorial roles for several prominent journals and has participated in various technical and organizing committees, showcasing her commitment to advancing her field.

In summary, Dr. Deepika Koundal is a distinguished academic figure dedicated to teaching and research in computer science, with a strong focus on empowering students and contributing to the academic community through extensive research and editorial work.
