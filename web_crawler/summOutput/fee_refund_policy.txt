FEE REFUND POLICY
UPES/Registrar-Order/Academic/2024-25/01
Date: 05.02.2024
Interim Fee Refund Policy for Academic Session 2024-25 as per the UPES policy is
applicable for all the programs running in the University.
Full fee to be refunded if the student is taking withdrawal till 31st July 2024 after
deduction of Rs. 1000/- as administrative charges. The refund may take upto 45 working
days to be processed.
The policy is subject to amendment any time as per the UGC guidelines.
