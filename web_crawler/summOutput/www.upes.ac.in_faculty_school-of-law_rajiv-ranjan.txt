**Profile Summary of Rajiv Ranjan, Assistant Professor of Law**

**Contact Information:**
- Email: rajiv.ranjan@ddn.upes.ac.in

**Current Position:**
Rajiv Ranjan is an Assistant Professor of Law (Senior Scale) at the School of Law, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun. 

**Education:**
- Bachelor’s Degree: School of Law, KIIT Deemed to be University, graduated in 2018.
- Master’s Degree: National Law School of India University, Bangalore.
- Ph.D.: Currently pursuing at the National University of Advanced Legal Studies (NUALS), Kochi.

**Research Interests:**
Rajiv's research focuses on various aspects of law, including:
- Human Rights
- Property Legislation
- Procedural Laws
- Gender Inequality in Prison Health Systems
- Digital Divide and Access to Education

**Recent Projects and Publications:**
1. **Research Proposal:** His project titled “Assessing Incarcerated Women’s Physical and Mental Health Status in India: Correcting Gender Inequality in Prison Health” has received financial support from the National Commission of Women, New Delhi, amounting to Rs. 2,14,000.
   
2. **Publications:**
   - Co-authored a chapter titled "Codification of Emerging Jurisprudence of Digital Monetary Sovereignty and Human Rights from the Intersection of CBDCs and Artificial Intelligence" in the book *Law, Economics and Artificial Intelligence* published by Bloomsbury.
   - Authored an article “Introduction of Advanced Directive/Living Will in India: Analysis of Common Cause (A Regd. Society) vs. Union of India” published in the *Galgotias Journal of Legal Studies* (ISSN: 2321-1997, Volume 4, Issue 1, Jan-Jun 2019).
   - A chapter titled "Will Child Marriage Ever End? A Socio-Legal Examination of Child Marriage, Its Societal Ramifications, and the Erosion of Youth Identity in Indian Society" has been accepted for publication in *Child Protection: Assessing Multi-disciplinary Response Mechanisms in the Indian Perspective* by Routledge Publications.

**Conferences and Presentations:**
- Presented a paper titled “Women and Property in India: An Analysis of How Ownership of Property Transformed Feminine Identity” at the 8th International Conference on Law and Economics, 2022, organized by National Law University, Delhi.
- Presented a paper titled “Pondering the Idea of Bestowing Legal Personhood on Natural Resources: A New Dimension to Sustainable Development” at the International Conference on Climate Justice and Sustainable Environment (ICCJSE) 2023.
- His paper “Insurgencies In Nagaland: A Tainted Effort Towards Solution” has been selected for presentation at the Serious and Organised Crime Conference 2023 organized by the Hume Institute for Postgraduate Studies, Switzerland.

**Previous Experience:**
Before his current role at UPES, Rajiv Ranjan served as:
- Assistant Professor at the School of Law, Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Chennai (2021-2023).
- Assistant Professor at Marwadi University, Rajkot (2020-2021).

**Teaching Philosophy:**
Rajiv Ranjan emphasizes creating a supportive and inclusive learning environment. His approach encourages students to explore and connect with the material actively. He focuses on fostering critical thinking, collaboration, and a growth mindset, viewing education as a dynamic partnership between teacher and student, aimed at instilling lifelong curiosity and a love for learning.

**Scholarly Activities:**
Rajiv has participated in various research projects, notably "Digital Divide and Access to Education," funded by NUALS, which investigates educational disparities in the digital age.

This detailed summary encapsulates Rajiv Ranjan's academic and professional journey, highlighting his contributions to law education and research.
