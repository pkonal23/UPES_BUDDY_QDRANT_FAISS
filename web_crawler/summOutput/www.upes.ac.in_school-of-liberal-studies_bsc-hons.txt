**University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES): Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Liberal Studies Program**

**Introduction**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)'s School of Liberal Studies offers a prestigious Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Honors program in Liberal Studies, providing students with an exceptional foundation in various fields. With three esteemed specializations, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)'s Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Liberal Studies Program has earned a remarkable reputation for its interdisciplinary approach and unwavering commitment to academic excellence.

**Specializations**

The Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Liberal Studies Program presents three distinct specializations for students to pursue:

* Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Honors in Economics with Data Science
* Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Honors in Economics and Finance
* Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Honors in Psychology and Behaviour

**Curriculum**

The School of Liberal Studies at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) has meticulously designed its curriculum to foster interdisciplinary understanding, combining scientific research with liberal arts education. This unique approach empowers students with a holistic perspective and an in-depth comprehension of complex issues.

**Research Focus**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)'s Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Liberal Studies Program places a significant emphasis on nurturing research skills. Students engage in internships, projects, and collaborative endeavors with faculty members, honing their critical thinking, analytical, and problem-solving abilities.

**Industry Alignment**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)'s curriculum aligns seamlessly with industry requirements, ensuring that students acquire the knowledge and skills necessary for success in their future careers. Regular updates keep the program current with the latest trends and advancements.

**Faculty**

The School of Liberal Studies at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) boasts an esteemed faculty of experienced researchers and field experts. They provide expert guidance and mentorship to students, fostering their development as skilled researchers and critical thinkers.

**Career Opportunities**

Graduates of University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)'s Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Liberal Studies Program have a vast array of career opportunities to explore, including:

* Research and Development
* Academia
* Scientific Writing
* Policy-Making
* Further Studies in Medicine, Law, or Business

**Additional Program Information**

* **Admission Eligibility:** XII Std from an IB Board with a minimum score of 24 OR Valid SAT Score OR Minimum 50% marks in class X and XII (any Stream)
* **Fee Structure:** Detailed information on the fee structure is available online.
* **Hostel Facilities:** On-campus hostel accommodations are provided for both male and female students on a first-come, first-serve basis.
