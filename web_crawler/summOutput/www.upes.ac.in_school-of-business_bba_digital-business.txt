The UPES School of Business offers a comprehensive BBA in Digital Business program designed to equip students with essential skills and knowledge for thriving in the rapidly evolving digital landscape. The program spans three years and includes a diverse curriculum that covers core business areas such as marketing, finance, human resources, and operations management, while also providing specialized courses in digital marketing and digital media laws.

### Program Structure and Specializations
- **Semesters Overview**: The program is structured into six semesters, with courses ranging from Digital Marketing and Business Communication to Operations Research and Financial Management. 
- **Specializations**: Students can specialize in two key areas:
  1. **Digital Marketing**: Focused on strategies including search engine optimization, social media marketing, and content creation.
  2. **Digital Media Laws**: Covers intellectual property rights, cyber laws, and ethical practices in the digital landscape.

### Admission and Eligibility
- **Eligibility Criteria**: Applicants must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII.

### Curriculum Highlights
- The curriculum emphasizes practical learning through hands-on projects, case studies, and industry internships, preparing students for real-world challenges.
- Courses include Digital Business, Business Ethics and CSR, Spreadsheet Modelling, and more, ensuring a well-rounded education.

### Industry Exposure and Career Opportunities
- The program incorporates guest lectures, seminars, and networking opportunities with industry professionals, enhancing students' connections and industry knowledge.
- Graduates can pursue various roles such as digital marketing managers, e-commerce specialists, social media strategists, data analysts, and business consultants across diverse industries.

### Future Scope and Industry Trends
- Key trends shaping the future of digital business include:
  - **Digital Marketing Automation**: Utilizing AI and automation tools for campaign effectiveness.
  - **Data-Driven Decision Making**: Leveraging analytics for informed business strategies.
  - **Content Marketing and Storytelling**: Focusing on engaging content creation.
  - **Influencer Marketing**: Collaborating with influencers for brand promotion.
