**Master of Business Administration in Digital Business (Web Design and Development)**

**Program Details**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Business offers a Master of Business Administration in Digital Business (Web Design and Development) to cater to the growing demand for business professionals with a unique amalgamation of cross-functional expertise and digital skills. This program acknowledges the need for individuals who can architect business systems and processes using advanced tools and techniques in the digital era. The recent pandemic has further underscored the importance of innovative business models that minimize manual intervention and enable seamless transactions across the business value chain.

The Master of Business Administration in Digital Business (Web Design and Development) program at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Business is tailored to equip students with specialized expertise in specific domains, encompassing fintech, digital marketing, analytics, and more. The curriculum is designed in collaboration with renowned IT and analytics firms, ensuring its relevance to the industry's current requirements. Students will receive comprehensive training from esteemed faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. Additionally, industry professionals will contribute their insights and practical knowledge, providing students with a holistic learning experience.

**Program Highlights**

* Core courses in finance, marketing, strategy, operations, leadership, and business ethics provide a solid foundation in general business management principles.
* Students undertake a major project (dissertation) to delve deeper into a specific area of interest and showcase their expertise in web design and development.
* Summer internships and live projects with companies offer real-world exposure and opportunities to apply knowledge in finance, marketing, operations, strategy, and leadership.
* The Master of Business Administration in Digital Business program emphasizes the digital transformation of business processes and strategies, covering emerging technologies, digital marketing, e-commerce, data analytics, and the impact of technology on various business functions.
* Elective courses within the Master of Business Administration program allow students to specialize in web design and development, covering topics such as web programming languages, responsive design, UX design, and web project management.
* Students benefit from industry visits, guest lectures, and real-world case studies, while the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Business provides state-of-the-art facilities, including modern smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, a well-stocked library, and advanced analytical tools.

**Future Scope / Industry Trends**

The future scope of a Master of Business Administration in Digital Business with a specialization in Web Design and Development is promising. Businesses are increasingly relying on digital platforms and user-centric web experiences, generating numerous opportunities for professionals with this skill set. In the flourishing e-commerce industry, individuals with a blend of business acumen, digital marketing knowledge, and web design and development skills can contribute to the success of online stores by creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces. The integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) technologies into web design and development processes presents prospects for professionals to create intelligent and dynamic web experiences. Additionally, the growing importance of cybersecurity and data privacy compliance opens avenues for experts in web design and development to implement secure coding practices and privacy measures.

The demand for web design and development services continues to escalate, enabling professionals to work as freelancers or establish their own agencies. Furthermore, diverse industries such as healthcare, finance, and education require specialized web solutions, allowing professionals with expertise in digital business and web design to specialize in developing industry-specific web applications and platforms. With a Master of Business Administration in Digital Business, individuals can combine their management skills with web design and development expertise to establish their own ventures or offer specialized services to clients.

**Career Opportunities**

The Master of Business Administration program in Digital Business, specializing in Web Design and Development at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Business, offers a wide range of career opportunities. Students have the opportunity to explore their career interests, network with prospective employers, and potentially secure job placements. The field of digital business encompasses various industries and functions, providing diverse career prospects. Students can explore job opportunities in business manufacturing, service, and consulting domains, thereby expanding their career options.

The following professional paths in the field of Digital Business are available for students to pursue:

* UI/UX Designer
* Web Developer
* Digital Product Manager
* E-commerce Manager
* Digital Marketing Manager
* Web Project Manager
* Entrepreneur

**Placements**

In recent years, there has been a significant improvement in job placements for students pursuing a Master of Business Administration in Digital Business with a focus on Web Design and Development. Numerous reputable companies such as Flipkart, Reliance Industries, Adani, GMR, Accenture, Deloitte, Xiaomi, TATA Croma, and Fractal Analytics, along with various digital agencies, technology companies, e-commerce companies, marketing agencies, consulting firms, and startups, provide attractive employment opportunities for these graduates. Moreover, there is a growing interest among medium-sized startups in hiring Master of Business Administration in Digital Business postgraduates. The range of job roles available for Master of Business Administration graduates includes Digital Marketing Manager, E-commerce Specialist, E-commerce Manager, Product Manager, Procurement Analyst, and Business Analyst. The starting salary for fresh Master of Business Administration in Digital Business graduates typically falls within the range of INR 4-15 lakh per annum. The salary offered is contingent upon the candidate's skills, knowledge, and experience.

**Eligibility Criteria**

The minimum eligibility criteria for Master of Business Administration in Digital Business (Web Design and Development) to be fulfilled by interested students is as follows:

* Minimum 50% marks in Class X, XII and Graduation.
* Graduation from a recognized University in any stream.

**Selection Criteria**

The selection criteria for students interested in pursuing Master of Business Administration in Digital Business (Web Design and Development) offered by University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is based on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams / CUET followed by a Personal Interview.
