**Seema Shukla: Award-Winning Academic, Filmmaker, and Researcher**

**Profile Summary**

Seema Shukla is an Assistant Professor (SS) at the School of Liberal Studies at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). Seema Shukla's expertise lies in sound design, media content creation, and research. Seema Shukla holds a degree from the Satyajit Ray Film and Television Institute Kolkata and Punjabi University.

**Work Experience**

Prior to Seema Shukla's academic endeavors, Seema Shukla was actively involved in the Bollywood and Tollywood industries. Seema Shukla's diverse roles included sound designer, ADR engineer, scriptwriter, and editor. Seema Shukla also participated in several international projects. Some notable Bollywood projects to Seema Shukla's credit include "Ada," "Dabangg," "All the Best," and "My Name is Khan."

**Film and Documentary Achievements**

Seema Shukla has directed award-winning documentary and sound-designed films that have garnered international recognition at renowned film festivals such as Barcelona, Berlin, and Roma. Seema Shukla's directorial documentary, "Santitaion," received the Best Critique award at the International Documentary Film Festival in 2022. Seema Shukla's short film, "Samlangita," was selected for the International Samskrita Short Film Festival in 2023.

**Research Interests**

Seema Shukla's research interests encompass film, cinema, sound, sound designing, gender, virtual reality, and augmented reality.

**Teaching Philosophy**

Seema Shukla firmly believes in fostering a supportive and inclusive learning environment for Seema Shukla's students. Seema Shukla integrates traditional teaching methods with innovative technologies to enhance student understanding. In practice-based classes, Seema Shukla collaborates with Seema Shukla's students as a teammate, guiding them towards creating impactful content. Critical thinking, problem-solving, and hands-on exercises are integral to Seema Shukla's teaching approach.

**Courses Taught**

Seema Shukla teaches a range of courses, including ideation, script writing, production, audiovisual content creation, sound recording, and designing. Seema Shukla trains students in the art of creating audiovisual content, podcasts, short YouTube videos, and films using smartphone technologies.

**Awards and Recognition**

Seema Shukla's directorial efforts have earned Seema Shukla numerous national and international awards. "Santitaion" and "Samlangita" are testaments to Seema Shukla's filmmaking prowess. Additionally, Seema Shukla has won several research paper awards at international media conferences and published in esteemed journals such as Scopus, ABDC, Springer Nature, and UGC Care.
