The content outlines the educational opportunities and customizable degree options available for students enrolled in the B.Tech. Computer Science program at UPES (University of Petroleum and Energy Studies). Students have the chance to specialize in various cutting-edge fields such as Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), Blockchain Technology, Internet of Things (IoT) and Smart Cities, and Big Data. Additionally, they can pursue minors in diverse subjects including Constitution and Indian Polity, Ethical Hacking, Digital Marketing, Graphic Design, and Robotics.

To enhance professional development, UPES offers a variety of Life Skills courses that cover essential competencies like Creativity, Decision Making, Personal Branding, Leadership, and Teamwork. Among the signature courses available are 'Ethical Leadership in the 21st Century,' 'Environment and Sustainability,' 'Start Your Start-up,' 'Technologies of the Future,' and 'Finding Your Purpose in Life.' These courses aim to develop critical thinking, design thinking, persuasive communication, and effective data handling skills.

UPES promotes a tailored educational experience, allowing students to customize their academic and skill-based learning paths to align with their interests and career aspirations. The university comprises eight specialized schools, including the School of Advanced Engineering, School of Computer Science, School of Law, School of Business, School of Health Sciences, School of Design, School of Modern Media, and School of Liberal Studies. This structure provides students with a vast array of subject combinations, ensuring they acquire both depth and breadth of knowledge.

In addition to core subjects, students can select focused specializations and exploratory subjects from other schools. The curriculum also includes mandatory internships across social sectors, government/public sectors, and industries, which are essential for practical experience and professional readiness.

Overall, UPES empowers students to design their own degree, fostering a well-rounded education that balances knowledge, skills, and human values, while preparing them for the multifaceted demands of the global workplace.
