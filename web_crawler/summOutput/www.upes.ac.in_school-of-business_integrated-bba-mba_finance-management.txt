The Integrated BBA MBA program with a specialization in Finance Management at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to equip students with essential business and finance skills. This program prepares graduates for a wide range of career opportunities within the financial sector, including roles such as financial analyst, investment manager, risk analyst, and corporate finance executive.

### Program Overview
- **Specialization Focus**: The program emphasizes financial analysis, investment strategies, risk assessment, and financial planning. Students gain expertise in financial institutions and markets, behavioral finance, and other relevant areas.
- **Curriculum Structure**: The curriculum spans eight semesters, incorporating courses like Financial Management, Business Accounting, Security Analysis, and Corporate Restructuring. It utilizes diverse teaching methods such as case studies, workshops, and industry expert masterclasses to enhance critical and analytical thinking skills.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates are well-positioned to enter various sectors, including:
- **Corporate Finance Departments**
- **Investment Banks**
- **Financial Consulting Firms**
- **Asset Management Companies**
- **Insurance Companies**
- **Government Organizations**

The employment prospects for Finance Management graduates are robust, with starting salaries ranging from INR 6-30 lakh per annum, depending on individual skills and experience. Notable companies that recruit from this program include Indiabulls, Reliance Industries, HDFC Bank, Accenture, and Deloitte.

### Skill Development
The program focuses on developing critical skills such as:
- Analytical and quantitative analysis
- Financial modeling
- Problem-solving
- Strategic thinking
- Communication and teamwork

Students engage in practical experiences through major projects, summer internships, and live projects, which are crucial for applying theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios.

### Networking and Exposure
UPES provides students with extensive networking opportunities through interactions with industry professionals, alumni, and guest speakers. The institution also facilitates student exchange programs and international collaborations to enhance global exposure.

### Admission Criteria
To apply for the program, candidates must meet the following eligibility criteria:
- A minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII.
- Admission is based on performance in entrance exams such as UPESMET, UGAT, or CUET.
