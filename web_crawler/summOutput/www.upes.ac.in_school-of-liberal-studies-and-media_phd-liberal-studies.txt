The Ph.D. in Liberal Studies and Media program at the School of Liberal Studies and Media, UPES, is a distinctive doctoral program that provides an interdisciplinary approach to rigorous academic study. It encompasses various fields, including Economics, English Literature, History, Politics, and Psychology, and is characterized by its focus on integrating insights from multiple disciplines to tackle complex contemporary issues.

Key Features of the Program:
- **Interdisciplinary Research**: The program encourages students to explore various fields, fostering critical thinking, analytical skills, and incisive writing.
- **Mentorship**: Students receive guidance from distinguished faculty members, enhancing their research experience with insights from leading scholars.
- **Career Opportunities**: Graduates can pursue diverse career paths, including teaching positions in universities worldwide, research-based consultancy roles, and positions in NGOs, think tanks, and public administration. The program prepares students for impactful roles in academia, research, and industry.

The program is structured around seven core problem areas: Citizenship, Education, Environment, Gender, Health, Technology, and Urbanization, which represent pressing global challenges. This thematic framework supports research that transcends traditional academic boundaries and emphasizes a commitment to a just and sustainable future.

The curriculum includes a strong emphasis on empirical and data-driven research methodologies, fostering a scientific approach to problem-solving. Students engage in praxis and action-based research, gaining practical insights that prepare them to address real-world challenges effectively. Opportunities for international exposure through seminars and conferences further enrich the academic experience.

For further inquiries or to apply, prospective students can reach out via email at **phd@upes.ac.in**. The program not only aims to develop scholars who can contribute meaningfully to their fields but also encourages active engagement with the interdisciplinary nature of contemporary issues for positive societal impact.
