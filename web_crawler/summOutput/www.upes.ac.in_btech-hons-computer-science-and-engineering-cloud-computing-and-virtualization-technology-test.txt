**Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, Pune**

**Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering**

**Specialization: Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology**

**Course Overview**

Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, Pune offers a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology. This program is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to succeed in the rapidly growing field of cloud computing.

**Eligibility Criteria**

Candidates seeking admission to the Bachelor of Technology program in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology must have successfully completed their 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized board with a minimum aggregate score of 60%. Additionally, candidates must have secured a valid score in the Maharashtra Common Entrance Test (MHT CET) or the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) Main.

**Course Duration**

The Bachelor of Technology program in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology is a four-year, full-time undergraduate program.

**Course Curriculum**

The course curriculum for the Bachelor of Technology program in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology includes a comprehensive range of subjects, including:

* Data Structures and Algorithms
* Object-Oriented Programming
* Database Management Systems
* Operating Systems
* Computer Networks
* Cloud Computing
* Virtualization Technology
* Big Data Analytics
* Machine Learning
* Artificial Intelligence

**Career Prospects**

Graduates of the Bachelor of Technology program in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology will have a wide range of career opportunities in the IT industry. Potential job roles include:

* Cloud Engineer
* Virtualization Specialist
* Data Scientist
* Software Developer
* DevOps Engineer
