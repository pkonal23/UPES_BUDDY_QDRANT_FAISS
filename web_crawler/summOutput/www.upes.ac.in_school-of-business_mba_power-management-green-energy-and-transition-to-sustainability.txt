The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and Transition to Sustainability) offered by UPES School of Business is a specialized two-year program designed for students aiming to excel in leadership roles within the power and renewable energy sectors. 

### Eligibility Requirements:
To qualify for the program, applicants must meet the following criteria:
- Minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation.
- Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.

### Selection Criteria:
Candidates are selected based on their performance in:
- UPES Management Entrance Test (UPESMET) or any National Level Exams.
- Personal Interview.

Students with a minimum eligible score in MAT/CMAT (70 percentile), CAT (50 percentile), XAT (50 percentile), GMAT (400 or above), or NMAT (120 or above) may be exempt from the entrance test and will directly proceed to the Group Discussion and Interview stage.

### Program Structure:
The program includes a comprehensive curriculum that integrates core business principles with specialized knowledge in power management. Key features include:
- **Hands-on Learning**: Summer internships, live projects, and industrial tours to provide practical experience.
- **Major Project (Dissertation)**: Students apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios.
- **Industry Engagement**: Sessions with industry experts, case studies, and guest lectures to enhance learning.

### Curriculum Highlights:
Key subjects covered in the program include:
- Sources of Energy
- Business Communication
- Operations Management
- Financial Management
- Power Pricing & Power Trading
- Energy Storage & Management
- Sustainability frameworks and corporate social responsibility.

### Future Scope / Industry Trends:
The MBA program prepares graduates for mid and senior management roles in the power and green energy industries, both in India and globally. The industry is currently facing a shortage of skilled professionals, and the program aims to fill this gap. Graduates can expect to work in various capacities, such as:
- Power and Green Energy Regulatory and Financial Consultant
- Energy Efficiency Consultant
- Sustainability Consultant
- Business Analyst

### Placement Success:
The program has a strong placement record, with graduates securing positions in prestigious organizations such as Accenture, Infosys, Wipro, Ernst and Young, PWC, Deloitte, and Adani Power. Starting salaries range from INR 7-30 lakh per annum, reflecting the expertise and exposure of the graduates.

### Facilities and Learning Environment:
UPES provides state-of-the-art facilities, including modern smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, and advanced analytical tools. This environment is conducive to learning and prepares students for the evolving landscape of sustainable energy and digital advancements.
