The BBA in Foreign Trade (Analytics and Market Research) offered by UPES School of Business is a comprehensive program designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the global business landscape. This program emphasizes practical experience through major projects, summer internships, and live collaborations with companies, allowing students to apply analytics and market research concepts in real-world situations.

### Curriculum Highlights
- The curriculum includes a mix of core subjects and specialized courses such as:
  - Business Analytics
  - Global Sourcing
  - Trade Negotiations
  - Market Research Methodologies
  - Statistical Analysis and Data Visualization
- Students engage in real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts, enhancing their understanding of international trade documentation, procedures, and regulations.
- The program promotes global perspectives through exchange programs, research projects, seminars, and internships.

### Future Scope and Industry Trends
Graduates specializing in Analytics and Market Research are expected to play a vital role in shaping global trade dynamics, with increasing demand from government agencies, international organizations, and businesses. The specialization is particularly relevant in the context of regional trade blocs like the EU, ASEAN, and AfCFTA, preparing graduates for careers that require adept navigation of global markets through data-driven insights.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates can expect to pursue various roles, including:
- International Business Analyst
- Market Research Analyst
- Trade Compliance Specialist
- Export-Import Manager
- Business Intelligence Analyst
- International Sales and Marketing Manager

The program's emphasis on analytics and market research positions graduates favorably in both public and private sectors, with starting salaries typically ranging from INR 6-10 lakh per annum, dependent on individual skills and market conditions.

### Admission Process
To apply for the BBA in Foreign Trade (Analytics and Market Research), candidates must meet the following eligibility criteria:
- A minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII.
- Selection is based on performance in entrance exams such as UPESMET-UG, UGAT, or CUET.

### Facilities and Resources
UPES School of Business provides modern amenities, including smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and advanced analytical tools, creating a conducive learning environment for students.
