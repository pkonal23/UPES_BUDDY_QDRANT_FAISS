The MBA program in International Business offered by the UPES School of Business is designed to equip students with essential knowledge and skills for success in the global business environment. The curriculum is comprehensive, covering a wide array of subjects, including International Trade Documentation, International Business Laws, Operations Management, and Financial Management. It emphasizes practical experience through simulations, industry visits, and guest lectures from professionals, preparing students to navigate the complexities of international trade and management.

**Key Features of the Program:**
- **Core Subjects:** The program spans multiple semesters, with courses such as Accounting for Managers, Business Ethics and CSR, International Finance Management, and Strategic Management. 
- **Skill Development:** Students will develop critical skills such as negotiation, leadership, problem-solving, and cultural adaptability, which are crucial for making informed decisions in a global context.
- **Hands-On Experience:** The program incorporates hands-on projects and major dissertations, allowing students to engage with real-world business challenges. 
- **Technological Proficiency:** Students gain experience with advanced software and analytics tools like Power-BI, Gretl, and Excel, enhancing their analytical capabilities.

**Admission Process:**
To be eligible for the program, candidates must have:
- A minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.
- A valid MAT/CMAT score of 70 percentile or above can exempt students from the UPES Management Entrance Test, while scores from CAT, XAT, GMAT, and NMAT are also accepted.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates can pursue careers in various sectors, including manufacturing, consulting, and global organizations. The program prepares students to take on roles in multinational corporations and entrepreneurial ventures, addressing the increasing demand for professionals skilled in international business due to globalization, digital transformation, and sustainability trends.

**Contact Information:**
For more detailed information regarding the fee structure and application process, prospective students are encouraged to reach out to the UPES School of Business directly.

In conclusion, the MBA in International Business at UPES School of Business offers a robust foundation for students aiming to excel in the dynamic field of international business, with a strong emphasis on practical skills, industry connections, and global perspectives.
