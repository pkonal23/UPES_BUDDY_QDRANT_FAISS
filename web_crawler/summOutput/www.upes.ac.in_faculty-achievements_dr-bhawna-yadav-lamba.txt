Dr. Bhawna Yadav Lamba, a Professor at the UPES School of Advanced Engineering, has been awarded the prestigious S. R. Bhatnagar Memorial Research Award for the year 2023-24. This award recognizes her excellence in research within the fields of tribology, lubricants, and related disciplines. Alongside a citation, Dr. Lamba received a cash prize of INR 15,000. The award ceremony took place during the 77th & 78th Annual Convention of the Oil Technologists Association of India and the International Conference on "Application of Oils and Fats in FMCG Sector," held on March 15-16, 2024, at the National Agricultural Sciences Complex.
