**Manorma Bhandari**

**Academic Accomplishments:**

Ms. Manorma Bhandari, a Ph.D. Scholar at the Department of Pharmaceutical Sciences, has achieved recognition for her research endeavors. At the International Conference on Nanopharmaceuticals and Neurological Disorders (ICNAND2023), held from February 2-4, 2023, at Indira Gandhi National Tribal University (IGNTU) in Amarkantak, Madhya Pradesh, India, Ms. Bhandari received the 2nd Prize for Best E-Poster Presentation.

Ms. Manorma Bhandari's presentation, titled "Identifying the potential natural compounds against mGluR5 using in silico approach," showcased her research on the exploration of potential herbal medicines for the treatment of autism, with a focus on targeting synaptogenic pathways. Her research interests encompass in silico drug discovery, natural compound isolation, identification from medicinal plants, and in vitro and in vivo screening.
