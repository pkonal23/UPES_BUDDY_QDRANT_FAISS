Dr. Suvendu Manna, along with colleagues Amit Kumar Sharma and Ravi Kumar Patel, has achieved a significant milestone in the field of water purification by securing an Indian Patent (number 412047) in 2022. The patent pertains to a novel process of water filtration that utilizes nano adsorbents incorporated into porous clay pot filters. This innovative approach aims to enhance the efficiency of water filtration, addressing critical sustainability challenges.

For further information or inquiries, Dr. Suvendu Manna is associated with the School of Advanced Engineering, where he focuses on sustainability-related research and projects. The eligibility criteria for related programs or initiatives may be available through the institution's official channels.
