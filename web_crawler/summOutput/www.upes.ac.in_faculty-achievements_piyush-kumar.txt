Dr. Piyush Kumar, an Assistant Professor in the Selection Grade at the School of Health Sciences & Technology (SOHST), has achieved a significant milestone by publishing a book titled "Oxides for Medical Applications," which he edited for Elsevier. This publication delves into a variety of oxide materials that possess optical, magnetic, and electronic properties, specifically focusing on their applications in the biomedical field. 

The book is available for purchase at the following link: [Oxides for Medical Applications](https://shop.elsevier.com/books/oxides-for-medical-applications/kumar/978-0-323-90538-1). 

This achievement showcases Dr. Kumar's contributions to the field and highlights the importance of oxide materials in medical technologies.
