Professor Gautam R. Desiraju from the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, has been honored with the Van der Waals Award in the Senior Scientist category during the 3rd International Conference on Noncovalent Interactions (ICNI-III), which took place in Belgrade, Serbia, from June 17th to 21st, 2024. This prestigious award is a recognition of his significant contributions to the fields of crystal engineering and noncovalent interactions, which are vital components of molecular chemistry. Professor Desiraju's research has notably advanced the understanding of intermolecular forces, with a particular focus on hydrogen bonding, underscoring his influential role and leadership in the scientific community.
