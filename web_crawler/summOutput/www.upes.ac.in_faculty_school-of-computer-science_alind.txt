Alind is an Assistant Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, where he has been employed following his post-graduation. He holds an M.Tech degree from the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, obtained in 2015, and a B.Tech degree from Uttar Pradesh Technical University, Lucknow, awarded in 2012. Currently, he is pursuing a PhD at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, Dehradun. With over five years of experience in academics and research, Alind specializes in the fields of Optimization, Game Design, and Development.

His research interests extend beyond traditional academic pursuits; Alind is also heavily invested in intellectual property protection. He has applied for patents for his innovative inventions and technologies, demonstrating his commitment to developing novel solutions and ensuring their exclusivity for potential commercialization. Additionally, he actively pursues copyrights for his creative works, including software, designs, and written materials, highlighting his dedication to originality and intellectual integrity.

In his teaching role, Alind emphasizes Software Engineering and Application Development, particularly in courses such as Introduction to Game Programming and Advanced Game Programming Algorithms. He adopts a hands-on learning approach, guiding students through practical projects that allow them to apply their knowledge and develop real-world software applications. His teaching philosophy is centered on delivering tangible outcomes, encouraging students to engage actively in their learning while fostering a strong connection between theoretical knowledge and practical application. This methodology not only enhances students' understanding of software development concepts but also equips them with essential technical skills and real-world experience necessary for their future careers.

Alind has made significant contributions to his field through numerous research publications in esteemed journals and conference proceedings. These works reflect his ability to critically analyze complex problems, propose innovative solutions, and effectively communicate his findings within the academic community.
