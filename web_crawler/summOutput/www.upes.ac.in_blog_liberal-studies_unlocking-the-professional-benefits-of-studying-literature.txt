2. **Effective Communication**: Clear communication skills are necessary for negotiating and collaborating with clients and colleagues, as well as for presenting arguments persuasively in court.
3. **Adaptability**: As AI and ML technologies transform the legal profession, lawyers must adapt to new tools and methodologies to remain relevant and effective.
4. **Creative Problem Solving**: Lawyers should be able to think outside the box to develop innovative solutions to legal challenges.
5. **Client-Centric Approach**: Understanding and addressing client needs is fundamental for building successful attorney-client relationships.
