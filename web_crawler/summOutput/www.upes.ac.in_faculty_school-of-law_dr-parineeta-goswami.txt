Dr. Parineeta Goswami is an accomplished Assistant Professor at the School of Law, UPES, with a rich background in various legal subjects. Her teaching repertoire includes Insolvency and Bankruptcy Laws, Banking Laws & Negotiable Instruments, Company Law II, Cyber Law, Corporate Governance, Family Law I, and Law of Contract I & II. Additionally, she possesses expertise in Financial Institutions & Banking Law, Clinical Legal Education (covering Alternative Dispute Resolution, Drafting, Pleading, and Conveyancing, Professional Ethics & Professional Accounting Systems), Financial Market Regulations, and Law of Torts.

Dr. Goswami's research interests are focused on Corporate Law, the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, Banking & Insurance Law, Arbitration, and Contract Law. She is an active member of several legal associations, including IIER, GALTER, SASCV, LDRN, and JustAI. Furthermore, she contributes to the academic community by serving on the advisory and editorial boards of KnowLaw and as a peer reviewer for the Law and Safety Journal. Her leadership is evident through her role as chair of various national legal events and as a coordinator for several university committees.

She has authored influential works, such as "Legal Aspects of Recovery and Reduction of Non-Performing Assets in Banking Industries" and "Emergency Arbitration Procedures," and has published numerous research papers in Scopus and UGC CARE indexed journals. Dr. Goswami is frequently invited to be a resource person at national and international conferences, where she presents her research findings.

With over five years of teaching and research experience and two years in industry, Dr. Goswami has previously held positions as an Assistant Professor at Manipal University and SRM University, as well as serving as a Senior and Junior Research Fellow at Rajiv Gandhi National University of Law. She has also contributed to education through video lectures for the SWAYAM Prabha DTH Channel, focusing on topics such as "Inter Parental Child Removal: Challenges" and "Issues Relating to NRI Marriages in India - Need for Legislative Reforms."

Dr. Goswami holds a doctorate from Rajiv Gandhi National Law University, Punjab, and has a commendable academic record, including a 9.83 CGPA in her Ph.D. coursework. She has received various accolades, including a Certificate of Merit for a course on the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, the Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) from UGC, and a silver medal for her LL.M. from Amity University, where she also received the Shri Baljit Shastri Award. Her academic journey began with a B.Com from the International College for Girls, Jaipur, followed by an LL.B. from the University of Rajasthan.
