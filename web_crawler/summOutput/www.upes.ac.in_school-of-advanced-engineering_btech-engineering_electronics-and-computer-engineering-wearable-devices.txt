**B.Tech. Electronics & Computer Engineering (Wearable Devices)**

The B.Tech. Electronics & Computer Engineering (Wearable Devices) program at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies School of Advanced Engineering is designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of the burgeoning field of wearable technologies. The curriculum delves into the design and development of innovative wearable devices, integrating electronics and computer engineering principles.

**Program Details**

* Students explore sensor technology, gaining expertise in gathering data from the human body.
* Students acquire proficiency in wireless communication protocols for establishing efficient connections between wearable devices and external systems.
* The program emphasizes embedded systems, training students in programming and optimizing hardware and software components for optimal device performance.
* Human-computer interaction is a key focus, enabling students to create user-friendly wearable devices that cater to end-user needs.

**Program Highlights**

* Specialization in wearable devices, empowering students to lead the revolution in wearable technology and IoT advancement.
* Advanced courses on microcontrollers, sensors, and ubiquitous computing equip students with technical expertise for wearable device design and development.
* Real-time operating systems courses provide hands-on experience in building responsive wearable technology for diverse user requirements.
* The program contributes to the expanding IoT ecosystem, fostering wearable device development that transforms industries and enhances user experiences.
* Practical learning through real-world wearable technology projects prepares students to address real-world challenges and drive innovation.

**Future Scope and Industry Trends**

* The program aligns with the growing demand for wearable technology experts in the healthcare industry.
* Graduates can pursue advanced studies in biomedical engineering through master's and PhD programs.
* Key industry players like GE Healthcare, GeBBS Healthcare Solutions, and Apple are prominent recruiters in this specialization.

**Career Opportunities**

* Wearable device research and development
* Product design
* Embedded systems engineering
* Healthcare technology sectors

**Placements**

* Exceptional placements open doors to promising career opportunities.
* Industry-aligned curriculum equips students with cutting-edge knowledge and practical skills.
* Graduates are highly sought after by top organizations in the wearable device industry and related sectors.

**Eligibility**

* Minimum 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with 50% in PCM (Physics/Chemistry and Mathematics) in Class XII.

**Selection Criteria**

* University of Petroleum and Energy Studies Engineering Aptitude Test (UPESEAT) / Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) / Board Merit / SAT/ Common UPES Engineering Admission Test (CUET) performance
