The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Digital Business with a specialization in Technology Infrastructure Management is a forward-thinking program designed to equip graduates for a variety of dynamic career opportunities in the rapidly evolving technological landscape. This program emphasizes the importance of staying current with emerging technologies and industry trends to maximize career potential.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates can pursue various roles, including but not limited to:
- IT Manager
- Cybersecurity Analyst
- Network Administrator
- Technology Consultant
- IT Governance Specialist
- Systems Analyst
- IT Compliance Officer
- Cloud Security Specialist
- Digital Marketing Technologist
- Digital Business Strategist
- Sustainability Technology Analyst
- Business Analyst in Digital Transformation

**Program Structure:**
The curriculum is designed to blend essential business principles with specialized courses in technology infrastructure. Key areas of focus include:
- Principles of Management
- Cloud Computing and Virtualization
- Network Management
- Cybersecurity in Business
- IT Governance and Compliance

The program spans six semesters, with courses such as:
- Business Communication
- Business Analytics
- Environmental Sustainability & Climate Change
- Digital Business
- Operations Research
- Research Methodology & Report

Students are encouraged to engage in hands-on experiences through major projects, summer internships, and live projects with companies, which provide real-world exposure and practical insights.

**Global Perspective:**
The program offers opportunities for student exchange, study abroad, and collaboration with leading global partner management institutes, enhancing the educational experience with a global context.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
To be eligible for the program, students must achieve a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII. Admission is based on performance in the Entrance Exam (UPESMET-UG) / UGAT / CUET.

**Facilities and Support:**
The UPES School of Business provides state-of-the-art facilities, including modern smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, and a comprehensive library, all aimed at fostering an environment conducive to academic excellence.

**Conclusion:**
The BBA in Digital Business with a specialization in Technology Infrastructure Management prepares graduates to become leaders in the digital landscape. By integrating technology into business operations and focusing on cybersecurity and data management, graduates are well-positioned to guide organizations through digital transformations and contribute to their success in a technology-driven world.

For further inquiries or to apply, interested individuals can contact UPES School of Business directly.
