The content outlines a comprehensive B.Tech program in Aerospace Engineering with a specialization in Avionics, emphasizing the importance of leadership and teamwork in the field. The program prepares students for a variety of career paths in aviation, aerospace, and related industries, including roles such as Spacecraft Systems Engineer, Propulsion Engineer, and Avionics Engineer. 

### Program Overview:
- **Specializations**: The curriculum covers various aspects of aerospace engineering, including flight mechanics, aerodynamics, propulsion systems, and avionics, with opportunities for specialization in areas like space systems engineering and unmanned aerial systems.
- **Hands-On Experience**: Students engage in laboratory exercises such as wind tunnel testing, flight simulations, and structural analysis, allowing them to apply theoretical concepts in practical situations.
- **Industry Trends**: The demand for avionics engineers is increasing due to advancements in unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and the automotive industry, highlighting the need for advanced navigation and communication systems.

### Academic Structure:
- **Semesters**: The program spans eight semesters, with courses including:
  - Semester 1: Introduction to Aerospace Engineering, Basic Electrical and Electronics, Critical Thinking and Writing.
  - Semester 2: Heat Transfer for Aerospace, Mechanics of Materials, Environmental Studies.
  - Semester 3: Aerodynamics I, Aircraft Systems and Instruments.
  - Semester 4: Propulsion I, Aircraft Structures-I.
  - Semester 5: Aircraft Systems and Fabrication, Aircraft Materials and Processes.
  - Semester 6: Propulsion II, Structural Engineer.
  - Semester 7: Flight Mechanics-I, Aircraft Structures-II.
  - Semester 8: Capstone projects and electives.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates can pursue various roles in the aerospace industry, including:
- Aircraft and spacecraft design
- Avionics systems engineering
- Research and development in defense and space technology
- Positions in government agencies and private aerospace companies.

### Placement Statistics:
The program boasts a 100% placement record over the past five years, with notable recruiters such as Airbus, Boeing India, and TATA Advanced Systems. The highest package offered was Rs. 10 lakhs per annum, with an average package of Rs. 4.82 lakhs per annum.

### Eligibility Criteria:
Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII. Selection is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.
