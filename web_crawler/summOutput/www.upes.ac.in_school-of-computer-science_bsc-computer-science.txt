The B.Sc. program at UPES School of Computer Science is designed to provide students with a robust foundation in computer science principles and practical skills, preparing them for a dynamic career in technology. This comprehensive undergraduate program covers a wide array of subjects including programming languages, data structures, algorithms, database management, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, and more. 

**Program Structure and Curriculum:**
The curriculum is tailored to meet the growing demand for skilled professionals in the tech industry, with a strong emphasis on hands-on learning through laboratory sessions, industry projects, and internships. Students develop critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and innovative approaches to real-world challenges. The program includes specialized courses and practical experience in various fields such as software development, data analysis, and cybersecurity.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
To enroll in the B.Sc. program, candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in both class X and XII, with Mathematics, Computer Science, or Information Technology as a core subject in class XII.

**Specializations:**
Students can choose from several specializations within the B.Sc. program, including:
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Cyber Security and Digital Forensics

These specializations are designed to equip students with focused expertise in emerging fields, enhancing their career prospects.

**Career Opportunities and Salary:**
Graduates of the program are well-prepared for various roles in the technology sector such as software development, data analysis, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence. The average salary after completing the B.Sc. program can vary significantly based on factors like job role, industry, location, and individual performance.

**Industry Connections and Placement:**
UPES maintains strong industry collaborations that facilitate internships, industry visits, and placement opportunities. The dedicated placement cell at UPES provides training and career guidance, resulting in a high placement record for graduates. The program is designed in consultation with leading tech companies to ensure it meets industry standards and demands.

**Hostel Facilities:**
The university offers hostel facilities for both boys and girls on a first-come, first-serve basis, with accommodations provided on a triple-sharing basis.

**Contact Information:**
For more detailed information regarding the B.Sc. program, including the fee structure and scholarship opportunities, prospective students are encouraged to visit the UPES website or contact the admissions office directly.

**Application Deadline:**
The last date to apply for the program is February 28, 2025.

Overall, the B.Sc. program at UPES School of Computer Science promises a holistic educational experience, empowering students to excel in the dynamic world of computer science and shape the future of technology.
