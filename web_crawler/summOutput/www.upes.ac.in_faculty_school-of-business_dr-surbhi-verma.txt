**Profile Summary of Dr. Surbhi Verma**

Dr. Surbhi Verma is an accomplished academic currently serving as an Assistant Professor - Senior Scale at the School of Business, UPES. Her teaching philosophy is centered on fostering an engaging and interactive learning environment that promotes student participation through open discussions and critical thinking. She effectively employs the case study method to bridge theoretical concepts with real-world applications, utilizing multimedia tools and collaborative learning techniques to create a dynamic classroom experience.

**Teaching Experience**
Surbhi Verma teaches various finance-related courses, including Financial Management, Corporate Finance, Portfolio Management, and International Financial Management. Her teaching methodology integrates lectures, active participation, brainstorming, problem-solving, and case studies, ensuring that her students are well-prepared for both academic and professional success. Prior to her current role at UPES, she was a Lecturer at Guru Gobind Singh College of Commerce (University of Delhi) and served as a Teaching Assistant for MBA students during her Ph.D. studies at IIT Roorkee.

**Publications and Conferences**
Dr. Verma has published articles in respected ABDC-indexed journals, including the Australasian Accounting, Business and Finance Journal and the International Journal of Accounting and Finance. She has participated in several significant international conferences, such as the 4th Annual International Capital Markets Conference at NISM Mumbai, the International Conference on Sustainable Goals at IIM Bodh Gaya, and the International Finance and Accounting Conference at IIM Jammu. Additionally, she has worked on a project with IFKON-STARBAG, further enhancing her practical experience in the finance sector.

**Awards and Grants**
Dr. Verma is a recipient of the University Grants Commission (UGC) Research Fellowship, which supports her Ph.D. studies. She also received a grant from the National Institute of Securities Markets (NISM) to present her paper at the 4th Annual International Capital Markets Conference in Mumbai in 2023.
