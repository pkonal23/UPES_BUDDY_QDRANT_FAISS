The Ph.D. in Communication program at the UPES School of Liberal Studies aims to develop a vibrant community of scholars dedicated to advancing knowledge in communication and media studies. This program is designed to prepare doctoral candidates to become thought leaders and experts in their fields, contributing to the academic community and industry.

Key Dates for the Ph.D. Program:
- **Application Deadline:** August 9, 2024
- **Interview Dates:** July 18, 19, & 22, 2024
- **RET Result Declaration:** July 30, 2024
- **Future Scope/Industry Trends Interview Result Declaration:** August 8, 2024
- **Last Date to Pay Fee:** August 4, 2024
- **Commencement of Session:** June 10, 2024
- **Orientation Date:** June 26, 2024
- **Submission of Research Proposal:** July 3, 2024

The program offers both full-time and part-time enrollment options, allowing scholars to align their academic pursuits with professional commitments. Doctoral candidates will gain valuable research experience in humanities, social sciences, and media studies, equipping them to tackle contemporary challenges in these evolving fields. Graduates will have diverse career opportunities in higher education, media research, policy analysis, and the not-for-profit sector, among others.

The curriculum emphasizes rigorous research and practical applications, fostering critical thinking skills essential for navigating the complexities of communication and media studies. Scholars will engage deeply with relevant literature and theories, participate in academic debates, and produce peer-reviewed articles, enhancing both their personal academic growth and the reputation of UPES as a research institution.

Financial support is available for full-time scholars, who receive a monthly stipend of Rs. 30,000. The program also provides access to contingency and travel funds for conducting research and presenting findings at conferences.

For more information, prospective applicants can reach out via email at **phd@upes.ac.in**. The program invites scholars to apply and join a community that is committed to pushing the boundaries of knowledge in communication and media studies.
