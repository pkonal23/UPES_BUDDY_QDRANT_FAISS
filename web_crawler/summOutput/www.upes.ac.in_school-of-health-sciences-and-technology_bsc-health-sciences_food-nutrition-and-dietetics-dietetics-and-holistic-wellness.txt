**Program: Bachelor of Science in Food Nutrition & Dietetics (Dietetics & Holistic Wellness)**

**Offered by:** School of Health Sciences and Technology, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)

**Duration:** Undergraduate program

**Focus:**

- Comprehensive understanding of nutrition and dietetics
- Holistic wellness, encompassing physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual aspects of health

**Highlights:**

- Extensive laboratory sessions, workshops, and industry internships for practical application of knowledge
- Cultivates essential skills such as communication, critical thinking, and teamwork
- Partnerships with renowned institutions for global exposure and opportunities
- Collaboration with organizations like UN World Food Program, All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) Rishikesh, and Zeon Life Sciences Ltd. for cutting-edge practices

**Future Scope:**

- Promising career prospects in food nutrition, dietetics, and holistic wellness
- Growing demand for personalized nutrition, plant-based diets, and integrative nutrition approaches
- Increasing emphasis on technology, nutrition tracking, and well-being
- Connection between nutrition and mental health is gaining attention
- Rising demand for holistic practices in corporate wellness and healthcare settings

**Career Opportunities:**

- Clinical dietitians
- Nutrition consultants
- Health and wellness coaches
- Food researchers
- Public health nutritionists
- Corporate wellness advisors
- Private nutrition consultancy firms
- Wellness ventures

**Placements:**

- Dedicated placement cell facilitates numerous job prospects
- Graduates are equipped with practical skills and in-depth knowledge
- Employment opportunities in hospitals, healthcare facilities, wellness centers, corporate organizations, and research institutions

**Admissions:**

**Eligibility:**

- Minimum 50% Marks at X & XII with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Math, and English as Major Subject in XII

**Selection Criteria:**

- Personal Interview
