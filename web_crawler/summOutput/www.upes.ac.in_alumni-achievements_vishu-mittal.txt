Mr. Vishu Mittal, a distinguished alumnus of the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), graduated in 2013 with a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) degree, specializing in Electronics Engineering. In 2012, he was selected for the prestigious MITCAS global link program, which facilitated his internship at École Polytechnique de Montréal in Canada. His academic excellence was recognized when he was awarded the University Silver Medal in 2013. Following his undergraduate studies, Mr. Mittal pursued a Master of Science (M.S.) at the Technical University of Munich in Germany.

Currently, Mr. Mittal is employed as a Senior GNSS Research and Development Engineer at Trimble, where he continues to contribute to advancements in the field. His journey exemplifies the achievements of UPES alumni and highlights the opportunities available through international programs and collaborations.
