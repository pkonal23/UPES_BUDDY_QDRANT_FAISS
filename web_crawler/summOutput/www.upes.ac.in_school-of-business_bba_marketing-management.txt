The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a comprehensive undergraduate program in BBA Marketing Management, designed to provide students with a robust foundation in marketing principles and practices over a three-year curriculum. The program encompasses a diverse range of subjects, including market research, consumer behavior, product development, pricing, promotion, and distribution, ensuring students gain a holistic understanding of the marketing field.

### Program Structure
- **Duration**: 3 years
- **Core Courses**: Include Business Communication, Business Ethics and CSR, Consumer Behavior, Marketing Research, Financial Management, and Operations Management among others.
- **Capstone Course**: Allows students to apply their knowledge to real-world marketing challenges, enhancing critical thinking and analytical skills.
- **Practical Exposure**: Students benefit from industry visits, guest lectures, and case studies, providing valuable insights into current marketing practices.

### Future Scope and Industry Trends
The marketing sector is rapidly evolving, with significant employment opportunities emerging from renowned companies such as LG, Reliance Industries, Havells, Asian Paints, Vodafone, Deloitte, and Amazon. Graduates can pursue various roles, including:
- Brand Manager
- Product Manager
- Social Media Manager
- Marketing and Sales Manager

The program emphasizes the importance of adapting to industry trends such as digital marketing, data analytics, customer experience, and sustainability, preparing graduates for successful careers in diverse sectors including consumer goods, retail, technology, healthcare, and advertising. 

### Career Opportunities
Graduates can explore careers in:
- Marketing Agencies
- Consulting Firms
- Entrepreneurship
- Government and Education Sectors

Starting salaries for BBA Marketing graduates range from INR 6-30 lakh per annum, influenced by individual skills and market demand.

### Global Opportunities
UPES provides unmatched global exposure through semester abroad programs at prestigious institutions, student exchanges, and extensive placement assistance lasting up to five years.

### Admission Process
To apply for the BBA in Marketing Management, candidates must meet the following eligibility criteria: 
- A minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII.
- Admission is based on performance in UPESMET-UG, UGAT'25, Board Exams, or CUET.
