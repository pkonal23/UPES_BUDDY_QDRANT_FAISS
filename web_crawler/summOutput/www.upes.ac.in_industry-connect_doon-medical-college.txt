The UPES School of Health Sciences and Technology has established a partnership with Doon Medical College to enhance collaboration in various academic and research initiatives. This collaboration includes conducting joint research projects, facilitating the exchange of students and visiting research scholars, and sharing academic information and resources. Additionally, both institutions will organize joint conferences and provide internship opportunities for students.

Doon Medical College, located in Dehradun, is recognized as a center of excellence for medical training and patient care, with a commitment to maintaining the highest standards in training, education, research, and patient care.
