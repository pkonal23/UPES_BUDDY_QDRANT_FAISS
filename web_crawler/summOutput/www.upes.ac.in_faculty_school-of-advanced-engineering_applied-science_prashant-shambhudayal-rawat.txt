**Dr. Prashant Rawat - Profile Summary**

**Contact Information:**
- Email: psrawat@ddn.upes.ac.in

**Research Interests:**
Dr. Prashant Rawat specializes in experimental atomic, molecular, and optical physics (AMOP), focusing on electron and photon interactions with matter. His expertise includes intense radiation-matter interactions in gas phases, utilizing petawatt and femtosecond lasers, as well as electron interactions with atoms and molecules at low (0 â€“ 20 eV) and intermediate (up to 1 KeV) energies. He employs various techniques such as spectroscopy, photoelectron spectroscopy, and mass spectroscopy in his research. Recently, he has also engaged in safety studies related to nuclear instrumentation and installations.

**Academic and Professional Experience:**
Dr. Rawat has nearly two decades of research experience at prestigious institutions both in India and abroad. He earned his Ph.D. from the Physical Research Laboratory (PRL) in Ahmedabad and has held postdoctoral positions at PRL, the Institute for Plasma Research (IPR) in Gandhinagar, and the Federal University of Sao Carlos (UFSCAR) in Brazil. He also served as a Visiting Fellow at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) in Mumbai and worked as a Research Professor at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) in South Korea for nearly three years before joining the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in March 2010.

At UPES, Dr. Rawat has taught a variety of courses, including Physics, Astronomy, Astrophysics, Nuclear Power Generation, Nuclear Physics, Radiation Detection and Measurement, and Philosophy of Science. He has played a significant role in guiding Ph.D. scholars and fostering academic collaborations with institutions like ARIES. His administrative roles at UPES include serving as the Head of the Department of Nuclear Science and Technology from July 2013 to July 2018, Proctor at the College of Advanced Engineering from July 2011 to June 2013, Director of Student Affairs from July to October 2017, and Associate Dean (Innovation) from October 2017 to October 2018.

**Awards and Grants:**
Dr. Rawat's research contributions have been recognized through several awards and grants. He secured a sponsored grant from BRNS, DAE (GOI) for developing detectors for the ADITYA-U tokamak at IPR, Gujarat. He has also received prestigious Research Scholarships during his Ph.D. at PRL, distinguished Post-doctoral Fellowships at PRL and IPR, and the esteemed FAPESP fellowship during his postdoctoral work in Brazil. His leadership at UPES was acknowledged with the Award of Merit for Excellence on two occasions.

**Teaching Philosophy:**
Dr. Rawat advocates for a learner-centric educational approach, aiming to stimulate critical thinking among students while creating a conducive learning environment. He emphasizes the integration of technology to enhance the educational experience, making learning both enjoyable and effective.

**Outreach and Community Involvement:**
In addition to his academic responsibilities, Dr. Rawat actively participates in outreach activities aimed at engaging students from the local community and across India. His commitment to the field is further exemplified by his life membership in the Indian Physics Association.
