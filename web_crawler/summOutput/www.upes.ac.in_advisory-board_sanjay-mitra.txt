Sanjay Mitra is a distinguished academic and former civil servant with a notable career in the Indian Administrative Service (IAS), where he served in West Bengal, having joined the service in 1982. He held several significant positions, including Chief Secretary, Joint Secretary to the Prime Minister, Secretary of Road Transport and Highways, and Defence Secretary, before retiring in 2019.

Currently, he is a Professor of Practice at the School of Public Policy at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi. In addition to his academic role, he is actively involved in various committees and boards. He serves as a Member of the Supreme Court Committee on Road Safety, is a Visiting Faculty member at the Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Kolkata, and is part of the Board of Governors at The Neotia University in Kolkata. Furthermore, he is a Board Member of L&T Independent Company, focusing on Infrastructure and Transport.

In terms of education, Sanjay Mitra holds a Master of Science (M.Sc) in Physics from Delhi University, a Master of Arts (MA) in Economics from Himachal University, and a Master of Public Administration (MPA) from the Kennedy School at Harvard University.
