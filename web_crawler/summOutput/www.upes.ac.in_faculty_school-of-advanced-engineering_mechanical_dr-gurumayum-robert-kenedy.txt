Dr. Gurumayum Robert Kenedy is an Assistant Professor in the School of Advanced Engineering at UPES Dehradun, having joined on August 21, 2023. His academic background includes a Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (NTUST), a Master of Technology with distinction from the National Institute of Technology (Agartala), and a Bachelor of Technology from Pondicherry University. He has also received a full scholarship for the NTUST Doctoral Programme.

Dr. Kenedy's research primarily focuses on phase transformation studies and materials characterization of high entropy alloys, along with metallic and composite materials. His expertise encompasses various advanced techniques such as Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM), Field Emission Scanning Electron Microscopy (FESEM), cryogenic tensile testing, tensile testing, Electron Dispersive Spectroscopy (EDS), STEM-HADDF, X-ray Diffraction (XRD), Thermogravimetric Analysis (TGA), Magneto-Thermogravimetric Analysis (MTGA), metallography, and microhardness testing.

Before his current position, Dr. Kenedy gained valuable experience as a research scholar at NTUST, an engineer at Accenture, and an intern at HMT in India. His tenure at Accenture (2007-2011) included collaborations with notable companies such as Wyeth, Avanade, and British Petroleum. He has also been involved in various scholarly activities, including the Taiwan-Russia Joint Project, and has received the Accenture People Developer Award.

In terms of teaching, Dr. Kenedy emphasizes understanding his audience's knowledge and goals to tailor his curriculum effectively. He teaches courses such as Introduction to Materials Science and Engineering, Manufacturing Processes, and Additive Manufacturing.

Additionally, Dr. Kenedy is passionate about chess, having played at a national level and drawing a match with former World Chess Champion Grandmaster Vishwanathan Anand.
