Abhirup Khanna is an accomplished Assistant Professor (Senior Scale) at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun, India, with a robust background in education and research. An alumnus of The University of Melbourne, Australia, he brings over eight years of experience to his role, significantly contributing to both the institution and the broader academic community.

In his current position, Abhirup specializes in cloud computing and teaches various IBM-related subjects to B.Tech Computer Science students, including courses such as Introduction to Cloud Computing & Virtualization, Cloud Computing Architecture, Cloud Deployment Models, Container Orchestration, and DevOps, catering to 2nd, 3rd, and 4th-year students. He is recognized for his dynamic teaching style and his ability to inspire and motivate learners.

Abhirup has an impressive research portfolio, having published numerous papers in reputable journals and presented his work at international conferences and workshops. His scholarly activities include conducting research, publishing scholarly works, engaging in conferences, and fostering collaborations with peers, reflecting his commitment to advancing knowledge and promoting intellectual development. Notably, he received the Best Research Paper Award at the 2nd International Conference on Next Generation Computing Technologies in 2016.

His research interests lie in the fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Blockchain technology, with a focus on interdisciplinary applications. His innovative contributions have been recognized through multiple publications and presentations at prestigious events, showcasing his potential to drive transformative changes in these cutting-edge fields.

Abhirup's teaching philosophy emphasizes empowering students to develop independence and critical thinking skills. He believes in nurturing an inclusive and supportive learning environment that recognizes the unique strengths and potential of each student. His ultimate goal is to inspire students to embrace lifelong learning and make meaningful contributions to society.
