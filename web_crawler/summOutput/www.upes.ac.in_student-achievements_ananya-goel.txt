Ananya Goel, a B.Tech student specializing in Computer Science Engineering with a focus on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, has achieved notable recognition by winning the Best Research Paper Award at the AICTE-sponsored National Conference VSIIT'21. Her award-winning paper, titled "Exploring the Application of IoT Technology in the Development of Smart Cities," demonstrates her deep understanding and significant contributions to the field of Internet of Things (IoT) technology, particularly in its application towards the advancement of smart city initiatives. Additionally, her research has been acknowledged through its publication as chapter 12 in the book "Visualizing and Sensitizing Innovations," further establishing her influence within the academic and research communities.
