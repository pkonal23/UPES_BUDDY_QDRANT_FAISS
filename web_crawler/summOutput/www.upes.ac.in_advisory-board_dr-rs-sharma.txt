Dr. Ram Sharma, an esteemed figure in the field of reproductive health, holds the title of Fellow of the National Academy of Medical Sciences (FNAMS). He has a distinguished career, having served as an Emeritus Scientist and Head of the Division of Reproductive Biology, Maternal and Child Health at the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) in New Delhi. Additionally, he has held the position of Head of Capital Project Work and Procurement at ICMR.

Dr. Sharma's impactful research has led to the development of crucial guidelines, including the "National Guidelines for Accreditation, Supervision and Regulation of ART Clinics in India." He has also played a significant role in drafting the Assisted Reproductive Technology (Regulation) Bill and Rules, as well as the Surrogacy (Regulation) Bill. His contributions extend to creating guidelines for the prevention and management of infertility within the public health care system.

Throughout his career, Dr. Sharma has been recognized with numerous prestigious awards, including lifetime achievement awards from ACE (Manipal University), the Indian Society for the Study of Reproduction and Fertility (ISSRF), YUVA ISAR, and ISAR.
