Afrikiko is an innovative cafe project designed to celebrate and showcase the vibrant essence of African culture. The cafe's design integrates traditional African motifs, textiles, and art, aiming to create a warm and inviting atmosphere for visitors. The menu is inspired by a variety of African cuisines, reflecting the continent's rich and diverse culinary heritage. A key focus of the project is sustainability, emphasizing the use of local materials and the promotion of African cuisine.

The project is associated with Siddhartha Mukherjee, who serves as a mentor and is an Assistant Professor at the School of Design. The information also indicates that this is a student project, though specific eligibility criteria for participation are not detailed in the provided content.
