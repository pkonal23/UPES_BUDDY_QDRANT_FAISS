The Ph.D. program in Electrical Engineering at the UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a comprehensive and structured curriculum aimed at preparing candidates for leadership roles in academia, research, and industry. The program emphasizes research and innovation, allowing graduates to contribute significantly to various sectors such as Research and Development (R&D), academia, electrical design, renewable energy, sustainability, smart grids, power systems, electronics, semiconductors, and electric vehicles (EVs).

Graduates from this program have diverse career opportunities, including roles in cutting-edge research institutions and prestigious technology companies, where they engage in projects related to renewable energy solutions and semiconductor design. The program's robust industry connections and emphasis on interdisciplinary collaboration ensure that graduates are well-equipped to tackle the dynamic challenges present in the electrical engineering landscape.

Key features of the program include:

- **State-of-the-art Facilities**: The program boasts a Battery Testing Lab funded by DST-FIST, which allows students to conduct advanced experiments in battery management systems, charging, and discharging processes.
- **Mentorship and Faculty**: Students receive guidance from world-class faculty members, many of whom come from prestigious institutions such as IITs and NITs, ensuring a high-quality educational experience.
- **Research Focus**: The heart of the program lies in original research, culminating in a dissertation that contributes new knowledge to the field. Candidates receive training in proposal development, literature review, and dissertation structuring.
- **Financial Support**: Students can receive a monthly stipend of up to ₹30,000 during their studies. Exceptional candidates holding NET-JRF or INSPIRE fellowships may also receive an annual cash grant of ₹2,00,000.
