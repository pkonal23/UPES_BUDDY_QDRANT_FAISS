Neeraj Rathore is an Assistant Professor at the Senior Scale level, with a robust academic and professional background in Computer Science and Engineering. He is dedicated to fostering an interactive and engaging learning environment, emphasizing practical applications and real-world examples to facilitate student understanding of complex concepts. Neeraj actively encourages student participation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, which are essential for a deep comprehension of the subjects he teaches.

With over 15 years of experience in academia, Neeraj specializes in a variety of subjects including Data Analytics, Business Analytics, Web Programming, Data Mining, Python Programming, Data Visualization, and Database Management Systems (DBMS). He holds a Master of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering and is currently pursuing a Ph.D. in the same field. His research interests are particularly focused on salient object detection in images, specifically in single and multi-focused images under noisy conditions. He aims to develop algorithms and techniques that enhance the accuracy of object identification in these challenging scenarios.

Neeraj has made significant contributions to scholarly activities, having published research papers in reputable journals and conferences. His notable works cover a range of topics including M-Governance, cloud computing security, smart security and privacy, salient object detection, and face and iris recognition. Additionally, he has authored a book chapter on optimized cloud storage data analysis using machine learning models.

Before joining the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in 2022, Neeraj served as an Assistant Professor at DIT University in Dehradun from August 2017 to September 2022. His earlier academic roles include positions at Shivalik College of Engineering (September 2012 - May 2017) and Uttaranchal Institute of Technology (February 2009 - September 2012). Throughout these roles, he has been engaged in mentoring students and coordinating various academic activities.

Neeraj's contributions to academia have been recognized with several accolades, including a Certificate of Appreciation from the Vice-Chairman of Shivalik College of Engineering, Dehradun. 

For further communication, Neeraj can be reached via email at neeraj.rathore@ddn.upes.ac.in. His profile is affiliated with the School of Business at UPES.
