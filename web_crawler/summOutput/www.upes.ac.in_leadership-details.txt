**Chancellor: Dr. Sunil Rai**

**University:** University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)

**Mission:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) prioritizes empowering students to realize their aspirations and dreams through customized educational plans tailored to their unique goals. University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) fosters a personalized learning environment with flexibility and continuous mentorship.

**Educational Approach:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) integrates technical knowledge with ethical principles and leadership skills to cultivate exceptional individuals. Recognizing the societal significance of educators, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) emphasizes their role in addressing contemporary challenges such as security, environmental protection, energy, and healthcare.

**Vision:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) aspires to establish itself as a leading institution renowned for its high-quality academic programs and research. Through industry-aligned initiatives, an esteemed faculty, and cutting-edge facilities, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) aims to prepare students for emerging challenges and foster entrepreneurial mindsets.

**Student Focus:**

Students are the cornerstone of University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). The university provides an environment that promotes critical thinking and problem-solving skills, empowering students to confidently apply their classroom knowledge through hands-on experimentation.

**Collaborations and Partnerships:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) collaborates with industries and research centers to provide opportunities for joint projects, training, and internships. This collaboration enhances students' skills and contributes to the university's consistently high placement records.

**Programs and Faculty:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a diverse range of programs spanning Petroleum and Energy, Infrastructure, Transportation, IT, Design, Planning and Policy, Management, and Law. The university's faculty comprises scholars and industry experts, providing students with personalized attention and mentorship.

**Campus and Atmosphere:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) invites visitors to experience its state-of-the-art campus, which fosters an engaging and supportive learning environment. The university's enthusiasm for education is evident in its commitment to innovation and excellence.
