**UPES School of Health Sciences & Technology's Bachelor of Technology Food Technology (Food Process Engineering) Program**

**Program Overview**

The Bachelor of Technology Food Technology (Food Process Engineering) program offered by UPES School of Health Sciences & Technology is a comprehensive and specialized course that focuses on the crucial food industry sector. The program is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to transform raw food into edible products through various processes. By applying engineering principles and techniques, students learn to design, optimize, and manage processes within the food industry, ensuring efficiency, safety, quality, and sustainability in food production.

Throughout the program, students gain expertise in diverse areas such as food preservation, food safety, heat and mass transfer, food packaging, food rheology, food process automation, food quality, sensory evaluation, new product development, and food sustainability. These topics cover a broad range of essential aspects in the food process engineering field, preparing students to address the challenges and demands of the industry effectively.

**Curriculum and Practical Experience**

The curriculum is thoughtfully designed to combine theoretical knowledge with practical hands-on experiences, enabling students to understand the real-world applications of food process engineering concepts. Additionally, the program may provide opportunities for industrial training and internships, allowing students to gain exposure to the industry and develop essential industry-specific skills.

**Program Highlights**

* Strong academic-industry collaborations, providing students with unique opportunities to enhance their educational experience and prepare for successful careers in the food industry.
* Impressive array of advanced equipment for food process engineering and analysis, including ICP-OES, GCMS, DSC, TGA, XRD, FTIR, Rheometer, Viscometer, Spray Drier, and various fundamental food analysis apparatus.
* Curriculum aligned with industry demands, ensuring graduates are well-equipped with the knowledge and skills required to excel in the food processing sector.
* Well-prepared graduates for successful careers in food process engineering, equipped with both theoretical knowledge and practical experience gained through academic-industry collaborations and advanced equipment usage.

**Future Scope and Industry Trends**

The future scope of the Bachelor of Technology Food Technology (Food Process Engineering) program is promising, with several emerging trends in the field:

* Adoption of automation and Industry 4.0 technologies like robotics, AI, and data analytics for improved productivity and safety.
* Growing importance of sustainable practices, eco-friendly solutions, and clean label ingredients due to environmental concerns.
* Exploration of novel food processing technologies such as high-pressure processing and cold plasma treatments for enhanced safety and nutritional profiles.
* Personalized nutrition based on individual needs and preferences becoming possible with advances in technology and data analytics.
* Rise of plant-based foods and alternative proteins, creating opportunities for optimizing production processes.

**Career Opportunities**

Graduates of the Bachelor of Technology Food Technology (Food Process Engineering) program have excellent career prospects in various industries, including:

* Food processing companies
* Beverage manufacturing
* Quality control and assurance
* Research and development
* Food safety management

They can pursue roles as food technologists, process engineers, production managers, and regulatory compliance specialists. With the food industry experiencing continuous growth and innovation, this program opens doors to exciting opportunities where graduates can contribute to the development of safe and sustainable food products that meet global demands.

**Eligibility and Selection Criteria**

To be eligible for the Bachelor of Technology Food Technology (Food Process Engineering) program, students must meet the following minimum criteria:

* Minimum 50% Marks at X & XII with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Math's, and English as Major Subject in XII.
* Selection for the program is based on individual's performance in UPESEAT / JEE Mains / Board Merit / SAT/CUET.
