**About the Program: BBA (Green Energy & Sustainability) - Sustainable Development**

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers the BBA (Green Energy & Sustainability) - Sustainable Development program, which is meticulously designed to equip students with a thorough understanding of sustainable energy sources, energy transitions, and sustainability practices. This curriculum aims to prepare graduates for managerial roles in diverse industries related to green energy, sustainable development, the environment, and governance, both in India and globally. The course content is meticulously crafted to provide students with the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed in this dynamic field.

**Program Highlights:**

* **Practical Experience:** Students undertake substantial projects and dissertations, allowing students to gain hands-on experience and apply their knowledge in the field of Green Energy & Sustainability Development.
* **Industry Exposure:** The program offers opportunities for students to engage in practical learning through internships and live projects with companies, gaining valuable industry experience.
* **Career Flexibility:** The BBA (Green Energy & Sustainability) - Sustainable Development program offers flexibility for students to choose between pursuing careers in operations, consulting in Green Energy & Sustainability, or further education for specialized roles.
* **Research Focus:** Emphasis is placed on research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships, encouraging students to explore and contribute to the advancement of sustainable practices.
* **Industry Insights:** Students are exposed to real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by industry experts, providing students with practical insights and a broader perspective on the field.
* **Advanced Facilities:** UPES offers cutting-edge facilities, including modern classrooms, an Analytics Lab, a well-stocked library, and advanced analytical tools.
* **International Exposure:** Students have the chance to participate in student exchange, study abroad, and semester exchange programs with leading global partner institutes, enhancing students' international exposure.

**Future Scope and Industry Trends:**

The BBA (Green Energy & Sustainability) - Sustainable Development program prepares students for managerial roles in the rapidly expanding field of green energy and sustainability. With the increasing global emphasis on these areas, professionals with knowledge and expertise in this domain are in high demand. The program's focus on hands-on learning, through internships and on-the-job training, provides students with a competitive edge in the job market. The curriculum covers a broad range of topics, including solar energy resource management, regulatory frameworks, water resource and hydro power management, E-vehicles and energy storage, and green energy pricing and emission trading. Real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from experts enhance students' understanding of the industry. Additionally, the program provides exposure to software and technology, making graduates more employable.

**Career Opportunities:**

The BBA (Green Energy & Sustainability) - Sustainable Development offered by UPES's School of Business opens up numerous career paths for students. Graduates have the opportunity to work with renowned organizations, gaining valuable experience in the corporate world through summer internships, live projects, and placements. The program enables students to explore their career interests, collaborate with potential employers, and potentially secure job opportunities. The field of green energy and sustainability offers a wide range of career options across various industries, encompassing diverse functions. Students can explore placements in three distinct verticals: Green Energy, Sustainability, and Governance.
