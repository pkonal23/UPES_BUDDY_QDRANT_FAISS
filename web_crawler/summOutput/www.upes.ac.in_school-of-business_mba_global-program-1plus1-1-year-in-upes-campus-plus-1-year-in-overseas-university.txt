The MBA Global program at UPES School of Business is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of global business dynamics, combining theoretical knowledge with practical application. This two-year program allows students to spend one year at the UPES campus and another year at an esteemed international partner university, offering a globally recognized degree and exposure to diverse job opportunities worldwide.

**Curriculum Overview:**
The curriculum covers a wide range of subjects, including:
- Managerial Economics
- Organizational Behavior
- Marketing Management
- Business Communication
- Research Methods
- Analytics
- Global Business Environment

Additionally, it integrates real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from experts to enhance practical insights into international business scenarios. The program emphasizes decision-making skills through analysis of real-world cases and encourages active participation in dynamic classroom environments, which include traditional lectures, group discussions, and cooperative problem-solving activities.

**Skills Development:**
Students are equipped with cutting-edge tools such as Power-BI, Gretl, advanced Excel, and analytics tools like R-studio, enhancing their analytical and technical skills. The program also focuses on developing an understanding of human behavior, enabling students to analyze strategic human resource requirements and influence outcomes effectively.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates of the MBA Global program can pursue diverse career pathways, including:
- Global Business Managers
- International Marketing Directors
- Global Supply Chain Directors
- Global Strategy Consultants
- International Business Development Managers
- Global HR Directors
- International Trade Compliance Managers
- Global Financial Analysts
- Cross-Cultural Trainers
- Entrepreneurs establishing global-focused ventures

**Global Mindset and Cultural Awareness:**
The program fosters a global mindset, preparing students for successful careers in an interconnected world. Through international internships, study abroad experiences, and collaborative projects, students develop adaptability, effective communication, and collaboration skills necessary for navigating different cultures and business practices.

**Admission Process:**
To apply for the MBA Global program, candidates must meet the following eligibility criteria:
- Minimum 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation
- Graduation from a recognized University in any stream

Selection is based on performance in UPESMET or other national-level exams, followed by a personal interview.

**Facilities:**
UPES School of Business features modern smart classrooms, a well-equipped Analytics Lab, and a comprehensive library, providing an enriching environment for academic and professional growth.

**Contact Information:**
For detailed information regarding the fee structure, program highlights, and how to apply, prospective students are encouraged to visit the UPES School of Business website or contact the admissions office directly.

In summary, the MBA Global program at UPES School of Business is structured to cultivate adaptable and effective leaders, equipped with the knowledge and experience necessary to succeed in the globalized business landscape while contributing positively to their communities.
