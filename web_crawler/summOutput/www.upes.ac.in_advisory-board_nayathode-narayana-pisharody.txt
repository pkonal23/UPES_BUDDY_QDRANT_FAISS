Nayathode Narayana Pisharody is a highly experienced professional specializing in Industrial and Fire Safety. He serves as the Associate Director in the Industrial & Fire Safety Group at the Directorate of Health Safety & Environment within the Nuclear Power Corporation of India Limited (NPCIL), located in Mumbai. 

His educational background includes a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering, which he earned from the University of Kerala in 1987. To further his expertise in safety, he completed an Advanced Diploma in Industrial Safety from the Central Labour Institute in Sion, Mumbai, in 2012. Additionally, he holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Human Resource Management, which he obtained from Indira Gandhi National Open University in 2016. 

Nayathode Narayana Pisharody's extensive qualifications and experience make him a key figure in the field of safety within the nuclear power sector.
