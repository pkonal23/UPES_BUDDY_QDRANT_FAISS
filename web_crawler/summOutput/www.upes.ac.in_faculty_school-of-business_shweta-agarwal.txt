Dr. Shweta Agarwal is an Assistant Professor at the School of Business, UPES, specializing in Business Analytics. Since joining in December 2021, she has taught various courses including Predictive Modelling, Business Analytics, Programming for Analytics, Data Management, and Data Preparation. Dr. Agarwal has a strong background in statistical modeling, machine learning, and data mining. She utilizes advanced analytical tools and programming languages such as Python, R, and SQL to assist students in developing predictive models that forecast business trends and extract actionable insights from complex datasets through data visualization and statistical techniques.

Dr. Agarwal emphasizes creating a supportive learning environment tailored to the diverse needs of managers. She believes in the importance of IT skills for managerial decision-making and strategic planning. To enhance learning, she incorporates case studies and real-world examples, and conducts technology demonstrations to familiarize students with the latest IT tools and trends relevant to future management roles.

Her research, which culminated in her doctoral thesis titled "Stock Market Response to Information Diffusion through Internet: An Empirical Study in the Indian Context," investigates the impact of online information, including social media and news articles, on stock market performance in India. Dr. Agarwal has published her findings in esteemed international journals and is proficient in various software tools including MS Excel, SPSS, Amos, Tableau, SAS, MATLAB, as well as programming languages like Python, R Studio, and SQL.

Dr. Agarwal has received several accolades, including the 2019 Eminent Research Scholar Award on Research Day at IIIT Allahabad, and the Best Paper Award at the 10th International Conference on Sustainable Growth and Innovation in 2016. She has also participated in faculty development programs and workshops, such as the Multivariate Data Analysis at IIM Kozhikode and a Machine Learning and Data Analytics workshop at IIITA.

Her scholarly contributions include reviewing articles for reputable journals, further highlighting her engagement with the academic community. Dr. Agarwal holds an MBA and a Ph.D. in Information Systems from the Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad, completed in 2021. Her research interests encompass Predictive Analysis, Business and Data Analysis, Investor Behaviour, and Capital Markets.
