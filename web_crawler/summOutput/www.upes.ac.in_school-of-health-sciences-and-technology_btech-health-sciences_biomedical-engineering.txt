The B.Tech. Biomedical Engineering program at UPES (University of Petroleum and Energy Studies) is a comprehensive course designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to innovate in the healthcare sector. It integrates engineering principles with life sciences, enabling students to develop advanced biomedical devices and algorithms. The curriculum is structured across eight semesters, incorporating practical learning experiences, rigorous project work, and internships with industry partners to enhance students' problem-solving abilities and prepare them for real-world applications.

**Program Structure and Curriculum:**
- The program covers a wide range of subjects, including but not limited to:
  - Fundamentals of Robotics
  - Microbiology & Microbial Technology
  - Bioengineering
  - Artificial Organs
  - Biomedical Signal Processing
  - Biophysics
  - Biodesign Engineering
  - Molecular Biology and Genetics
  - Data Visualization
  
- Students will also engage in specialized modules, such as MRI, X-Ray, and CT, along with electives that focus on areas like Biomedical Data Analysis, Bioinformatics, and Biomedical Robotics.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
- To be eligible for the program, students must have a minimum of 50% marks in their 10th and 12th grades, with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics, and English as major subjects in 12th grade.
- Selection is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates of the program can pursue diverse career paths, including:
- Biomedical Materials Engineer
- Biomedical Researcher
- Clinical Researcher
- Tissue Engineer
- Biomedical Data Analyst
- Biomedical Device Engineer
- Healthcare Consultant

The program prepares students to work in various settings such as hospitals, laboratories, and pharmaceutical companies, focusing on the development of medical devices and tools that support healthcare professionals and patients.

**Future Scope and Industry Trends:**
The field of biomedical engineering in India is rapidly expanding, driven by significant government investment in research and development. This growth presents numerous opportunities for graduates to engage in innovative medical projects, contributing to advancements in healthcare technology. The program emphasizes environmental sustainability by promoting the use of biodegradable materials and low-carbon footprint algorithms in biomedical applications.

**Placement Opportunities:**
UPES boasts strong industry connections, ensuring that graduates have access to numerous placement opportunities. The program's focus on practical skills and industry-oriented training has resulted in a consistently high placement record, with many students securing job offers from leading healthcare organizations, both domestically and internationally.
