Dr. Surya Prakash is a distinguished environmental earth scientist specializing in disaster management, environmental management, and engineering geology, with a focus on community-based disaster risk management. He currently holds the position of Professor and Head of the Geo-Meteorological Risks Management Division, as well as the CBRN (Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear), Industrial, and Cyber Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) at the National Institute of Disaster Management (NIDM), which operates under the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India.

In addition to his primary role at NIDM, Dr. Prakash serves as the Chairman of the Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) and the Institute's Academic Improvement Plan (IAIP). He also holds the position of Chief Information Security Officer, overseeing the institute's information security protocols.

On an international level, Dr. Prakash is the National Coordinator for India at the International Association for Promoting Geoethics, based in Italy. He is also a member of the Board of Representatives (BoR) at the International Consortium on Landslides, contributing to global efforts in landslide risk management.

For further information, Dr. Surya Prakash can be contacted through the National Institute of Disaster Management, Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India.
