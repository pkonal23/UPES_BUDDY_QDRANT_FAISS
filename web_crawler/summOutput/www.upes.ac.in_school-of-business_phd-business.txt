The Ph.D. program at the School of Business offers a comprehensive, research-oriented curriculum designed for aspiring scholars and industry professionals. It allows for an in-depth exploration of advanced concepts in business administration, guided by experienced faculty. The program supports original research, enabling students to publish in top-tier journals and prepare for careers in academia, research institutions, or leadership roles in industry. The program duration ranges from 3 to 6 years.

**Key Dates and Fees:**
- Last Date to Apply: August 9, 2024
- Last Date to Pay Fee: August 4, 2024
- Program Semester Fee: Rs 1,00,000 (with a 75% waiver for eligible candidates, making it INR 25,000)
- Monthly stipend for selected full-time Ph.D. scholars: INR 30,000

**Eligibility Criteria:**
Candidates must possess a Master's degree or an equivalent professional degree with a minimum of 55% marks or a grade 'B' in the UGC 7-point scale. A relaxation of 5% is available for candidates from SC/ST/OBC (non-creamy layer)/Differently-abled categories and for those who obtained their Master's degree before September 19, 1991. This relaxation applies to qualifying marks without including grace marks.

**Research Opportunities and Career Prospects:**
Graduates can pursue academic roles as professors or researchers, or enter the corporate sector as consultants or strategists. They may also find opportunities in government, think tanks, and international organizations. The program equips graduates with advanced analytical and critical thinking skills, suitable for leadership, policy-making, and entrepreneurial ventures. Average salaries for Ph.D. holders range from INR 8-20 lakhs per annum, influenced by specialization, industry, and location.

**Scholarships:**
UPES offers scholarships based on university policy, including a 75% fee waiver for eligible candidates.

**Contact Information:**
For inquiries, candidates can reach out via email at **phd@upes.ac.in**.

**Additional Information:**
- The program emphasizes research excellence, interdisciplinary collaboration, and intellectual growth.
- Students are encouraged to engage in industry-relevant research and benefit from strong industry linkages.
- The university boasts a 99% placement record, with support for internships and pre-placement training.

Overall, the Ph.D. program at UPES School of Business is structured to foster a research-intensive environment, providing students with the tools and support necessary to contribute significantly to their fields.
