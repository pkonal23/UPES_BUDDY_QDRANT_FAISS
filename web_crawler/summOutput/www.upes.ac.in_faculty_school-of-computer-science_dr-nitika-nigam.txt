Dr. Nitika Nigam is an Assistant Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), having joined in July 2023. Her academic background includes a Ph.D. from the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) in Varanasi, where she was a Research Scholar from July 2018 to June 2023. Her doctoral research concentrated on Computer Vision and Applied Artificial Intelligence, particularly in Deep Learning. She also holds an M.Tech degree from Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT) Gorakhpur and a B.Tech from Uttar Pradesh Technical University (UPTU) Lucknow.

Dr. Nigam has made significant contributions to the field, with publications in reputable journals such as IEEE Transactions, including IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing and IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, which have impact factors of 8.2 and 10.4, respectively. Additionally, she has presented posters at A* and A conferences and has served as a reviewer for the aforementioned IEEE Transactions.

Her primary research interests lie in Machine Learning and Deep Learning, with a specific focus on system deployment. In her teaching role, she emphasizes creating a supportive and motivating environment for students, believing that this is crucial for their growth. Dr. Nigam teaches courses in Python and C Programming and is dedicated to inspiring curiosity and ambition in her students. She has been recognized for her academic excellence, having passed the University Grants Commission examination for Lectureship (NET) in 2017 and qualifying for the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) in 2016.

For contact, Dr. Nitika Nigam can be reached via email at nitika.nigam@ddn.upes.ac.in. Her profile reflects her commitment to fostering a deep understanding of Computer Vision, Image and Video Processing, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning among her students.
