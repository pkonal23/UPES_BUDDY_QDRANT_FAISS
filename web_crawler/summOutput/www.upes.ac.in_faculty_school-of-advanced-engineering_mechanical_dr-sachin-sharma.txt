Dr. Sharma's teaching philosophy emphasizes creating a dynamic and engaging learning environment that promotes critical thinking and creativity. He tailors his teaching methods to accommodate diverse learning styles, focusing on the practical application of theoretical concepts. His goal is to inspire students to become lifelong learners and to explore new ideas collaboratively.

Throughout his career, Dr. Sharma has held various roles, including serving as an Academic Coordinator at LPU, where he oversaw academic operations and established a laboratory to enhance practical learning. His teaching portfolio includes courses such as Heat and Mass Transfer, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning, Modeling and Optimization, Electric and Hybrid Vehicles, Heat Exchanger Design, Fluid Mechanics, and Automotive HVAC. Dr. Sharma is also actively involved in mentoring students on major and minor projects, fostering a collaborative learning environment that enhances their critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Notably, he mentored a team that excelled at the FMAE BAJA EVENT in 2019, achieving nine awards, including the overall championship.
