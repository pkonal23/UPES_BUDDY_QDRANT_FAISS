Ruchi Bajpai, an alumnus of UPES, achieved notable success by being selected as a Sub-Lieutenant in the Indian Navy's executive branch. She was recommended by the 33 SSB Bhopal for the Short Service Commission Women (SSCW) IT entry on her first attempt. Ruchi successfully passed all necessary evaluations, including physical, intelligence, and medical tests. She is set to join the Indian Naval Academy in Ezhimala on August 20, 2023. Ruchi graduated in 2023 with a B.Tech degree in Computer Science Engineering, specializing in Cyber Security and Forensics.
