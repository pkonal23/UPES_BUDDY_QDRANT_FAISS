The MBA in Finance Management program at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is a comprehensive two-year postgraduate degree designed to equip students with a robust foundation in business and management, alongside specialized finance knowledge. The program emphasizes essential skills such as analytical and quantitative abilities, financial modeling, problem-solving, strategic thinking, communication, teamwork, and leadership through various methodologies including group projects, case studies, and presentations.

### Key Features of the Program:
- **Curriculum**: Covers a wide range of finance-related subjects including Financial Institutions and Markets, Behavioral Finance, Financial Accounting, Corporate Finance, Investment Analysis, Risk Management, and ESG Investing. The curriculum also includes practical experiences through major projects, summer internships, and live projects with companies.
- **Career Opportunities**: Graduates can pursue diverse roles such as Investment Banker, Portfolio Manager, Risk Analyst, Financial Analyst, and roles in marketing and sales. The program prepares students for employment in multinational corporations, financial institutions, consulting firms, and entrepreneurship.
- **Industry Trends**: The program highlights the importance of staying updated with industry trends such as FinTech, data analytics, digital banking, ESG investing, and cybersecurity, which are increasingly shaping the finance landscape.

### Admission Criteria:
- **Eligibility**: Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream. Additionally, students with a minimum eligible score in MAT, CMAT, CAT, XAT, GMAT, or NMAT may be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and may directly proceed to Group Discussion and Interview.
- **Selection Process**: The selection criteria include performance in entrance exams followed by a personal interview.

### Networking and Career Development:
Students have opportunities to network with industry professionals through seminars and conferences, which can significantly enhance their career prospects in finance. The program has witnessed strong placement success, with starting salaries ranging from INR 6-30 lakh per annum, depending on the candidate's skills and exposure.
