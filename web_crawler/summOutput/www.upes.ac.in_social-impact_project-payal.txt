Project Payal focuses on providing education to underprivileged, meritorious girls from nearby villages, recognizing it as a significant investment in social development. The initiative aims not only to educate these girls but also to foster their overall personality development. The project underscores the importance of empowering girls through education, which can have far-reaching benefits for communities and society at large.
