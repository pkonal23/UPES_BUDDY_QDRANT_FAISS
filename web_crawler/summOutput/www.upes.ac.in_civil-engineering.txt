The B. Tech. Civil Engineering program at UPES offers a comprehensive four-year undergraduate curriculum designed to create industry-ready professionals. The program focuses on advanced construction technologies, infrastructure development, and sustainable practices, and is structured across eight semesters with various specialized courses.

### Specializations
Students can choose from three specializations:
1. **Transportation Engineering**
2. **Environmental Engineering**
3. **Construction Management**

This flexibility allows students to tailor their education according to their interests and career aspirations. Additionally, the program includes a unique dual-degree opportunity, enabling students to study at UPES for two years and then at prestigious international universities such as the University of Exeter and Indiana University Purdue University Indianapolis (IUPUI).

### Curriculum Highlights
The curriculum includes core subjects such as:
- Fluid Mechanics
- Structural Design
- Concrete Technology
- Engineering Economics & Estimation
- Advanced Geotechnical Engineering
- Building Information Modelling
- Various labs including Physics, Chemistry, and Concrete Technology

Emerging technologies like AI, Robotics, and Virtual/Augmented Reality are integrated into the curriculum, ensuring students are well-versed in contemporary engineering practices.

### Research and Practical Experience
Students benefit from:
- Access to state-of-the-art research labs and computational facilities.
- Up to six months of research experience at national and international institutes.
- Collaborations with industry leaders such as L&T and consultants like Ascela and TraNXT Advisory to ensure real-world relevance.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates have a wide range of career prospects in both the private and public sectors, including roles such as:
- Design Engineer
- Site Engineer
- Project Manager
- Positions in government organizations like PWD, NHAI, and ISRO.
- Academic roles in esteemed institutions (IITs/NITs) or opportunities for higher education (MS, M. Tech, Ph.D.).

The program boasts a high placement rate of 90%, with the highest package reaching 8 lakhs, reflecting its strong industry connections and the demand for skilled civil engineers.

### Admission Criteria
To apply for the program, students must meet the following eligibility criteria:
- Minimum 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics in Class XII.
- Selection is based on performance in UPESEAT, JEE Mains, Board Merit, SAT, or CUET.
