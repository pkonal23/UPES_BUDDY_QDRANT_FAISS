On March 27, 2022, an International Legal Conference is set to take place at the Schwarzmann Center Rotunda Building, organized by the School for Life at the Social Justice Center, UPES. The event is open to anyone interested in attending. The conference will focus on advanced technologies, security, and their societal implications.

The conference aims to provide a personalized learning environment for students, emphasizing flexibility and continuous mentoring. 

For further inquiries or to register for the event, attendees can contact via the provided email address, Life@UPES. The physical address for the venue is 152 Road, St, Goov, Bandra West. 

This event promises to be an enriching experience for all participants, addressing critical issues at the intersection of law, technology, and society.
