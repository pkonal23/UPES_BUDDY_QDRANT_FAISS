Dr. Gaurav Bhardwaj is an Assistant Professor with a keen research interest in Robotics and Artificial Intelligence (AI). He earned his PhD from the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department at IIT Roorkee in 2023, where his doctoral research focused on trajectory planning, simulation, and control of bipedal robots operating on non-flat terrains. Prior to his PhD, he completed an MTech in Information Technology from IIIT Allahabad in 2017, specializing in Software Engineering.

Dr. Bhardwaj has contributed to the academic community through various research papers published in esteemed SCI journals, including *Applied Intelligence* and the *International Journal of Control Automation and Systems*, as well as presenting at national and international conferences. He holds a design patent for a bipedal robot registered in 2024 and possesses industrial experience in developing robotics products for the agriculture and healthcare sectors. Notably, he has successfully deployed products at the Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) campus under the mentorship of principal scientists.

In terms of accolades, Dr. Bhardwaj has achieved significant academic recognition, qualifying for the CSIR Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) with an All India Rank of 55, and he has passed the GATE examination twice in 2014 and 2016. He has also secured funding totaling INR 40 Lakhs from the Department of Science and Technology (DST) under the Pankh Scheme at IHUB Divyasampark IIT Roorkee for his startup initiatives, alongside a grant of INR 5 Lakhs from PAU PABI in 2024.

Before joining UPES, Dr. Bhardwaj served as the Managing Director and CEO of his startup, PaiByTwo Private Limited, which he founded during his PhD in 2022. His startup has attracted INR 50 Lakhs in funding from various sources, including IHUB Divyasampark IIT Roorkee, PAU PABI, and incubation support from AIC IIT Delhi and ABIC ICRA Delhi. His work primarily revolves around developing intelligent bipedal agrobots aimed at precision spraying and crop monitoring.

Dr. Bhardwaj's teaching philosophy emphasizes aligning educational practices with industrial requirements. He aims to foster innovation among students, equipping them with skills that meet industry standards through real-time projects and specialized tools for various learning aspects.

For further inquiries or contact, Dr. Gaurav Bhardwaj can be reached via email at gaurav.bhardwaj@ddn.upes.ac.in. His areas of expertise include Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Haptics, Healthcare Robotics, and Exoskeletons.
