**BBA in Human Resource Program at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Business**

**Program Overview**

The BBA in Human Resource program at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Business prepares students for a comprehensive understanding of Human Resource Management.

**Curriculum**

The three-year curriculum encompasses a wide range of Human Resource topics, including:

* Human Resource Management
* Organizational Behavior
* Compensation and Benefits
* Employment Law
* Training and Development
* Recruitment and Selection
* Employee Relations
* Performance Management
* Strategic Human Resource Management

**Emphasis on Practical Experience**

The program emphasizes practical experience through two internships. These internships provide students with the opportunity to apply their knowledge in real-world settings, gaining insights into both the social and corporate sectors.

**Program Highlights**

* Major projects, summer internships, and live projects ensure hands-on experience in Human Resources.
* Real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures provide practical knowledge and skills.
* Rankings: 39th in India in Management Category by National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2023, top 25 Business Schools in India according to India Today Ranking Issue 2021.
* Industry alliances with renowned organizations, such as KPMG, TATA Power, Hindustan Petroleum Corporation Limited (HPCL), and Essar Oil, offer networking, internships, and industry insights.
* Global opportunities include a semester abroad at the University of California, Berkeley, student exchange programs, and co-teaching by University Canada West (UCW) and University of London Business School (ULBS).
* Placement assistance for up to five years, membership in Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB), and progression opportunities for master's programs at universities such as Nottingham Trent University and ULaw Business School.

**Future Scope**

The BBA in Human Resource program has a promising future scope with industry trends such as:

* Algorithmic HR
* Total Wellbeing
* Managing Workforce Ecosystems
* Redefining Remote and Hybrid Work Strategies
* Evolving Role of Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO)
* HR entering the Metaverse
* Creating Purpose-driven Organizations
* Inclusion and Equity in HR Practices
* Upskilling Leaders and Managers
* Investing in Deskless Workers

**Career Opportunities**

Graduates of the BBA in Human Resource program can pursue various roles, such as:

* HR Generalist
* Talent Acquisition Specialist
* Compensation and Benefits Analyst
* Training and Development Manager
* Employee Relations Consultant

The graduates can find employment in diverse industries, including corporate organizations, consulting firms, non-profit organizations, government agencies, and multinational corporations.

**Placements**

Students have received placement opportunities from prestigious companies, including Nykaa, Reliance Industries, Infosys, HDFC Bank, Adani Group, IBM, Larsen & Toubro, Tata Consultancy Services (TCS), Accenture, Spicejet, Genpact, Vedanta, Maruti Suzuki, Lead Squared, KPMG, Xiaomi, Tata Croma, Odex, Transystem, Amazon Industries, and others.

**Eligibility**

* Minimum of 50% marks in both Classes X and XII.

**Selection Criteria**

* Performance in the UPES Management Entrance Test - Undergraduate (UPESMET-UG), Under Graduate Aptitude Test 2023 (UGAT'23), or Central Universities Common Entrance Test (CUET).
