The M.Tech. program in Health Safety & Environmental Engineering is designed for candidates holding a B.Tech or BE degree in various engineering disciplines, including Chemical, Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Industrial Safety, Petroleum Engineering, Geo-Science Engineering, Fire Safety Engineering, Electronics, and Geo Informatics, with a minimum of 60% marks or equivalent. The program lasts for two years and is governed by the University Grants Commission (UGC), All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), and National Board of Accreditation (NBA).

### Admission Process:
- Candidates must submit an application form, which can be obtained online or offline at the admission office by paying a fee of Rs. 1750.
- An UPES MEET Admit Card will be issued upon successful submission of the application.
- Required documents include self-attested copies of academic records, a Permanent Account Number (PAN), and an Aadhaar Card Number.
- The selection process involves screening of applications and personal interviews.

### Eligibility Criteria:
- Candidates with a B.Tech/BE in relevant fields must have a minimum of 60% marks. GATE or NET qualification is preferred.
- Students must have passed all semesters in the first attempt without supplementary exams and should have a minimum attendance of 75% during the semester.

### Placement Facilities:
- UPES offers placement assistance to enrolled students who meet eligibility conditions, although it does not guarantee placement.
- In the 2021-22 academic year, 760 students were placed, with recruiters including major companies like Airbus, Boeing, Deloitte, Ernst & Young, KPMG, PwC, and Wipro.

### Scholarships and Grants:
- Students with valid GATE scores of 70 percentile and above may receive a scholarship of Rs. 12,400 per month during their registration with the university.
- Additional grants up to Rs. 50,000 are available for outcome-based research as per university regulations.

### Campus and Facilities:
- UPES features state-of-the-art facilities, including three world-class auditoriums, a library, advanced labs, and a 24x7 medical facility with a team of doctors.
- The university provides on-campus hostel accommodations on a first-come, first-served basis, with separate residential blocks for boys and girls.
