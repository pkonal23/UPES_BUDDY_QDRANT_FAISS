Dr. Devnaad Singh is a distinguished academic specializing in supply chain management and operations, with a particular focus on the integration of Industry 4.0 technologies. He holds a Ph.D. in Operations Management and has a robust research background in supply chain resilience, especially concerning the application of AI, Machine Learning, and Big Data Analytics within the Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) sector. His scholarly work has been featured in esteemed journals such as the Business Process Management Journal and the Journal of Enterprise Information Management.

Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the School of Business at the University of Petroleum and Energy Sciences, Dr. Singh brings a blend of academic rigor and practical industry insight. His professional experience includes a management traineeship at Charoen Pokphand Group in Bangkok, where he worked alongside CEOs of major brands like Chester's Chicken, 7-Eleven, and True Communications, enhancing sales performance and operational efficiency. He has also led projects at the Defense Research and Development Organization.

Dr. Singh's teaching philosophy emphasizes professional development and practical application. He has successfully mentored over 60 students, focusing on developing their presentation and research skills. His courses are designed to create dynamic learning environments, incorporating industry visits and practical experiences, which illustrates his commitment to linking theoretical knowledge with real-world applications. He has taught a variety of courses across technical and management disciplines, including:

- Core courses in Humanities for Engineers and Professional Communications at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology.
- Business courses such as Organization Change and Development, IT Tools for Business, and Production and Operation Management at Chandigarh Business School of Administration.
- Principles and Practices of Management, integrating theoretical concepts with practical applications.
- A course on Mentoring and Professional Development, guiding students in their career paths.

Dr. Singh has received recognition for his academic contributions, including the Best Paper Award at the Academy for Global Business Advancement (AGBA) 16th World Congress held at IIT Delhi in 2019. He has also completed a Global Online Certification Course on AI-Powered Supply Chains from NITIE Mumbai and BITS Pilani, and is certified in Digital Transformation of Supply Chain from AICTE. He has participated as a judge for the Smart India Hackathon 2024.

His research interests lie in supply chain resilience, the integration of Industry 4.0 technologies, and the application of AI and Big Data Analytics in operations. He has published extensively, with eight peer-reviewed journal articles, and has presented at international conferences, including the 5th International Conference on Supply Chain Management at the National University of Singapore. Dr. Singh actively engages in faculty development programs and collaborates with various institutions both in India and internationally.
