Dr. Nidhi Chauhan is an esteemed researcher and educator specializing in nanobiotechnology, with a focus on biosensors, nanotechnology, and diagnostics. Her significant achievements include being selected as the ICMR-DHR International Fellow and ranking as the Best Scientist in Nanoscience and Nanotechnology at Amity University. She has also received the SERB International Research Experience Fellow award and has been recognized among the top 2% of World Scientists by Stanford University. Dr. Chauhan has been honored with several prestigious accolades, including a Golden Letter of Appreciation from Nobel Laureate Prof. Barry J. Marshall, the Best Scientist Medal from the International Association of Advanced Materials (IAAM), and the Shakuntala Amir Chand Award from the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR). These awards underscore her exceptional contributions to the scientific community.

Dr. Chauhan is a Senior Associate Professor at the School of Health Sciences and Technology at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), where she actively mentors PhD scholars and Master's students. Her teaching philosophy emphasizes creating an engaging and inclusive learning environment, fostering critical thinking and a passion for scientific inquiry among her students. She has extensive teaching experience in various courses related to bioscience and nanotechnology, including Basic Bioscience, Biochemistry, and Advanced Characterization Techniques.

Her professional background includes previous roles as an Associate Professor and Assistant Professor at the Amity Institute of Nanotechnology, as well as positions at the Indian Council of Medical Research (ICMR) and Teerthanker Mahaveer Dental College. Dr. Chauhan's research has led to over 125 publications in reputable journals, and she serves as a reviewer for numerous esteemed publications, ensuring the quality and integrity of scientific research.

In addition to her academic roles, Dr. Chauhan is actively involved in professional organizations such as the American Chemical Society (ACS) and the Society of Biological Chemists. She also holds editorial positions in journals including MDPI-Molecules and Frontiers in Nutrition. Her commitment to societal advancement is evident in her focus on developing affordable and accessible medical technologies.

For further inquiries, Dr. Chauhan can be contacted via email at **nidhi.chauhan@ddn.upes.ac.in**. Her extensive expertise in nanobiotechnology and biosensors, combined with her dedication to teaching and research, positions her as a leading figure in her field, contributing significantly to the advancement of healthcare accessibility and innovation.
