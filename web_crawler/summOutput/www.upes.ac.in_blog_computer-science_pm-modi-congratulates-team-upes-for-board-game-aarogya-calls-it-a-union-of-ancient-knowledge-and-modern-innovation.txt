Choosing between a Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) Honours degree and a standard B.Sc. degree is a significant decision for students interested in science and technology. The differences in scope, curriculum, and career opportunities are crucial factors to consider. 

The UPES editorial team highlights various educational paths for tech-savvy individuals, emphasizing the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) as a suitable choice for those wanting a deeper understanding of technology. Machine Learning (ML) is identified as a transformative force in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), enhancing decision-making in various fields, including engineering. 

Prominent UPES alumnus Parminder Singh Sethi is noted for his exceptional achievements as a software engineer, holding over 78 patents from the US Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO). UPES has also gained recognition for its participation in Toycathon 2021, where students and faculty presented their innovative gaming ideas to Prime Minister Narendra Modi. The Prime Minister praised their project, highlighting the integration of traditional knowledge and modern technology.

Dr. Ashish Karn from UPES discussed the board game "Arogya," developed as part of the Toycathon, which focuses on themes of fitness and health. The game aims to promote awareness about malnutrition and preventive healthcare, with plans for a simplified version for children.

Furthermore, UPES is investing ₹50 crore to establish an AI Center of Excellence, aiming to drive groundbreaking research and innovation. The university's commitment to fostering tech entrepreneurship is evident, as demonstrated by its alumni securing ₹4 crore in seed funding for a tech start-up.

For students contemplating their future after a B.Tech degree, the options of pursuing an M.Tech or MBA are explored, emphasizing the importance of a solid educational foundation in the rapidly evolving job market. 

In summary, UPES provides a robust platform for aspiring tech professionals through various programs and initiatives, encouraging innovation and collaboration in the fields of computer science and engineering.
