The BBA in Oil & Gas Management (Oil & Gas Operations) program at UPES School of Business is a comprehensive course designed to cultivate skilled managers with an in-depth understanding of the oil and gas industry. This flagship program integrates general management principles with specialized knowledge in oil and gas, ensuring students are well-prepared for real-world challenges. 

### Curriculum Overview:
The curriculum includes a variety of domain-specific subjects such as:
- Natural Gas Business
- Oil & Gas Business Fundamentals
- Refining
- Petroleum Exploration
- Petrochemical Business
- Refinery Management
- Business Analysis
- Financial Management
- Marketing Research

Courses are taught by renowned instructors from around the globe, providing students with a diverse and international perspective.

### Program Features:
- **Hands-On Learning**: The program emphasizes experiential learning through major projects, summer internships, and live industry projects, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge in practical settings.
- **Industry Exposure**: Students benefit from real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by industry experts, enhancing their understanding of practical challenges in the oil and gas domain.
- **Digital Skills Integration**: The curriculum incorporates IT skills, including SAP and SCADA, to prepare students for the industry's digital transformation.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates can pursue various roles within the energy sector, including:
- Oil & Gas Regulatory and Financial Consultant
- Energy Analyst/Subject Matter Expert in IT and Consultancy
- ESG Specialist
- CGD Business Development Manager
- Oil & Gas Transportation and Distribution Manager
- Business Development and Marketing roles
- Procurement Management

With a growing demand for skilled professionals in the oil and gas sector, the program equips students for mid and senior management roles both nationally and internationally. Graduates have secured positions in prestigious firms such as Ernst and Young, PWC, Deloitte, KPMG, SHELL, and others, with starting salaries ranging from INR 3-9 lakh per annum, depending on skills and industry exposure.

### Admission Criteria:
To be eligible for the BBA in Oil & Gas Management, students must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII. Admissions are based on performance in entrance exams like UPESMET-UG, UGAT, or CUET.

### Facilities:
The UPES School of Business is equipped with modern smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and advanced analytical tools to enhance the learning environment.
