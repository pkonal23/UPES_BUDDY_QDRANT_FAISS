The content provides a comprehensive overview of the Bachelor of Legislative Law (LL.B.) program offered by the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). The LL.B. program is designed to equip students with essential legal knowledge applicable across various sectors, including the judiciary, legislative bodies, civil society, and more.

### Key Highlights:

1. **Program Structure**:
   - The LL.B. is a 3-year program, while the integrated BA LL.B. or BBA LL.B. is a 5-year course that combines legal education with humanities or business studies.
   - Specializations offered include Banking & Insolvency Laws, Sports Law, Corporate Law, Taxation Law, International Economic Law, and Disruptive Technologies Law.

2. **Admission Criteria**:
   - Candidates must score the required marks in the UPES School of Law entrance exam (ULSAT) or have a valid score in CLAT or LSAT-India.
   - Students with a merit rank in CLAT or LSAT India are exempt from the Group Discussion and Personal Interview components of the admission process.
   - For BA LL.B. (Hons.) or BBA LL.B. (Hons.), a minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII is required.
   - Up to 20% of seats may be filled based on Board Merit or CLAT AIR Category.

3. **Application Process**:
   - Interested candidates can apply online at [UPES Admissions](https://admission.upes.ac.in/apply) or offline by visiting the admission office and paying an application fee of Rs. 1750.

4. **Career Prospects**:
   - Graduates can pursue various roles, including Legal Manager, Advocate, Legal Advisor, Public Prosecutor, and positions in corporate legal departments, government agencies, and law firms.
   - The university offers a pre-placement boot camp to prepare students for interviews and group discussions.

5. **Facilities and Support**:
   - UPES provides a secure campus with 24/7 medical facilities, transport, and hostel accommodations for students on a first-come, first-served basis.
   - The campus features three world-class auditoriums and extensive library resources with over 200,000 publications.

6. **Important Dates**:
   - The last date to apply for the LL.B. program is February 28, 2025.

7. **Contact Information**:
   - For inquiries related to admissions, scholarships, or other queries, prospective students can reach out via the official UPES website or contact the admission office directly.

In summary, the LL.B. program at UPES is structured to provide students with a robust foundation in legal principles, combined with practical training and placement support, ensuring they are well-prepared for a successful career in law.
