Dr. Aradhya Singh is an accomplished academic currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), having previously held positions at Amity Law School, Amity University, and Law College Dehradun, Uttaranchal University. She was also a Full-Time Residential Research Scholar at the Faculty of Law, Banaras Hindu University. 

Her research interests encompass a wide range of legal topics, particularly focusing on gender studies, environmental law, taxation, and criminal behavior. Dr. Aradhya has made significant contributions to legal academia through numerous published articles in reputable journals. Notable publications include:

1. "An Analysis of the Efficacy of Alcohol Regulations and The Incompetence of the Laws to Prevent Alcohol Misuse," published in the Journal for ReAttach Therapy and Developmental Diversities (November 2023).
2. "Trade Secrets: A Critical Analysis of National Innovation Act," published in the International Journal of Research and Analytical Reviews (May 2019).
3. "Environmental Degradation Vis-à-vis Hazardous Impact of Plastic on the Environment," published in the International Journal of Enviro Legal Research (November 2018).
4. "An Analysis of Gender-Based Intimate Partner Violence and the Defence of Provocation," published in the International Journal of Research and Analytical Reviews (June 2022).
5. "The Orphans of Justice: Children of Incarcerated Parents," published in IMS Law Review: Student's Edition (2022).
6. "Provocation as a Defence to Murder: A Feminist Perspective," published in the Mahamana Student Law Journal (2021).
7. "Role of Feminism in Perpetuating Patriarchal Hegemony While Recovering the Social Subordination of Women," published in UPES LAW REVIEW (2021).
8. "The Ill-Fated Repatriation of the Rohingyas vis-à-vis Citizenship (Amendment) Act, 2019," published in Shodh Drishti (February 2020).
9. "Killing in the Name of Honour in India vis-à-vis Right to Life and Liberty: Contemporary Issues and Challenges," published in Shodh Drishti (January 2018).
10. "Environmental Sustainability in Meghalaya: Traditional and Modern Approaches in Conservation of Water Resources," forthcoming in Lex Terra (June 2024).

Dr. Aradhya has received numerous accolades for her research presentations, including Best Paper Presenter Awards at various national and international conferences, such as the National Seminar on “Revisiting Women-Centric Laws” and the International Conference on Law and Social Transformation.

Her academic credentials include a Ph.D. from the Faculty of Law at Banaras Hindu University, an LL.M. from the National Law Institute University, Bhopal, and a B.A.LL.B. (Gold Medalist) from Law College Dehradun, Hemwati Nandan Bahuguna Garhwal Central University. She is also UGC-NET (Law) qualified.

In her teaching, Dr. Aradhya adopts Piaget’s educational philosophy, encouraging students to learn actively through engagement and exploration. She teaches courses in Taxation Laws, Environmental Law, Jurisprudence, and Gender Studies, among others, and emphasizes the integration of interdisciplinary perspectives to foster critical thinking.
