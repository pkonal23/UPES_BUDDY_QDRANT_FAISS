**Introduction:**

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) Experience Centre is an immersive space designed to provide prospective students with an engaging and personalized experience. The core idea behind the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) Experience Centre (EC) is to establish a positive connection with visitors through a journey of engagement and visualization in an inspiring environment.

**Purpose:**

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) Experience Centre aims to showcase the university's offerings and provide visitors with an opportunity to:

* Meet University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) Academic Experts
* Experience the scenic campus through virtual reality
* Participate in interactive workshops

**Features:**

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) Experience Centre seamlessly combines content, infrastructure, brand message, and technology to create an inviting, engaging, and informative environment for guests. Visitors are actively engaged through multiple content-rich experiences that showcase the unique solutions offered by University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES).

**Services:**

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) Experience Centre provides the following services:

* **Opportunity to meet University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) Academic Experts:** Prospective students can interact with academic experts to gain insights into the university's programs and faculty.
* **Experience the scenic campus through virtual reality:** Visitors can explore the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) campus virtually, experiencing its facilities and environment.
* **Interactive workshops:** University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) Experience Centre hosts interactive workshops for prospects, applicants, and teachers to provide hands-on learning experiences.
