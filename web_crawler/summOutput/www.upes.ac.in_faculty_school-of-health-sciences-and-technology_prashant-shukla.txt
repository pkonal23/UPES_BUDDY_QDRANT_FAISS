Dr. Prashant Shukla is an Associate Professor in the Department of Pharmaceutical Sciences at UPES Dehradun, recognized for his significant contributions to pharmaceutical education and research. His accolades include serving as a reviewer for AAPS Pharmsci360 and receiving the CDRI Director's Special Incentive Award for Excellence in Research. He has also demonstrated exceptional performance in the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) in Pharmaceutical Sciences.

Dr. Shukla teaches a variety of subjects including Pharmaceutics, Biopharmaceutics, and Industrial Pharmacy, where he emphasizes practical details essential for the pharmaceutical industry. His lectures extend to Regulatory Principles, covering current Good Manufacturing Practices, Good Clinical Practices, Good Laboratory Practices, and Intellectual Property Rights.

His research interests focus on drug delivery and pharmacokinetics, particularly in developing new therapeutic options for inflammatory diseases such as psoriasis and enhancing the therapeutic efficacy of poorly soluble drugs through improved solubility and targeted delivery methods. Dr. Shukla actively communicates his research findings through articles and contributions to the broader scientific community. He is dedicated to mentoring students, supervising research projects, and fostering their intellectual growth.

Dr. Shukla's teaching philosophy revolves around creating an engaging and inclusive learning environment that promotes critical thinking and curiosity. He adopts a student-centered approach, tailoring his teaching methods to accommodate diverse learning styles and encouraging active participation. His goal is to inspire students to become independent thinkers and ethical professionals capable of making impactful contributions to their fields.

Dr. Shukla's professional experience spans over a decade in both academia and the pharmaceutical industry, including previous roles as a Scientist at Aurigene Discovery Technologies Limited in Hyderabad and as an Assistant Professor at Amity University Uttar Pradesh.
