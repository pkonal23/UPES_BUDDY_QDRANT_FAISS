He has a rich background that includes extensive experience in academia, the IT industry, and research laboratories. His primary research interests focus on computer vision and deep learning, particularly in developing and optimizing Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) models.

His work included applications in disease detection, identifying bone fractures, and segmenting body regions. He also led initiatives in face detection and biometric authentication, contributing to advancements in healthcare diagnostics and security systems.
