Ms. Himani Nautiyal is an accomplished academic in the field of Pharmaceutical Sciences with 7.5 years of experience. Before her current role at UPES, she served as an assistant professor at the Siddhartha Institute of Pharmacy and SDBIT in Dehradun. She holds an M. Pharm. in Pharmacology from Uttarakhand Technical University, completed in 2013, and is currently pursuing a PhD at UPES.

Her teaching methodology is designed to enhance student understanding of complex concepts, enabling them to cultivate professional mental frameworks in the pharmacy domain. Ms. Nautiyal excels in breaking down intricate problems, making them accessible to her students. She has completed a faculty development program focused on managing online courses and co-creating MOOCs, organized by the Teaching Learning Centre at Ramanujan College, University of Delhi, and sponsored by the Ministry of Human Resource Development and Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya's National Mission on Educators and Instruction. This training informs her innovative teaching strategies, which emphasize discussion, assessment, and activities that foster critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Ms. Nautiyal has a robust research profile, having published numerous research and review articles in national and international journals. She has presented her work at various conferences, earning recognition as the best presenter, and has been actively involved in faculty development programs and seminars. She has served as the convenor for both national and international conferences and has supervised more than 12 B. Pharm. students and six M. Pharm. students in their respective final year and research projects.

Her academic qualifications include qualifying for the Graduate Pharmacy Aptitude Test (GPAT) in 2015 and participating in a PCI-sponsored Continuing Education Programme (CEP) in 2018. Additionally, she attended a 21-day workshop on "Downstream Processing of Enzymes and Proteins" at the CSIR-IIP Campus in Mokahampur, Dehradun, sponsored by UCOST.

In terms of courses taught, Ms. Nautiyal covers a wide range of subjects within Pharmacy, including pharmacokinetics and pharmacodynamics, Human Anatomy and Physiology, Pharmacology and Toxicology, Pharmaceutical Microbiology, and Biochemistry. Her research interests focus on Molecular Pharmacology, Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome, and the use of preclinical animal models to screen the therapeutic potential of herbal drugs.

For further inquiries, Ms. Himani Nautiyal can be contacted via email at himani.nautiyal@ddn.upes.ac.in. She is affiliated with the School of Health Sciences and Technology at UPES.
