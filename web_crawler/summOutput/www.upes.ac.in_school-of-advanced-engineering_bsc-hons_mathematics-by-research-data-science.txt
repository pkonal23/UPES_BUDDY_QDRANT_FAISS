**Program Details**

The **UPES School of Advanced Engineering's B.Sc. (Hons.) Mathematics in Data Science** program is designed to provide students with a comprehensive foundation in the mathematical, statistical, and computational techniques essential for success in the field of data science. This specialized program aims to equip students with the skills needed to analyze complex datasets, extract valuable insights, and collaborate effectively with domain experts to optimize systems.

Students delve into various aspects of mathematics and statistics, including algebra, differential equations, and multivariate statistics. Students are also introduced to cutting-edge technologies like Hadoop and Spark for handling big data challenges. The curriculum emphasizes the integration of mathematical principles with data science applications, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared to address the evolving demands of the industry.

**Program Highlights**

* Strong foundation in both mathematics and data science
* Project-based learning for hands-on experience
* Opportunities for advanced academic pursuits and research
* Dedicated placement support for industry roles
* Assistance for competitive exams like JAM

**Future Scope and Industry Trends**

The program provides a promising future scope in the growing field of data science. With the increasing demand for skilled professionals in machine learning, deep learning, and AI, graduates are equipped to develop and implement advanced models for intricate challenges. The focus on big data analysis opens up opportunities in healthcare, finance, and other sectors. The program bridges the gap between mathematical expertise and practical data science skills, nurturing graduates for success in the data-driven industries.

**Career Opportunities**

Graduates of the program are poised for a diverse range of career opportunities. With their advanced quantitative and analytical skills, graduates can transition into roles as Data Scientists, Quantitative Analysts, Business Intelligence Analysts, Operation Research Analysts, Research Associates, Data Consultants, or Software Developers.

**Placements**

The program offers exceptional placement opportunities. Graduates are well-prepared to excel in the dynamic field of data analysis and interpretation, thanks to a curriculum designed to equip them with a solid foundation in both mathematics and data science. Graduates have consistently secured placements in top-tier companies and organizations, spanning industries such as technology, finance, healthcare, and more.

**Eligibility Criteria**

Prospective students must meet the following eligibility criteria:

* Minimum 50% marks in Class X and XII (PCM/B)

**Selection Criteria**

Selection is based on the candidate's performance in a personal interview.
