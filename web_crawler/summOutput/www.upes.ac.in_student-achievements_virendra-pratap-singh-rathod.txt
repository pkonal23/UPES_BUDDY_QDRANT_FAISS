Virendra Pratap Singh Rathod, a fourth-year student pursuing a B.A. LL.B (Hons. in Criminal Laws) at the School of Law, Batch 1, has received the esteemed Gold Medal in the Justice K.C. Dhulia Memorial Essay Competition. This significant accomplishment was acknowledged by the Hon'ble Chief Justice of India, Dr. D.Y. Chandrachud, highlighting the importance of his achievement within the legal community. 

The details surrounding this accolade reflect both the competitive nature of the essay competition and the high standards upheld by the School of Law. The recognition serves not only as an individual achievement for Rathod but also as a testament to the quality of education and mentorship provided by the institution.
