The South Asian Futures Fellowship Programme (SAFFP), hosted by the Council for Strategic and Defense Research (CSDR) in New Delhi, India, is designed to support 15 Early Career Researchers (ECRs) aged 25-35 from the South Asian region. The focus of the fellowship is to explore topics at the intersection of Non-Traditional Security (NTS) and geopolitics within South Asia. 

The fellowship spans a duration of 12 months, commencing on August 1, 2023, and concluding on July 30, 2024. During this period, the selected Fellows will receive supervision and guidance from the SAFFP Programme Team. A key requirement for five of the selected Fellows includes a commitment to a 30-day residency at a non-domicile South Asian think tank as part of their fellowship experience.

Dr. Pavan Kumar is noted as a selected Fellow for the South Asian Futures Fellowship Programme for the year 2023, indicating his achievement and participation in this prestigious initiative.
