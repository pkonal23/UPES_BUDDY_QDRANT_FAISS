Dr. Alok Aggarwal is a prominent academician in the field of Computer Science and Engineering, currently serving as a Professor at the School of Computer Science (SoCS) at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), Dehradun, India, since April 2018. He has a rich professional background that includes over 25 years of experience, with 21 years dedicated to academia and 4 years in the software industry. 

Dr. Aggarwal has held significant positions in various institutions, such as Professor & Director at JP Institute of Engineering & Technology in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, and Professor & Associate Dean at Chandigarh University, Mohali, Punjab. His teaching portfolio is extensive, covering a wide range of subjects at both B. Tech. and M. Tech. levels, including Advanced Mobile Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Smart Cities, Software Project Management, and many others.

His academic credentials are robust, with a Ph.D. in Mobile Computing from IIT Roorkee (2010), a Master’s degree in Computer Science & Engineering (2001), and a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science (1995). Dr. Aggarwal has published a total of 255 research papers, which include 82 refereed international journal publications, 125 refereed international conference publications, and several patents and books. His research interests encompass Machine Learning, AI, Power Control Management, Mobile Ad-Hoc Networks (MANET), and Wireless Sensor Networks.

Dr. Aggarwal has been recognized with numerous awards throughout his career, including the Star Researcher Award from UPES in 2021 and 2022, and the Best Researcher award at the Global Education and Corporate Leadership Summit in 2019. He has also received the Rajiv Gandhi Education Excellence Award in 2015 and various accolades from The Institute of Engineers and the Meerut Engineers Association.
