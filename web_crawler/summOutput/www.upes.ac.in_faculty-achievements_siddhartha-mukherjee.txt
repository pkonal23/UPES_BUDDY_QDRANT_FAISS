Professor Siddhartha Mukherjee, an esteemed faculty member of the School of Design at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), has been awarded the prestigious HUDCO award for his significant work titled "Dehradoon - An Illustrated Journey of a City." This recognition highlights his exemplary contributions in the fields of Urban Design, Regional Planning, Inner City Revitalization, and Conservation. 

The award was presented to Professor Mukherjee by the Union Minister of Housing and Urban Affairs, alongside officials from HUDCO, during a ceremony held on April 25, 2023, at the Stein Auditorium in the India Habitat Centre, New Delhi. This event celebrated HUDCO's foundation day and marked a significant achievement in Professor Mukherjee's career, underscoring the impact of his collaborative project with the esteemed Ar Maulishree Mishra.

For further information, Professor Siddhartha Mukherjee is associated with the School of Design at UPES.
