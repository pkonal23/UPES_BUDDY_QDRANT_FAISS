Dr. Manjeet Singh Goyat is a distinguished Senior Associate Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), with a robust background in physics, nanoscience, and materials engineering. He holds dual master's degrees in physics and nanoscience & technology, along with a Ph.D. in Materials Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee. His research primarily focuses on polymer matrix nanocomposites, hybrid polymer nanocomposites, and superhydrophobic coatings, among other advanced materials.

Dr. Goyat has been awarded several prestigious research grants, including:

1. **DST SERB-SIRE Fellowship Grant** from the Department of Science & Technology (DST), India, for research at the University of Southern Denmark.
2. **DST SERB-SURE Scheme Grant** aimed at addressing challenges in carbon fibre-reinforced polymer composites, particularly benefiting the aerospace sector.
3. **Seed Funding Project** from UPES to enhance the longevity of flexible Li-ion batteries.
4. An **International Travel Grant** from DST, India, which allowed him to participate in a conference in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

His research interests encompass a wide range of topics, including Polymer Nanocomposites, Hybrid Nanocomposites, Novel Nanomaterials, Superhydrophobic Coatings, Liquid Body Armor Systems, and Transparent Conducting Electrodes.

Dr. Goyat has a rich teaching history, having started as an Assistant Professor in August 2013 and gradually advancing through various academic ranks to his current position. He teaches courses in Composite Materials, Aircraft Materials, Quantum Mechanics, and more, employing a teaching philosophy that emphasizes inquiry, critical thinking, and real-life applications of scientific principles. He encourages students to explore multiple solutions and adopt innovative approaches to learning.

In addition to his teaching responsibilities, Dr. Goyat has made substantial contributions to the academic community through his research, having published over 60 peer-reviewed papers and delivered 20 speeches at international conferences. He is also actively involved in mentoring emerging researchers, having supervised one Ph.D. student and guided numerous undergraduate projects, including those from international students.

Dr. Goyat can be contacted via email at **msgoyat@ddn.upes.ac.in**. His commitment to advancing materials engineering and fostering scientific collaboration underscores his valuable role in academia and research.
