Shimali, a Ph.D. scholar from the School of Health Sciences and Technology, was selected for a one-week hands-on training program at the CSIR-IHBT (Council of Scientific and Industrial Research - Institute of Himalayan Bioresource Technology). Her selection was particularly notable as it was based solely on the quality of her statement of purpose, standing out among 107 participants. The training focuses on 'Entrepreneurship Skill Development on Enzyme Bioprocessing.' 

The program had a total of 20 available seats, indicating a competitive selection process. The mentor for this training is Dr. Piyush Kumar, who is associated with the School of Health Sciences & Technology.
