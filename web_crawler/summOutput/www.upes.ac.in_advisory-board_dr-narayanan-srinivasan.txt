Dr. Narayanan Srinivasan serves as the Professor and Head of the Department of Cognitive Science at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur. Prior to his current role, he dedicated 17 years to the Centre of Behavioural and Cognitive Sciences at the University of Allahabad. His research focuses on various mental processes, with particular emphasis on consciousness, attention, emotions, and decision-making, employing a range of methodologies to explore these areas. Dr. Srinivasan is recognized as a fellow of several prestigious organizations, including the Association for Psychological Science, the National Academy of Psychology (India), and the Psychonomic Society.
