The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) program, which is a four-year undergraduate course designed to provide students with a comprehensive foundation in various engineering and technology disciplines. The curriculum integrates theoretical knowledge with practical applications through laboratory work and projects, preparing graduates for careers in diverse fields such as IT, manufacturing, telecommunications, aerospace, and more.

### Key Features of the B.Tech. Program at UPES:

1. **Specializations Offered**:
   - Aerospace Engineering
   - Chemical Engineering
   - Civil Engineering
   - Computer Science Engineering
   - Electrical Engineering
   - Electronics and Communication Engineering
   - Mechanical Engineering
   - Biotechnology
   - VLSI Design and Technology
   - Fire and Safety Engineering
   - Applied Petroleum Engineering
   - And more specialized fields like AI & ML, Cloud Computing, and Cyber Security.

2. **Eligibility Criteria**:
   - Candidates must have a minimum of 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with at least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) in Class XII.
   - Admissions can be based on scores from national-level entrance exams such as JEE Mains, UPESEAT (UPES Engineering Aptitude Test), SAT, or CUET.

3. **Career Opportunities**:
   - B.Tech. graduates are highly sought after in various sectors including government and defense, research organizations, and can pursue higher education (Master's or Doctoral programs). The demand for B.Tech. graduates is anticipated to increase, providing a promising career trajectory in technology and innovation.

4. **Facilities and Campus Life**:
   - UPES boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including three world-class auditoriums, advanced laboratories, and a secure campus with round-the-clock monitoring. It also offers accommodation options for students on a first-come, first-serve basis.

5. **Global Exposure**:
   - UPES provides opportunities for international exposure through partnerships with universities in countries like the UK, New Zealand, and Australia.

6. **Accreditation**:
   - All B.Tech. programs at UPES are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and other international bodies.

7. **Application Process**:
   - Interested candidates can apply online or in person at the admission office by paying an application fee of Rs. 1750. The last date to apply is February 25, 2025.
