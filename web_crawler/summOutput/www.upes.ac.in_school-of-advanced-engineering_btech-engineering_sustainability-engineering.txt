The B.Tech. in Applied Petroleum Engineering offered at UPES School of Advanced Engineering provides students with a robust education, industry exposure, and practical training, preparing them for careers in the dynamic aerospace and petroleum sectors. The school boasts state-of-the-art laboratories, including structural, geotechnical, fluid mechanics, environmental engineering, and avionics labs, among others, which facilitate hands-on learning experiences.

**Eligibility Criteria:**
To enroll in the B.Tech. program, students must have a minimum of 50% marks in Class X and XII, along with 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) in Class XII.

**Career Opportunities:**
Graduates can pursue careers in various fields such as aerospace, aviation, chemical manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and environmental engineering. The program prepares students for roles in research and development organizations, defense establishments, and private industries related to oil and gas exploration, construction, and more. **Industry Collaboration:**
UPES has established partnerships with leading companies like L&T, ONGC, Tata Power, and Airbus, which enhances students' internship and project opportunities. The curriculum is developed in consultation with industry experts to ensure relevance and readiness for the workforce.

**Placement Support:**
The university boasts a placement record of over 90%, offering students opportunities to interact with top recruiters during its annual placement drives. Students receive additional support through personality enhancement programs and pre-placement training for interviews and group discussions.

**Facilities and Learning Approach:**
The School emphasizes experiential learning, innovative teaching methods, and real-world project assignments. It offers a variety of specializations within the B.Tech. program, including Aerospace Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and VLSI Design and Technology. The curriculum encourages creative problem-solving and critical thinking.

**Hostel Facilities:**
UPES provides on-campus hostel accommodation for students on a first-come, first-served basis, with separate facilities for boys and girls.

**Contact Information:**
For more information about the programs, eligibility criteria, fee structure, and hostel facilities, prospective students can visit the UPES School of Advanced Engineering's official website or contact the admissions office directly. 

In summary, pursuing a B.Tech. at UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a comprehensive educational experience, industry connections, and strong placement support, making it a compelling choice for aspiring engineers.
