Dr. Panduranga Ravi Teja is an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science, specializing in advanced networking and telecommunications, particularly in the development and optimization of 5G network technologies. His research is characterized by a multidisciplinary approach, involving international collaborations and industry consultations, aimed at enhancing telecommunications technology through innovative device-to-device communication and resource allocation strategies.

Dr. Ravi Teja's academic career includes significant roles in various institutions: he served as a Research Fellow in the Department of Information Technology at NIT Raipur from 2018 to 2022, and prior to that, he was an Assistant Professor in the Department of ECE at KL University, Vijayawada (2017-2018), and at Kamala Institute of Technology and Sciences, Huzurabad (2012-2017).

His teaching focuses on the practical applications of theoretical concepts in courses related to 5G networks, Probability and Random Process, Statistics, Engineering Mathematics, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning (AI/ML). Dr. Ravi Teja emphasizes the integration of hardware and software in creating interconnected environments, with additional expertise in Digital Electronics, Wireless Sensors and Networks, and the Internet of Things (IoT). His teaching philosophy is centered on engaging students through hands-on experiences and complex problem-solving to prepare them for future challenges in engineering and technology.

Dr. Ravi Teja has contributed significantly to scholarly activities, publishing numerous papers in SCI and SCOPUS indexed journals, focusing on resource allocation in 5G networks and the application of machine learning in telecommunications. His research employs game theory as a core tool to optimize network interactions, enhance efficiency, reliability, and sustainability, which is crucial for the development of smart city infrastructures and next-generation telecommunications services.
