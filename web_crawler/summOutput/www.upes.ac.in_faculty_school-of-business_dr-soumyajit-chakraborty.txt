Professor Soumyajit Chakraborty is an Assistant Professor at the School of Business at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). His academic journey includes a post-doctoral fellowship at the Centre for Studies in Social Sciences Calcutta (CSSSC) and a faculty position in the Department of Economics at the University of New Mexico (UNM), where he also completed his Ph.D. in economics. Prior to his tenure at UNM, he worked at Ranaghat College of the University of Kalyani and the Institute of Development Studies in Kolkata (IDSK).

Prof. Chakraborty brings a unique perspective shaped by his upbringing in Calcutta and education in Albuquerque. He advocates for a liberal education and prefers interactive classroom dialogues over traditional homework assignments, occasionally taking on the role of a learner himself. He identifies five key objectives for effective teaching: fostering understanding over rote memorization, valuing student diversity, providing thought leadership and approachability, gaining personal insights from the teaching experience, and promoting the development of human capital.

His accolades include the UNM Leadership Award for outstanding student mentorship, various scholarships during his academic pursuits, and multiple awards in economics, including the Gerald and Vivian Boyle Memorial Award and the Ruddar Dutt Young Scholar in Labor Economics Award. He also holds gold medals for his bachelor's and master's degrees in economics from Visva-Bharati (Central) University.

Prof. Chakraborty's teaching expertise covers a range of economics disciplines, including Applied Microeconomics, Public Policy, and Development Economics, among others. He has a specific focus on development and public economics, with research interests that explore human capital accumulation and its interplay with various markets in emerging economies, healthcare access, and socio-ecological issues related to return migration. His academic credentials include a Ph.D. from UNM, an M.Phil. from the University of Calcutta, and both M.A. and B.A. (Honors) from Visva-Bharati.

In addition to his teaching and research, he collaborates with a diverse group of professionals, including economists and sociologists, on multidisciplinary projects addressing issues such as access to drinking water and financial inclusion. Prof. Chakraborty is affiliated with the Global Labor Organization (GLO) and serves on the board of the Himalayan Policy Research Consortium (HPRC). He is also dedicated to mentoring disadvantaged undergraduate students in their pursuit of advanced degrees.

For contact, Professor Soumyajit Chakraborty can be reached via email at soumyajit.c@ddn.upes.ac.in. His interests outside of academia include culinary exploration, music, and behavioral photography.
