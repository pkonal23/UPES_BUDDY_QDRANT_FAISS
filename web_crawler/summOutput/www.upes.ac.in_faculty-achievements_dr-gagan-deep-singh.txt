A nine-day workshop on "Artificial Intelligence and Coding" was successfully held at the District Institute of Education and Training (DIET) in Dehradun, organized in alignment with the National Education Policy 2020. The workshop took place from March 2, 2023, and was aimed at enhancing the skills of teachers through innovative educational activities under the Samagra Shiksha initiative.

Dr. Gagan Deep Singh, an Assistant Professor at the School of Computer Science, UPES Dehradun, served as a mentor during this training program. The workshop encompassed a variety of topics, including cyber security, coding fundamentals, and the role of artificial intelligence within the framework of the National Education Policy. Participants engaged in hands-on activities that allowed them to create interactive stories, games, and animations, providing them with practical experience in these areas.

The initiative was well-received, with Dr. Rakesh Jugran, the Principal of DIET Dehradun, expressing appreciation for the program and indicating the possibility of future collaborations for additional training initiatives.
