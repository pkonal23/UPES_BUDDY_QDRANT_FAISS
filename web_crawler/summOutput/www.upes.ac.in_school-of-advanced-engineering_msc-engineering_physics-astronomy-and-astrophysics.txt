The document outlines the M.Sc. program in Physics with a specialization in Astronomy and Astrophysics offered by the UPES School of Advanced Engineering. This program is designed for students passionate about exploring the universe and aims to provide a comprehensive understanding of astronomical phenomena and technologies.

### Program Overview:
- **Specialization**: Astronomy & Astrophysics
- **Course Structure**:
  - **Semester 1**: Introduction to Computational Physics, Mathematical Physics
  - **Semester 2**: Classical Mechanics, Electrodynamics
  - **Semester 3**: Statistical Mechanics, Advanced Quantum Mechanics, Atomic, Molecular & Laser Physics, Planetary Sciences
  - **Semester 4**: Dissertation, Summer Internship, and a focus on Condensed Matter Physics and Digital Electronics.
  
### Eligibility Criteria:
- Minimum of 50% in 10th and 12th grades (with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology) and 50% in graduation in a relevant subject.

### Unique Features:
- **MoU with ARIES**: The program has an exclusive Memorandum of Understanding with the Aryabhatta Research Institute of Observational Sciences (ARIES) in Nainital, allowing students to engage in experiments and research alongside scientists.
- **Practical Exposure**: The curriculum emphasizes hands-on experience with advanced observational technologies, including telescopes and satellites, to analyze cosmic phenomena.

### Career Opportunities:
Graduates can pursue various career paths, including:
- Academic roles (teaching faculty at universities)
- Planetarium instructors
- Data scientists specializing in astronomical data analysis
- Research associates or scientists in observatories or research institutions
- Astronomical instruments engineers, contributing to the development of scientific instruments used in space exploration.

### Industry Trends:
The program acknowledges the growing space tourism industry and the demand for skilled professionals in astronomical techniques and data analysis, indicating a promising future for graduates in this field.

### Placement Support:
UPES offers robust placement support, with a strong track record of graduates securing positions in esteemed organizations and research institutions, thanks to the school's industry connections and comprehensive curriculum.
