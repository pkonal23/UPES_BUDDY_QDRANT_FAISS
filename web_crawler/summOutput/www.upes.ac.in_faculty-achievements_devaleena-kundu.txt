Devaleena Kundu, a faculty member at the School of Liberal Studies and Media, recently showcased her research at an International Symposium held at the University of Edinburgh. She presented a paper titled "Graves Matter: Photographing Cemeteries and their Imperial Legacy," which explored the evolving aesthetics of mourning rituals and commemoration as reflected in colonial-era cemeteries in India. This presentation contributed to the symposium's broader theme of "Photographs and Afterlives."
