Dr. Amrita Chaskar is an Assistant Professor in the School of Business at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). She holds a Ph.D. in crisis communication from BITS Pilani K K Birla Goa Campus, which she completed in April 2024. Prior to her current role, Dr. Chaskar served as an Academic Associate in Communication at IIM Indore and has approximately two years of industry experience in Human Resources.

Her research interests focus primarily on organizational crisis communication, particularly the strategies employed by the hospitality industry in Goa during the COVID-19 pandemic. Dr. Chaskar has published several research articles in reputable Scopus-indexed international journals and aims to expand on her doctoral findings to explore further aspects of crisis communication and its implications for organizational resilience and reputation.

In recognition of her teaching abilities, Dr. Chaskar received the Best Teaching Assistant Award from the Teaching Learning Centre at BITS Goa during her doctoral studies, underscoring her commitment to creating a positive learning environment. Her teaching philosophy emphasizes a transformative educational experience that fosters empathy, nurtures individual growth, and empowers students to reach their full potential.
