**Career Opportunities in Chemical Engineering:**
Chemical engineers in the pharmaceutical sector can pursue diverse roles, including:
1. **Drug Development and Process Design** - Innovating new drug formulations and delivery methods.
2. **Manufacturing and Quality Control** - Ensuring compliance with industry standards and optimizing production processes.
3. **Equipment Design and Optimization** - Designing and optimizing machinery for efficient pharmaceutical manufacturing.
4. **Regulatory Compliance and Safety** - Developing protocols to minimize emissions and manage hazardous materials.
5. **Research and Innovation** - Collaborating on advanced drug delivery systems and exploring new manufacturing technologies.
