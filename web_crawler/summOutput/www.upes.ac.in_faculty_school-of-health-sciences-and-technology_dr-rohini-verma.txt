Dr. Rohini Verma is an accomplished Assistant Professor at the School of Health Sciences and Technology, with a robust background in natural products, particularly focusing on their role in combating lifestyle disorders. She has a total of 8 years of experience in this field, having earned her Master's and Ph.D. from the National Institute of Pharmaceutical Education and Research (NIPER), where she specialized in the identification and isolation of novel compounds from natural sources.

Her recent publication, co-authored with S. Gupta, is titled "Targeting angiogenesis, inflammation, and oxidative stress in obesity," featured in the book "Targeting Angiogenesis, Inflammation, and Oxidative Stress in Chronic Diseases" (2024, pp. 27-56). Dr. Verma is also credited with a US Patent for a "Valerian composition for sleep management" (20221002782), which is currently filed under OmniActive Health Technologies.

Dr. Verma's professional journey includes a significant role as a Senior Expert in Extraction R&D at OmniActive Health Technologies in Pune from 2021 to 2022, where she was involved in developing herbal phytopharmaceuticals that meet regulatory standards in the EU and the US. She has presented her research at numerous national and international conferences, showcasing her commitment to advancing knowledge in her field. 

Her scholarly contributions include publications in reputable journals, such as "Natural Product Research" and "Medicinal Chemistry." Some notable works include her 2023 paper on the "Isolation and biological evaluation of demethylcassiarin B from Senna siamea Lam."

Dr. Verma's teaching philosophy emphasizes student-centered learning, fostering an interactive and collaborative environment that encourages critical thinking and active engagement. She teaches various courses, including Herbal Drug Technology, Pharmacognosy and Phytochemistry, Standardization of Herbal Drugs, Pharmacotherapeutics, Pharmacology, Global Regulation of Clinical Trials, and Management of Clinical Trials.

Dr. Verma's academic accolades include fully funded opportunities to present at the Second Russian GxP Summit in Sochi (June 2018), as well as qualifying for the NIPER Fellowship for both her Master's (2013) and Ph.D. (2015) programs, and the Graduate Pharmacy Aptitude Test (GPAT) in 2013.
