The MBA Power Management program at the School of Business, UPES, offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to prepare students for leadership roles in the power and renewable energy sectors. The program emphasizes hands-on learning through internships, live projects, and strong industry connections, allowing students to gain valuable experience and insights into corporate culture.

### Career Opportunities
Graduates of this program can pursue various roles in the Energy Sector, including:
- Power and Green Energy Regulatory and Financial Consultant
- Power and Green Energy Analyst/SME in IT and Consultancy
- ESG Specialist
- Power and Carbon Trading Expert
- Energy Efficiency Consultant
- Fuel Procurement Analyst
- Green Energy Project Manager
- Sustainability Consultant
- Business Analyst
- Energy Storage and E-vehicle Expert
- Electricity Generation/Distribution Design and Planning
- Procurement Management
- Electricity Network Design and Planning

### Program Structure
The program spans four semesters, covering essential topics such as:
- Sources of Energy
- Business Communication in Power Sector
- Operations Management
- Quantitative Techniques
- Accounting for Managers
- Business Research
- Power Pricing & Power Purchase Agreements
- Regulatory Framework

Students also engage in a major project to apply their knowledge practically.

### Eligibility Criteria
To apply for the MBA Power Management program, candidates must have:
- A minimum of 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.
- Valid scores from national entrance exams (MAT, CMAT, CAT, XAT, GMAT, NMAT) with specific minimum percentile requirements.

### Admission Process
Candidates are selected based on their performance in the UPES Management Entrance Test (UPESMET) or other national level exams, followed by a personal interview.

### Industry Trends and Future Scope
The energy sector is experiencing significant growth, particularly in green energy and digital innovations. India's target of achieving 500 GW of installed renewable energy and green hydrogen production by 2030 creates a high demand for skilled professionals in power management. Graduates can expect entry-level salaries ranging from INR 6 to 30 lakh per annum, influenced by their skills and exposure.

### Facilities and Learning Environment
UPES provides modern smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and access to advanced analytical tools, fostering an ideal learning environment. The program also includes opportunities for student and faculty exchange, research projects, and seminars with global partner management institutes.

### Specializations
The program offers specializations in:
1. **Power Business and Regulation**: Focuses on resource management, power systems, financing projects, and energy policy.
2. **Green Energy and Transition to Sustainability**: Covers sustainable business practices, corporate social responsibility, and the latest trends in green energy.
