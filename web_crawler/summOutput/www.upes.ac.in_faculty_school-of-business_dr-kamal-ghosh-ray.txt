Dr. Kamal Ghosh Ray is a prominent academic and consultant specializing in Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A) and corporate finance, currently affiliated with the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). His teaching portfolio includes subjects such as Cost Accounting, Corporate Valuation, Strategic Financial Management, and Financial Institutions and Markets. 

Dr. Ghosh Ray's research interests focus on topics like the valuation of Indian IPOs, the implications of Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) factors on M&A deal pricing, and the perceived synergy in M&A transactions. He has authored a comprehensive 900+ page book titled "Mergers and Acquisitions: Strategy, Valuation and Integration," and has published multiple peer-reviewed papers in ABDC-B category and Scopus-indexed journals. His scholarly work has been presented at various national and international conferences, including prestigious institutions such as MIT and Queensland University.

With over 47 years of experience in both academia and industry, Dr. Ghosh Ray has held significant positions, including full Professor of Finance at Ahmedabad University and dean/director roles at multiple institutions. His extensive industry background includes over 22 years in M&A consulting, where he has provided training to senior executives from leading corporations like Adani, Coca-Cola, and Exxon Mobil. He was awarded a Senior Honorary Fellowship from the Institute for Mergers, Acquisitions, and Alliances in Vienna in 2015 and is an active member of an international research group on M&A.

Dr. Ghosh Ray employs an interdisciplinary, application-oriented approach in his teaching, utilizing the POGIL (Process Oriented Guiding Inquiry Learning) method and real-time data for financial decision-making. He emphasizes collaborative learning among students and integrates Excel spreadsheets into his finance courses.
