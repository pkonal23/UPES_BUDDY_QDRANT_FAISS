Dr. Juhi Agrawal is an accomplished academic with over 10 years of teaching experience, currently serving as an Assistant Professor (Selection Grade) in the School of Computer Science at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. She has been in this position for the last 8 years and previously worked as a Lecturer in Computer Science Engineering at IMS Unison University from August 2013 to June 2015.

Dr. Juhi holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering and has a solid educational background, having earned her B.Tech in Information Technology from Lovely Professional University (LPU), Punjab, and her M.Tech in Information Technology from Graphic Era University (GEU), Dehradun. Her primary research interests include Flying ad-hoc networks (FANETs), Vehicular ad-hoc networks (VANETs), Sensor networks, Semantic web technology, and Machine Learning. She has made notable contributions to her field, with over 20 publications in SCI and Scopus-indexed journals, including six SCI-indexed articles specifically on FANETs.

Dr. Juhi is committed to enhancing students' foundational knowledge through active participation and practical engagement in the classroom. She utilizes hardware demonstrations, visualizations, and computer programs to simplify complex concepts and places a strong emphasis on hands-on lab work, ensuring that students receive immediate feedback on their problem-solving efforts. Her teaching philosophy is centered around adapting her methods to accommodate diverse learning styles and providing personalized support to her students. She designs innovative assignments and tests that challenge students to think creatively and apply their knowledge to real-world situations.

In addition to her teaching and research responsibilities, Dr. Juhi has been actively involved in academic community service. She is a member of several committees, including the NAAC and NBA auditing and curriculum development team, and has participated in organizing and technical program committees for various conferences, such as NGCT-15 and ICGCET-2016. Furthermore, she serves on the reviewer board for the Journal of Supercomputing (SUPE), published by Springer, and other prestigious journals.
