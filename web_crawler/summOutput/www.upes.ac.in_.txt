**University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)**

**Overview:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is a premier institution dedicated to fostering academic excellence, global connectivity, and entrepreneurial spirit. With a focus on multidisciplinary and holistic learning, UPES provides an immersive and personalized educational experience.

**Academic Excellence:**

Students at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) benefit from the guidance of globally acclaimed and award-winning academicians. The university offers an immersive learning environment that nurtures intellectual curiosity and critical thinking.

**Global University:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) promotes global citizenship through exclusive international collaborations. These partnerships provide opportunities for students to engage with diverse perspectives, expand their horizons, and develop a global mindset.

**Start-up University:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) supports entrepreneurial endeavors through its unique incubator program, "Runway." This program offers globally-renowned expertise, mentorship, and resources to transform business ideas into successful ventures.

**Student Excellence:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is committed to student success. It offers top placements, entrepreneurial mentoring, and other opportunities that accelerate career growth and empower students to make a positive impact on society.

**Purpose:**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) believes in the power of education to create a more equitable and sustainable future. The university implements diverse social initiatives to promote equal opportunities and contribute to global well-being.

**Legacy and Rankings:**

With over 20 years of legacy, University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) has established itself as a leading private university in India. It has consistently ranked among the top universities in the country by QS World University Rankings and NIRF.
