Anjali Bhatt is an accomplished legal educator with a strong focus on corporate law, currently serving as an Assistant Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun. She has a rich teaching portfolio that includes courses such as Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR), Insolvency and Bankruptcy, Company Law, International Taxation, and International Commercial Arbitration. Anjali qualified the UGC-NET examination in 2018, showcasing her commitment to legal academia and is presently pursuing a Ph.D. in Law, concentrating on the intersection of ADR and Artificial Intelligence (AI), specifically examining how AI can be effectively integrated into dispute resolution processes.

Anjali holds dual degrees in Business Administration and Bachelor of Laws (BBA LL.B) with a specialization in Corporate Laws from UPES. She further pursued a Master of Laws (LL.M) with a specialization in Business Laws from the National Law School of India University in Bangalore. Before her tenure at UPES, she enriched her teaching experience at Renaissance Law College in Indore.

Anjali's teaching philosophy emphasizes the importance of project-based learning and interactive lecture sessions. She aims to create an engaging and participatory classroom atmosphere that encourages students to actively engage in discussions, ask questions, and share their insights. By integrating project-based learning into her pedagogy, she promotes critical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration, thus empowering students to take ownership of their learning and become independent thinkers.
