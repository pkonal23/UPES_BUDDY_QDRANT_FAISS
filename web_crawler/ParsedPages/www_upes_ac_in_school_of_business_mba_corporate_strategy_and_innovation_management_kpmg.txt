Program details
The
MBA Strategy & Consulting in Collaboration with KPMG (Corporate Strategy & Innovation Management)
program at UPES is an intensive and carefully crafted course that equips students with the skills and knowledge to excel in the dynamic global business landscape. By focusing on the interplay between corporate strategy and innovation, the program ensures that graduates are well-prepared to take on decision-making roles within diverse business enterprises.
The core courses offered in this program cover a wide range of crucial topics, including Strategic Management, Innovation Management, Corporate Strategy, and more. Through these courses, students gain a deep understanding of strategic thinking, decision-making, and innovation management. The practical components of the program, such as case studies and real-world projects, provide students with hands-on experience, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for success in corporate strategy and innovation management.
Upon graduation, students can explore diverse career opportunities in sectors like transportation, logistics, aviation, and consulting. With their specialized knowledge in business strategy and innovation management, graduates are well-suited for roles such as strategic planners, operations managers, supply chain consultants, and business development managers. Additionally, they may have the chance to work on projects focusing on strategic planning and process improvement within transportation organizations, allowing them to make a significant impact in their chosen fields. Overall, the MBA Strategy & Consulting program in collaboration with KPMG at UPES offers a holistic and practical approach to corporate strategy and innovation management. It provides students with a competitive edge in the job market and prepares them to tackle the challenges of a rapidly evolving business landscape.
Program Highlights
Students undertake major projects (dissertations) and participate in summer internships and live projects with companies to gain practical industry experience.
The
MBA Strategy & Consulting in Collaboration with KPMG (Corporate Strategy & Innovation Management)
program offers flexibility, allowing students to choose career paths in either business or consulting based on their interests and strengths.
Encouragement for student and faculty exchange, research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships, providing students with a global perspective.
Exposure to real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts to enhance practical knowledge and understanding.
Students are equipped with a range of software and technology skills, including SAP, Six Sigma, advanced Excel, and analytics tools like R-studio.
The UPES School of Business is well-equipped with modern smart classrooms, an Analytics Lab, a well-stocked library, and advanced analytical tools to support effective learning.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future of the
MBA Strategy & Consulting in Collaboration with KPMG (Corporate Strategy & Innovation Management)
program is promising, aligning with the dynamic business landscape. Graduates can expect strategic leadership roles, driving growth and competitiveness through corporate strategies. Opportunities abound in innovation management, fostering a culture of innovation and developing new products and services. Change management expertise is highly sought-after, given the rapid pace of change in businesses. Graduates can explore consulting, advisory, and entrepreneurial roles, leveraging their skills for successful start-ups and market opportunities. The program's focus on digital transformation, sustainability, and corporate social responsibility opens diverse career paths. Moreover, in the global business landscape, there are abundant opportunities for professionals with expertise in corporate strategy and innovation management to contribute to international market expansion. Overall, this MBA equips graduates with versatile skills and knowledge to thrive in various industries, ensuring a promising future in the ever-evolving business environment.
Career Opportunities
The
MBA Strategy & Consulting in Collaboration with KPMG (Corporate Strategy & Innovation Management)
program at UPES School of Business opens up numerous career pathways for graduates. Through internships, live projects, and placements, students gain valuable corporate experience and connect with potential employers, increasing their job prospects. The program prepares students for diverse roles across industries, including manufacturing, service, and consulting sectors. Career options include Corporate Development Manager, Strategic Planner, Innovation Manager, Change Management Consultant, Entrepreneur/Start-up Founder, Management Consultant, Business Development Manager, Product Manager, Digital Transformation Manager, and Sustainability Manager. These opportunities allow students to explore their interests and thrive in various business environments.
Placements
The
MBA Strategy & Consulting in Collaboration with KPMG (Corporate Strategy & Innovation Management)
program has witnessed remarkable improvements in placements over the recent years. Notable companies such as KPMG, Amazon, HDFC Bank, Adani, IBM, and many others, present enticing career prospects to MBA students. Additionally, mid-sized start-ups are also eager to hire fresh MBA postgraduates. At The School of Business, the highest salary for MBA freshers reaches INR 30 Lac per annum, while the top 10% average CTC stands at 18.06 Lacs per annum. It's important to note that salary packages are influenced by the candidate's skills, knowledge, and exposure to the industry.

Interested students must fulfill the minimum eligibility criteria for
MBA Strategy & Consulting in Collaboration with KPMG (Corporate Strategy & Innovation Management)
, which is as follows: Minimum 50% marks in Class X, XII and Graduation. Graduation From a recognized University in any stream.
Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for students interested in the
MBA Strategy & Consulting program in collaboration with KPMG (Corporate Strategy & Innovation Management)
offered by UPES depend on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams/ CUET followed by Personal Interview.
Non-Examination Pathways:
Students having a minimum eligible MAT /CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will only be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Valid 50 percentile and above score of CAT 2024/ XAT 2025 A score of minimum 400 or above for GMAT, a score of 120 and above in NMAT 2024 / 25 scores will also be accepted.
