Course Overview
About B.Des. Courses
B.Des. is a full-time undergraduate degree taught in the field of design. The 4-year B.Des degree course is offered in different types of specialisations. It helps students in understanding the theoretical aspect of design elements and their practical application in numerous fields.
The Bachelor of Design (B.Des.) degree equips one with design techniques and skills and also the usage of innovative tools useful in this field. The coursework combines the latest tech innovation with integral designing methodologies to help students make the best use of their expertise. The evolution in B.Des. has resulted in new specialisations which are highly advanced such as Game Design, Graphics & Communication Design, Animation & VFX and more.
B.Des. Specialisations
Core
Specialisation
B.Des.
Game Design
Animation & VFX
Graphics & Communication Design
User Experience and Interaction Design
Product Design
Mobility and Vehicle Design
Interior and Retail Space Design
B.Des. (Global Program) 2+2
2 Year in UPES Campus + 2 Years in Overseas University.
Specialization as per Partner University - Nottingham Trent University
B.Des. Career Prospects
Based on the specialisation that you choose with your UPES B.Des. course, you can explore a plethora of opportunities in sectors such as corporate houses, and media companies or be an entrepreneur with your own fashion house. The university also has a high rate of placement as it has been tied up with big names such as TCS, Infosys Limited, Royal Enfield, Hettich, Siemens Technology Services and many more.
There are numerous job profiles that you can pick from depending on personal interests and skill sets. Each comes with a promising future and a lucrative pay package. Interested students can also further enhance their chances of getting a senior job role by pursuing a master's in the same field. Here are some roles that B.Des. degree holders can easily pursue:
Graphic Designer
Textile Designer
Costume Designer
Weaving Consultant
Fashion Merchandiser

Exam Pathway: Online UPESDAT
Minimum 50% marks in class Xth and class XII.
Followed by an interview and portfolio presentation.
Non-Exam Pathway:
Board Merit: Minimum 60% marks in class Xth and class XII.
Minimum 50% marks in class X and XII with valid JEE Mains/ CUET/ UCEED/ NID/ NIFT scores.
The above eligible students are directly invited to interview and portfolio presentation
