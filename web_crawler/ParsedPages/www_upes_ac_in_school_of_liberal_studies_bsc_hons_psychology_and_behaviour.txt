**Bachelor of Science (Honours) Psychology and Behaviour Program at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)**

**Program Overview**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a Bachelor of Science (Honours) in Psychology and Behaviour program, providing students with a comprehensive understanding of mental processes and human behaviour. This program follows a multidisciplinary approach, integrating knowledge from various psychological domains, including clinical, developmental, organizational, cognitive, social psychology, cognitive neuropsychology, and organizational behaviour.

**Key Features**

The Bachelor of Science (Honours) Psychology and Behaviour program at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) emphasizes research-led teaching and provides access to a state-of-the-art psychology lab equipped with cutting-edge technology, including EEG, virtual reality headsets, psychometric assessments, and data analysis tools. Students have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in these areas.

A distinctive feature of this program is its focus on student-led initiatives that foster well-being. Students engage in real-world applications of psychological principles, contributing to societal challenges and the well-being of individuals and communities. They actively promote mental health awareness both within the university and the local community through the student-led newsletter "Mental Health Matters."

Students are encouraged to explore congruencies with their core fields of study by undertaking subjects from allied fields such as Political Science, History, Sociology, Mathematics, and Statistics. They also have the opportunity to attend research seminars, lecture series, conferences, and masterclasses facilitated by renowned academicians. This exposure enables them to stay abreast of advancements in their chosen field and gain practical experience in applying theoretical concepts.

**Assessment and Evaluation**

Students' progress is assessed through diverse methods such as presentations, posters, role plays, and podcasts. This multifaceted approach fosters their overall development and ensures a comprehensive learning experience.

**Career Opportunities**

Graduates of the Bachelor of Science (Honours) Psychology and Behaviour program at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) have a wide range of career paths open to them, including clinical psychology, counseling, research, education, organizational behaviour, cognitive neuroscience, and more.

**Eligibility Criteria**

Interested students must meet the following minimum eligibility criteria:

* 50% marks in both 10th and 12th grade in any stream.

**Selection Process**

Selection to the program is highly competitive, with an intake limited to 40 students. The selection process includes:

* Profile-based shortlisting.
* Interview with an Expert Panel.

For further information, prospective students can contact University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) directly through the enquiry form provided on the program website.
