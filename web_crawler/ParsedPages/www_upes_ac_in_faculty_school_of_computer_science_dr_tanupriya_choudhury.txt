Dr. Tanupriya Choudhury

Professor

Profile Summary
Dr. Tanupriya Choudhury is a full-time Professor at UPES, Dehradun, specializing in AI-ML Applications, Geo-Informatics, Brain Computing, Cloud Computing, and Disease Detection. With more than 15 years of academic and research experience, he has made significant contributions to advancing AI and machine learning applications. Dr. Choudhury has authored numerous publications in high-impact journals and conferences and is the recipient of prestigious awards for his innovative work. His research endeavors include leveraging cutting-edge technologies to address real-world challenges. A dedicated mentor and educator, he strives to inspire and shape the next generation of technologists. His LinkedIn can be found

Work Experience
Dr. Tanupriya Choudhury has held esteemed positions in academia, including roles as Professor and Associate Dean (Research) at Graphic Era University Dehradun, Professor at Symbiosis International Deemed University (Pune), Amity University Uttar Pradesh, Dronacharya College of Engineering Gurgaon, Lingaya’s University- Faridabad, Babu Banarasi Das Institute of Technology- Ghaziabad, SYSCON Solutions Pvt. Ltd., Kolkata etc.. Currently, he is a full-time Professor at UPES, Dehradun, at the School of Computer Science. His career highlights include leading impactful research projects, publishing extensively, and fostering academic excellence through innovative pedagogy and mentorship.
Research Interests
Dr. Choudhury’s research interests encompass AI-ML applications, specified algorithms, geoinformatics, AI in healthcare, brain computing, cloud computing, verifiable computing, etc. He is passionate about exploring machine learning and deep learning methodologies to solve complex problems in diverse domains.
Teaching Philosophy
Dr. Choudhury believes in fostering an interactive and collaborative learning environment. His teaching philosophy emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and the practical application of concepts. By integrating real-world examples and cutting-edge research into his pedagogy, he inspires students to excel academically and professionally in their career path.
Courses Taught
Dr. Choudhury has taught advanced courses in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Geo-Informatics, Business Intelligence, Computer Graphics, Statistical Analysis, Soft Computing, Programming Fundamentals, Data Structure, and Data Science. His teaching portfolio strongly emphasizes integrating theoretical knowledge with hands-on application to prepare students for industry challenges.
Awards and Grants
Dr. Choudhury has received accolades such as the National Research Excellence Award and the Best Paper Award at international conferences. He has secured research grants for AI-driven healthcare, IoT, and sustainable computing projects, showcasing his commitment to innovation and societal impact. His accolades can be found here.
Scholarly Activities
Dr. Choudhury has published good-quality research articles in prestigious journals, including IEEE, Elsevier, and Springer. His work has been cited extensively, reflecting its global impact. He has served as a keynote speaker, peer reviewer, and editorial board member for reputed journals. Dr. Choudhury actively collaborates with academia and industry to advance research in AI, Geo-Informatics, and healthcare innovation, demonstrating his dedication to academic excellence and interdisciplinary engagement.
Contact
tanupriya@ddn.upes.ac.in
