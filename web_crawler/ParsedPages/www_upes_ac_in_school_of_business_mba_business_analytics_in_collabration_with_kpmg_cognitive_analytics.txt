**MBA in Business Analytics with a Specialization in Cognitive Analytics**

**Offered by:**

* University of Petroleum and Energy Studies School of Business
* KPMG

**Program Highlights:**

* Cutting-edge curriculum designed to equip students with specialized knowledge in Cognitive Analytics.
* Focus on data visualization, natural language processing (NLP), and advanced analytics.
* Emphasis on real-world application and industrial relevance.
* Integration of cognitive technology into the curriculum.
* Alignment with the demands of the contemporary business environment.

**Program Structure:**

* **Semester 1:** Organizational Behavior, Marketing Management, Managerial Economics, Accounting for Managers, Quantitative Techniques for Managers, Business Communication, Programming for Analytics, Business Environment
* **Semester 2:** Human Resource Management, Financial Management, Operations Management, Predictive Modeling, Business Research Methods, Industrial Visit, Business Ethics and CSR, Managing Digital Transformation, Specialization Paper-II, Business Analytics
* **Semester 3:** Organizational Behavior, Marketing Management, Managerial Economics, Accounting for Managers, Quantitative Techniques for Managers, Business Communication, Programming for Analytics, Business Environment
* **Semester 4:** Social & Web Analytics, Innovation and Entrepreneurship, Dissertation, Environmental, Social and Governance, AI for Managers

**Eligibility Criteria:**

* Minimum 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation.
* Graduation From a recognized University in any stream.

**Selection Criteria:**

* Performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams
* Personal Interview

**Career Opportunities:**

* Cognitive Analytics Consultant
* NLP Engineer/Analyst
* Data Visualization Expert
* Analytics Project Manager
* Chief Analytics Officer (CAO)

**Placements:**

* Graduates are equipped for roles in Cognitive Analytics Consulting, Natural Language Processing, and Data Visualization.
* Salary packages are commensurate with candidates' expertise, experience, and exposure in Cognitive Analytics.
