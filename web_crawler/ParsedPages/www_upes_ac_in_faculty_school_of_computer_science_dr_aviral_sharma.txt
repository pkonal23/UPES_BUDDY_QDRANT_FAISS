Assistant Professor (Senior Scale)

Dr. Aviral Sharma

Assistant Professor (Senior Scale)

Profile Summary
Dr. Aviral Sharma holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Amity University, Noida, an M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence from UPES, and a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering from Shri Mata Vaishno Devi University. Throughout his career, he has published various research papers in reputable journals and has had the privilege of presenting his findings at international conferences and workshops.
Work Experience
Dr. Aviral Sharma is an Assistant Professor with extensive experience in academia. He has held positions as an Assistant Professor at several esteemed institutions. Prior, to joining UPES, He was working as Data Scientist with PrimeTrade.ai. He has worked as a consultant with companies like EY. His diverse teaching experiences have enriched his understanding of various educational environments, allowing him to effectively engage students and foster their intellectual growth.
Research Interests
Dr. Aviral Sharma’s research focuses on various aspects of Artificial Intelligence, with a particular emphasis on Financial Markets. He, in collaboration with his colleagues, has published a series of impactful papers in prestigious journals. Their work showcases the application of innovative techniques and algorithms to solve challenging problems in Finance, HFT, and quantitative trading.
Teaching Philosophy
Dr. Aviral Sharma's approach to teaching revolves around establishing a vibrant and captivating educational setting that nurtures students' inquisitiveness and analytical skills. He places great value on the effectiveness of interactive learning, where students are motivated to engage, cooperate, and put their knowledge into practice in practical situations. Dr. Sharma employs diverse teaching techniques such as lively discussions, experiential tasks, and critical thinking exercises to facilitate a profound comprehension of the subject matter. He underscores the significance of cultivating robust student-teacher connections by encouraging open dialogue and offering personalized assistance to guarantee each student's academic progress.
Courses Taught
Dr. Ajay Kumar has taught courses such as Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Data Structures, Data Communication and Computer Networks, Big Data with Hadoop, and Programming in Java, Python, and C. Through his teaching, Dr. Kumar strives to provide students with a solid foundation in fundamental concepts and practical skills. He emphasizes the importance of understanding algorithmic design principles, implementing efficient data structures, and comprehending the intricacies of data communication and computer networks.
Scholarly Activities
He has published various research papers in reputed journals and conferences. He has published several research papers in reputed SCI indexed.
Contact
aviral.sharma@ddn.upes.ac.in
