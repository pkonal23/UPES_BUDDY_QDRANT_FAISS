**Program Overview**

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers the Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital) - Media Management, Public Policy, and Strategic Communications, a comprehensive three-year undergraduate program. This program provides students with a well-rounded foundation in various aspects of mass media and digital communications.

**Curriculum and Pedagogy**

The curriculum, spanning six semesters, is designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in the ever-evolving global media industry. It combines theoretical knowledge with practical experience, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for the professional world. The program features industry collaborations, providing students with real-world insights and expertise.

**Specialization: Media Management, Public Policy, and Strategic Communications**

This specialization focuses on the crucial role of social media in shaping public opinion and policy decisions. Students learn how to harness social media tools effectively, master content creation, and develop successful marketing strategies. They also gain expertise in managing online presence, engaging audiences, and implementing impactful communication and policy initiatives.

**Career Prospects**

Upon completion of the program, graduates are equipped to excel in various roles within the media, corporate, policy institutions, and startup sectors. Graduates of the program can pursue careers in media management, public affairs, strategic communications, or policy analysis. The program prepares students to become proficient digital storytellers and effective communicators in today's rapidly changing media landscape.

**Program Highlights**

* Industry internships, on-the-job training, and major projects provide practical experience and insights.
* Graduates can opt for diverse job roles in the media and entertainment industry or pursue doctoral research.
* Training from top media professionals ensures students are up-to-date with industry trends and practices.
* Ultramodern studios and lab facilities facilitate hands-on experience in various aspects of digital production.

**Future Scope and Industry Trends**

The future scope of the Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital) program is promising, with trends focusing on data-driven decision making, AI integration, and targeted messaging. Graduates must excel in cross-platform communication, utilizing social media and micro-influencers effectively. Ethics, transparency, and crisis communication are crucial skills, along with advocacy journalism and visual storytelling. By embracing these trends, graduates can navigate the evolving media landscape, shape public discourse, and create positive impacts.

**Career Opportunities**

Graduates can explore diverse career opportunities in the dynamic media landscape, including media managers, communication strategists, public relations specialists, and digital content creators in industries such as journalism, advertising, corporate communication, and public affairs. With a strong foundation in both traditional and digital media practices, graduates are well-positioned to adapt and thrive in the ever-evolving world of communication.

**Placements**

The program offers promising placement opportunities with renowned media houses such as Times of India, Hindustan Times, Indian Express, TV Today, News 18, NDTV, TV9, ABP News, and others. Mid-sized content startups also actively seek graduates from the Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital) program. Freshers can expect starting salaries ranging from INR 4 to 10 lakh per annum.

**Eligibility and Selection**

Interested students must meet the minimum eligibility criteria: 50% in 10th & 12th in any stream. Selection is based on performance in a personal interview.
