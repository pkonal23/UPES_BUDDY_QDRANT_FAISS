**Dr. Achala Shakya**

**Academic Background**

Dr. Achala Shakya obtained her Ph.D. degree from the Computer Engineering Department of the National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra.

**Research Experience**

Dr. Achala Shakya has extensive research experience in the following areas:

* Image Fusion
* Segmentation
* Classification
* Optical, Radar, and Hyperspectral Remote Sensing
* Deep Learning
* Machine Learning
* Natural Language Processing
* Information Retrieval
* Web Development

Dr. Achala Shakya has collaborated on the NASA-ISRO Synthetic Aperture Radar Mission (NISAR) project, titled "Fusion of SAR and optical data for advanced LULC classification." Dr. Achala Shakya has also completed a pilot project on "Kernel-based Noise Clustering Fuzzy Classification" at the Indian Institute of Remote Sensing, Dehradun.

**Teaching Experience**

Dr. Achala Shakya has over 1.6 years of teaching experience. Dr. Achala Shakya is skilled in using innovative teaching methods, such as "flipping the classroom," to engage students in real-world problem-solving.

**Courses Taught**

Dr. Achala Shakya has taught a variety of courses, including:

* Digital Logic and Computer Organization
* Natural Language Processing
* Compiler Design
* Algorithm for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics

**Professional Affiliations and Activities**

Dr. Achala Shakya is an active member of the following organizations:
