**B.Com. LL.B. (Hons.) Program at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies School of Law**

This integrated undergraduate program, spanning five years, combines Commerce (B.Com.) and Law (LL.B.), providing a comprehensive foundation in both disciplines.

**Eligibility Criteria**

- Minimum 50% marks in class X and XII

**Selection Criteria**

- **Examination Pathway:** Admissions through University of Petroleum and Energy Studies Legal Studies Aptitude Test (ULSAT)
- **Non-Examination Pathway:**
  - Common Law Admission Test (CLAT) Merit / Law School Admission Test (LSAT) India Merit - for Bachelor of Arts LL.B. (Hons.), Bachelor of Commerce LL.B. (Hons.), Bachelor of Business Administration LL.B. (Hons.) - Candidates with minimum 50% Marks in Higher & Senior Secondary (10th & 12th) and CLAT All India Rank (AIR) ranking up to 15000/ LSAT India Percentile of 60 percentile or above will be exempted from appearing in ULSAT.
  - Board Merit: for Bachelor of Arts LL.B. (Hons.), Bachelor of Commerce LL.B. (Hons.), Bachelor of Business Administration LL.B. (Hons.) - Candidates with 75% marks in 10th and 12th main five subjects (English as one of the compulsory Subject) are exempted from ULSAT.
- Up to 20% of seats shall be filled through the Board Merit/CLAT All India Rank/L-SAT India Category.
