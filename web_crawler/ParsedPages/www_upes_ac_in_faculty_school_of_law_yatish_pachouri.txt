Dr. Yatish Pachouri

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Dr. Yatish Pachauri is an Experienced Assistant Professor with a demonstrated history of working in the higher education industry. Skilled in Legal Assistance, Legal Research, Legal Writing, and Research. Strong education professional with a Master of Laws – LLM (Gold Medalist) focused on Energy Law and Ph.D. in Renewable Energy Sector specifically solar Sector.
Work Experience
Before joining UPES, Dr. Yatish Pachauri served as Assistant Professor of Law at the School of Law, Woxsen University, Hyderabad, where he played a pivotal role as a founding member. Preceding his tenure at Woxsen, he contributed to the academic landscape at JECRC University, Jaipur, and Jaipur National University, Jaipur. With a wealth of experience in legal education, Mr. Yatish brings a diverse and comprehensive perspective to his role, enriching the academic environment and nurturing the next generation of legal minds.
Research Interests
Energy Law I Intellectual Property Rights I Constitutional Law I Cyber Law I Company Law
Teaching Philosophy
Dr. Yatish Pachauri teaching philosophy revolves around the integration of real cases to enhance practical understanding. Believing that true learning emerges from real-world applications, he structures lessons around tangible examples. By dissecting actual cases, students gain insight into the complexities of their field, fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Mr. Yatish emphasizes the invaluable connection between theory and practice, ensuring students grasp concepts within a context they will encounter in their professional journeys. This approach not only cultivates a deeper comprehension of the subject matter but also prepares students for the challenges they may encounter in their respective careers.
Courses Taught
Dr. Yatish teaches the Renewable Energy Law and Policy, and Company Law. He investigates how the existing policy on Renewable Energy is viable to deal with the challenges faced by RE sector more specifically Solar Sector.
Awards and Grants
Dr. Yatish Pachauri has received the Gold Medal for Best PG student at School of Law, UPES in 2017-2018 for his Academic Excellence.
Scholarly Activities
His work has a strong approach towards RE sector in India. Further His Works also emphasis on the Betterment of Solar Sector by adopting best practices from other countries in Solar Sector in India.
Contact
yatish.pachauri@ddn.upes.ac.in
