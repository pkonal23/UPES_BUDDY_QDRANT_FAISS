Dr. Mohd Imran

Assistant Professor - Senior Scale

Profile Summary
Dr. Mohd Imran is a recognised marketing area professor with his core expertise in marketing analytics, digital consumer behaviour and sustainable marketing domains. He looks forward to sharing the value-added management education with management students and professionals.
Work Experience
Before joining UPES, Dr. Imran was Assistant Professor, Marketing in School of Business, Woxsen University, Hyderabad. Earlier he has also worked in The ICFAI University, Dehradun, Institute of Advanced Management and Research, Ghaziabad and Shri Ram College, Muzaffarnagar  in the capacity of Assistant Professor/Lecturer.
Research Interests
Marketing Analytics I Digital Consumer Behaviour I Sustainable Marketing
Teaching Philosophy
Dr. Imran’s teaching philosophy is grounded in the understanding that it is his responsibility to create a climate that supports and encourages student learning. He has incorporated some elements of “flipping the classroom” into his classes such as pre-reading assignments, in which students read assigned sections of the textbook and/or other resources prior to coming to class where they complete a short quiz. Quiz material is not repeated in class but instead is used as the foundation for in-class assignments that involve students working with real data and situations. Using authentic problems helps students move from novice to expert thinking.
Courses Taught
Dr. Imran teaches marketing management, advanced marketing management, international marketing, consumer behaviour, brand management, integrated marketing communications etc.
Awards and Grants
Dr. Imran received the Best Teacher Award from the IIOR Chandigarh and Global Thinkerz Society Chandigarh. He has also been confirmed as a Best Teacher two times by The ICFAI University, Dehradun. Additionally, He has also been felicitated by many other societies for innovative teaching practices time to time.
Scholarly Activities
Dr. Imran has many research articles, case study, conference/seminar proceedings etc. on his account. He has also presented his scholarly work in several national and international conferences/seminars and case colloquiums etc.
Contact
mohd.imran@ddn.upes.ac.in
