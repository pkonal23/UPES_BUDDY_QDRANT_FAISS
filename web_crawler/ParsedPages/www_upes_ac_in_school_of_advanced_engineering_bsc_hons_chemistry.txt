Program Details
The
B.Sc. Chemistry
program offered by UPES is a comprehensive 3-year course that provides students with a strong foundation in chemistry while also allowing them to specialize in either Polymer Chemistry or Industrial Chemistry. For those choosing the Polymer Chemistry specialization, the program will delve into the science of polymeric materials and composites. Students will gain hands-on experience in preparing polymers in laboratory settings and learn various characterization techniques. Additionally, they will be required to complete a dissertation in this field, enabling them to deepen their knowledge and expertise. The specialization in Polymer Chemistry opens up a wide array of career opportunities in academia, research institutions, government laboratories, and the industry, where the demand for experts in this field is constantly growing.
Alternatively, students can opt for the Industrial Chemistry specialization, which focuses on industrially important chemicals, their impact on the environment, and various analytical techniques. The program will emphasize the adoption of green methods for chemical synthesis and improved chemical processes aimed at energy conservation and pollution reduction. Students will also be equipped with industry-relevant knowledge to contribute towards process efficiency, optimization, quality control, and assurance. Moreover, they will develop hazard and risk assessment capabilities and foster a focus on environmental sustainability. Graduates specializing in Industrial Chemistry will find diverse career opportunities across various industrial sectors.
In conclusion, the
B.Sc. Chemistry
program by UPES not only imparts a solid grounding in chemistry but also allows students to choose a specialized path that aligns with their interests and career goals. Whether they opt for Polymer Chemistry or Industrial Chemistry, students will receive a well-rounded education and practical skills that will prepare them for successful careers in the field of chemistry and related industries.
Program Highlights
3-year
B.Sc. Chemistry
program under NEP 2020 with a total of 160 credits (20 credits in each semester) offering multiple exit and entry options for flexibility and choice.
The
B.Sc. Chemistry
program has diverse curriculum with a mix of major, minor, skill enhancement, ability enhancement, and multidisciplinary courses, providing a well-rounded education in Chemistry.
Research electives and specializations to foster a culture of inquiry and innovation, encouraging students to explore their interests in specific areas of chemistry.
Placement support to help students transition smoothly from academics to the professional world, ensuring career opportunities after graduation.
World-class experienced faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure to facilitate a stimulating learning environment and practical hands-on experience.
Support for JAM (Joint Admission Test for M.Sc. programs in IITs and NITs) preparation and direct application for PhD in the relevant domain for students pursuing the 3-year BSc by research, offering a seamless pathway for higher studies and research opportunities.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The 3-year
B.Sc. Chemistry
program by research at UPES School of Advance Engineering offers students a thorough education, industry exposure, and practical training, preparing them for a prosperous career in chemistry. Graduates of this program have a broad scope of opportunities ahead. They can choose to advance their studies with a Master's in Science (M.Sc.) or pursue a doctorate program. Alternatively, they can opt for job roles in Research & Development or various industries. This program equips students with essential skills and knowledge, opening doors to a promising future in the field of chemistry.
Career Opportunities
Completing the
B.Sc. Chemistry
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up diverse career opportunities for graduates. With a strong foundation in chemistry, students are well-equipped for roles such as Research Scientist, Scientific Officer, and Consultant Chemist, where they can contribute to cutting-edge research and development projects. Moreover, graduates can excel in positions like Quality Manager and Quality Control Chemist, ensuring high standards in industrial processes and product quality. Additionally, opportunities as a Chemical Analyst allow them to delve into analytical aspects. Aspiring educators can pursue a career as Science Educators, imparting knowledge to the next generation. Furthermore, the program nurtures entrepreneurship skills, empowering graduates to forge their path as successful entrepreneurs in the chemical industry.
Placements
The
B.Sc. Chemistry
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering boasts an exceptional track record in placements, offering lucrative opportunities for its graduates. The university's commitment to fostering a strong industry-academia relationship has facilitated tie-ups with leading companies and research institutions, resulting in a high employability rate for its students. Through rigorous academics, practical training, and exposure to cutting-edge research, students are equipped with the knowledge and skills sought after by employers. The dedicated placement cell further enhances the prospects of students by organizing career fairs, workshops, and internships, thereby providing them with a competitive edge in the job market. As a result, UPES School of Advanced Engineering's
B.Sc. Chemistry
graduates find themselves well-placed in prominent positions across diverse industries.

Program Details
The
B.Sc. Chemistry
program offered by UPES is a comprehensive 3-year course that provides students with a strong foundation in chemistry while also allowing them to specialize in either Polymer Chemistry or Industrial Chemistry. For those choosing the Polymer Chemistry specialization, the program will delve into the science of polymeric materials and composites. Students will gain hands-on experience in preparing polymers in laboratory settings and learn various characterization techniques. Additionally, they will be required to complete a dissertation in this field, enabling them to deepen their knowledge and expertise. The specialization in Polymer Chemistry opens up a wide array of career opportunities in academia, research institutions, government laboratories, and the industry, where the demand for experts in this field is constantly growing.
Alternatively, students can opt for the Industrial Chemistry specialization, which focuses on industrially important chemicals, their impact on the environment, and various analytical techniques. The program will emphasize the adoption of green methods for chemical synthesis and improved chemical processes aimed at energy conservation and pollution reduction. Students will also be equipped with industry-relevant knowledge to contribute towards process efficiency, optimization, quality control, and assurance. Moreover, they will develop hazard and risk assessment capabilities and foster a focus on environmental sustainability. Graduates specializing in Industrial Chemistry will find diverse career opportunities across various industrial sectors.
In conclusion, the
B.Sc. Chemistry
program by UPES not only imparts a solid grounding in chemistry but also allows students to choose a specialized path that aligns with their interests and career goals. Whether they opt for Polymer Chemistry or Industrial Chemistry, students will receive a well-rounded education and practical skills that will prepare them for successful careers in the field of chemistry and related industries.
Program Highlights
3-year
B.Sc. Chemistry
program under NEP 2020 with a total of 160 credits (20 credits in each semester) offering multiple exit and entry options for flexibility and choice.
The
B.Sc. Chemistry
program has diverse curriculum with a mix of major, minor, skill enhancement, ability enhancement, and multidisciplinary courses, providing a well-rounded education in Chemistry.
Research electives and specializations to foster a culture of inquiry and innovation, encouraging students to explore their interests in specific areas of chemistry.
Placement support to help students transition smoothly from academics to the professional world, ensuring career opportunities after graduation.
World-class experienced faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure to facilitate a stimulating learning environment and practical hands-on experience.
Support for JAM (Joint Admission Test for M.Sc. programs in IITs and NITs) preparation and direct application for PhD in the relevant domain for students pursuing the 3-year BSc by research, offering a seamless pathway for higher studies and research opportunities.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The 3-year
B.Sc. Chemistry
program by research at UPES School of Advance Engineering offers students a thorough education, industry exposure, and practical training, preparing them for a prosperous career in chemistry. Graduates of this program have a broad scope of opportunities ahead. They can choose to advance their studies with a Master's in Science (M.Sc.) or pursue a doctorate program. Alternatively, they can opt for job roles in Research & Development or various industries. This program equips students with essential skills and knowledge, opening doors to a promising future in the field of chemistry.
Career Opportunities
Completing the
B.Sc. Chemistry
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up diverse career opportunities for graduates. With a strong foundation in chemistry, students are well-equipped for roles such as Research Scientist, Scientific Officer, and Consultant Chemist, where they can contribute to cutting-edge research and development projects. Moreover, graduates can excel in positions like Quality Manager and Quality Control Chemist, ensuring high standards in industrial processes and product quality. Additionally, opportunities as a Chemical Analyst allow them to delve into analytical aspects. Aspiring educators can pursue a career as Science Educators, imparting knowledge to the next generation. Furthermore, the program nurtures entrepreneurship skills, empowering graduates to forge their path as successful entrepreneurs in the chemical industry.
Placements
The
B.Sc. Chemistry
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering boasts an exceptional track record in placements, offering lucrative opportunities for its graduates. The university's commitment to fostering a strong industry-academia relationship has facilitated tie-ups with leading companies and research institutions, resulting in a high employability rate for its students. Through rigorous academics, practical training, and exposure to cutting-edge research, students are equipped with the knowledge and skills sought after by employers. The dedicated placement cell further enhances the prospects of students by organizing career fairs, workshops, and internships, thereby providing them with a competitive edge in the job market. As a result, UPES School of Advanced Engineering's
B.Sc. Chemistry
graduates find themselves well-placed in prominent positions across diverse industries.

Program Details
The
B.Sc. Chemistry
program offered by UPES is a comprehensive 3-year course that provides students with a strong foundation in chemistry while also allowing them to specialize in either Polymer Chemistry or Industrial Chemistry. For those choosing the Polymer Chemistry specialization, the program will delve into the science of polymeric materials and composites. Students will gain hands-on experience in preparing polymers in laboratory settings and learn various characterization techniques. Additionally, they will be required to complete a dissertation in this field, enabling them to deepen their knowledge and expertise. The specialization in Polymer Chemistry opens up a wide array of career opportunities in academia, research institutions, government laboratories, and the industry, where the demand for experts in this field is constantly growing.
Alternatively, students can opt for the Industrial Chemistry specialization, which focuses on industrially important chemicals, their impact on the environment, and various analytical techniques. The program will emphasize the adoption of green methods for chemical synthesis and improved chemical processes aimed at energy conservation and pollution reduction. Students will also be equipped with industry-relevant knowledge to contribute towards process efficiency, optimization, quality control, and assurance. Moreover, they will develop hazard and risk assessment capabilities and foster a focus on environmental sustainability. Graduates specializing in Industrial Chemistry will find diverse career opportunities across various industrial sectors.
In conclusion, the
B.Sc. Chemistry
program by UPES not only imparts a solid grounding in chemistry but also allows students to choose a specialized path that aligns with their interests and career goals. Whether they opt for Polymer Chemistry or Industrial Chemistry, students will receive a well-rounded education and practical skills that will prepare them for successful careers in the field of chemistry and related industries.
Program Highlights
3-year
B.Sc. Chemistry
program under NEP 2020 with a total of 160 credits (20 credits in each semester) offering multiple exit and entry options for flexibility and choice.
The
B.Sc. Chemistry
program has diverse curriculum with a mix of major, minor, skill enhancement, ability enhancement, and multidisciplinary courses, providing a well-rounded education in Chemistry.
Research electives and specializations to foster a culture of inquiry and innovation, encouraging students to explore their interests in specific areas of chemistry.
Placement support to help students transition smoothly from academics to the professional world, ensuring career opportunities after graduation.
World-class experienced faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure to facilitate a stimulating learning environment and practical hands-on experience.
Support for JAM (Joint Admission Test for M.Sc. programs in IITs and NITs) preparation and direct application for PhD in the relevant domain for students pursuing the 3-year BSc by research, offering a seamless pathway for higher studies and research opportunities.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The 3-year
B.Sc. Chemistry
program by research at UPES School of Advance Engineering offers students a thorough education, industry exposure, and practical training, preparing them for a prosperous career in chemistry. Graduates of this program have a broad scope of opportunities ahead. They can choose to advance their studies with a Master's in Science (M.Sc.) or pursue a doctorate program. Alternatively, they can opt for job roles in Research & Development or various industries. This program equips students with essential skills and knowledge, opening doors to a promising future in the field of chemistry.
Career Opportunities
Completing the
B.Sc. Chemistry
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up diverse career opportunities for graduates. With a strong foundation in chemistry, students are well-equipped for roles such as Research Scientist, Scientific Officer, and Consultant Chemist, where they can contribute to cutting-edge research and development projects. Moreover, graduates can excel in positions like Quality Manager and Quality Control Chemist, ensuring high standards in industrial processes and product quality. Additionally, opportunities as a Chemical Analyst allow them to delve into analytical aspects. Aspiring educators can pursue a career as Science Educators, imparting knowledge to the next generation. Furthermore, the program nurtures entrepreneurship skills, empowering graduates to forge their path as successful entrepreneurs in the chemical industry.
Placements
The
B.Sc. Chemistry
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering boasts an exceptional track record in placements, offering lucrative opportunities for its graduates. The university's commitment to fostering a strong industry-academia relationship has facilitated tie-ups with leading companies and research institutions, resulting in a high employability rate for its students. Through rigorous academics, practical training, and exposure to cutting-edge research, students are equipped with the knowledge and skills sought after by employers. The dedicated placement cell further enhances the prospects of students by organizing career fairs, workshops, and internships, thereby providing them with a competitive edge in the job market. As a result, UPES School of Advanced Engineering's
B.Sc. Chemistry
graduates find themselves well-placed in prominent positions across diverse industries.
