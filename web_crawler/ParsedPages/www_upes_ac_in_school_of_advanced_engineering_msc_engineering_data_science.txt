Program Details
The
M.Sc. Data Science
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is a comprehensive 2-year master's program designed to equip students with a strong foundation in mathematics, data analysis, statistical modeling, and machine learning techniques. Throughout the course, students will delve into the world of big data, learning how to efficiently use smart devices and leverage statistical methods to interpret and extract valuable insights from vast datasets. The program emphasizes practical learning and equips students with essential programming skills in popular languages such as Python or R, which are widely used in the data science industry. Moreover, students will be trained in data visualization techniques to effectively communicate complex information in a clear and visually appealing manner, a crucial skill in the modern data-driven world.
A significant highlight of the program is the opportunity for students to engage in real-world data science projects, gaining valuable hands-on experience and working on practical challenges faced by the industry. In the second year, students will have the chance to apply the knowledge and techniques acquired during their coursework to a self-selected data science topic, undertaking a dissertation project that allows them to showcase their expertise and creativity in solving real-world data problems.
Overall, the
M.Sc. Data Science
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a comprehensive and well-rounded curriculum that prepares students to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of data science. Graduates of this program will be equipped with the necessary theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and research experience to make a significant impact in various industries and domains where data-driven decision-making is crucial.
Program Highlights
The
M.Sc. Data Science
program offers a cutting-edge curriculum that covers statistical analysis, machine learning, big data analytics, and data visualization, equipping students with the skills required to excel in the data science field.
Students get the opportunity to work on real-world projects in collaboration with industry partners, gaining practical experience and building a strong portfolio to showcase their expertise to potential employers.
The program is taught by experienced faculty members who are experts in the field of data science, providing valuable insights and mentorship to students throughout their academic journey.
The School of Advanced Engineering at UPES provides access to modern laboratories and resources, allowing students to conduct in-depth research and experiments in data science.
The university offers dedicated career support and placement assistance to students, connecting them with top recruiters and job opportunities in the data science industry.
The
M.Sc. Data Science
program encourages an interdisciplinary approach by integrating knowledge from various domains, fostering well-rounded professionals with the ability to tackle complex data-driven challenges effectively.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The
M.Sc. Data Science
program by UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers promising future prospects for students. With the increasing demand for skilled graduates in mathematics, big data, and data science, opportunities are plentiful in both public and private sectors. The program emphasizes interdisciplinary knowledge to equip graduates with a wide-ranging skill set. Graduates will be well-prepared for a successful career in data science, with specialized skills in machine learning and AI to contribute to the development of intelligent systems and predictive models. Moreover, they can explore employment opportunities in industries where data-driven decision-making and operations are critical. The program also focuses on predictive analytics, business intelligence, and research and development aspects, making it a comprehensive and sought-after choice for aspiring data scientists.
Career Opportunities
Completing the
M.Sc. Data Science
program by UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up a plethora of promising career opportunities for graduates. With a strong foundation in data analysis, statistical modeling, and machine learning, students can embark on roles such as Data Scientist, extracting valuable insights from complex datasets. They can also pursue careers as Data Analysts, examining and interpreting data to facilitate data-driven decision-making. As a Machine Learning Engineer, graduates can design and develop advanced algorithms for predictive models. Additionally, they can become Big Data Engineers, handling vast datasets and optimizing data processing pipelines. Other possibilities include roles as Business Intelligence Analysts, Data Architects, and even entrepreneurship and consultancy opportunities. Furthermore, the program equips individuals to pursue careers in scientific research or academia, contributing to the ever-evolving field of data science.
Placements
The
M.Sc. Data Science
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering has consistently achieved remarkable placements, providing students with excellent career prospects. Through a blend of rigorous academic training and practical exposure, graduates emerge as highly skilled data scientists sought after by leading industries. The university's strong industry connections and dedicated placement cell play a crucial role in facilitating lucrative job opportunities for students. Many renowned companies across various sectors actively recruit from the program, acknowledging the students' proficiency in data analytics, machine learning, and data-driven decision-making. With a track record of successful placements, the M.Sc. Data Science program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering stands as a testament to its commitment to producing competent data science professionals.

Program Details
The
M.Sc. Data Science
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is a comprehensive 2-year master's program designed to equip students with a strong foundation in mathematics, data analysis, statistical modeling, and machine learning techniques. Throughout the course, students will delve into the world of big data, learning how to efficiently use smart devices and leverage statistical methods to interpret and extract valuable insights from vast datasets. The program emphasizes practical learning and equips students with essential programming skills in popular languages such as Python or R, which are widely used in the data science industry. Moreover, students will be trained in data visualization techniques to effectively communicate complex information in a clear and visually appealing manner, a crucial skill in the modern data-driven world.
A significant highlight of the program is the opportunity for students to engage in real-world data science projects, gaining valuable hands-on experience and working on practical challenges faced by the industry. In the second year, students will have the chance to apply the knowledge and techniques acquired during their coursework to a self-selected data science topic, undertaking a dissertation project that allows them to showcase their expertise and creativity in solving real-world data problems.
Overall, the
M.Sc. Data Science
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a comprehensive and well-rounded curriculum that prepares students to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of data science. Graduates of this program will be equipped with the necessary theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and research experience to make a significant impact in various industries and domains where data-driven decision-making is crucial.

program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is a comprehensive 2-year master's program designed to equip students with a strong foundation in mathematics, data analysis, statistical modeling, and machine learning techniques. Throughout the course, students will delve into the world of big data, learning how to efficiently use smart devices and leverage statistical methods to interpret and extract valuable insights from vast datasets. The program emphasizes practical learning and equips students with essential programming skills in popular languages such as Python or R, which are widely used in the data science industry. Moreover, students will be trained in data visualization techniques to effectively communicate complex information in a clear and visually appealing manner, a crucial skill in the modern data-driven world.

A significant highlight of the program is the opportunity for students to engage in real-world data science projects, gaining valuable hands-on experience and working on practical challenges faced by the industry. In the second year, students will have the chance to apply the knowledge and techniques acquired during their coursework to a self-selected data science topic, undertaking a dissertation project that allows them to showcase their expertise and creativity in solving real-world data problems.

Overall, the
M.Sc. Data Science
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a comprehensive and well-rounded curriculum that prepares students to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of data science. Graduates of this program will be equipped with the necessary theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and research experience to make a significant impact in various industries and domains where data-driven decision-making is crucial.

Program Highlights
The
M.Sc. Data Science
program offers a cutting-edge curriculum that covers statistical analysis, machine learning, big data analytics, and data visualization, equipping students with the skills required to excel in the data science field.
Students get the opportunity to work on real-world projects in collaboration with industry partners, gaining practical experience and building a strong portfolio to showcase their expertise to potential employers.
The program is taught by experienced faculty members who are experts in the field of data science, providing valuable insights and mentorship to students throughout their academic journey.
The School of Advanced Engineering at UPES provides access to modern laboratories and resources, allowing students to conduct in-depth research and experiments in data science.
The university offers dedicated career support and placement assistance to students, connecting them with top recruiters and job opportunities in the data science industry.
The
M.Sc. Data Science
program encourages an interdisciplinary approach by integrating knowledge from various domains, fostering well-rounded professionals with the ability to tackle complex data-driven challenges effectively.

Career Opportunities
Completing the
M.Sc. Data Science
program by UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up a plethora of promising career opportunities for graduates. With a strong foundation in data analysis, statistical modeling, and machine learning, students can embark on roles such as Data Scientist, extracting valuable insights from complex datasets. They can also pursue careers as Data Analysts, examining and interpreting data to facilitate data-driven decision-making. As a Machine Learning Engineer, graduates can design and develop advanced algorithms for predictive models. Additionally, they can become Big Data Engineers, handling vast datasets and optimizing data processing pipelines. Other possibilities include roles as Business Intelligence Analysts, Data Architects, and even entrepreneurship and consultancy opportunities. Furthermore, the program equips individuals to pursue careers in scientific research or academia, contributing to the ever-evolving field of data science.
