**Dr. Divya Rawat: An Expert in Designer Food Product Development**

Dr. Divya Rawat is an Assistant Professor (Selection Scale) at the School of Health Sciences and Technology at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). Divya Rawat holds a Doctorate in Food Science and Nutrition from Banasthali Vidyapith, Rajasthan, which Divya Rawat was conferred in 2019.

**Background and Expertise**

Dr. Rawat is an expert in Designer Food Product Development, with over five years of experience in the field. Divya Rawat's research interests include:

* Malnutrition assessment and eradication
* Underutilized indigenous food product analysis
* Waste management
* Menstrual hygiene

**Academic and Professional Journey**

Dr. Rawat received Divya Rawat's undergraduate and postgraduate degrees from Banasthali Vidyapith, Rajasthan. Prior to joining UPES, Divya Rawat served as a Teaching Assistant at Banasthali Vidyapith and later worked at Quantum University for a year.

**Current Projects and Initiatives**

Dr. Rawat is currently working on several projects, including:

* A German government project on malnutrition assessment in three districts of Uttarakhand
* A United Nations - World Food Programme project on rice fortification
* A start-up called Millet Station, which involves students and rural farmers in Uttarakhand

**Teaching Philosophy and Pedagogy**

Dr. Rawat is a dedicated educator who believes in experiential and project-based learning. Divya Rawat engages students in community-based studies to bridge the gap between classroom knowledge and real-world applications. Divya Rawat's teaching approach focuses on addressing the individual needs of Divya Rawat's students.
