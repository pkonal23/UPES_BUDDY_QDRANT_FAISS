**Dr. Hemant Petwal**

Dr. Hemant Petwal is an Assistant Professor (Senior Scale) in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). Dr. Hemant Petwal holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from the Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology (2021).

**Expertise**

Dr. Hemant Petwal specializes in soft computing and optimization algorithms. Dr. Hemant Petwal received a silver medal for his M. Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from Graphic Era University (2015) and secured first position in university ranking for his B. Tech in Information Technology from Punjab Technical University (2010).

**Experience**

Prior to joining UPES, Dr. Hemant Petwal was an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science Engineering and Technology at Bennett University in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh. Dr. Hemant Petwal also served as a full-time Teaching Associate during his Ph.D. at Thapar University, assisting in lectures, tutorials, and laboratory sessions. Additionally, Dr. Hemant Petwal worked as an Oracle developer for Path Infotech Limited for 2.5 years.

**Research Interests**

Dr. Hemant Petwal's research interests include, but are not limited to:

* Soft computing and its applications in real-world aspects
* Optimization techniques
* Machine learning

**Publications and Patents**

Dr. Hemant Petwal has published over seven research papers and articles in SCI/SCIE indexed journals and conferences. Dr. Hemant Petwal also holds two patents, one Indian and one International.

**Memberships**

Dr. Hemant Petwal serves as a reviewer board member for the following publications:

* Computer Modelling in Engineering & Science (SCI Indexed Journal)
* Journal of Scalable Computing: Practice and Experience (Scopus Index Journal)
* IEEE International Conference on Advanced Networks and Telecommunications Systems (ANTS'23) (Scopus indexed Conference)
