Maria Devi Angerhofer

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Maria Devi Angerhofer is working as an Assistant Professor in School of Law, UPES, Dehradun.
Work Experience
Before joining UPES, Prof. Angerhofer had experience of working as a researcher at Indian Law Institute, New Delhi and at Lloyd Law College, Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh.
Research Interests
Constitution I Indian Penal Code I Administrative Law.
Teaching Philosophy
Prof. Angerhofer proudly believes that healthy classroom discussion can bring new thoughts and ideas. She often engages students into current social legal issues. Her teaching philosophy creates a climate that supports and encourages students’ active involvement and participation.
Courses Taught
Prof. Angerhofer teaches Constitution and Criminal Law.
Scholarly Activities
Prof. Angerhofer’ article, "Intellectual Property Rights and Public Policy on the Role of Plain Packaging and Health Care in India, Anil Kumar and Angerhofer, Maria Devi (2021), was published in " Indonesian Journal of International Law: Vol. 18: No. 4, Article 2.DOI: 10.17304/ijil. vol18.4.820 available at https://scholarhub.ui.ac.id/ijil/vol18/iss4/2).
She has participated and presented papers into various National and International Conferences.
Contact
maria.devi@ddn.upes.ac.in
