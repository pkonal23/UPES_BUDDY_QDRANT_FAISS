Nilav Mazumdar
Assistant Professor

Nilav Mazumdar

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Professor Nilav Mazumdar is an expert in the domain of Creative and communication expanding over visual strategy, content planning, brand and advertising. He has been an industry expert for over a decade providing creative and communication solutions to leading brands.
Work Experience
Prof. Nilav Mazumdar has an experience of over 5 years in teaching. Before joining UPES, he was a leading professional in advertising and creative communications, lending his expertise to brands such as Mondelez (formerly Cadbury), FICCI, Hewlett Packard, and Tata Motors, to name a few.
Research Interests
Rural communication, Digital Future, Music and visuals
Teaching Philosophy
Prof. Nilav Mazumdar’s teaching philosophy is grounded in the understanding that it is his responsibility to create an environment where students are involved in the learning process from an all-round perspective - personal growth, practical knowledge and strategic thought process. The aim is to not only build creative professionals, but creative individuals.
Courses Taught
Advertising, visual communications, Motion Graphics, Design Management, Elements of video and filmmaking, Storytelling and creative writing.
Contact
nmazumdar@ddn.upes.ac.in
