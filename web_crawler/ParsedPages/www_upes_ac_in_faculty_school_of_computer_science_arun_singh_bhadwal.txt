**Arun Singh Bhadwal**

**Position:** Assistant Professor

**Summary:**

Arun Singh Bhadwal is an Assistant Professor at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), pursuing research in drug molecule design using data-assisted techniques from deep learning. Arun Singh Bhadwal has developed an innovative approach to molecular representation, addressing challenges in SMILES representation, and has devised a solution to address the problem of posterior collapse in variational autoencoders.

**Research Interests:**

- Drug Design
- Recurrent Neural Network
- Variational Autoencoder
- Conditional VAE
- Bayesian Optimization

**Teaching Philosophy:**

Arun Singh Bhadwal's teaching emphasizes creating a supportive learning environment. Arun Singh Bhadwal incorporates "flipped classroom" elements, assigning pre-reading materials with quizzes. In-class discussions focus on practical applications with real data and situations, fostering experiential learning and critical thinking.

**Courses Taught:**

- Compiler Design
- Operating System
- Database Management System
