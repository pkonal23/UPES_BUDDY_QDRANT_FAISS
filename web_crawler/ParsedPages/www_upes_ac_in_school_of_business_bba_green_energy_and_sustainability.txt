**University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Business: Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Green Energy and Sustainability**

**Program Overview**

This three-year full-time management program, offered by University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Business, is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of non-conventional energy sources, energy transition, and sustainability. The program aims to equip graduates with the skills and knowledge necessary for management roles in the green energy, sustainable development, environment, and governance sectors.

**Specializations**

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Green Energy and Sustainability program offers two specializations:

* **Sustainable Development**
* **Green Energy Business**

These specializations allow students to tailor their studies to their career goals and specific interests.

**Curriculum**

The curriculum of the BBA in Green Energy and Sustainability program is designed to provide students with a holistic learning experience under the guidance of experienced faculty with industry expertise. It incorporates theoretical knowledge with practical components, including:

* Summer internships
* Live projects with companies
* Industrial tours
* Major projects (dissertations)
* Guest lectures from industry experts

**Program Highlights**

Students in the BBA in Green Energy and Sustainability program can expect the following highlights:

* Practical exposure through major projects and summer internships
* Flexibility to choose between operations and consulting in Green Energy and Sustainability or pursue higher education
* Research opportunities, seminars, conferences, and internships
* State-of-the-art facilities, including smart classrooms, Analytics Lab, library, and analytical tools
* Opportunities for student exchange, study abroad, and semester exchange programs with University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) partner institutes worldwide

**Career Prospects**

Graduates from the BBA in Green Energy and Sustainability program are well-equipped for a variety of career paths in the rapidly growing field of green energy and sustainability. They can work with renowned organizations in various areas, including:

* Renewable energy project development
* Energy efficiency
* Policy and advocacy
* Green energy market research
* Project management
* Entrepreneurship
* Sustainability consulting
* Environmental policy analysis
* Sustainable project management
* Corporate sustainability
* Nonprofit and NGO work
* Sustainable urban planning
* Environmental education
* Academic research

**Admission and Eligibility**

Interested students must meet the minimum eligibility criteria:

* Minimum 50% marks in Classes X and XII

Selection is based on performance in:

* UPES Management Entrance Test (UPESMET-UG)
* UGAT'23
* Common University Entrance Test (CUET)
