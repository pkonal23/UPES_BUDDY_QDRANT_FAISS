Shahid Sultan Hajam

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Shahid Sultan Hajam received his Ph.D. from National Institute of Technology Srinagar, J&K. He received his M. Tech from Central University of Kashmir in 2018 and B. Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology Srinagar, J&K.
Work Experience
Before joining UPES, Shahid was pursuing Ph.D. from NIT Srinagar, J&K.
Research Interests
Resource Management in Internet of Things | Applications of Machine Learning in IoT | Precision Agriculture, | Federated Learning | Optimization
Teaching Philosophy
Shahid Sultan's teaching philosophy revolves around placing students at the centre of the learning experience and establishing meaningful connections between education and real-world applications. He is committed to creating an inclusive and engaging classroom environment that empowers students to explore, question, and collaborate. By employing diverse teaching strategies and encouraging active participation, he strives to develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and lifelong learning skills.
Courses Taught
Data Structures | Operating Systems | Algorithms | Internet of Things | Programming
Scholarly Activities
Shahid has published several research papers in national and international journals and conferences. Besides, he has also helped students with their projects and motivated them to pursue research work.
Contact
shahid.sultan@ddn.upes.ac.in
