Program details
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering
program with a specialization in
Environmental Engineering
at UPES School of Advanced Engineering focuses on training students to become skilled professionals capable of addressing critical environmental challenges in urban infrastructure development. The curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the water supply and sewerage systems, two essential components of city infrastructure. Students will learn about the planning, design, and maintenance of these systems, ensuring efficient water distribution and sewage treatment. The program also covers vital aspects of water treatment plants, where students will study various purification processes to ensure the availability of clean and safe drinking water to the population. Similarly, in-depth knowledge of sewage treatment plants will be imparted to enable students to develop effective solutions for sewage treatment and waste management, contributing to environmental sustainability.
Moreover, the curriculum includes studying air and noise quality concepts and the implementation of monitoring and control procedures to mitigate pollution and protect public health. With a strong focus on solid waste management strategies, students will learn to design waste disposal and recycling methods to reduce environmental impact.
Throughout the program, students will engage in practical applications, research projects, and industry internships to gain hands-on experience and build a strong foundation for their future careers. Graduates from this program will be equipped to play a vital role in developing and implementing sustainable solutions for environmental challenges, making a positive impact on society and the planet.
Program Highlights
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers modern and advanced concepts of environmental engineering, ensuring students are well-equipped to tackle real-world challenges in the field.
Students will have the unique opportunity to integrate Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques into environmental prediction modelling, empowering them to analyze and solve complex environmental problems using cutting-edge technology.
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program provides access to a state-of-the-art research lab equipped with advanced instruments, allowing students to conduct in-depth research and experiments in the field of environmental engineering.
Emphasis is placed on practical learning, enabling students to gain hands-on experience in applying theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios, fostering a deeper understanding of environmental engineering principles.
The program boasts a team of expert faculty members with extensive experience in the field of environmental engineering, ensuring high-quality education and mentorship for students.
Students will have the opportunity to engage with industry experts, attend workshops, and participate in internships, providing them with valuable industry exposure and enhancing their employability in the environmental engineering sector.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program is promising and vital for human survival. With the increasing concern over anthropogenic waste and its proper disposal, billions of dollars are being invested to address this issue and ensure a safe and sustainable environment for healthy living. The field of environmental engineering plays a crucial role in finding solutions to these challenges, and its importance is evident from the thousands of research papers focused on converting waste into useful products. Industrial trends clearly indicate the indispensable contribution of environmental engineering to society's well-being. Pursuing this program offers great potential for making a positive impact on the environment and human lives.
Career Opportunities
After completing the
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering, graduates are equipped with a promising array of career opportunities in the field of environmental engineering. With a comprehensive understanding of sustainable development, water resource management, waste treatment, and pollution control, graduates can excel in roles such as environmental consultant, water resources engineer, air quality specialist, waste management engineer, and sustainability coordinator. Additionally, opportunities in government agencies, research institutions, and private sectors await them, where they can contribute towards creating a greener and healthier world, addressing pressing environmental challenges, and fostering sustainable solutions for a brighter future.
Placements
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering boasts exceptional placement opportunities for its graduates. The program equips students with comprehensive knowledge and skills in environmental engineering, enabling them to address pressing global challenges related to sustainability and resource management. UPES's strong industry connections and collaborations with leading organizations result in a high employability rate for its graduates. Throughout the years, students have secured rewarding positions in renowned companies, environmental consultancies, governmental agencies, and research institutions. The program's emphasis on practical training, innovative research, and a strong theoretical foundation prepares students to excel in their careers and contribute significantly to the field of environmental engineering.

Eligibility
Interested students must meet the minimum eligibility criteria for
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
as follows: Minimum 50% marks in Classes X and XII. with 50% in PCM (Physics/Chemistry and Mathematics) in Class XII.
Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for students who wish to pursue
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
at UPES depend on the individual's performance in UPESEAT / JEE / Board Merit / SAT/ CUET.

Program details
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering
program with a specialization in
Environmental Engineering
at UPES School of Advanced Engineering focuses on training students to become skilled professionals capable of addressing critical environmental challenges in urban infrastructure development. The curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the water supply and sewerage systems, two essential components of city infrastructure. Students will learn about the planning, design, and maintenance of these systems, ensuring efficient water distribution and sewage treatment. The program also covers vital aspects of water treatment plants, where students will study various purification processes to ensure the availability of clean and safe drinking water to the population. Similarly, in-depth knowledge of sewage treatment plants will be imparted to enable students to develop effective solutions for sewage treatment and waste management, contributing to environmental sustainability.
Moreover, the curriculum includes studying air and noise quality concepts and the implementation of monitoring and control procedures to mitigate pollution and protect public health. With a strong focus on solid waste management strategies, students will learn to design waste disposal and recycling methods to reduce environmental impact.
Throughout the program, students will engage in practical applications, research projects, and industry internships to gain hands-on experience and build a strong foundation for their future careers. Graduates from this program will be equipped to play a vital role in developing and implementing sustainable solutions for environmental challenges, making a positive impact on society and the planet.
Program Highlights
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers modern and advanced concepts of environmental engineering, ensuring students are well-equipped to tackle real-world challenges in the field.
Students will have the unique opportunity to integrate Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques into environmental prediction modelling, empowering them to analyze and solve complex environmental problems using cutting-edge technology.
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program provides access to a state-of-the-art research lab equipped with advanced instruments, allowing students to conduct in-depth research and experiments in the field of environmental engineering.
Emphasis is placed on practical learning, enabling students to gain hands-on experience in applying theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios, fostering a deeper understanding of environmental engineering principles.
The program boasts a team of expert faculty members with extensive experience in the field of environmental engineering, ensuring high-quality education and mentorship for students.
Students will have the opportunity to engage with industry experts, attend workshops, and participate in internships, providing them with valuable industry exposure and enhancing their employability in the environmental engineering sector.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program is promising and vital for human survival. With the increasing concern over anthropogenic waste and its proper disposal, billions of dollars are being invested to address this issue and ensure a safe and sustainable environment for healthy living. The field of environmental engineering plays a crucial role in finding solutions to these challenges, and its importance is evident from the thousands of research papers focused on converting waste into useful products. Industrial trends clearly indicate the indispensable contribution of environmental engineering to society's well-being. Pursuing this program offers great potential for making a positive impact on the environment and human lives.
Career Opportunities
After completing the
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering, graduates are equipped with a promising array of career opportunities in the field of environmental engineering. With a comprehensive understanding of sustainable development, water resource management, waste treatment, and pollution control, graduates can excel in roles such as environmental consultant, water resources engineer, air quality specialist, waste management engineer, and sustainability coordinator. Additionally, opportunities in government agencies, research institutions, and private sectors await them, where they can contribute towards creating a greener and healthier world, addressing pressing environmental challenges, and fostering sustainable solutions for a brighter future.
Placements
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering boasts exceptional placement opportunities for its graduates. The program equips students with comprehensive knowledge and skills in environmental engineering, enabling them to address pressing global challenges related to sustainability and resource management. UPES's strong industry connections and collaborations with leading organizations result in a high employability rate for its graduates. Throughout the years, students have secured rewarding positions in renowned companies, environmental consultancies, governmental agencies, and research institutions. The program's emphasis on practical training, innovative research, and a strong theoretical foundation prepares students to excel in their careers and contribute significantly to the field of environmental engineering.

B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
as follows: Minimum 50% marks in Classes X and XII. with 50% in PCM (Physics/Chemistry and Mathematics) in Class XII.
Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for students who wish to pursue
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
at UPES depend on the individual's performance in UPESEAT / JEE / Board Merit / SAT/ CUET.

Program details
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering
program with a specialization in
Environmental Engineering
at UPES School of Advanced Engineering focuses on training students to become skilled professionals capable of addressing critical environmental challenges in urban infrastructure development. The curriculum is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the water supply and sewerage systems, two essential components of city infrastructure. Students will learn about the planning, design, and maintenance of these systems, ensuring efficient water distribution and sewage treatment. The program also covers vital aspects of water treatment plants, where students will study various purification processes to ensure the availability of clean and safe drinking water to the population. Similarly, in-depth knowledge of sewage treatment plants will be imparted to enable students to develop effective solutions for sewage treatment and waste management, contributing to environmental sustainability.
Moreover, the curriculum includes studying air and noise quality concepts and the implementation of monitoring and control procedures to mitigate pollution and protect public health. With a strong focus on solid waste management strategies, students will learn to design waste disposal and recycling methods to reduce environmental impact.
Throughout the program, students will engage in practical applications, research projects, and industry internships to gain hands-on experience and build a strong foundation for their future careers. Graduates from this program will be equipped to play a vital role in developing and implementing sustainable solutions for environmental challenges, making a positive impact on society and the planet.
Program Highlights
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers modern and advanced concepts of environmental engineering, ensuring students are well-equipped to tackle real-world challenges in the field.
Students will have the unique opportunity to integrate Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning techniques into environmental prediction modelling, empowering them to analyze and solve complex environmental problems using cutting-edge technology.
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program provides access to a state-of-the-art research lab equipped with advanced instruments, allowing students to conduct in-depth research and experiments in the field of environmental engineering.
Emphasis is placed on practical learning, enabling students to gain hands-on experience in applying theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios, fostering a deeper understanding of environmental engineering principles.
The program boasts a team of expert faculty members with extensive experience in the field of environmental engineering, ensuring high-quality education and mentorship for students.
Students will have the opportunity to engage with industry experts, attend workshops, and participate in internships, providing them with valuable industry exposure and enhancing their employability in the environmental engineering sector.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program is promising and vital for human survival. With the increasing concern over anthropogenic waste and its proper disposal, billions of dollars are being invested to address this issue and ensure a safe and sustainable environment for healthy living. The field of environmental engineering plays a crucial role in finding solutions to these challenges, and its importance is evident from the thousands of research papers focused on converting waste into useful products. Industrial trends clearly indicate the indispensable contribution of environmental engineering to society's well-being. Pursuing this program offers great potential for making a positive impact on the environment and human lives.
Career Opportunities
After completing the
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering, graduates are equipped with a promising array of career opportunities in the field of environmental engineering. With a comprehensive understanding of sustainable development, water resource management, waste treatment, and pollution control, graduates can excel in roles such as environmental consultant, water resources engineer, air quality specialist, waste management engineer, and sustainability coordinator. Additionally, opportunities in government agencies, research institutions, and private sectors await them, where they can contribute towards creating a greener and healthier world, addressing pressing environmental challenges, and fostering sustainable solutions for a brighter future.
Placements
The
B.Tech. Civil Engineering (Environmental Engineering)
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering boasts exceptional placement opportunities for its graduates. The program equips students with comprehensive knowledge and skills in environmental engineering, enabling them to address pressing global challenges related to sustainability and resource management. UPES's strong industry connections and collaborations with leading organizations result in a high employability rate for its graduates. Throughout the years, students have secured rewarding positions in renowned companies, environmental consultancies, governmental agencies, and research institutions. The program's emphasis on practical training, innovative research, and a strong theoretical foundation prepares students to excel in their careers and contribute significantly to the field of environmental engineering.

Students will learn about the planning, design, and maintenance of these systems, ensuring efficient water distribution and sewage treatment. The program also covers vital aspects of water treatment plants, where students will study various purification processes to ensure the availability of clean and safe drinking water to the population. Similarly, in-depth knowledge of sewage treatment plants will be imparted to enable students to develop effective solutions for sewage treatment and waste management, contributing to environmental sustainability.
Moreover, the curriculum includes studying air and noise quality concepts and the implementation of monitoring and control procedures to mitigate pollution and protect public health. Graduates from this program will be equipped to play a vital role in developing and implementing sustainable solutions for environmental challenges, making a positive impact on society and the planet.
