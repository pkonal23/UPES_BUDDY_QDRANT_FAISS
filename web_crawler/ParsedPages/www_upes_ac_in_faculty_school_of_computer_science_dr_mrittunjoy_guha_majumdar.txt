**Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar** (hereinafter referred to as "Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar") is an Assistant Professor (Selection Grade) at the School of Computing Sciences and Advanced Engineering at UPES, Dehradun. Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar also holds a joint appointment at the Centre for Excellence for Quantum Technology at UPES.

**Education and Research Experience**

Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar earned his doctorate from the Cavendish Laboratory at the University of Cambridge. Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar's research interests center on non-classical correlations, particularly higher-dimensional entanglement, and its deployment in quantum information processing, with a focus on quantum error mitigation. Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar has also worked on the physics of information, exploring the intersection of entropy, info-dynamics, and biological systems.

**Work Experience**

Prior to joining UPES, Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar served as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Physics at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham and as a Postdoctoral Fellow at Harvard University. Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar has also held positions as a Senior Postdoctoral Researcher at the Indian Institute of Science, a Guest Faculty at the Department of Physics, University of Delhi, and an Institute Postdoctoral Fellow at Tata Institute of Fundamental Research.

**Teaching Philosophy**

Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar's teaching philosophy emphasizes nurturing constructive reflexivity and multidimensional, multidisciplinary human resource development in students. Dr. Mrittunjoy Guha Majumdar has incorporated innovative teaching methods, including Open ChatGPT tests and self-diagnostic testing frameworks, to promote critical reasoning, design thinking, and experiential training.
