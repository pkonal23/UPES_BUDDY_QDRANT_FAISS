SUMMERISE: Rise Up! Create Your Own Art!

SUMMERISE: Rise Up! Create Your Own Art!

About
This summer, the Communication Cluster at the School of Design at UPES is proud to launch "Summerise: Rise Up! Create Your Own Art!"—a series of eight innovative workshops designed to unleash the creative potential within every young mind. Each workshop is crafted to provide hands-on learning experiences, guiding participants through the intricate processes of creating art in various forms, from screen printing to interactive game design, and even stop-motion animation.
These workshops are led by our experienced faculty members who are experts in their respective fields, ensuring an enriching educational journey. Whether it's transforming everyday materials into beautiful toys, exploring the electrifying world of conductive inks, or diving into the golden era of Bollywood through typographic art, each session is structured to spark creativity and foster a deeper understanding of art and design. Participants will engage in activities that range from ideation and prototyping to the final presentation of their projects, gaining practical skills and theoretical knowledge along the way.
We invite all young enthusiasts from 10th to 12th grade to join us in this exciting summer adventure. Don't miss the chance to explore your creative streak, meet like-minded peers, and possibly discover a new passion. It’s more than just a workshop series—it’s a platform to innovate, express, and shine. Come, rise up and create your own art with us this summer at UPES!

Workshop 1: Press & Impress
Instructor:
Prof. Suchit Ahtekar
Date:
June 10 - 14, 2024
Timings:
11:00 AM to 01:00 PM & 02:00 PM to 04:00 PM
Lunch Break:
UPES Food Court
Materials Required
We will provide all the necessary materials. Just bring along fabric or T-shirts (old or new) that you'd like to print on and get ready to have fun!
Workshop Brief
Dive into the vibrant world of screen printing with our Screen Printing workshop. This hands-on experience will teach you the art of transferring stunning designs onto fabric. From mixing photo emulsions to pulling the squeegee across the screen, you’ll learn every step of the process. This five-day workshop will take you through every step of the screen printing process, from conceptualization to the final print. Here's what each day offers:
Day 1: Screen Printing 101
- Dive into the basics of screen printing. Understand the complete process step-by-step, setting the foundation for the days to come.
Day 2: Design Day
- Start from scratch to create your unique design. Learn about composition and the principles of effective screen printing design.
Day 3: Sunlight and Screens
- Use sunlight to expose your screen, preparing it for the printing phase. This day focuses on the technical aspects of screen preparation.
Day 4 & 5: Print Proficiency -
Spend these two days mastering the art of screen printing on various materials. Learn the nuances of printing on both paper and fabric, ensuring each participant can produce high-quality work.
This workshop is perfect for those looking to delve into the world of screen printing, whether you're a budding artist or a seasoned crafter. By the end, you'll have the skills to continue creating your pieces and the inspiration to explore new projects.
Payment Details and Eligibility
Workshop Registration Fee: ₹11,000/- (₹7,000/- for UPES Staff/Students)
Eligibility: Students in the 10th - 12th grade
For more details, please write to us at
summerise@ddn.upes.ac.in
or connect with us via WhatsApp at
+91-9163524908

Workshop 2: Motion Crafters
Instructor:
Prof. Vivek Pandey
Date:
June 10 - 14, 2024
Timings:
11:00 AM to 01:00 PM & 02:00 PM to 04:00 PM
Lunch Break:
UPES Food Court
Materials Required
We will provide all the necessary materials. Just bring along a pen drive to save your work on and please bring an additional flat, single-color outfit without any patterns on them - green colour excluded.
Workshop Brief
Bring your stories to life with our Stop-Motion Extravaganza! In this interactive workshop, you'll discover the enchanting process of stop-motion animation, creating your own animated sequence with the simplest of materials. It's an adventure in storytelling and patience, where every frame counts. Each day focuses on a pivotal aspect of filmmaking, designed to inspire and challenge:
Day 1: Scene Setter -
Kick off with introductions, discover interests in film production, brainstorm and discuss ideas for a narrative or concept video, and get inspired by a showcase of student projects.
Day 2: Plotting the Course -
Solidify your narrative, delve into shot selection, and storyboard your vision, setting the stage for your film's visual flow.
Day 3: Capturing the Vision -
Transition into hands-on film production, starting with meticulous shoot planning and the execution of pilot clips that capture your story's essence.
Day 4: Action in Motion -
Engage fully in the filmmaking process with intensive acting, shooting, and real-time production work, bringing your storyboard to life.
Day 5: The Final Cut -
Conclude with a robust post-production phase, refine your film for final presentation, and celebrate your achievements with an exhibition and feedback session.
This workshop is ideal for aspiring filmmakers looking to deepen their understanding and skills in film production, offering hands-on experience in a collaborative and dynamic environment.
Payment Details and Eligibility
Workshop Registration Fee: ₹11,000/- (₹7,000/- for UPES Staff/Students)
Eligibility: Students in the 10th - 12th grade

Learn how to embed sound triggers or interactive features into canvas projects, enhancing the interactivity of the artwork.

Finalize your projects, prepare presentations, and showcase your completed interactive canvases. The session concludes with a feedback round and celebration of the creativity displayed.

Complete your unique toys, prepare them for a journey home, and celebrate your creative journey by sharing your finished products and workshop experiences with the group.

Day 2: Material Mastery -
Discover and assess various upcycled materials, learn about their unique characteristics, and envision their innovative applications in crafting toys.

Day 3: Prototype Play -
Advance the design process by assembling initial prototypes of your envisioned toys, turning concepts into tangible forms.

Day 4: Perfect and Perform -
Refine your toy designs focusing on enhancing their visual appeal, functionality, and longevity, while also experimenting with still visual effects.

Day 5: Toy Showcase -
Complete your unique toys, prepare them for a journey home, and celebrate your creative journey by sharing your finished products and workshop experiences with the group.

This workshop is ideal for individuals eager to explore the creative potential of upcycling and to take home not just a toy, but a story of transformation and creativity.
