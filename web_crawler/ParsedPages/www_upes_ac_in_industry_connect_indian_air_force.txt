Indian Air Force

UPES partners with the Indian Air Force to provide academic support, scholarships, reserved seats, and tailored programs for personnel and their families.

UPES partners with the Indian Air Force to provide academic support, scholarships, reserved seats, and tailored programs for personnel and their families.

UPES has signed 3 Memoranda of Understanding (MoUs) with the Indian Air Force to provide a range of academic programs and support for the Indian Air Force community, including serving personnel, veterans, and their families.
Under this partnership, UPES will reserve seats and offer scholarships of up to 30% for the wards and spouses of Indian Air Force personnel enrolling in full-time undergraduate programs. Eligible programs include BBA, B.Tech., LL.B., B.Sc. (Hons), BA, and BA (Hons). Scholarships will be awarded based on academic performance in class 12 exams, CUET, or JEE scores.
Key Highlights of the Partnership
Tailored PhD Programs:
UPES will offer part-time PhD programs specifically designed for Indian Air Force officers. These programs will focus on areas such as Aviation and Aeronautics Engineering, Management, Science and Technology, Legal Studies, and Logistics and Supply Chain Management, aligning with the Indian Air Force's research and development priorities.
Support through UPES Online:
UPES Online, the digital learning vertical of UPES, will provide a variety of courses for Indian Air Force personnel and their families, including MBA, BBA, BCA, Postgraduate Programs (PGPs) and certificate courses.
Key benefits include a 20% fee waiver, a 5% relaxation in eligibility criteria (minimum 45% marks), and access to live online classes and recorded sessions, ensuring a flexible and seamless learning experience.
Holistic Learning Environment
This collaboration aims to enhance the educational and professional development of IAF personnel and their families, fostering a culture of lifelong learning and growth.
