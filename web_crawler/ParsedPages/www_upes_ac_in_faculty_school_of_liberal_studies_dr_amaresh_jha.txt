**Dr. Amaresh Jha: A Distinguished Media Professional and Educator**

Dr. Amaresh Jha, an esteemed Associate Professor at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), has an illustrious career spanning the realms of media and academia.

**Professional Experience and Accomplishments**

Dr. Amaresh Jha's media journey commenced as a Television Journalist with renowned organizations such as Zee News, News Express, TV9, India News, and India TV. Dr. Jha's expertise extended to news production for DD News. Notably, Dr. Jha serves as the Editor (Political and Internal Affairs) of the renowned International Journal of Politics and Media. Additionally, Dr. Amaresh Jha is a respected reviewer and article editor for prestigious journals published by SAGE, DRDO, and CG Network, USA.

Dr. Amaresh Jha's academic leadership was evident in his tenure as the Head of the School of Media and Entertainment at GD Goenka University, where Dr. Jha diligently oversaw academic administration, research, curriculum design, and development. Dr. Jha's teaching experience also includes significant contributions at Amity University, where Dr. Jha led the UG Program at Amity School of Communication, and at Galgotias University.

**Research Interests and Contributions**

Dr. Amaresh Jha's research endeavors focus on Political Communication, Media Psychology, and Immersive Experiences. Dr. Jha's intellectual curiosity has led to the development of an innovative model for resolving disputes arising from data encryption-related issues. Furthermore, Dr. Jha authored a significant book titled 'Advocating for Rights: Media Advocacy in RTI,' which sheds light on the power of media in safeguarding rights.

**Teaching Philosophy and Pedagogical Approach**

Dr. Amaresh Jha's teaching philosophy revolves around fostering critical thinking, creativity, and ethical responsibility among his students. Dr. Jha employs a dynamic blend of interactive and participatory activities, including discussions, group projects, case studies, and hands-on media production exercises. This student-centered approach encourages the application of theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios, fostering active participation and enriching the learning process.

**Courses Taught and Scholarly Activities**

Dr. Amaresh Jha's teaching repertoire encompasses a wide range of courses, including Web Journalism, Data Journalism, Multimedia Storytelling, Television Production, Digital Media Production, Social Science Research Methods, Academic Writing, and Communication Research. Dr. Jha also imparts knowledge in Research Methodology to doctoral scholars as part of their mandatory coursework.

Dr. Amaresh Jha's scholarly contributions extend beyond the classroom. Dr. Jha has chaired two consecutive International Conferences on Strategic Communication, demonstrating his leadership in the field. Dr. Jha's research has garnered recognition through the publication of over 30 articles in reputable journals and presentations at international conferences. Dr. Amaresh Jha has also actively supervised doctoral scholars, guiding two candidates to successful PhD defenses and two others to thesis submission. Dr. Jha's expertise in doctoral evaluations is reflected in his assessment of over five doctoral theses.

**Awards and Recognitions**

In recognition of his academic excellence, Dr. Amaresh Jha was awarded a research grant in 2019 from the Indian Council of Social Sciences Research, New Delhi, under the Impactful Policy Research in Social Sciences (IMPRESS) scheme.
