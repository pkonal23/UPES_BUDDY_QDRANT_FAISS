Program details
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program at UPES School of Business offers a holistic education that blends business management principles with specialized knowledge of the aviation industry. Graduates emerge as well-rounded professionals, well-versed in finance, marketing, operations, and human resources, ready to contribute to the dynamic field of aviation. The curriculum emphasizes strategic planning, decision-making, and organizational management skills applicable across industries, providing a strong foundation for a successful career.
Within this program, the specialization in Aviation Service Operations stands out as a distinctive feature. This concentration addresses the unique challenges of managing and optimizing service operations in the aviation industry. Students delve into the principles of service management, including customer expectations, service design, quality, and satisfaction within the aviation context. The course also places a significant focus on technology's role in enhancing service operations and delves into customer relationship management strategies tailored to the aviation sector.
The three-year course at UPES, Dehradun, ensures a comprehensive learning experience for students. Alongside traditional classroom instruction, students undergo summer internships, engage in live projects with industry partners, participate in an industrial tour, and complete a dissertation (major project). Furthermore, the program enriches the learning experience by featuring talks from industry experts, providing students with insights into the latest developments in the aviation industry. This approach equips graduates not only with theoretical knowledge but also with practical skills and industry exposure, positioning them for success in the rapidly growing field of aviation management.
Program Highlights
The
BBA in Aviation Management (Aviation Service Operations)
emphasizes hands-on learning through major projects (dissertations), ensuring students apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios.
Students engage in summer internships and live projects with companies, providing real-world exposure and enhancing their readiness for the aviation industry.
The
BBA in Aviation Management (Aviation Service Operations)
program offers flexibility, allowing students to specialize in airport operations, cargo handling, airline services, ground handling, or consulting, broadening their career prospects in the aviation sector.
Emphasis on global exposure is evident through student and faculty exchange programs, research initiatives, seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering a well-rounded educational experience.
Students benefit from practical insights through exposure to real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by industry experts, enriching their understanding of aviation management.
The curriculum integrates cutting-edge technology, exposing students to advanced software tools such as Excel and analytics tools like R-Studio, ensuring they are well-equipped with relevant skills for the modern aviation industry.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The
BBA in Aviation Management (Aviation Service Operations)
program offers a strategic pathway for students aspiring to mid-management roles in diverse sectors such as airlines, airports, e-commerce, and warehousing. Recognizing the current shortage of skilled professionals in the aviation industry, the program prioritizes hands-on learning through internships and on-the-job training, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. With a curriculum emphasizing aviation industry essentials, including airport and airline operations, cargo handling, and safety management, students gain a comprehensive understanding of industry practices and challenges. Real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by experts further enrich their learning experience. The program's integration of industry-relevant software and technologies enhances students' employability, providing them with a competitive edge in the job market. By addressing the demand for proficient aviation management professionals, this program positions graduates for success in a dynamic and evolving industry.
Career Opportunities
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program at UPES School of Business presents diverse career avenues for students, offering opportunities to collaborate with esteemed organizations and acquire valuable corporate experience through internships, live projects, and placements. This program facilitates students in exploring their career interests, establishing connections with potential employers, and securing job offers. With a focus on three key verticals—airport operations, airline operations, and consulting—students can specialize in their preferred area, gaining in-depth knowledge and skills. Career possibilities include roles such as Airline Operations Manager, Revenue Management Analyst, Airport Business Development Executive, and more, providing a comprehensive range of options for professional growth.
Placements
In recent years, placements for
BBA in Aviation Management (Aviation Service Operations)
students have markedly improved. Esteemed companies such as GMR Group, Adani Group, Vistara Airlines, Spice Express, Accenture, Kuehne Nagel, TATA Airlines, and Indigo Airlines present attractive employment prospects. Moreover, burgeoning start-ups express a strong interest in recruiting graduates from this program. Job opportunities encompass roles like airline operations manager, airport operations supervisor, aviation sales executive, airport customer service manager, aviation consultant, air cargo operations coordinator, aviation safety officer, airport business development executive, aviation training coordinator, and airline marketing specialist. These roles span diverse sectors such as airline management, airport operations, sales and marketing, cargo handling, safety and security, business development, and training. Fresh BBA in Aviation Management graduates can anticipate a starting salary ranging from INR 4 to 10 lakh per annum, contingent upon their skills, knowledge, and exposure.

Program details
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program at UPES School of Business offers a holistic education that blends business management principles with specialized knowledge of the aviation industry. Graduates emerge as well-rounded professionals, well-versed in finance, marketing, operations, and human resources, ready to contribute to the dynamic field of aviation. The curriculum emphasizes strategic planning, decision-making, and organizational management skills applicable across industries, providing a strong foundation for a successful career.
Within this program, the specialization in Aviation Service Operations stands out as a distinctive feature. This concentration addresses the unique challenges of managing and optimizing service operations in the aviation industry. Students delve into the principles of service management, including customer expectations, service design, quality, and satisfaction within the aviation context. The course also places a significant focus on technology's role in enhancing service operations and delves into customer relationship management strategies tailored to the aviation sector.
The three-year course at UPES, Dehradun, ensures a comprehensive learning experience for students. Alongside traditional classroom instruction, students undergo summer internships, engage in live projects with industry partners, participate in an industrial tour, and complete a dissertation (major project). Furthermore, the program enriches the learning experience by featuring talks from industry experts, providing students with insights into the latest developments in the aviation industry. This approach equips graduates not only with theoretical knowledge but also with practical skills and industry exposure, positioning them for success in the rapidly growing field of aviation management.
Program Highlights
The
BBA in Aviation Management (Aviation Service Operations)
emphasizes hands-on learning through major projects (dissertations), ensuring students apply theoretical knowledge in practical scenarios.
Students engage in summer internships and live projects with companies, providing real-world exposure and enhancing their readiness for the aviation industry.
The
BBA in Aviation Management (Aviation Service Operations)
program offers flexibility, allowing students to specialize in airport operations, cargo handling, airline services, ground handling, or consulting, broadening their career prospects in the aviation sector.
Emphasis on global exposure is evident through student and faculty exchange programs, research initiatives, seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering a well-rounded educational experience.
Students benefit from practical insights through exposure to real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by industry experts, enriching their understanding of aviation management.
The curriculum integrates cutting-edge technology, exposing students to advanced software tools such as Excel and analytics tools like R-Studio, ensuring they are well-equipped with relevant skills for the modern aviation industry.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The
BBA in Aviation Management (Aviation Service Operations)
program offers a strategic pathway for students aspiring to mid-management roles in diverse sectors such as airlines, airports, e-commerce, and warehousing. Recognizing the current shortage of skilled professionals in the aviation industry, the program prioritizes hands-on learning through internships and on-the-job training, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. With a curriculum emphasizing aviation industry essentials, including airport and airline operations, cargo handling, and safety management, students gain a comprehensive understanding of industry practices and challenges. Real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by experts further enrich their learning experience. The program's integration of industry-relevant software and technologies enhances students' employability, providing them with a competitive edge in the job market. By addressing the demand for proficient aviation management professionals, this program positions graduates for success in a dynamic and evolving industry.
Career Opportunities
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program at UPES School of Business presents diverse career avenues for students, offering opportunities to collaborate with esteemed organizations and acquire valuable corporate experience through internships, live projects, and placements. This program facilitates students in exploring their career interests, establishing connections with potential employers, and securing job offers. With a focus on three key verticals—airport operations, airline operations, and consulting—students can specialize in their preferred area, gaining in-depth knowledge and skills. Career possibilities include roles such as Airline Operations Manager, Revenue Management Analyst, Airport Business Development Executive, and more, providing a comprehensive range of options for professional growth.
Placements
In recent years, placements for
BBA in Aviation Management (Aviation Service Operations)
students have markedly improved. Esteemed companies such as GMR Group, Adani Group, Vistara Airlines, Spice Express, Accenture, Kuehne Nagel, TATA Airlines, and Indigo Airlines present attractive employment prospects. Moreover, burgeoning start-ups express a strong interest in recruiting graduates from this program. Job opportunities encompass roles like airline operations manager, airport operations supervisor, aviation sales executive, airport customer service manager, aviation consultant, air cargo operations coordinator, aviation safety officer, airport business development executive, aviation training coordinator, and airline marketing specialist. These roles span diverse sectors such as airline management, airport operations, sales and marketing, cargo handling, safety and security, business development, and training. Fresh BBA in Aviation Management graduates can anticipate a starting salary ranging from INR 4 to 10 lakh per annum, contingent upon their skills, knowledge, and exposure.

Future Scope / Industry Trends

program offers a strategic pathway for students aspiring to mid-management roles in diverse sectors such as airlines, airports, e-commerce, and warehousing. Recognizing the current shortage of skilled professionals in the aviation industry, the program prioritizes hands-on learning through internships and on-the-job training, bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. With a curriculum emphasizing aviation industry essentials, including airport and airline operations, cargo handling, and safety management, students gain a comprehensive understanding of industry practices and challenges. Real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by experts further enrich their learning experience. Career Opportunities

program at UPES School of Business presents diverse career avenues for students, offering opportunities to collaborate with esteemed organizations and acquire valuable corporate experience through internships, live projects, and placements. Furthermore, the program enriches the learning experience by featuring talks from industry experts, providing students with insights into the latest developments in the aviation industry. This approach equips graduates not only with theoretical knowledge but also with practical skills and industry exposure, positioning them for success in the rapidly growing field of aviation management.

These roles span diverse sectors such as airline management, airport operations, sales and marketing, cargo handling, safety and security, business development, and training. Fresh BBA in Aviation Management graduates can anticipate a starting salary ranging from INR 4 to 10 lakh per annum, contingent upon their skills, knowledge, and exposure.
