Non-Exam Pathway
CLAT Merit / L-SAT India Merit/ CUET
For BA LL.B. (Hons.)/ BBA LL.B. (Hons.) - Candidates with minimum 50% marks in class X & XII and CLAT AIR ranking up to 15000 / L-SAT India Percentile of 60 percentile or above will be exempted from appearing in ULSAT.
Board Merit
For BA LL.B. (Hons.)/ BBA LL.B. (Hons.) - Candidates with 75% marks in class X and XII with the main five subjects (English as one of the compulsory subjects) are exempted from ULSAT.
Up to 20% of seats shall be filled through the Board Merit/ CLAT AIR/ UL-SAT India category. In case, any seat remains vacant UPES has the right to fill such seats through ULSAT.
Exam Process
Take a Demo Test:
Students are recommended to take a demo test to gain practice and resolve any queries before the examination.
Login Credentials for Online Exam:
Actual credentials (passkey) to appear for the exam on the chosen date will be provided to students one day prior to the exam on the registered email ID and mobile number. This should not be confused with the demo link passkey.
The examination Test window will be from
10 am to 3 pm.
Students can log in during the given timeframe and begin with the online test. Please note that the timer starts when the student logs in.
