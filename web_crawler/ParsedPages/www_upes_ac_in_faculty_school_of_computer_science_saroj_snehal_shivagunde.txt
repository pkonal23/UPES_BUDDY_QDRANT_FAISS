Saroj Snehal Shivagunde

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Dr. Saroj has worked in theoretical machine learning during her term as a PhD candidate at Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati. Her research focused on incremental learning and unlearning using multiple views. Before this, she obtained her MTech degree from Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati. It was during this time that she discovered her interest in teaching.
Work Experience
Dr. Saroj joined UPES in July 2022 as an assistant professor in the School of Computer Science.
Research Interests
Theoretical Machine Learning | Multi-view Learning | Incremental Learning
Teaching Philosophy
Dr. Saroj takes a flexible approach to teaching by identifying the collective need of her class and the individual needs of each student. She encourages active participation and believes in real-time feedback. Students can approach her anytime during office hours for any course-related queries.
Courses Taught
Machine Learning, Database Management System, Artificial Intelligence, Database Management System, Introduction to Game Programming
Contact
saroj.shivagunde@ddn.upes.ac.in
