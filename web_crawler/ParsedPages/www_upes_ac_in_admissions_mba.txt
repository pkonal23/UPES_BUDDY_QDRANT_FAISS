UPES offers MBA courses in a wide range of domains, from energy management to International business. Depending on the specialisation, the course curriculums and the fee structure vary from course to course. All the courses have been designed with meticulous detail which has led UPES to achieve a ranking of 41 in the National Institutional Ranking Framework or NIRF of the MHRD in the management category in 2024.

Other Information
MBA Admission
Modern workplaces and roles often require excellent management and organisational skills in addition to subject expertise and knowledge. Students need to be well-versed in the fundamentals of business and market operations and strategy formulation and decision-making.
A master’s degree in business administration can enable students to familiarise themselves with the works of a business setting. The degree can act as a great career launcher for those with different academic backgrounds seeking a firm standing in the business industry.
The
UPES School of Business

What is MBA course?

It is designed to provide individuals with advanced skills and knowledge in business and management. The MBA program is highly regarded globally and is intended for professionals seeking to enhance their managerial and leadership capabilities.
What is included in MBA course?
MBA program equips students with a strong foundation in business principles and prepares them to become ethical and innovative leaders. There are so many specialisations under MBA like digital business, power management, marketing, and human resource.
What is the eligibility criteria of MBA Course?
MBA is a postgraduate program, students who wish to enrol on this program must have graduated from a recognised university with a minimum of 50% marks. Students who are currently in the final year of graduation can also apply. Additionally, your marks on national-level tests like CAT or GMAT play an important  role.
What is the career scope after doing MBA Course?
Completing an MBA opens up a wide range of career opportunities across various industries. The career scope after doing an MBA is diverse, and it largely depends on your specialization, skills, interests, and prior work experience. Individuals can opt for specialisations like business management and leadership, marketing, finance and so on.

Does UPES provide placement opportunities?
UPES provides placement assistance to all enrolled students subject to their fulfilling eligibility conditions as prescribed in UPES Placement Regulations. However, the obligation of the University will be only to provide placement assistance and not placement guarantee.

Because of the relevant and ‘real world’ education and ‘on-the-job’ exposure, the industry-readiness of UPES students has been one of the key differentiators.
