If you dream of getting into an engineering program at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University, the gateway to your aspirations lies through AEEE (Amrita Engineering Entrance Exam). Conducted every year, the computer-based entrance exam known as the Amrita Entrance Examination-Engineering (AEEE) is administered to Class 12 graduates who wish to enroll in BTech and Integrated BSc-MSc programs. For BTech programs provided at all Amrita campuses in Amaravati, Amritapuri, Bengaluru, Chennai, Coimbatore, Faridabad, and Nagercoil (new as of 2025), admission is granted through AEEE.
In this blog, you will get an idea about AEEE 2025 exam date, AEEE registration, AEEE syllabus, AEEE slot booking, AEEE eligibility, Strategies to crack AEEE, and more for a comprehensive start to your preparation.
Overview of AEEE 2025
The AEEE is conducted annually for admissions to various undergraduate engineering programs at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. It is offered in two modes:
CBT (Computer-Based Test)
and
P&P (Pen-and-Paper)
, ensuring flexibility for candidates.
Key Details:
AEEE Applications:
Applications for AEEE 2025 must be submitted by January 20, 2025.
AEEE 2025 Exam Date:
Phase 1
to be conducted on February1st and 2nd.
Phase 2
Tentatively scheduled for April and May 2025 (exact dates to be announced).
AEEE Registration:
Expected to begin in December 2024. Keep an eye on the official website to complete your registration early.
AEEE Slot Booking:
Slot booking typically opens a few weeks before the exam. This is your chance to select a convenient exam date and time.
AEEE Exam Pattern:

Strategies to Crack AEEE 2025
Are you wondering how can I prepare for an entrance exam like AEEE? We have brought to you some study tricks to help you clear the exam with flying colours:
1. Know the Eligibility Criteria
Before diving into preparation, ensure you meet the AEEE eligibility criteria:
Minimum 60% aggregate in PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics) in Class 12.
Additional criteria may apply for certain campuses or programs.
2. Create a Study Plan
A well-structured study plan is key:
Allocate specific time slots for each subject.
Dedicate extra time to weaker topics.
3. Subject-Wise Preparation Tips
Each subject demands a tailored approach:
Mathematics:
Practice is critical. Focus on high-weightage topics like Calculus and Coordinate Geometry. Solve previous yearsâ€™ question papers and take mock tests.
Physics:
Understand the concepts thoroughly and apply them in problem-solving. Pay special attention to Mechanics and Electromagnetism.
Chemistry:
Memorize key formulas in Physical Chemistry, understand reaction mechanisms in Organic Chemistry, and focus on periodic properties in Inorganic Chemistry.
4. Mock Tests and Analysis
Regularly take mock tests to simulate exam conditions. Analyze your performance to identify and improve weak areas.
5. Revise Effectively
Revise formulas, theorems, and key concepts daily.
Use flashcards for quick recall of important points.
6. Tips for AEEE Slot Booking and Exam Day
Complete the AEEE slot booking process early to secure your preferred exam date and time.
Arrive at the exam centre with all required documents well ahead of time.
Stay calm and manage your time wisely during the exam.
Final Thoughts
Cracking AEEE requires dedication, strategic preparation, and consistent effort. Start early, focus on understanding concepts, and regularly evaluate your progress with mock tests. Keep yourself updated on the AEEE 2025 exam date, AEEE registration, and other important announcements.
Good luck on your journey to securing a seat at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham!
