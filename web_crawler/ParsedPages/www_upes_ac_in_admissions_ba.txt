Course Overview
About BA Courses
UPES offers a wide range of Bachelor of Arts (BA) courses in various modern and conventional disciplines, such as journalism. These BA programs are designed to be completed in 6 semesters over a span of 3 years.
The curriculum of the courses is thoughtfully crafted to provide students with a comprehensive education, allowing them to choose from various majors across multiple disciplines.
UPES also partners with leading bigwigs from the media and humanities domains to provide additional exposure and learning opportunities to the students. This strong industry connection is one of the reasons all BA courses at UPES are accredited by prominent bodies, including the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), University Grants Commission (UGC), Association of Indian Universities, and the International Association of Universities.
All BA programs at UPES are offered through the
School of Liberal Studies and Media
.

BA Career Prospects
A Bachelor of Arts (BA) in India can pave the way for excellent employment opportunities across various fields in humanities and art domains such as journalism, media studies and much more. Here is a list of some of the most promising career paths available for graduates with individual BA degrees from UPES Dehradun.
BA Journalism and Mass Communication:
Senior Media Journalists, Newsreaders, Newspaper Editors, Field Journalists and Story Advisors for a news channel.
BA Digital and Mass Media:
Brand consultants for OTT companies like Jio-Hotstar, VOOT, Netflix, Amazon Prime and others, TV/Film producers, online publication managers, digital media managers, and digital marketing heads.
BA (H) English:
Content writers, Editors in media agencies, digital marketers, public relation managers, content strategists, corporate communication specialists, and technical writers.
BA (H) Political Science:
Students gain practical experience and problem-solving skills that make them suitable for roles in the private sector, industry, policy-based government, and non-governmental think tanks.
BA (H) History:
Students gain practical experience and problem-solving skills that make them suitable for roles in research-driven public and private sector organizations, including museums, galleries, journalism, policy think tanks, and content creation for biopics and historical OTT platforms.

Eligibility Criteria
Minimum 50% marks in Class X and XII
BA Admission Process at UPES
Pursuing a Bachelor of Arts degree from UPES can be a great way to start your college education and eventually pursue an interesting career in the arts. Apart from an excellent faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure, UPES University is also the first Indian university to be awarded the QS Five Stars Global Rating for employability in 2020.
Choosing a broad curriculum-based bachelor’s degree such as a Bachelor of Arts can be a good idea for those who want to establish themselves as thought leaders in different fields. Moreover, the wide diversity of subjects included in the curriculum of a BA degree can prepare you for a wide range of domains and can allow you to switch seamlessly between them if you wish.
The admission process for enrolling in BA courses at UPES is rather simple. Interested applicants need to fill an application form online and submit it along with their photographs. The applications are scanned meticulously, and the shortlisted candidates are invited for a personal interview round.

What is the career scope after doing B.A. Course?
The career scope after completing a Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) course is diverse and offers a wide range of opportunities across various industries.
