Program Details
The BBA in Logistics Management program offered by UPES School of Business is a specialised curriculum designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of logistics principles and strategies, specifically tailored for the manufacturing sector and warehouse operations. The program places a strong emphasis on Warehouse Management, delving into crucial aspects such as warehouse processes, warehouse management systems, cost considerations, and safety protocols within a warehouse environment. Students gain insights into stock management, automated storage and retrieval systems, housekeeping practices, handling and storage equipment, inventory management, performance measurement, contract warehousing, risk assessment, warehouse layout and design, warehouse equipment legislations, energy usage, and waste and hazardous material management.
In addition to warehouse-focused modules, the program extends its reach into Logistics in the manufacturing sector. This entails a comprehensive exploration of factory operations, inbound and outbound logistics, inventory management, material handling, distribution mechanisms, and the associated environmental considerations. The curriculum aligns with the digital revolution, addressing industry 4.0 concepts, and incorporates a well-balanced content mix covering Materials Management, Demand Management, International Logistics, Multi-Modal Transportation, Retail, Logistics Information System, and Spreadsheet modeling. This holistic approach ensures that students not only understand the fundamental operational aspects of logistics and warehouse management but are also well-versed in the broader context of digital transformation and global logistics trends.
Overall, the BBA in Logistics Management program at UPES School of Business aims to produce well-rounded professionals with a strategic understanding of logistics principles, capable of navigating the complexities of the manufacturing sector and warehouse operations while being adept at integrating emerging technologies and global perspectives into their decision-making processes.
Program Highlights
•    The BBA in Logistics Management program emphasizes practical experience through major projects and dissertations, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios.
•    Students engage in summer internships and live projects with companies, providing valuable industry exposure and preparing them for the demands of the logistics management field.
•    The curriculum is designed to offer flexibility, enabling students to pursue careers in the service sector, operations, and consulting in logistics management or opt for higher education.
•    Students are actively encouraged to undertake research projects, participate in seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering a culture of continuous learning and networking.
•    The BBA in Logistics Management program incorporates real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts, enhancing students' understanding of the practical challenges and solutions in logistics management.
•    The UPES School of Business provides a conducive learning environment with modern smart classrooms, a well-equipped Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and advanced analytical tools, ensuring a technologically enriched educational experience.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The BBA in Logistics Management program equips students with the essential skills for entry-level managerial roles in the dynamic logistics and supply chain industry, both nationally and globally. The curriculum emphasizes experiential learning, offering students opportunities for internships and on-the-job training, providing a practical dimension to their education. This hands-on approach not only gives students a competitive advantage in the job market but also enables them to apply classroom knowledge to real-world problem-solving. The program focuses on developing strategic planning and decision-making skills tailored to the intricacies of logistics and supply chain management. Students gain insights into the impact of globalization on logistics operations, along with a comprehensive understanding of international trade, customs regulations, and cross-border logistics challenges. Overall, the BBA in Logistics Management program prepares graduates to navigate and excel in the complexities of the evolving logistics and supply chain landscape.
Career Opportunities
Earning a BBA in Logistics Management at the School of Business UPES opens up diverse career paths. Through internships, projects, and placements, students engage with renowned organizations, gaining expertise in corporate dynamics. Exploring professional interests, collaborating with potential employers, and discovering employment prospects are integral aspects. Career avenues span the service sector, encompassing third-party logistics (3PL) firms, hotels, airlines, banks, insurance, and EdTech firms. Roles such as supply chain analyst, key account manager, data analyst, purchase analyst, operations executive, logistics coordinator, project manager, warehouse manager, and customer service manager are viable options. Additionally, many students opt for advanced education in higher management to pursue leadership courses.
Placements
The BBA in Logistics Management program at UPES opens doors to promising career opportunities for students through summer internships and employment prospects. Reputed businesses eagerly welcome these graduates, while mid-sized companies actively seek to hire fresh BBA in Logistics Management graduates. The diverse range of employment options spans various sectors such as port and shipping, airlines, rail operations, manufacturing, warehousing, cold chain logistics, distribution, and consulting firms like third party (3PL) and fourth party (4PL) logistics. With starting salaries typically ranging between INR 4-10 lakh per annum, the remuneration is determined by the individual's skills, knowledge, and exposure in the field. This program equips students with the essential skills and knowledge required to excel in the dynamic and growing field of logistics management.

Program Details
The BBA in Logistics Management program offered by UPES School of Business is a specialised curriculum designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of logistics principles and strategies, specifically tailored for the manufacturing sector and warehouse operations. The program places a strong emphasis on Warehouse Management, delving into crucial aspects such as warehouse processes, warehouse management systems, cost considerations, and safety protocols within a warehouse environment. Students gain insights into stock management, automated storage and retrieval systems, housekeeping practices, handling and storage equipment, inventory management, performance measurement, contract warehousing, risk assessment, warehouse layout and design, warehouse equipment legislations, energy usage, and waste and hazardous material management.
In addition to warehouse-focused modules, the program extends its reach into Logistics in the manufacturing sector. This entails a comprehensive exploration of factory operations, inbound and outbound logistics, inventory management, material handling, distribution mechanisms, and the associated environmental considerations. The curriculum aligns with the digital revolution, addressing industry 4.0 concepts, and incorporates a well-balanced content mix covering Materials Management, Demand Management, International Logistics, Multi-Modal Transportation, Retail, Logistics Information System, and Spreadsheet modeling. This holistic approach ensures that students not only understand the fundamental operational aspects of logistics and warehouse management but are also well-versed in the broader context of digital transformation and global logistics trends.
Overall, the BBA in Logistics Management program at UPES School of Business aims to produce well-rounded professionals with a strategic understanding of logistics principles, capable of navigating the complexities of the manufacturing sector and warehouse operations while being adept at integrating emerging technologies and global perspectives into their decision-making processes.
Program Highlights
•    The BBA in Logistics Management program emphasizes practical experience through major projects and dissertations, allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios.
•    Students engage in summer internships and live projects with companies, providing valuable industry exposure and preparing them for the demands of the logistics management field.
•    The curriculum is designed to offer flexibility, enabling students to pursue careers in the service sector, operations, and consulting in logistics management or opt for higher education.
•    Students are actively encouraged to undertake research projects, participate in seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering a culture of continuous learning and networking.
•    The BBA in Logistics Management program incorporates real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts, enhancing students' understanding of the practical challenges and solutions in logistics management.
•    The UPES School of Business provides a conducive learning environment with modern smart classrooms, a well-equipped Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and advanced analytical tools, ensuring a technologically enriched educational experience.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The BBA in Logistics Management program equips students with the essential skills for entry-level managerial roles in the dynamic logistics and supply chain industry, both nationally and globally. The curriculum emphasizes experiential learning, offering students opportunities for internships and on-the-job training, providing a practical dimension to their education. This hands-on approach not only gives students a competitive advantage in the job market but also enables them to apply classroom knowledge to real-world problem-solving. The program focuses on developing strategic planning and decision-making skills tailored to the intricacies of logistics and supply chain management. Students gain insights into the impact of globalization on logistics operations, along with a comprehensive understanding of international trade, customs regulations, and cross-border logistics challenges. Overall, the BBA in Logistics Management program prepares graduates to navigate and excel in the complexities of the evolving logistics and supply chain landscape.
Career Opportunities
Earning a BBA in Logistics Management at the School of Business UPES opens up diverse career paths. Through internships, projects, and placements, students engage with renowned organizations, gaining expertise in corporate dynamics. Exploring professional interests, collaborating with potential employers, and discovering employment prospects are integral aspects. Career avenues span the service sector, encompassing third-party logistics (3PL) firms, hotels, airlines, banks, insurance, and EdTech firms. Roles such as supply chain analyst, key account manager, data analyst, purchase analyst, operations executive, logistics coordinator, project manager, warehouse manager, and customer service manager are viable options. Additionally, many students opt for advanced education in higher management to pursue leadership courses.
Placements
The BBA in Logistics Management program at UPES opens doors to promising career opportunities for students through summer internships and employment prospects. Reputed businesses eagerly welcome these graduates, while mid-sized companies actively seek to hire fresh BBA in Logistics Management graduates. The diverse range of employment options spans various sectors such as port and shipping, airlines, rail operations, manufacturing, warehousing, cold chain logistics, distribution, and consulting firms like third party (3PL) and fourth party (4PL) logistics. With starting salaries typically ranging between INR 4-10 lakh per annum, the remuneration is determined by the individual's skills, knowledge, and exposure in the field. This program equips students with the essential skills and knowledge required to excel in the dynamic and growing field of logistics management.

Future Scope / Industry Trends
The BBA in Logistics Management program equips students with the essential skills for entry-level managerial roles in the dynamic logistics and supply chain industry, both nationally and globally. The curriculum emphasizes experiential learning, offering students opportunities for internships and on-the-job training, providing a practical dimension to their education. This hands-on approach not only gives students a competitive advantage in the job market but also enables them to apply classroom knowledge to real-world problem-solving. The program focuses on developing strategic planning and decision-making skills tailored to the intricacies of logistics and supply chain management. Students gain insights into the impact of globalization on logistics operations, along with a comprehensive understanding of international trade, customs regulations, and cross-border logistics challenges. Overall, the BBA in Logistics Management program prepares graduates to navigate and excel in the complexities of the evolving logistics and supply chain landscape.
Career Opportunities
Earning a BBA in Logistics Management at the School of Business UPES opens up diverse career paths. Through internships, projects, and placements, students engage with renowned organizations, gaining expertise in corporate dynamics. Exploring professional interests, collaborating with potential employers, and discovering employment prospects are integral aspects. Career avenues span the service sector, encompassing third-party logistics (3PL) firms, hotels, airlines, banks, insurance, and EdTech firms. Program Details
The BBA in Logistics Management program offered by UPES School of Business is a specialised curriculum designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of logistics principles and strategies, specifically tailored for the manufacturing sector and warehouse operations. The program places a strong emphasis on Warehouse Management, delving into crucial aspects such as warehouse processes, warehouse management systems, cost considerations, and safety protocols within a warehouse environment. Students gain insights into stock management, automated storage and retrieval systems, housekeeping practices, handling and storage equipment, inventory management, performance measurement, contract warehousing, risk assessment, warehouse layout and design, warehouse equipment legislations, energy usage, and waste and hazardous material management.
In addition to warehouse-focused modules, the program extends its reach into Logistics in the manufacturing sector. This entails a comprehensive exploration of factory operations, inbound and outbound logistics, inventory management, material handling, distribution mechanisms, and the associated environmental considerations. The curriculum aligns with the digital revolution, addressing industry 4.0 concepts, and incorporates a well-balanced content mix covering Materials Management, Demand Management, International Logistics, Multi-Modal Transportation, Retail, Logistics Information System, and Spreadsheet modeling. This holistic approach ensures that students not only understand the fundamental operational aspects of logistics and warehouse management but are also well-versed in the broader context of digital transformation and global logistics trends.
Overall, the BBA in Logistics Management program at UPES School of Business aims to produce well-rounded professionals with a strategic understanding of logistics principles, capable of navigating the complexities of the manufacturing sector and warehouse operations while being adept at integrating emerging technologies and global perspectives into their decision-making processes.
