Program details
The Ph.D. in Communication offered by UPES School of
Liberal Studies
is a comprehensive and research-intensive program designed to cater to scholars with a keen interest in delving into the realms of social sciences and humanities, particularly focusing on communication and media studies. The program is structured to provide a robust academic environment that fosters intellectual curiosity and encourages innovative research within the broader spectrum of social sciences and humanities.
Doctoral scholars at the School of
Liberal Studies
play a pivotal role in advancing the field of communication and media studies, contributing significantly to the academic discourse and research landscape. The program not only emphasizes theoretical foundations but also encourages practical applications, ensuring that scholars develop a holistic understanding of the
Liberal Studies
landscape. By actively participating in cutting-edge research, the scholars contribute not only to their own academic growth but also to the overall development and reputation of UPES as a research-driven institution of higher education. The faculty, comprising seasoned researchers and experts in the field, provide mentorship and guidance to doctoral scholars, fostering an environment that promotes critical thinking and scholarly excellence.
In essence, the Ph.D. in Communication program at UPES School of
Liberal Studies
aims to create a vibrant community of scholars who are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of knowledge in communication and media studies. By offering a platform for rigorous research and intellectual exploration, the program prepares doctoral scholars to become thought leaders and experts in their respective fields, ultimately contributing to the advancement of the broader academic community and the industry at large.
Program Highlights
The Ph.D. in Communication program benefits from the guidance of seasoned mentors at UPES School of
Liberal Studies
, bringing a wealth of international experience to shape your Ph.D. journey.
Choose between full-time and part-time enrollment options, allowing you to tailor your academic pursuit to align with your professional commitments.
Full-time scholars receive a monthly stipend of Rs. 30,000, ensuring financial assistance throughout the program, facilitating focused research.
Access additional resources through contingency and travel funds, empowering you to conduct comprehensive research and present your work at relevant conferences.
Engage with a diverse curriculum and collaborative research opportunities, fostering an interdisciplinary environment that enriches your academic experience.
Beyond academic pursuits, our program emphasizes your overall growth, equipping you with the skills and knowledge needed for a successful career in
Liberal Studies
.
PEO / PSO
The Ph.D. program in Communication at UPES School of Liberal Studies is designed to cultivate scholars with a comprehensive understanding of media and communication studies. Throughout the program, scholars will engage deeply with relevant literature, theories, and concepts, fostering a solid theoretical foundation. Emphasizing practical application, they will leverage acquired knowledge from coursework, academic engagement, and thesis writing to make meaningful contributions to the evolving academic landscape of media studies. The program equips scholars with the ability to translate their learnings into real-world scenarios, honing skills in presenting theoretically sound arguments and actively participating in academic debates, seminars, and conferences. Additionally, scholars will develop the capacity to deliver well-prepared lectures, conduct seminars and workshops, and produce research-driven, peer-reviewed articles, thereby enhancing the research output of both the school and the university. Rigorous literature review activities will enable scholars to articulate key arguments within existing, new, and developing literature in the dynamic field of media and communication studies.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of a Ph.D. in Communication program is promising, offering scholars valuable research experience in the realms of humanities, social sciences, and media studies. This program provides a platform for scholars to engage in meaningful research, contributing to the advancement of knowledge in these diverse fields. As the intersection of media and society continues to evolve, a Ph.D. in Communication equips scholars with the expertise to address contemporary challenges and explore innovative solutions. Graduates of this program are poised to make significant contributions to academia and industry, shaping the discourse on media in the context of broader societal and cultural dynamics. With a focus on rigorous research, this Ph.D. in Communication program prepares scholars for leadership roles, fostering intellectual curiosity and critical thinking skills essential for navigating the complexities of Liberal Studies
landscapes.
Career Opportunities
A Ph.D. in Communication from the School of Liberal Studies opens doors to diverse career paths. Graduates can pursue roles in higher education, engaging in teaching and research. Additionally, they can contribute to media research, policy analysis, and make meaningful contributions in the media industry. Opportunities extend to the not-for-profit sector and government, offering a broad spectrum of impactful career choices for Ph.D. holders in Communication.

play a pivotal role in advancing the field of communication and media studies, contributing significantly to the academic discourse and research landscape. Liberal Studies
landscape. In essence, the Ph.D. in Communication program at UPES School of

aims to create a vibrant community of scholars who are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of knowledge in communication and media studies. PEO / PSO
The Ph.D. program in Communication at UPES School of Liberal Studies is designed to cultivate scholars with a comprehensive understanding of media and communication studies. Throughout the program, scholars will engage deeply with relevant literature, theories, and concepts, fostering a solid theoretical foundation. Emphasizing practical application, they will leverage acquired knowledge from coursework, academic engagement, and thesis writing to make meaningful contributions to the evolving academic landscape of media studies. The program equips scholars with the ability to translate their learnings into real-world scenarios, honing skills in presenting theoretically sound arguments and actively participating in academic debates, seminars, and conferences. Additionally, scholars will develop the capacity to deliver well-prepared lectures, conduct seminars and workshops, and produce research-driven, peer-reviewed articles, thereby enhancing the research output of both the school and the university. Rigorous literature review activities will enable scholars to articulate key arguments within existing, new, and developing literature in the dynamic field of media and communication studies.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of a Ph.D. in Communication program is promising, offering scholars valuable research experience in the realms of humanities, social sciences, and media studies. This program provides a platform for scholars to engage in meaningful research, contributing to the advancement of knowledge in these diverse fields. As the intersection of media and society continues to evolve, a Ph.D. in Communication equips scholars with the expertise to address contemporary challenges and explore innovative solutions. Graduates of this program are poised to make significant contributions to academia and industry, shaping the discourse on media in the context of broader societal and cultural dynamics. With a focus on rigorous research, this Ph.D. in Communication program prepares scholars for leadership roles, fostering intellectual curiosity and critical thinking skills essential for navigating the complexities of Liberal Studies
landscapes.
Career Opportunities
A Ph.D. in Communication from the School of Liberal Studies opens doors to diverse career paths. Graduates can pursue roles in higher education, engaging in teaching and research. Additionally, they can contribute to media research, policy analysis, and make meaningful contributions in the media industry. Opportunities extend to the not-for-profit sector and government, offering a broad spectrum of impactful career choices for Ph.D. holders in Communication.
