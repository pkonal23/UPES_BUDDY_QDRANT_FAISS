Program details
The MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) program at UPES School of Business is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge required for mid and senior management roles in diverse industries such as logistics, e-commerce, manufacturing, warehousing, aviation, shipping, research, and consulting. The program addresses the significant shortage of proficient supply chain professionals in the industry by providing hands-on learning experiences, including internships and on-the-job training. This practical approach allows students to apply classroom concepts to real-world scenarios, enhancing their competitiveness in the job market. The curriculum of the program is comprehensive, covering key areas such as procurement, inventory management, transportation, warehousing, and distribution. Students benefit from exposure to real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by experts in the field. The program also stands out by incorporating cutting-edge software and technology training, including SAP, Oracle, and Tally, enhancing students' employability.
A distinctive feature of the program is the Business Process in Supply Chain specialization. This specialization delves into crucial aspects such as Business Process Disruptions, emphasizing the process view of business, performance rating, critical success factors, risk analysis, process innovation, solution implementation, IT applications, business process re-engineering, and change management. Additionally, the program integrates Total Quality Management (TQM) and Six Sigma, introducing quality concepts relevant to the supply chain, TQM principles, statistical process control, TQM tools, quality systems, and hands-on experience with MINITAB.
The pedagogical approach emphasizes real-world practical learning through case studies, practical applications, seminars, workshops, and guest lectures by industry experts. Students also gain exposure to the industry environment through projects, industrial immersion, site visits, and on-the-job internships. This multifaceted approach ensures that graduates of the MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) program are well-prepared for the challenges of the dynamic and evolving global supply chain landscape.
Program Highlights
•    The MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) program emphasizes practical experience through summer internships, live projects with companies, and a major dissertation, ensuring students gain real-world exposure in logistics and supply chain management.
•    Tailored to match the dynamic needs of the energy industry, the curriculum enables students to specialize in natural gas and energy trading, opening up diverse job opportunities in sectors such as energy, finance, and related fields.
•    Students are encouraged to engage in live industry and research projects, attend seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering a comprehensive understanding of the supply chain domain and enhancing their managerial skills.
•    The MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) program integrates real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by industry experts, providing students with practical insights and a deeper understanding of the challenges and opportunities within the logistics and supply chain management field.
•    The management school at UPES boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including modern smart classrooms, a well-equipped analytics Lab, a richly stocked library, and advanced analytical tools, ensuring a conducive learning environment for students.
•    Students have the chance to broaden their horizons through participation in student exchange, study abroad, and semester exchange programs with leading global partner management institutes, facilitating exposure to international perspectives and networks.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) program at the School of Business, UPES, offers a plethora of career prospects for students. Through summer internships, live projects, and placements, students have the chance to immerse themselves in the corporate culture, gaining valuable experience with renowned organizations. The program facilitates career exploration, encouraging students to collaborate with potential employers and potentially secure job opportunities. Supply chain careers span various industries and functions, providing students with diverse placement options in manufacturing, service, and consulting sectors. The comprehensive curriculum equips students with the necessary skills to thrive in the dynamic field of logistics and supply chain management, ensuring a promising future in this rapidly evolving business process.
Career Opportunities
The MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) program offered by UPES School of Business equips graduates with a diverse skill set, opening up a plethora of career opportunities in the dynamic field of supply chain management. Graduates can pursue roles such as supply planner, sourcing specialist, inventory planner/specialist, transport planner, and production planner. Additionally, the program prepares students for positions in supply chain design and planning, e-commerce specialisation, procurement and supply management, as well as freight transportation and distribution network design and planning. With a comprehensive curriculum and industry-focused approach, UPES School of Business ensures that its graduates are well-prepared for success in various sectors of the rapidly evolving supply chain landscape.
Placements
The MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management program at UPES School of Business has witnessed a commendable surge in placement success in recent years. Graduates from this program are highly sought after by esteemed companies such as Flipkart, Reliance Industries, HUL, GATI KWE, Adani Wilmar, Accenture, Deloitte, Kuehne Nagel, Xiaomi, TATA Croma, Odex, Transystem, and PI Industries. Notably, a growing number of mid-sized start-ups are also expressing interest in hiring these postgraduates. Job roles awaiting MBA graduates in this field include Demand Planner, Supply Planner, Logistics Network Design, Supply Chain Analyst, Production Planning, Procurement, and Business Analyst. The starting salary for freshers typically ranges between INR 6-30 lakh per annum, contingent upon individual skills, knowledge, and exposure. This program thus opens doors to a diverse array of rewarding career opportunities in the dynamic world of logistics and supply chain management.

Graduation From a recognized University in any stream.
Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for individuals interested in enrolling in the MBA program in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) at UPES School of Business rely on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams / followed by Personal Interview.
Non-Examination Pathways:
Students having a minimum eligible MAT /CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will only be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Program details
The MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) program at UPES School of Business is designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge required for mid and senior management roles in diverse industries such as logistics, e-commerce, manufacturing, warehousing, aviation, shipping, research, and consulting. The program addresses the significant shortage of proficient supply chain professionals in the industry by providing hands-on learning experiences, including internships and on-the-job training. This practical approach allows students to apply classroom concepts to real-world scenarios, enhancing their competitiveness in the job market. The curriculum of the program is comprehensive, covering key areas such as procurement, inventory management, transportation, warehousing, and distribution. Students benefit from exposure to real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by experts in the field. The program also stands out by incorporating cutting-edge software and technology training, including SAP, Oracle, and Tally, enhancing students' employability.
A distinctive feature of the program is the Business Process in Supply Chain specialization. This specialization delves into crucial aspects such as Business Process Disruptions, emphasizing the process view of business, performance rating, critical success factors, risk analysis, process innovation, solution implementation, IT applications, business process re-engineering, and change management. Additionally, the program integrates Total Quality Management (TQM) and Six Sigma, introducing quality concepts relevant to the supply chain, TQM principles, statistical process control, TQM tools, quality systems, and hands-on experience with MINITAB.
The pedagogical approach emphasizes real-world practical learning through case studies, practical applications, seminars, workshops, and guest lectures by industry experts. Students also gain exposure to the industry environment through projects, industrial immersion, site visits, and on-the-job internships. This multifaceted approach ensures that graduates of the MBA in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) program are well-prepared for the challenges of the dynamic and evolving global supply chain landscape.

Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for individuals interested in enrolling in the MBA program in Logistics & Supply Chain Management (Business Process in Supply Chain) at UPES School of Business rely on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams / followed by Personal Interview.
Non-Examination Pathways:
Students having a minimum eligible MAT /CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will only be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Valid 50 percentile and above score of CAT 2024/ XAT 2025 A score of minimum 400 or above for GMAT, a score of 120 and above in NMAT 2024 / 25 scores will also be accepted.
