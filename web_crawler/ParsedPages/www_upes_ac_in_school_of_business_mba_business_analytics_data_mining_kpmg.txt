**Master of Business Administration in Business Analytics in Collaboration with KPMG: Enhancing Business Decision-Making through Data Mining**

**Program Overview**

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) offers a Master of Business Administration in Business Analytics in collaboration with the renowned accounting firm, KPMG. This program integrates business administration principles with advanced analytics techniques to equip students with the skills necessary to address complex business challenges. The partnership with KPMG enriches the curriculum with real-world insights, industry best practices, and expert lectures from KPMG professionals.

**Program Highlights**

* Combines core business courses with specialized analytics courses and hands-on projects.
* Emphasizes data mining, predictive analytics, machine learning, statistical modeling, data visualization, and big data management.
* Provides opportunities for students to engage in industry projects, internships, and networking events.
* Offers specialization tracks in either analytics and data-driven decision-making or consulting.
* Equips students with proficiency in programming and analytical tools such as SPSS, Tableau, Power BI, R-studio, Python, SQL, and more.
* Fosters collaboration and networking through student and faculty exchange, research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships with industry professionals and UPES global partner management institutes.
* Provides state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, an Analytics Lab, well-stocked library, and advanced analytical tools.
* Offers opportunities for student exchange programs, study abroad, and semester exchange programs to gain international exposure.

**Future Scope and Industry Trends**

The demand for professionals skilled in business analytics and data mining is rapidly growing as organizations recognize the value of data-driven decision-making. This specialized Master of Business Administration program at UPES equips students with the skill set to meet this demand and excel in a competitive business landscape.

**Career Opportunities**

Upon successful completion of the Master of Business Administration in Business Analytics program, graduates are sought after by various industries and organizations. Potential career opportunities include:

* Data Analyst
* Business Intelligence Manager
* Data Scientist
* Analytics Consultant
* Business Analyst
* Data Mining Specialist
* Risk Analyst

**Placements**

The Master of Business Administration in Business Analytics program boasts impressive placement prospects, with renowned companies such as Flipkart, Reliance Industries, HUL, Accenture, Deloitte, Xiaomi, and PI Industries hiring graduates. Freshers can expect starting salaries ranging from INR 6 to 30 lakh per annum, based on their skills, knowledge, and experience.

**Eligibility Criteria**

To be eligible for the Master of Business Administration in Business Analytics program in collaboration with KPMG, applicants must meet the following criteria:

* Minimum 50% marks in Class X, XII, and Graduation.
* Graduation from a recognized university in any stream.

**Selection Criteria**

Selection for the program is based on the applicant's performance in UPESMET, National Level Exams, or CUET, followed by a personal interview.
