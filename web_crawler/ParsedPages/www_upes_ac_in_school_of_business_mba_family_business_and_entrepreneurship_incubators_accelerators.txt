Program details
The
Family Business and Entrepreneurship (Incubators & Accelerators)
with a specialization in Incubators & Accelerators at UPES School of Business is designed to equip students with the necessary knowledge and skills to support and nurture startups. The curriculum blends core business management courses with specialized courses focused on incubation and acceleration strategies. Through the core courses, students will gain a strong foundation in various business disciplines, including finance, marketing, operations, and leadership. These courses provide essential knowledge and skills required to effectively understand and manage businesses. The specialization courses then dive into the unique aspects of incubators and accelerators, covering topics such as startup evaluation, business model innovation, funding strategies, and scaling strategies.
To ensure practical learning, the program emphasizes hands-on experiences such as live projects, case studies, and internships in startup incubators, accelerators, or related organizations. Students will have the opportunity to engage with industry experts and successful entrepreneurs through workshops, guest lectures, and networking events, enabling them to gain valuable insights into the startup ecosystem. Field visits to prominent startup incubators, accelerators, and entrepreneurial ecosystems further enhance the students' understanding of the thriving startup culture in India. These visits provide a firsthand experience of the real-world challenges faced by startups in the incubation and acceleration process. In addition, a capstone project allows students to apply their knowledge and skills to address these challenges.
Upon completion of the program, graduates will be well-prepared for careers in startup incubators, accelerators, venture capital firms, corporate innovation teams, and entrepreneurship development organizations. They will possess the necessary expertise to evaluate, select, and support startups, contribute to business model innovation, facilitate funding and investment, and drive the growth and scaling of startups.
Program Highlights
The
MBA in Family Business and Entrepreneurship (Incubators & Accelerators)
students gain a solid foundation in essential business disciplines while focusing on incubation and acceleration strategies, equipping you with a well-rounded understanding of business management principles.
Engage in hands-on experience through live projects, case studies, and internships in startup incubators, accelerators, or related organizations, preparing you for real-world challenges in supporting and nurturing startups.
Interact with industry experts and successful entrepreneurs through workshops, guest lectures, and networking events, gaining valuable insights and building a strong network within the startup ecosystem.
Visit prominent startup incubators, accelerators, and entrepreneurial ecosystems, gaining exposure to the thriving startup culture in India and staying updated with the latest trends and practices.
Apply your knowledge and skills to address real-world challenges faced by startups, integrating your learnings from the program and showcasing your abilities to potential employers.
Graduates are well-equipped to pursue careers in startup incubators, accelerators, venture capital firms, corporate innovation teams, and entrepreneurship development organizations, making a meaningful impact on the entrepreneurial ecosystem in India.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope for graduates of
MBA in Family Business and Entrepreneurship
, specializing in incubators and accelerators, is incredibly promising. They can manage and support startups, helping them grow within incubators and accelerators. Graduates can contribute to building a robust startup ecosystem by collaborating with government organizations, industry associations, and non-profit entities. They can analyze investment opportunities, provide funding, and offer support to early-stage startups through venture capital firms or angel investing networks. Graduates can facilitate partnerships between established companies and startups, fostering innovation and mutual growth. They can also provide specialized consulting services, including strategic guidance, business planning, and market analysis to startups. Some graduates may even venture into entrepreneurship themselves, utilizing their expertise in incubation and acceleration to launch and expand their own startups. Additionally, graduates can deliver programs, workshops, and mentor aspiring entrepreneurs in educational institutions and entrepreneurship development organizations. They can also influence policies and regulations that support entrepreneurship and the growth of startups. Through these various avenues, graduates can have a significant impact on the startup ecosystem in India, supporting the growth of innovative ventures and contributing to the overall development of the entrepreneurial landscape.
Career Opportunities
Completing an
MBA in Family Business and Entrepreneurship (Incubators & Accelerators)
from UPES School of Business opens up a wide array of promising career opportunities. Graduates can explore roles as business incubator managers, where they can support and mentor aspiring entrepreneurs, providing valuable guidance and resources to help them launch successful ventures. Alternatively, they can pursue positions as business development managers in accelerators, facilitating the growth of early-stage companies through strategic partnerships, funding, and networking opportunities. Furthermore, graduates can embark on entrepreneurial journeys themselves, leveraging the knowledge and skills acquired during the program to establish and manage their own startups or family businesses. The program equips individuals with the expertise to thrive in the dynamic world of entrepreneurship, empowering them to make a significant impact in the business ecosystem.
Below is a list of professional paths that students in the field of
MBA in Family Business and Entrepreneurship (Incubators & Accelerators)
may choose to pursue.
Incubator / Accelerator Manager
Start-up Ecosystem Developer
Venture Capital

Placements
The placements for the
MBA in Family Business and Entrepreneurship (Incubators & Accelerators)
program offered by UPES School of Business are highly promising and cater specifically to the needs of aspiring entrepreneurs and those interested in managing family businesses. With a focus on practical learning and industry exposure, UPES ensures that students are well-prepared to succeed in the dynamic startup ecosystem. The program provides valuable opportunities for students to work with leading incubators and accelerators, allowing them to gain hands-on experience, network with industry experts, and access resources to nurture their entrepreneurial ventures. UPES School of Business has established strong ties with various incubators and accelerators, both nationally and internationally, providing students with a wide range of placement opportunities in these prestigious organizations. Graduates of this program are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and networks necessary to excel as entrepreneurs or effectively manage family businesses in today's competitive business environment.
