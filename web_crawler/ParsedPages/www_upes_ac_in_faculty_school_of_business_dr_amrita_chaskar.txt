Dr. Amrita Chaskar

Assistant Professor - Senior Scale

Profile Summary
Dr. Amrita Chaskar completed her Ph.D. in the area of crisis communication from BITS Pilani K K Birla Goa Campus in April 2024. She has joined UPES as an Assistant Professor in School of Business, marking the start of her teaching career.
Work Experience
Before joining UPES, Dr. Chaskar worked with IIM Indore as an Academic Associate in the area of Communication. Additionally, she has around 2 years of industry experience in the area of HR.
Research Interests
Organizational crisis communication I Crisis communication and crisis leadership | Crisis communication and reputation | Crisis communication and organizational resilience
Teaching Philosophy
Dr. Chaskar’s teaching philosophy is rooted in the belief that it is a transformative process that goes beyond imparting knowledge within the classroom walls. She believes that it involves fostering empathy, nurturing individual growth, and empowering learners to achieve their full potential. She is committed to creating a supportive and inclusive learning environment.
Courses Taught
Business Communication
Awards and Grants
Dr. Chaskar received the Best TA Award during her doctoral programme from the Teaching Learning Centre- BITS Goa, demonstrating her dedication and commitment to foster a positive learning environment for the students.
Scholarly Activities
Dr. Chaskar has published research articles in reputed Scopus-indexed international journals. Her doctoral research focused on the crisis communication strategies of Goa's hospitality industry during the COVID-19 pandemic. She plans to build on her research findings and apply these insights to delve deeper into the area of organizational crisis communication and the related outcomes.
Contact
Amrita.Chaskar@ddn.upes.ac.in
