Avita Katal

Assistant Professor (SG)

Profile Summary
Avita Katal is a dynamic and resourceful teaching professional with a Master of Technology and Bachelor of Engineering Degree in Computer Science, backed by a decade of experience.  In addition to her academic qualifications, she has also completed a Post Graduate Certificate in Academic Practice (PGCAP). Avita is actively involved in various aspects of education, demonstrating a passion for research, curriculum development, teacher mentoring, student career preparation, and community work. She possesses a genuine interest in fostering the cognitive and social growth of her students, ensuring their holistic development. As an effective communicator and resourceful teacher, Avita has the ability to enhance student performance, promising to shape a better world for her students by empowering them with knowledge.  Avita is a self-driven leader with excellent interpersonal skills, allowing her to effectively collaborate with staff members at all levels. She values building quality relationships with her students, fostering a positive and supportive learning environment.
Work Experience
Avita Katal is an accomplished academic with a decade of experience in education. She started as an Assistant Professor at Graphic Era Hill University and later joined the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES). Now holding the position of Assistant Professor (Selection Grade) at UPES, she received the prestigious Academic Excellence Award for the year  2021 and 2022 in  School of Computer Science, UPES. Ms. Katal also actively contributes to student development as a Faculty Coordinator for UPES-CSA student chapter. Her commitment to education and research makes her a valuable asset in academia.
Research Interests
Avita Katal's research interests encompass a diverse range of areas, including Cloud Computing, Fog/Edge Computing, MANET, and Curriculum Development, reflecting her commitment to advancing technology and education. Her Ph.D. work is aligned with UN Sustainable Development Goal 7: Affordable and Clean Energy, focusing on designing energy-efficient container placement and consolidation techniques in cloud data centers. Moreover, her Post Graduate Diploma in Teaching and Academic Practice enhances her pedagogical skills, enabling her to incorporate innovative teaching methods. This combination of research expertise, specialized training, and teaching acumen empowers her to make significant contributions to technological advancements, sustainability, and transformative education practices.
Teaching Philosophy
Avita Katal's student-centered teaching philosophy focuses on active engagement, critical thinking, and practical application of knowledge. She creates a supportive and inclusive learning environment, encouraging students to collaborate, question, and connect theory with real-world examples. By integrating industry projects, internships, and guest lectures, she equips students with the skills for career success. Avita prioritizes staying updated with pedagogical approaches and incorporates the Post Graduate Certificate in Academic Practice (PGCAP) to enhance her teaching. Her goal is to empower students as independent learners, fostering lifelong learning and personal growth in an inclusive and stimulating setting.
Courses Taught
Avita Katal is a specialist in Cloud Computing and Virtualization subjects, teaching topics like Introduction to Virtualization & Cloud Computing, Cloud Computing Architecture, Container Orchestration and Infrastructure Automation. She equips students with skills to create cloud applications, manage cloud infrastructures, implement virtualization techniques, and work with database systems in cloud environments. Her expertise extends to various subjects like Knowledge Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Java Programming, Web Technologies, and more. With practical labs and mentorship, Avita actively supports B.Tech students in the domain of cloud computing. Her extensive teaching experience and diverse course offerings prepare students for successful careers in technology and research.
Awards and Grants
Avita Katal received the prestigious Academic Excellence Award for 2021-2022 and 2022-2023 in the School of Computer Science, UPES recognizing her exceptional teaching and research dedication. She was also honored as the Silver Medalist during her M.Tech course, showcasing her academic excellence. Avita's involvement in innovative student projects, such as "Heart Attack Detector" and "Microservices based SaaS application for unified communication in Campus," earned her team the first runner-up position in Project Parliament. These accolades highlight her ability to apply her knowledge to practical solutions and underscore her commitment to excellence and innovation in computer science.
Scholarly Activities
Avita Katal is a prolific researcher who has made significant contributions to various fields through her publications. Her research papers delve into diverse topics such as energy efficiency in cloud computing data centers, VM migration and container placements and consolidation in cloud data centers, curriculum design in higher education, and effective teaching strategies. She has also explored emerging technologies like Fog Computing, IoT and Blockchain. Avita's books cover subjects such as telemedicine, database management systems, and fog computing, providing comprehensive insights and frameworks. Through her publications, Avita Katal has advanced knowledge and practice in areas including energy efficiency, education, healthcare, and emerging technologies. Avita Katal's contributions to the research field have garnered significant recognition, with their work receiving over 1600 citations. This substantial citation count reflects the impact and influence of their research within the academic community. Her work demonstrates a deep commitment to research excellence and a desire to drive progress in these fields.
Contact
akatal@ddn.upes.ac.in
