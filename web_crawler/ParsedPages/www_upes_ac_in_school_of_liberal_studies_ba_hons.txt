**Program Overview**

The Bachelor of Arts (Honours) program at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) in Dehradun, India, is a four-year undergraduate program designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of their chosen specializations: English, History, and Political Science. The program is structured to equip students with critical thinking, analytical, and strong communication skills.

**Course Structure**

During the first three years of the Bachelor of Arts (Honours) program, students complete core subjects. In the final year, students have the opportunity to undertake an internship or a research project in their chosen area of specialization. This allows students to gain practical experience and apply their knowledge and skills to real-world situations.

**Faculty and Curriculum**

The Bachelor of Arts (Honours) program is taught by experienced faculty members who are experts in their respective fields. The curriculum is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in literature and a range of transferable skills that are highly valued by employers.

**Career Opportunities**

Graduates of the Bachelor of Arts (Honours) program can pursue careers in fields such as publishing, journalism, media, advertising, education, and research. The dual specialization offered by the program enables students to gain a unique combination of skills that are highly valued by employers.

**Eligibility Criteria**

To be eligible for admission to the Bachelor of Arts (Honours) program, applicants must meet the following criteria:

* XII Std from an IB Board with a minimum score of 24
* Valid SAT Score
* Minimum 50% marks in class X and XII (any Stream)
* Math/Eco/Political Sc. in 12th is preferred
* Non-Mathematics background students will undergo the Remedial Mathematics module in Semester 1
