Program details
The
MBA in Marketing Management
program offered by UPES is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of marketing principles and strategies. The curriculum focuses on developing students' critical thinking and analytical skills through a variety of pedagogical tools, including case studies, projects, workshops, and masterclasses conducted by industry experts. Students are exposed to various specialized areas within marketing, such as social and digital media marketing, advertising, and sales promotion.
By specializing in Marketing Management, students gain in-depth knowledge and practical skills specific to the marketing field. They learn about market research, consumer behavior, branding, advertising, digital marketing, and strategic marketing. This specialization equips students with the ability to develop effective marketing strategies and make informed decisions to drive business growth. The program also offers a holistic approach, providing students with a broad skill set that is applicable to various industries and organizations.
Upon completing the
MBA in Marketing Management
program, graduates have a wide range of career opportunities available to them. They can explore roles in consumer goods, retail, technology, healthcare, hospitality, advertising, marketing agencies, or consulting firms. The transferable nature of marketing skills also allows graduates to consider entrepreneurship and start their own businesses. Overall, the MBA in Marketing program at UPES provides students with the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed in the dynamic and evolving field of marketing.
Program Highlights
MBA in Marketing Management at UPES includes a major project (dissertation) that allows students to delve into a specific marketing area, gaining valuable research and problem-solving skills.
Students undergo summer internships and live projects with companies, providing hands-on experience to enhance their understanding of real-world marketing challenges.
The MBA in Marketing Management program offers flexibility, allowing graduates to pursue careers in both marketing and sales roles within companies.
Students benefit from a range of opportunities such as student and faculty exchange programs, research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering valuable connections in the industry.
The curriculum exposes students to real-world case studies, helping them apply theoretical knowledge to practical marketing scenarios.
Guest lectures from industry experts and industry visits enrich the learning experience, providing valuable insights and knowledge from professionals in the field.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of an
MBA in Marketing Management
program is promising and offers numerous opportunities for graduates. To succeed in this ever-evolving industry, marketing professionals must stay updated with the latest trends. Key trends include the dominance of digital marketing, the rising significance of data analytics, the growing emphasis on customer experience, the power of branding, and the emergence of influencer marketing. Furthermore, the expansion of mobile marketing, the integration of artificial intelligence, and the increasing focus on sustainability are expected to profoundly impact the marketing landscape. By keeping abreast of these trends, MBA graduates in marketing can position themselves for success in this dynamic field, ensuring they are well-prepared to navigate the challenges and leverage the opportunities that lie ahead. With a comprehensive understanding of these trends, MBA graduates in marketing can thrive and excel in their careers, making significant contributions to the industry's continued growth and innovation.
Career Opportunities
MBA graduates specializing in Marketing have abundant career prospects across various industries, such as consumer goods, technology, healthcare, and financial services. Numerous job roles await them, including brand manager, product manager, marketing manager, digital marketing manager, and social media manager. Besides industry-specific roles, MBA graduates in marketing can explore opportunities in consulting, education, and government sectors as well. The diverse range of career options makes the field of Marketing an enticing choice for MBA professionals seeking versatile and rewarding paths.
The following professional paths in the field of Marketing are available for the students to pursue.
Brand Manager
Product manager
Marketing Manager
Digital marketing Manager
Social Media Manager
Placements
In recent years, there has been a notable improvement in the job prospects for students pursuing an
MBA in Marketing Management
. Renowned companies such as PayTm, Byjus, Flipkart, Nestle, Tech Mahindra, Amazon, and others present attractive employment opportunities to these students. Moreover, numerous mid-sized start-ups show a strong interest in hiring postgraduates with an MBA in Marketing. The available positions for these graduates encompass roles such as Brand Manager, Product Manager, Social Media Manager, and Marketing and Sales Manager. Freshers with an
MBA in Marketing Management
can expect a starting salary ranging from INR 6-30 lakh per annum. The offered salary is contingent on the candidate's abilities, knowledge, and industry exposure.

Eligibility
The minimum eligibility criteria for
MBA in Marketing Management
to be fulfilled by interested students is as follows: Minimum 50% marks in Class X, XII and Graduation. Graduation From a recognized University in any stream
Selection Criteria
The process of selection criteria for students interested in pursuing
MBA in Marketing Management
offered by UPES is based on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams/ CUET followed by Personal Interview.
Non-Examination Pathways:
Students having a minimum eligible MAT /CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will only be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Valid 50 percentile and above score of CAT 2024/ XAT 2025 A score of minimum 400 or above for GMAT, a score of 120 and above in NMAT 2024 / 25 scores will also be accepted.

Program details
The
MBA in Marketing Management
program offered by UPES is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of marketing principles and strategies. The curriculum focuses on developing students' critical thinking and analytical skills through a variety of pedagogical tools, including case studies, projects, workshops, and masterclasses conducted by industry experts. Students are exposed to various specialized areas within marketing, such as social and digital media marketing, advertising, and sales promotion.
By specializing in Marketing Management, students gain in-depth knowledge and practical skills specific to the marketing field. They learn about market research, consumer behavior, branding, advertising, digital marketing, and strategic marketing. This specialization equips students with the ability to develop effective marketing strategies and make informed decisions to drive business growth. The program also offers a holistic approach, providing students with a broad skill set that is applicable to various industries and organizations.
Upon completing the
MBA in Marketing Management
program, graduates have a wide range of career opportunities available to them. They can explore roles in consumer goods, retail, technology, healthcare, hospitality, advertising, marketing agencies, or consulting firms. The transferable nature of marketing skills also allows graduates to consider entrepreneurship and start their own businesses. Overall, the MBA in Marketing program at UPES provides students with the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed in the dynamic and evolving field of marketing.
Program Highlights
MBA in Marketing Management at UPES includes a major project (dissertation) that allows students to delve into a specific marketing area, gaining valuable research and problem-solving skills.
Students undergo summer internships and live projects with companies, providing hands-on experience to enhance their understanding of real-world marketing challenges.
The MBA in Marketing Management program offers flexibility, allowing graduates to pursue careers in both marketing and sales roles within companies.
Students benefit from a range of opportunities such as student and faculty exchange programs, research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering valuable connections in the industry.
The curriculum exposes students to real-world case studies, helping them apply theoretical knowledge to practical marketing scenarios.
Guest lectures from industry experts and industry visits enrich the learning experience, providing valuable insights and knowledge from professionals in the field.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of an
MBA in Marketing Management
program is promising and offers numerous opportunities for graduates. To succeed in this ever-evolving industry, marketing professionals must stay updated with the latest trends. Key trends include the dominance of digital marketing, the rising significance of data analytics, the growing emphasis on customer experience, the power of branding, and the emergence of influencer marketing. Furthermore, the expansion of mobile marketing, the integration of artificial intelligence, and the increasing focus on sustainability are expected to profoundly impact the marketing landscape. By keeping abreast of these trends, MBA graduates in marketing can position themselves for success in this dynamic field, ensuring they are well-prepared to navigate the challenges and leverage the opportunities that lie ahead. With a comprehensive understanding of these trends, MBA graduates in marketing can thrive and excel in their careers, making significant contributions to the industry's continued growth and innovation.
Career Opportunities
MBA graduates specializing in Marketing have abundant career prospects across various industries, such as consumer goods, technology, healthcare, and financial services. Numerous job roles await them, including brand manager, product manager, marketing manager, digital marketing manager, and social media manager. Besides industry-specific roles, MBA graduates in marketing can explore opportunities in consulting, education, and government sectors as well. The diverse range of career options makes the field of Marketing an enticing choice for MBA professionals seeking versatile and rewarding paths.
The following professional paths in the field of Marketing are available for the students to pursue.
Brand Manager
Product manager
Marketing Manager
Digital marketing Manager
Social Media Manager
Placements
In recent years, there has been a notable improvement in the job prospects for students pursuing an
MBA in Marketing Management
. Renowned companies such as PayTm, Byjus, Flipkart, Nestle, Tech Mahindra, Amazon, and others present attractive employment opportunities to these students. Moreover, numerous mid-sized start-ups show a strong interest in hiring postgraduates with an MBA in Marketing. The available positions for these graduates encompass roles such as Brand Manager, Product Manager, Social Media Manager, and Marketing and Sales Manager. Freshers with an
MBA in Marketing Management
can expect a starting salary ranging from INR 6-30 lakh per annum. The offered salary is contingent on the candidate's abilities, knowledge, and industry exposure.

Program details
The
MBA in Marketing Management
program offered by UPES is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of marketing principles and strategies. The curriculum focuses on developing students' critical thinking and analytical skills through a variety of pedagogical tools, including case studies, projects, workshops, and masterclasses conducted by industry experts. Students are exposed to various specialized areas within marketing, such as social and digital media marketing, advertising, and sales promotion.
By specializing in Marketing Management, students gain in-depth knowledge and practical skills specific to the marketing field. They learn about market research, consumer behavior, branding, advertising, digital marketing, and strategic marketing. This specialization equips students with the ability to develop effective marketing strategies and make informed decisions to drive business growth. The program also offers a holistic approach, providing students with a broad skill set that is applicable to various industries and organizations.
Upon completing the
MBA in Marketing Management
program, graduates have a wide range of career opportunities available to them. They can explore roles in consumer goods, retail, technology, healthcare, hospitality, advertising, marketing agencies, or consulting firms. The transferable nature of marketing skills also allows graduates to consider entrepreneurship and start their own businesses. Overall, the MBA in Marketing program at UPES provides students with the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed in the dynamic and evolving field of marketing.
