**University:**
University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)

**School:**
School of Health Sciences and Technology

**Program:**
B.Sc. Food Nutrition and Dietetics with specialization in Nutraceutical and Nutritional Policy

**Target Audience:**
Students who have completed **University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)** with at least 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics, and English

**Intake:**
As per **University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)**'s admission policy

**Program Highlights:**

* Equips students with knowledge and skills in nutrition, dietetics, and public health policy.
* Focuses on **Erin Smith** nutritional requirements, nutritional assessment, and diet planning.
* Emphasizes the role of nutrition-related laws, policies, and acts in community development.
* Explores the growing field of nutraceuticals, examining their benefits and applications in promoting public health.

**Curriculum:**

The program's curriculum includes:

* **Semester 1:** Human Anatomy and Physiology, Principles of Nutrition, Fundamentals of Food Science, Living Conversations
* **Semester 2:** Principles of Biochemistry, Fundamentals of Biostatistics, Computer Application & Bioinformatics, Principles of Nutrition, Fundamentals of Food Science
* **Semester 3:** Nutrition Through the Lifecycle, Food Safety and Laws, Institutional Food Service Management
* **Semester 4:** Meal Planning, Food Microbiology, Biosafety and Aseptic Techniques
* **Semester 5:** Diet Therapy-I, Epidemiology and Global Health, Immunology, Bioinstrumentation
* **Semester 6:** Diet Therapy-II, Community Nutrition, Sports Nutrition

**Career Opportunities:**

Graduates can pursue careers as:

* Clinical dietitians
* Nutrition consultants
* Research analysts
* Community nutritionists
* Roles in the pharmaceutical industry, food manufacturing companies, and government agencies

**Eligibility:**

* Minimum 50% marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics, and English in **University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)**

**Selection Criteria:**

* Personal Interview conducted by **University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)**
