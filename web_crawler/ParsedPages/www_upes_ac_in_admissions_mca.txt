Course Overview
About MCA Courses
UPES offers different specialisations for MCA courses catering to a wide range of subjects in the technical and financial domains. The duration of all the specialisations is three years or six semesters.
These MCA specialisations can help students switch career domains to other disciplines if they wish to do so in the future. With UPES receiving the QS I-Gauge E-Lead Certification for online learning, students can rest assured about the quality of education, regardless of the delivery model.
Here are the details of each MCA course specialisation.
MCA Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
The course gives students a broad understanding of the concepts of Artificial Intelligence like Data Structures, Algorithms, and Design Processes. Students will also learn about the applications of these concepts in real-world situations such as Natural Language Processing, Robotics, or Computer Vision.
MCA Data Science
The MCA Data Science course offers a comprehensive foundation in the concepts and practices of Data Science, including data structures, algorithms, and statistical analysis. Students will explore essential skills in data visualization, data manipulation, and machine learning algorithms. The course emphasizes practical applications, preparing students to analyze and interpret large datasets in areas such as finance, healthcare, and marketing, enabling them to drive data-driven decisions in real-world scenarios.
MCA Cyber Security and Digital Forensics
The MCA Cyber Security and Digital Forensics course equips students with essential skills to protect and investigate digital environments. The curriculum covers core concepts such as network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking. Students will also learn advanced techniques in digital forensics, enabling them to trace cyber threats, conduct incident response, and analyze digital evidence. This course prepares students to address real-world cyber challenges, safeguarding data integrity and ensuring secure digital practices across various industries.

MCA Career Prospects
The innumerable attractive career prospects in the tech space after graduation is one of the primary USPs of an MCA degree from UPES.
UPES has a proven track record of providing career placements to 94% of its students. More than 560 companies including Hyundai, Vedanta, Halliburton, and L&T Infotech hire students for different roles.
A few interesting job roles for each MCA course specialisation are mentioned below.
Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
: Students can explore roles like machine learning engineer, data scientist, AI data analyst or a business intelligence developer.
Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics:
Graduates specializing in Cyber Security and Digital Forensics can pursue roles like cyber security analyst, digital forensics investigator, penetration tester, network security engineer, and information security manager. These professionals are instrumental in protecting data, preventing cyber threats, and investigating security breaches.
Data Science
: Students specializing in Data Science can explore roles such as data analyst, machine learning engineer, data engineer, business intelligence analyst, and data architect. These roles involve interpreting complex data, creating predictive models, and helping businesses make data-driven decisions across industries.

Yes. An MCA in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning can get you roles like machine learning engineer, data scientist, AI data analyst or business intelligence developer
Has UPES taken any measures to safeguard its students considering the present circumstances?
Yes, UPES has moved its technical interviews for admission online and has introduced online learning for most of its courses.
