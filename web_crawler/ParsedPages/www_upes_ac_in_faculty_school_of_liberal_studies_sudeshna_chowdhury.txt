**Sudeshna Chowdhury**

Sudeshna Chowdhury, an Associate Professor at the School of Liberal Studies at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES), is a highly experienced journalist, educator, and communication expert.

**Academic and Professional Background**

With a Master's degree in Journalism from Indiana University, Bloomington, specializing in Global Journalism, Sudeshna Chowdhury has worked as a journalist in both India and the United States. Sudeshna Chowdhury's journalistic experience spans various renowned media outlets, including Deutsche Welle, The Atlantic, Thomson Reuters Foundation, The Christian Science Monitor, Inter Press Service, Bloomberg UTV, WFHB 91.3 FM, Hindustan Times, and Mid-DAY.

Additionally, Sudeshna Chowdhury has held the position of communication expert at the Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) of the United Nations in New York. Sudeshna Chowdhury's research interests lie in Gender / War, Peace and Conflict, Radio, and Narrative Journalism.

**Teaching Philosophy and Courses Taught**

As a Professor of Practice, Sudeshna Chowdhury emphasizes hands-on experience in reporting and writing. Sudeshna Chowdhury encourages her students to engage in "shoe leather" reporting to develop strong foundational skills in field reporting. Sudeshna Chowdhury instills the value of professionalism, expecting her students to produce output that meets industry standards.

Chowdhury teaches a diverse range of courses, including those that explore the emerging role of social media in driving political and social changes, the convergence between global media and digital networks, and professional studio work with multicamera setup and floor management techniques.

**Awards and Grants**

Sudeshna Chowdhury's journalistic work has garnered recognition from various organizations worldwide, and Sudeshna Chowdhury's work has been featured by national and international entities.

**Scholarly Activities**

In her role as a communication lead at the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations, Sudeshna Chowdhury managed the media landscape in North America and actively worked to enhance the organization's profile within the region. Sudeshna Chowdhury has also worked as a Science Journalist in Boston, interviewing prominent scientists in the field of science and technology worldwide. Additionally, Sudeshna Chowdhury has served as a UN correspondent in New York.
