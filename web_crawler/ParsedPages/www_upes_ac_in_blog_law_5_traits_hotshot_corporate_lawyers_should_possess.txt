5 traits hotshot corporate lawyers should possess
Mansha Dhingra
Published 17/06/2020
Corporate law involves working closely with companies and organizations to advise them about legal rights, obligations, and responsibilities
The legal field is considered prestigious as it helps an individual or organisation get justice within the ambit of law. It is one of the reasons why many students are opting for law in the present times. There are plenty of specializations in the law field to choose from. These include banking, finance and insurance law, criminal law, corporate law, medical and forensic law, labour law, and many more.
One of the most viable law courses is BBA LLB Hons. with a specialization in corporate law. The curriculum in this law specialization offers in-depth knowledge of the field. Corporate law involves working closely with companies and organisations to advise them about legal rights, obligations, and responsibilities. But before you set out to pursue an LLB course, you must ensure that you possess the essential personality traits to become a successful lawyer. Given below are a few traits that make a lawyer stand above the rest:
Perseverance:
If you are determined to find a solution to every problem, no matter what, then corporate law is the right option for you. If there is a problem, you must be ready find the best solution possible.
Sharp Analytical Skills
: Being a lawyer means having to distill enormous amounts of information to reach a logical conclusion. Often, there is more than one way for a conflict resolution; you must choose the best suitable option for your client.
Listening skills:
If you are a good listener and observer, it will contribute immensely to your corporate law career. The ability to listen carefully and patiently to your clients will help you solve tasks more efficiently.
Being a lawyer means having to distill enormous amounts of information to reach a logical conclusion
Multitasking:
If you can tackle multiple tasks competently by finding the best solution for each one of them, then the field of law is a perfect choice for you. There will be many problems and responsibilities that will be thrown your way throughout the day. No two problems will be alike. Regardless of that, you’ll have to competently resolve them.
Decision-making:
As a corporate lawyer, you must make decisions that are in the best interest of your clients. There will be many instances where you’ll have to concentrate on details, analyse, and then exercise good judgement.
Pubic Speaking:
As a lawyer, you must have public speaking abilities to be able to communicate your point of view with ease. You will have to go through various trials to gain justice for your clients. You would need to be assertive, persuasive, friendly, clear, factual, and have the ability to gauge other’s reactions.
If you are someone who has all these traits, then a career in corporate law will be worthwhile for you. Hard work, determination, passion and a degree from a reputed university, will set you firmly on your path to becoming a hotshot corporate lawyer.
Mansha Dhingra
The writer is a part of the UPES editorial team
