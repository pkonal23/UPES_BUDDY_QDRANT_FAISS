Snehlata Shrivastava

IAS (Retd.) Former Secretary-General, Lok Sabha & Secterary, Ministry of Law & Justice

Snehlata Shrivastava has worked as First Woman Secretary-General, Lok Sabha (Parliament of India) from 2017 to 2020, as the senior-most officer of Parliament’s Lower House and was responsible to assist Lok Sabha Speaker to run the Parliament House as per rules and conventions and did all administrative duties for the Secretariat employees. International Parliament Diplomacy was handled during various Parliaments abroad. Before that, as an officer of the Indian Administrative Service (IAS) during the 35 years of Government Service, she held various positions in the State Government of Madhya Pradesh and served in various capacities in different Ministries of Govt. of India - Ministry of Home Affairs, Ministry of Finance and Ministry of Law and Justice. Retired as Secretary, Department of Justice in 2017. As Special / Additional Secretary, Department of Financial Services, Ministry of Finance, also dealt with Banking, Insurance & Pension Funds and was Government appointed Director on the Boards of Industrial Development Bank of India (IDBI), Life Insurance Corporation (LIC), General Insurance Corporation (GIC) & National Bank for Agriculture & Rural Development (NABARD).
