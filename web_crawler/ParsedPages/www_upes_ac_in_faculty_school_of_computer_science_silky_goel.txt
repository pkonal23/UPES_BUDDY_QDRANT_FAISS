Silky Goel

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Silky Goel’s research interest lies in Medical Imaging Analysis, Machine Learning and Deep Learning in healthcare. She acquired B.Tech (CSE) from Uttarakhand Technical University, Dehradun, M.Tech (Computer Engineering) from IIIT Bhubaneswar, and pursuing PhD from UPES, Dehradun
Work Experience
Silky Goel has 7+ years of teaching and industry experience.? She joined UPES in 2019 and before she worked with Graphic Era Hill University from 2016-2019.
Research Interests
Computer Vision I Image Processing I Medical Image Processing I Data Analysis I Machine Learning I Artificial Intelligence I Deep Learning I Crowd Analysis
Teaching Philosophy
Silky Goel's teaching philosophy is firmly rooted in the belief that it is their responsibility to create a supportive and encouraging learning environment for students to integrate group activity in the classroom, real-world applications, and continuous assessment to empower learners.
Courses Taught
Data Structures and Algorithm
Design and Analysis of Algorithm
Computer Organization
Operating System
System Provisioning and Configuration Management
Distributed Computing
Database Management System
Awards and Grants
Silky Goel cleared GATE in 2014.
Scholarly Activities
Silky Goel actively mentors numerous projects across different academic levels and some of these projects under his guidance have been submitted for several conferences. She has made significant contributions to research with publications ranging from journal articles and conferences to book chapters in their research on computer vision and image processing.
Google Scholar
:
https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=vfEIbdsAAAAJ&hl=en
Contact
sgoel@ddn.upes.ac.in
