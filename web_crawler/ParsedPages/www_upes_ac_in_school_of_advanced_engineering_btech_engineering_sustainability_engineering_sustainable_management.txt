**Program Overview**

The B.Tech. Sustainability Engineering (Sustainability Management) program offered by the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Advanced Engineering provides a comprehensive understanding of sustainable development, addressing its social, environmental, and economic aspects. Emphasizing the roles of various stakeholders, including governments, businesses, and communities, the program equips students to tackle real-world sustainability challenges.

**Key Features**

* Dual degree opportunity with collaborations with renowned international universities like University of Exeter, University of Aberdeen UK, and Indiana University Purdue University Indianapolis (IUPUI).
* Integration of principles from alternative energy, transportation, earth and environmental systems, and Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) frameworks to align with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (UN-SDGs).
* Exposure to diverse cultures and international perspectives through global academic stints.
* Emphasis on hands-on projects, industry internships, and real-world simulations to enhance practical skills.
* Alignment with the UN-SDGs, equipping graduates to contribute to global sustainability objectives.

**Career Opportunities**

Graduates of the B.Tech. Sustainability Engineering (Sustainability Management) program are equipped with the skills and knowledge to pursue diverse career paths in sustainability and environmental management, including:

* Sustainability specialists
* Sustainability managers
* Sustainability directors
* Environmental analysts
* Renewable energy specialists
* Green project coordinators

Graduates can find employment in private companies, PSUs, and NGOs, holding positions such as:

* Graduate Engineer Trainee
* Assistant Manager (Sustainability)
* Sustainability Executive
* Manager Consultant
* Auditor
* Assessor (with a focus on fire and safety)

**Placements**

The program boasts an impressive placement record with graduates securing positions in leading companies, consulting firms, and organizations that prioritize sustainable practices. This reflects the program's effectiveness in preparing professionals who can drive positive change in sustainability and environmental management.

**Eligibility Criteria**

Interested students must meet the minimum eligibility criteria for the B.Tech. Sustainability Engineering (Sustainability Management) program:

* Minimum 50% marks in Classes X and XII, with 50% in PCM (Physics/Chemistry and Mathematics) in Class XII.

**Selection Criteria**

The selection process relies on the individual's performance in University of Petroleum and Energy Studies Engineering Aptitude Test (UPESEAT) / Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) / Board Merit / Scholastic Aptitude Test (SAT) / Common UPES Entrance Test (CUET).
