Purpose Overview

Purpose Overview

UPES is a gender diverse and inclusive university running various social initiatives like merit-based scholarships for girl students under project Shakti, sports (Vijay) scholarships, freeships (Jyoti) to underprivileged students and domicile scholarships for Uttarakhand residents. UPES also runs Project Naman, an initiative to create a difference in the lives of martyrs’ families.
