How to Apply

We value the commitment of each individual and thoroughly assess and evaluate each application we receive on an equal basis.

Enrolment Process
Step 1
Choose your course
Choose your course from 7 schools at UPES
Step 2
Start your application
Fill-in your personal details in the Application form for AY 2025-26
Step 3
Pay your application fee
Pay the Application fee of Rs. 1750/-
Step 4
Submit your application
Complete your academic details and submit the form to complete the application.
NOTE:
Please be careful while typing Email ID & Mobile Number as all communication will be sent on registered contact details. Changes in Email ID & Mobile Number are not allowed.
What Documents Do I Need To Submit?
Qualification Details
10th Marksheet
12th Marksheet
National Level Exam Score (If any)
Documents Required
Migration / Transfer Certificate
Student Aadhar Card
PAN Card of Student and Parents
Additional Documents in case of Uttarakhand Domicile
Domicile Certificate / Sthai Niwas Praman Patra of Applicant
Family Income Proof: Parents’ ITR of the current year (Family Income should be less than 12LPA to avail the benefit)
Student Aadhar Card
After Applying?
You’ve submitted your application and now you’re wondering about, what happens next
Find out more about how we’ll be in touch and the different responses you might get.
Post Completion
UPES will be sending communication regarding further admission process based on selection criteria (refer to UPES website).
SMS will be sent on the Registered Number for Entrance Exam / Personal Interviews wherein candidates are required to register on a preferred date and time slot.
Post appearing in Entrance Exam or Personal Interview notification will be sent from UPES on registered contact details, candidates can also check results on UPES website.
After Selection
A provisional offer letter will be issued and candidates will be required to pay the acceptance fee in order to reserve/block their seat before the Offer Expiry date.
