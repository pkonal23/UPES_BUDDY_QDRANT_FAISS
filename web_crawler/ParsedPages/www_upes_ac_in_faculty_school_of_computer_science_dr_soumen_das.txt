Dr. Soumen Das

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Soumen Das is an Assistant Professor and skilled researcher specializing in Computer Vision, Image Processing, and Gesture recognition. He has a Ph.D. from the National Institute of Technology Silchar. His  work focuses on improving human computer interaction and development of  sign language recognition system for deaf community. He has published several papers in reputed conferences and journals. Dr. Das is passionate about collaborating with others and advancing computer science in these exciting areas.
Work Experience
Before joining UPES, he has worked in ITER, Siksha O Anusandhan University, Bhubuneswar Odisha
Research Interests
Computer Vision, Image processing, Gesture recognition, Sign Language recognition and Medical Image processing.
Teaching Philosophy
Prof. Soumen believes in a supportive and engaging teaching approach. He focuses on practical knowledge and critical thinking. He encourages hands-on learning, real-life examples, and active participation. He nurtures curiosity, collaboration, and effective communication to help students grasp complex concepts and develop a lasting interest in computer science.
Courses Taught
DBMS, Operating System, Web Programming Fundamentals
Contact
soumen.das@ddn.upes.ac.in
