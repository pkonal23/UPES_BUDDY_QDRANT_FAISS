Dr. Abhishek Bharti

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Dr Abhishek Bharti is a dedicated academician with a strong background in statistics and demography. He earned his Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) from Banaras Hindu University in 2021, where he also completed his Master of Science (M.Sc.) in 2013.
Dr Bharti is committed to academic excellence and research. His research interests span Applied Statistics, Mathematical Demography, Stochastic Processes, and Public Health.
As an accomplished researcher, he has published several impactful papers in esteemed journals. Notable among them are papers on conception rate estimation, parity progression ratio trends, spatial statistics analysis on infant mortality, and determinants of maternity care utilization.
Dr Abhishek Bharti is an esteemed member of various professional associations, including the International Indian Statistical Association (IISA), Indian Society for Medical Statistics (ISMS), and Indian Association for the Study of Population (IASP).
With a relentless passion for advancing statistical knowledge and its applications in public health and demography, Dr. Bharti continues to make significant strides in academia, contributing to the growth and development of the field.
Work Experience
Since July 2022, Dr Bharti is working as an Assistant Professor at the School of Business, UPES, Dehradun. In his current role as Assistant Professor at UPES, School of Business, Dehradun, Dr. Bharti is actively engaged in teaching and research activities.
Research Interests
Dr. Bharti's interests lie at the intersection of applied statistics, mathematical demography, stochastic processes, and public health. These areas provide a rich foundation for investigating and understanding various phenomena related to populations, health, and statistical analysis.
Teaching Philosophy
Dr. Bharti's teaching philosophy revolves around cultivating a solid foundation in statistical theory and methods while emphasizing their practical application in the specific subject area. He believes in creating an interactive and engaging learning environment where students actively participate in data analysis, research projects, and problem-solving activities. By emphasizing critical thinking, data interpretation, and effective communication of statistical results, He aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to analyse complex data sets, make informed decisions, and contribute meaningfully to their field of study through the lens of statistics.
Courses Taught
Dr. Abhishek Bharti has good teaching experience and has successfully delivered courses in Business Mathematics and Business Statistics for BBA students. His expertise also extends to teaching Business Research Methods for MBA students. With a comprehensive understanding of the subject matter, Dr. Bharti employs effective teaching methodologies, fosters student engagement, and ensures a practical application of concepts in the business context. His courses provide students with a strong foundation in quantitative analysis, statistical techniques, and research methodologies, equipping them with the necessary skills to make data-driven decisions in their business careers.
Awards and Grants
In June 2013, Dr. Bharti have been awarded the Junior Research Fellowship (UGC NET-JRF), a prestigious fellowship of the University Grants Commission. This fellowship provided financial support for his Ph.D. research work.
Scholarly Activities
Dr. Bharti is a professional actively involved in scholarly activities, with a focus on research cooperation in the fields of statistics and public health. His extensive contributions and memberships in prestigious organizations underline his dedication to advancing knowledge and fostering collaboration within these domains. Dr. Bharti holds a Life Membership with the International Indian Statistical Association (IISA), the Indian Society for Medical Statistics (ISMS), and the Indian Association for the Study of Population (IASP).
These renowned organizations bring together statisticians and data scientists from around the globe. These memberships provide him with opportunities to engage in international research cooperation, participate in conferences, and collaborate with experts in the field.
These life memberships and Dr. Bharti's active involvement in research cooperation exemplify his dedication to advancing statistical knowledge and public health research. His scholarly activities within these organizations contribute to the growth of statistical methodologies, facilitate interdisciplinary collaboration, and drive advancements in the field of public health.
