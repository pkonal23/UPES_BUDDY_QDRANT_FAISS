**Dr. Uday Bhaskar Sharma** is an Assistant Professor at the **University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Liberal Studies**. Uday Bhaskar Sharma's area of expertise is Mathematics, particularly in combinatorics and group theory. Dr. Sharma holds the position of Programme Lead for the B. Sc. in Applied Mathematics and Statistics offered by the School of Liberal Studies. Uday Bhaskar Sharma's research findings have been published in both national and international journals of high repute.

Prior to joining UPES, Uday Bhaskar Sharma engaged in postdoctoral research at two esteemed institutions: the **Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Pune** and the **Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) Mumbai**. Dr. Sharma obtained his Ph.D. from the **Institute of Mathematical Sciences, Chennai**.

Uday Bhaskar Sharma's research interests lie within the broad field of mathematics, encompassing group theory, combinatorics, and representation theory. Dr. Sharma has published four papers to date and is actively working towards the publication of two additional papers.

Uday Bhaskar Sharma's teaching philosophy emphasizes student-centric learning approaches. Dr. Sharma employs real-world examples and leverages his extracurricular skills in origami and art to create relevant models and visuals for his students. Additionally, Uday Bhaskar Sharma firmly believes in providing hands-on learning experiences through rigorous tutorial sessions.

In the winter semester from January to April 2023, Dr. Sharma taught an introductory course in algebra titled 'Algebra-I' to B.Sc. students at the School of Liberal Studies. The course introduced the fundamental concepts of group theory. Uday Bhaskar Sharma also imparted knowledge in Discrete Mathematics, covering Logic and Graph Theory, as part of the PhD. course 'Critical Approaches to Liberal Arts'.

Dr. Sharma's academic achievements include receiving the University Gold Medal during his Master's studies at the **University of Hyderabad** in 2011.

Beyond his teaching and research responsibilities, Uday Bhaskar Sharma has co-designed the course grid for the B.Sc. Applied Mathematics and Statistics program at the School of Liberal Studies.

- "Branching rules for Unitary and Symplectic matrices," Communications in Algebra (48), No. 7, (2020) 2958–2985. DOI: 10.1080/00927872.2020.1726366
