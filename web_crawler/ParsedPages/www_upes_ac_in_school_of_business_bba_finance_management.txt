Program details
The
BBA in Finance Management
program offered by UPES School of Business is a comprehensive three-year postgraduate degree designed to equip students with a strong foundation in business and management principles, coupled with in-depth knowledge and skills in finance. The program covers a wide range of subjects, including financial accounting, corporate finance, investment analysis, and risk management. This diverse curriculum ensures that students develop a holistic understanding of finance and its applications in the business world.
One of the key highlights of the program is the emphasis on practical learning. Students have the opportunity to apply their knowledge and skills to real-world financial problems through a capstone course. This experiential learning approach enables students to bridge the gap between theory and practice, enhancing their employability and preparing them for the challenges of the finance industry.
The
BBA in Finance Management
program at UPES also focuses on the development of critical skills that are highly valued in the finance field. Students acquire analytical and quantitative abilities, financial modeling skills, problem-solving aptitude, and strategic thinking capabilities. Additionally, the program promotes the enhancement of communication, teamwork, and leadership skills through group projects, case studies, and presentations. These skills are not only crucial for success in finance roles but also transferable to various other industries, broadening the career prospects for graduates.
Program Highlights
Students undertake major projects, summer internships, and live projects with companies, providing them with hands-on experience in the finance industry.
Students are exposed to real-world case studies, allowing them to analyze and solve financial problems in a practical context.
The
BBA in Finance Management
program has industry alliances with renowned organizations such as KPMG, TATA Power, HPCL, Essar Oil, fostering networking opportunities and industry insights for students.
Students have the chance to study abroad at prestigious universities like the University of California, Berkeley, University of Sunderland, and IPN Mexico, broadening their international perspective.
The
BBA in Finance Management
program offers progression opportunities for further studies with reputable institutions such as Nottingham Trent University and ULaw Business School, enabling students to pursue higher education in finance.
UPES has been ranked 39th in India in the Management Category by NIRF 2023, and it is a member of AACSB. The program is also ranked among the top 25 Business Schools in India according to India Today Ranking Issue 2021, ensuring quality education and recognition for graduates.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope for a
BBA in Finance Management
program is highly promising. With the ever-evolving and interconnected nature of the business world, organizations will continuously seek professionals who possess a solid foundation in business administration and a comprehensive understanding of financial management principles. The convergence of finance and technology creates abundant prospects for graduates. They can pursue careers in fintech companies, utilizing their finance expertise to develop innovative financial products, enhance financial services, or delve into emerging fields such as blockchain and cryptocurrency. The demand for finance professionals with international expertise is also increasing in our globalized business landscape. Graduates can secure positions in multinational corporations, financial institutions, or international consulting firms, engaging in cross-border transactions, foreign exchange management, and formulating global financial strategies. Moreover, graduates specializing in finance can explore opportunities in investment banking. They can contribute to mergers and acquisitions, initial public offerings, and financial advisory services, assisting businesses in raising capital and making strategic financial decisions. With the continuous evolution of the financial sector and the need for financial expertise across various industries, a
BBA in Finance Management
program offers a promising pathway to a successful and fulfilling career.
Career Opportunities
BBA Finance
students have the opportunity to discover their career passions, engage with prospective employers, and potentially land desirable job positions. The field of finance offers a wide spectrum of career options across various industries, encompassing a multitude of roles and responsibilities.

Placements
The placement scenario for students in the
BBA in Finance Management
program has witnessed notable advancements in recent years. Numerous prestigious companies, including Indiabulls, Reliance Industries, HUL, HDFC Bank, Adani Wilmar, Accenture, Deloitte, Lead Squared, KPMG, Xiaomi, TATA Croma, Odex, Transystem, Amazon, and PI Industries, among others, present highly rewarding employment prospects to these students. Moreover, several mid-sized start-ups display a strong interest in recruiting postgraduates with a BBA in Finance degree.

Program details
The
BBA in Finance Management
program offered by UPES School of Business is a comprehensive three-year postgraduate degree designed to equip students with a strong foundation in business and management principles, coupled with in-depth knowledge and skills in finance. The program covers a wide range of subjects, including financial accounting, corporate finance, investment analysis, and risk management. This diverse curriculum ensures that students develop a holistic understanding of finance and its applications in the business world.
One of the key highlights of the program is the emphasis on practical learning. Students have the opportunity to apply their knowledge and skills to real-world financial problems through a capstone course. This experiential learning approach enables students to bridge the gap between theory and practice, enhancing their employability and preparing them for the challenges of the finance industry.
The
BBA in Finance Management
program at UPES also focuses on the development of critical skills that are highly valued in the finance field. Students acquire analytical and quantitative abilities, financial modeling skills, problem-solving aptitude, and strategic thinking capabilities. Additionally, the program promotes the enhancement of communication, teamwork, and leadership skills through group projects, case studies, and presentations. These skills are not only crucial for success in finance roles but also transferable to various other industries, broadening the career prospects for graduates.

Program details
The
BBA in Finance Management
program offered by UPES School of Business is a comprehensive three-year postgraduate degree designed to equip students with a strong foundation in business and management principles, coupled with in-depth knowledge and skills in finance. The program covers a wide range of subjects, including financial accounting, corporate finance, investment analysis, and risk management. This diverse curriculum ensures that students develop a holistic understanding of finance and its applications in the business world.
One of the key highlights of the program is the emphasis on practical learning. Students have the opportunity to apply their knowledge and skills to real-world financial problems through a capstone course. This experiential learning approach enables students to bridge the gap between theory and practice, enhancing their employability and preparing them for the challenges of the finance industry.
The
BBA in Finance Management
program at UPES also focuses on the development of critical skills that are highly valued in the finance field. Students acquire analytical and quantitative abilities, financial modeling skills, problem-solving aptitude, and strategic thinking capabilities. Additionally, the program promotes the enhancement of communication, teamwork, and leadership skills through group projects, case studies, and presentations. These skills are not only crucial for success in finance roles but also transferable to various other industries, broadening the career prospects for graduates.
Program Highlights
Students undertake major projects, summer internships, and live projects with companies, providing them with hands-on experience in the finance industry.
Students are exposed to real-world case studies, allowing them to analyze and solve financial problems in a practical context.
The
BBA in Finance Management
program has industry alliances with renowned organizations such as KPMG, TATA Power, HPCL, Essar Oil, fostering networking opportunities and industry insights for students.
Students have the chance to study abroad at prestigious universities like the University of California, Berkeley, University of Sunderland, and IPN Mexico, broadening their international perspective.
The
BBA in Finance Management
program offers progression opportunities for further studies with reputable institutions such as Nottingham Trent University and ULaw Business School, enabling students to pursue higher education in finance.
UPES has been ranked 39th in India in the Management Category by NIRF 2023, and it is a member of AACSB. The program is also ranked among the top 25 Business Schools in India according to India Today Ranking Issue 2021, ensuring quality education and recognition for graduates.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope for a
BBA in Finance Management
program is highly promising. With the ever-evolving and interconnected nature of the business world, organizations will continuously seek professionals who possess a solid foundation in business administration and a comprehensive understanding of financial management principles. The convergence of finance and technology creates abundant prospects for graduates. They can pursue careers in fintech companies, utilizing their finance expertise to develop innovative financial products, enhance financial services, or delve into emerging fields such as blockchain and cryptocurrency. The demand for finance professionals with international expertise is also increasing in our globalized business landscape. Graduates can secure positions in multinational corporations, financial institutions, or international consulting firms, engaging in cross-border transactions, foreign exchange management, and formulating global financial strategies. Moreover, graduates specializing in finance can explore opportunities in investment banking. They can contribute to mergers and acquisitions, initial public offerings, and financial advisory services, assisting businesses in raising capital and making strategic financial decisions. With the continuous evolution of the financial sector and the need for financial expertise across various industries, a
BBA in Finance Management
program offers a promising pathway to a successful and fulfilling career.
