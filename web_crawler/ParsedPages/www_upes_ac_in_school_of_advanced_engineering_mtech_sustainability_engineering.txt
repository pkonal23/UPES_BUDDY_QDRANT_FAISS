Program Details
The
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is the first of its kind in India, aiming to equip graduates with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for environmental conservation, social equity, and economic sustainability. The curriculum is designed to address the United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals (UN-SDGs), ensuring that outgoing graduates have promising global job prospects. The program's uniqueness lies in its customized approach, focusing on creating sustainability experts capable of integrating interdisciplinary knowledge from engineering, sustainability, environmental science, policy, design, citizen science, health, and safety engineering.
The curriculum of the course is comprehensive, covering a wide range of topics, including fire engineering, chemical engineering, process safety engineering, environmental engineering, first aid and emergency services, electrical safety, and behavioral-based safety, among others. This approach ensures that students gain a deep understanding of the science, engineering, and management aspects relevant to handling unforeseen workplace catastrophes and implementing effective HSE management systems.
One of the strengths of the program lies in its innovative and high-quality learning opportunities, making it highly sought after worldwide. Graduates from the program have gained widespread acceptance, and many of them are successfully employed in various industries across the globe. The industry's demand for competent HSE professionals is consistently high, extending beyond sectors such as construction, manufacturing, and services. This program provides an excellent foundation for individuals seeking to pursue rewarding careers in health, safety, and environment engineering and management.
Program Highlights
A holistic blend of engineering, environmental science, and social aspects, equipping students to address sustainability challenges with a well-rounded perspective.
Emphasis on practical applications through industry collaborations, research projects, and field experiences, fostering real-world problem-solving skills.
Learn from renowned professors and industry experts with a deep understanding of sustainability principles and cutting-edge technologies.
Students of the
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program gain access to advanced laboratories, simulation tools, and sustainable infrastructure, facilitating innovative research and experimentation.
Focus on aligning projects and initiatives with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), preparing graduates to make a positive impact on global sustainability challenges.
Strong industry connections, internships, and placement support to kickstart rewarding careers in sustainable engineering, consulting, renewable energy, and more.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program is highly promising, given the increasing focus on climate change and sustainability. Graduates in this field can find abundant opportunities in various industries, including renewable energy providers, sustainable product development, clean transportation (electric and hybrid vehicles), power generation, and green building construction. Moreover, there is significant potential for conducting sustainability-related research in universities, both nationally and internationally, as well as contributing to policymaking in this domain. As the concept of sustainability is still evolving, students can even explore entrepreneurship and create innovative sustainable products to combat carbon emissions and contribute positively to society.
Career Opportunities
The
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up a plethora of promising career opportunities for graduates. As industries worldwide increasingly prioritize sustainability and environmental consciousness, skilled sustainability engineers are in high demand. Graduates from this program can pursue roles in renewable energy development, sustainable infrastructure planning, waste management, environmental consulting, and sustainable manufacturing processes. They may also find rewarding positions in research and development, policy analysis, and corporate sustainability management. With UPES' emphasis on practical learning and industry exposure, students are well-prepared to contribute to the global sustainability agenda and drive positive change in various sectors while enjoying fulfilling and impactful careers.
Placements
The
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering boasts an exceptional record of placements, solidifying its reputation as a leading choice for aspiring sustainability engineers. Graduates from this program have consistently secured lucrative job offers from renowned industries and organizations worldwide. The university's strong industry connections, state-of-the-art curriculum, and emphasis on practical training equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to address real-world sustainability challenges effectively. With an impressive placement rate, UPES School of Advanced Engineering continues to empower future sustainability leaders, making a significant impact in shaping a greener and more sustainable world.

Program Details
The
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is the first of its kind in India, aiming to equip graduates with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for environmental conservation, social equity, and economic sustainability. The curriculum is designed to address the United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals (UN-SDGs), ensuring that outgoing graduates have promising global job prospects. The program's uniqueness lies in its customized approach, focusing on creating sustainability experts capable of integrating interdisciplinary knowledge from engineering, sustainability, environmental science, policy, design, citizen science, health, and safety engineering.
The curriculum of the course is comprehensive, covering a wide range of topics, including fire engineering, chemical engineering, process safety engineering, environmental engineering, first aid and emergency services, electrical safety, and behavioral-based safety, among others. This approach ensures that students gain a deep understanding of the science, engineering, and management aspects relevant to handling unforeseen workplace catastrophes and implementing effective HSE management systems.
One of the strengths of the program lies in its innovative and high-quality learning opportunities, making it highly sought after worldwide. Graduates from the program have gained widespread acceptance, and many of them are successfully employed in various industries across the globe. The industry's demand for competent HSE professionals is consistently high, extending beyond sectors such as construction, manufacturing, and services. This program provides an excellent foundation for individuals seeking to pursue rewarding careers in health, safety, and environment engineering and management.
Program Highlights
A holistic blend of engineering, environmental science, and social aspects, equipping students to address sustainability challenges with a well-rounded perspective.
Emphasis on practical applications through industry collaborations, research projects, and field experiences, fostering real-world problem-solving skills.
Learn from renowned professors and industry experts with a deep understanding of sustainability principles and cutting-edge technologies.
Students of the
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program gain access to advanced laboratories, simulation tools, and sustainable infrastructure, facilitating innovative research and experimentation.
Focus on aligning projects and initiatives with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), preparing graduates to make a positive impact on global sustainability challenges.
Strong industry connections, internships, and placement support to kickstart rewarding careers in sustainable engineering, consulting, renewable energy, and more.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program is highly promising, given the increasing focus on climate change and sustainability. Graduates in this field can find abundant opportunities in various industries, including renewable energy providers, sustainable product development, clean transportation (electric and hybrid vehicles), power generation, and green building construction. Moreover, there is significant potential for conducting sustainability-related research in universities, both nationally and internationally, as well as contributing to policymaking in this domain. As the concept of sustainability is still evolving, students can even explore entrepreneurship and create innovative sustainable products to combat carbon emissions and contribute positively to society.
Career Opportunities
The
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up a plethora of promising career opportunities for graduates. As industries worldwide increasingly prioritize sustainability and environmental consciousness, skilled sustainability engineers are in high demand. Graduates from this program can pursue roles in renewable energy development, sustainable infrastructure planning, waste management, environmental consulting, and sustainable manufacturing processes. They may also find rewarding positions in research and development, policy analysis, and corporate sustainability management. With UPES' emphasis on practical learning and industry exposure, students are well-prepared to contribute to the global sustainability agenda and drive positive change in various sectors while enjoying fulfilling and impactful careers.
Placements
The
M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering boasts an exceptional record of placements, solidifying its reputation as a leading choice for aspiring sustainability engineers. Graduates from this program have consistently secured lucrative job offers from renowned industries and organizations worldwide. The university's strong industry connections, state-of-the-art curriculum, and emphasis on practical training equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to address real-world sustainability challenges effectively. With an impressive placement rate, UPES School of Advanced Engineering continues to empower future sustainability leaders, making a significant impact in shaping a greener and more sustainable world.

The program's uniqueness lies in its customized approach, focusing on creating sustainability experts capable of integrating interdisciplinary knowledge from engineering, sustainability, environmental science, policy, design, citizen science, health, and safety engineering.
The curriculum of the course is comprehensive, covering a wide range of topics, including fire engineering, chemical engineering, process safety engineering, environmental engineering, first aid and emergency services, electrical safety, and behavioral-based safety, among others. This approach ensures that students gain a deep understanding of the science, engineering, and management aspects relevant to handling unforeseen workplace catastrophes and implementing effective HSE management systems.
One of the strengths of the program lies in its innovative and high-quality learning opportunities, making it highly sought after worldwide. Graduates from the program have gained widespread acceptance, and many of them are successfully employed in various industries across the globe. The industry's demand for competent HSE professionals is consistently high, extending beyond sectors such as construction, manufacturing, and services. This program provides an excellent foundation for individuals seeking to pursue rewarding careers in health, safety, and environment engineering and management.

Future Scope / Industry Trends

program is highly promising, given the increasing focus on climate change and sustainability. Eligibility Criteria
Interested students must fulfil the minimum eligibility criteria for M. Tech. Sustainability Engineering as outlined below:
Minimum 60% marks at Higher and Senior Secondary level (10th and 12th).
B. Tech/B.E. in Electrical/ Mechanical/ Chemical/ EEE/ Electronics/ Instrumentation and Control Engineering or equivalent with minimum 60% marks. GATE/NET Qualified will be preferred.
