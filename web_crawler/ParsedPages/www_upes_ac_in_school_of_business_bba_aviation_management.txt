Program Details
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program offered by UPES School of Business is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the aviation industry. Over the course of three years, students will develop a strong foundation in business management principles, including finance, marketing, operations, and human resources. They will also gain specialized knowledge of the aviation industry, including strategic planning, decision-making, and organizational management within the aviation context.
One of the key focuses of the program is to equip students with the skills needed to analyze data, conduct research, and solve complex problems within the aviation management field. Through critical thinking and research methodologies, students will learn to address challenges specific to the aviation industry. The program also emphasizes the development of leadership qualities and teamwork skills, preparing students to work effectively in diverse teams and assume managerial roles within aviation organizations.
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program at UPES offers two specializations: Aviation Service Operations and Travel & Ground handling operations. In the Aviation Service Operations specialization, students will learn to manage and optimize service operations in the aviation industry. This includes understanding customer expectations, service design, quality management, and the role of technology in enhancing service operations. The Travel & Ground handling operations specialization focuses on managing travel agency operations and coordinating ground handling services to ensure a seamless and satisfying experience for passengers. Students will learn about customer service, effective communication, and problem-solving techniques specific to the travel and ground handling context.
Program Highlights
Students engage in major projects and summer internships, gaining hands-on experience in airport operations, cargo handling, airline services, ground handling, or consulting.
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program emphasizes real-world exposure through case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts.
Students gain proficiency in advanced software and technology, including tools like advanced Excel, R-studio, and analytics tools.
The UPES School of Business offers modern smart classrooms, a well-equipped Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and advanced analytical tools.
Students can participate in student exchange programs, study abroad experiences, and semester exchanges with renowned international partner institutes of UPES.
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program provides additional certifications, such as the IATA foundation in Travel and Tourism with Amadeus and the IATA Certification course in DGR Cat 6 for Cargo Operations in partnership with GMR Aviation Academy.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program offers students the necessary skills and knowledge for mid-management positions in industries such as airlines, airports, air cargo, e-commerce, warehousing, research, and consulting. With a shortage of competent professionals in the aviation sector, our program focuses on practical learning through internships and on-the-job training, giving students a competitive advantage. The curriculum covers essential topics including aviation industry fundamentals, airport and airline operations, cargo handling, ground handling operations, and aviation safety and security management. Students also benefit from real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by experts. Exposure to industry software and technologies further enhances employability and job prospects. Overall, the
BBA in Aviation Management
program equips students with comprehensive knowledge of the aviation industry and prepares them for rewarding careers in various sectors within the field.
Career Opportunities
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program offered by UPES School of Business presents diverse career prospects for students, providing them with opportunities to work alongside prestigious organizations and gain invaluable experience in the corporate realm. Through summer internships, live projects, and placements, students can explore their career interests, establish connections with potential employers, and potentially secure job offers. They can specialize in three key areas: airport operations, airline operations, and consulting. Career profiles in this field include roles such as Airline Operations Manager, Airline Sales and Marketing Executive, Airline Customer Service Manager, Revenue Management Analyst, Route Planner, Airline Ground Operations Supervisor, Airline Training Coordinator, Airline Operations Analyst, Airport Facilities Manager, Airport Business Development Executive, and Airport Airline Relationship Executive.
Placements
In recent years, the placement scenario for students pursuing a
BBA in Aviation Management
has greatly improved. Renowned companies such as GMR Group, Adani Group, Vistara Airlines, Spice Express, Accenture, Kuehne Nagel, TATA Airlines, and Indigo Airlines, among others, provide attractive employment opportunities for these students. Moreover, numerous mid-sized start-ups are eager to recruit
BBA in Aviation Management
graduates. The job prospects encompass various roles, including airline operations manager, airport operations supervisor, aviation sales executive, airport customer service manager, aviation consultant, air cargo operations coordinator, aviation safety officer, airport business development executive, aviation training coordinator, and airline marketing specialist. These positions offer opportunities to work in diverse areas such as airline management, airport operations, sales and marketing, cargo handling, safety and security, business development, and training. Freshers in
BBA in Aviation Management
can expect a starting salary typically ranging from INR 4-10 lakh per annum, which is influenced by their skills, knowledge, and exposure.

Program Details
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program offered by UPES School of Business is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the aviation industry. Over the course of three years, students will develop a strong foundation in business management principles, including finance, marketing, operations, and human resources. They will also gain specialized knowledge of the aviation industry, including strategic planning, decision-making, and organizational management within the aviation context.
One of the key focuses of the program is to equip students with the skills needed to analyze data, conduct research, and solve complex problems within the aviation management field. Through critical thinking and research methodologies, students will learn to address challenges specific to the aviation industry. The program also emphasizes the development of leadership qualities and teamwork skills, preparing students to work effectively in diverse teams and assume managerial roles within aviation organizations.
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program at UPES offers two specializations: Aviation Service Operations and Travel & Ground handling operations. In the Aviation Service Operations specialization, students will learn to manage and optimize service operations in the aviation industry. This includes understanding customer expectations, service design, quality management, and the role of technology in enhancing service operations. The Travel & Ground handling operations specialization focuses on managing travel agency operations and coordinating ground handling services to ensure a seamless and satisfying experience for passengers. Students will learn about customer service, effective communication, and problem-solving techniques specific to the travel and ground handling context.
Program Highlights
Students engage in major projects and summer internships, gaining hands-on experience in airport operations, cargo handling, airline services, ground handling, or consulting.
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program emphasizes real-world exposure through case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts.
Students gain proficiency in advanced software and technology, including tools like advanced Excel, R-studio, and analytics tools.
The UPES School of Business offers modern smart classrooms, a well-equipped Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and advanced analytical tools.
Students can participate in student exchange programs, study abroad experiences, and semester exchanges with renowned international partner institutes of UPES.
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program provides additional certifications, such as the IATA foundation in Travel and Tourism with Amadeus and the IATA Certification course in DGR Cat 6 for Cargo Operations in partnership with GMR Aviation Academy.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program offers students the necessary skills and knowledge for mid-management positions in industries such as airlines, airports, air cargo, e-commerce, warehousing, research, and consulting. With a shortage of competent professionals in the aviation sector, our program focuses on practical learning through internships and on-the-job training, giving students a competitive advantage. The curriculum covers essential topics including aviation industry fundamentals, airport and airline operations, cargo handling, ground handling operations, and aviation safety and security management. Students also benefit from real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by experts. Exposure to industry software and technologies further enhances employability and job prospects. Overall, the
BBA in Aviation Management
program equips students with comprehensive knowledge of the aviation industry and prepares them for rewarding careers in various sectors within the field.
Career Opportunities
The
BBA in Aviation Management
program offered by UPES School of Business presents diverse career prospects for students, providing them with opportunities to work alongside prestigious organizations and gain invaluable experience in the corporate realm. Through summer internships, live projects, and placements, students can explore their career interests, establish connections with potential employers, and potentially secure job offers. They can specialize in three key areas: airport operations, airline operations, and consulting. Career profiles in this field include roles such as Airline Operations Manager, Airline Sales and Marketing Executive, Airline Customer Service Manager, Revenue Management Analyst, Route Planner, Airline Ground Operations Supervisor, Airline Training Coordinator, Airline Operations Analyst, Airport Facilities Manager, Airport Business Development Executive, and Airport Airline Relationship Executive.
Placements
In recent years, the placement scenario for students pursuing a
BBA in Aviation Management
has greatly improved. Renowned companies such as GMR Group, Adani Group, Vistara Airlines, Spice Express, Accenture, Kuehne Nagel, TATA Airlines, and Indigo Airlines, among others, provide attractive employment opportunities for these students. Moreover, numerous mid-sized start-ups are eager to recruit
BBA in Aviation Management
graduates. The job prospects encompass various roles, including airline operations manager, airport operations supervisor, aviation sales executive, airport customer service manager, aviation consultant, air cargo operations coordinator, aviation safety officer, airport business development executive, aviation training coordinator, and airline marketing specialist. These positions offer opportunities to work in diverse areas such as airline management, airport operations, sales and marketing, cargo handling, safety and security, business development, and training. Freshers in
BBA in Aviation Management
can expect a starting salary typically ranging from INR 4-10 lakh per annum, which is influenced by their skills, knowledge, and exposure.

The Travel & Ground handling operations specialization focuses on managing travel agency operations and coordinating ground handling services to ensure a seamless and satisfying experience for passengers. Students will learn about customer service, effective communication, and problem-solving techniques specific to the travel and ground handling context.
