**Program Overview:**

The B.Tech. Automotive Engineering (E-Mobility) program by University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Advanced Engineering is a specialized program focusing on the growing demand for sustainable transportation solutions and the transition to electric vehicles. The curriculum offers comprehensive knowledge and skills related to electric mobility technologies and systems. Students delve into various aspects of electric vehicle technology, including energy storage, power electronics, and charging infrastructure. The students also gain a deep understanding of renewable energy integration and its role in the context of electric mobility. Through theoretical and practical learning, the students learn to design and implement electric drivetrains, battery management systems, and charging systems, which are crucial components of electric vehicles.

**Curriculum Highlights:**

* Covers essential topics such as electric vehicle architecture, battery technology, energy management, and sustainability in transportation.
* Provides hands-on experience through projects and internships, enhancing practical understanding and preparing the students for real-world challenges.
* Equips graduates with diverse career paths in the automotive industry, electric vehicle manufacturers, energy firms, research institutions, and government agencies.

**Career Opportunities:**

* Electric vehicle design engineer
* Powertrain engineer
* Battery systems engineer
* Charging infrastructure specialist
* Sustainable transportation consultant

**E-Mobility Trends and Industry:**

* The future scope of the program is promising, driven by the shift towards sustainable transportation and increasing adoption of electric vehicles.
* Advancements in battery technology will enhance EV performance and appeal, while the expansion of charging infrastructure and integration of renewable energy sources will further boost sustainability.
* The emergence of autonomous and connected EVs, along with electrification of public transportation and E-mobility services, will revolutionize transportation and offer eco-friendly solutions.

**Placement and Collaborations:**

* UPES has a strong placement record with leading automotive companies, electric vehicle manufacturers, and green technology firms.
* The program collaborates with industry partners for projects, workshops, internships, and placements, bridging the gap between academia and industry.

**Eligibility and Selection Criteria:**

**Eligibility:**
* Minimum 50% marks in Classes X and XII with 50% in PCM (Physics/Chemistry and Mathematics) in Class XII.

**Selection Criteria:**
* Performance in UPESEAT / JEE / Board Merit / SAT/ CUET
