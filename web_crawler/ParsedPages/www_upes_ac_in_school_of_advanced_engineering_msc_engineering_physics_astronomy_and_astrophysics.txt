Program details
The
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the science behind extra-terrestrial events and entities. The specialization allows students to delve into the fascinating realm of space exploration and study celestial bodies such as stars, planets, galaxies, and beyond.
Throughout the program, students will be introduced to various scientific observational technologies that have been developed to observe and comprehend the mysteries of far and distant worlds. They will gain expertise in using advanced telescopes, satellites, and other instruments to observe cosmic phenomena and collect valuable data for analysis. The curriculum is carefully curated to cover essential topics in theoretical and observational astrophysics, astrometry, cosmology, stellar evolution, and planetary science.
The
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering is ideal for students passionate about unraveling the secrets of the universe. With a strong emphasis on research and practical training, graduates will be equipped to pursue careers in academia, space research institutions, observatories, and other industries related to space exploration. By fostering a deep understanding of space science and cutting-edge technologies, this program prepares students to make significant contributions to the field of astronomy and astrophysics.
Program Highlights
Benefit from a highly skilled and research-oriented faculty, dedicated to delivering world-class education in astronomy and astrophysics.
Exclusive MoU with Aryabhatta Research Institute of Observational Sciences (ARIES), Nainital, enabling students to engage in experiments and research alongside ARIES Scientists.
Students can actively participate in experiments and collaborate with ARIES Scientists, gaining practical exposure and enhancing their research skills.
The
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program offers comprehensive placement support to help students embark on rewarding careers in the field of astronomy and astrophysics.
Assistance provided to students in preparing for competitive exams like NET/GATE, facilitating their pursuit of higher studies or career opportunities.
Experience an enriched learning environment that fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and innovation, nurturing future leaders in the field of astronomy and astrophysics.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program is promising, with opportunities arising from the rapidly growing space tourism industry, demanding skilled individuals proficient in astronomical techniques and data analysis. Additionally, graduates specializing in Astronomy & Astrophysics can find employment in industries involved in the production of astronomical instruments, lenses, and related accessories. This diverse field presents exciting career prospects for those seeking to explore the wonders of the universe and contribute to cutting-edge advancements in space-related technologies.
Career Opportunities
Completing the
M.Sc. in Physics
with a specialization in Astronomy & Astrophysics from UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up diverse career opportunities for graduates. With a solid foundation in the subject, they can pursue paths such as teaching faculty roles at universities or colleges, educating and inspiring the next generation of scientists. Alternatively, they could become planetarium instructors, engaging with the public and disseminating astronomical knowledge. For those inclined towards data analysis and technology, a career as a data scientist is an excellent option, utilizing their skills to study and interpret vast astronomical datasets. Additionally, they can embark on research journeys as research associates or scientists in prestigious observatories or research institutions. For individuals interested in engineering aspects, becoming astronomical instruments engineers allows them to contribute to the development and improvement of cutting-edge scientific instruments used in space exploration and observation.
Placements
The
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering has a stellar track record when it comes to placements. Graduates from this program have consistently secured promising career opportunities in various esteemed organizations and research institutions. The school's strong industry connections and comprehensive curriculum ensure that students are well-prepared with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, making them highly sought after by employers. Whether it's pursuing research in cutting-edge fields or working in industries related to space exploration and astrophysics, UPES graduates are equipped to excel and contribute significantly to the advancements in the field of Astronomy and Astrophysics.

Program details
The
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the science behind extra-terrestrial events and entities. The specialization allows students to delve into the fascinating realm of space exploration and study celestial bodies such as stars, planets, galaxies, and beyond.
Throughout the program, students will be introduced to various scientific observational technologies that have been developed to observe and comprehend the mysteries of far and distant worlds. They will gain expertise in using advanced telescopes, satellites, and other instruments to observe cosmic phenomena and collect valuable data for analysis. The curriculum is carefully curated to cover essential topics in theoretical and observational astrophysics, astrometry, cosmology, stellar evolution, and planetary science.
The
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering is ideal for students passionate about unraveling the secrets of the universe. With a strong emphasis on research and practical training, graduates will be equipped to pursue careers in academia, space research institutions, observatories, and other industries related to space exploration. By fostering a deep understanding of space science and cutting-edge technologies, this program prepares students to make significant contributions to the field of astronomy and astrophysics.
Program Highlights
Benefit from a highly skilled and research-oriented faculty, dedicated to delivering world-class education in astronomy and astrophysics.
Exclusive MoU with Aryabhatta Research Institute of Observational Sciences (ARIES), Nainital, enabling students to engage in experiments and research alongside ARIES Scientists.
Students can actively participate in experiments and collaborate with ARIES Scientists, gaining practical exposure and enhancing their research skills.
The
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program offers comprehensive placement support to help students embark on rewarding careers in the field of astronomy and astrophysics.
Assistance provided to students in preparing for competitive exams like NET/GATE, facilitating their pursuit of higher studies or career opportunities.
Experience an enriched learning environment that fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and innovation, nurturing future leaders in the field of astronomy and astrophysics.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program is promising, with opportunities arising from the rapidly growing space tourism industry, demanding skilled individuals proficient in astronomical techniques and data analysis. Additionally, graduates specializing in Astronomy & Astrophysics can find employment in industries involved in the production of astronomical instruments, lenses, and related accessories. This diverse field presents exciting career prospects for those seeking to explore the wonders of the universe and contribute to cutting-edge advancements in space-related technologies.
Career Opportunities
Completing the
M.Sc. in Physics
with a specialization in Astronomy & Astrophysics from UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up diverse career opportunities for graduates. With a solid foundation in the subject, they can pursue paths such as teaching faculty roles at universities or colleges, educating and inspiring the next generation of scientists. Alternatively, they could become planetarium instructors, engaging with the public and disseminating astronomical knowledge. For those inclined towards data analysis and technology, a career as a data scientist is an excellent option, utilizing their skills to study and interpret vast astronomical datasets. Additionally, they can embark on research journeys as research associates or scientists in prestigious observatories or research institutions. For individuals interested in engineering aspects, becoming astronomical instruments engineers allows them to contribute to the development and improvement of cutting-edge scientific instruments used in space exploration and observation.
Placements
The
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering has a stellar track record when it comes to placements. Graduates from this program have consistently secured promising career opportunities in various esteemed organizations and research institutions. The school's strong industry connections and comprehensive curriculum ensure that students are well-prepared with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, making them highly sought after by employers. Whether it's pursuing research in cutting-edge fields or working in industries related to space exploration and astrophysics, UPES graduates are equipped to excel and contribute significantly to the advancements in the field of Astronomy and Astrophysics.

Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
M.Sc. Physics (Astronomy & Astrophysics)
program is promising, with opportunities arising from the rapidly growing space tourism industry, demanding skilled individuals proficient in astronomical techniques and data analysis. Additionally, graduates specializing in Astronomy & Astrophysics can find employment in industries involved in the production of astronomical instruments, lenses, and related accessories. This diverse field presents exciting career prospects for those seeking to explore the wonders of the universe and contribute to cutting-edge advancements in space-related technologies.

Career Opportunities
Completing the
M.Sc. in Physics
with a specialization in Astronomy & Astrophysics from UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens up diverse career opportunities for graduates. With a solid foundation in the subject, they can pursue paths such as teaching faculty roles at universities or colleges, educating and inspiring the next generation of scientists. Alternatively, they could become planetarium instructors, engaging with the public and disseminating astronomical knowledge. For those inclined towards data analysis and technology, a career as a data scientist is an excellent option, utilizing their skills to study and interpret vast astronomical datasets. Additionally, they can embark on research journeys as research associates or scientists in prestigious observatories or research institutions. For individuals interested in engineering aspects, becoming astronomical instruments engineers allows them to contribute to the development and improvement of cutting-edge scientific instruments used in space exploration and observation.
