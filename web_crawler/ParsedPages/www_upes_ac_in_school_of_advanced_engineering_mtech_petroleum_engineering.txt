Program Details
The
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to address the imminent talent shortage in the oil and gas industry, known as the "Great Crew Change." As many experienced energy workers approach retirement, there is a pressing need for skilled professionals to take their place. The program aims to equip students with the necessary knowledge and expertise to handle the challenges of the industry efficiently and safely.
The curriculum of the program covers a wide range of subjects related to petroleum engineering. Students will delve into areas such as petroleum geology, drilling, formation evaluation, production, reservoir engineering, reservoir geomechanics, reservoir modeling and simulation, economics and risk management, enhanced oil recovery, HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment) issues in the oil and gas industry, and programming in petroleum engineering. This comprehensive approach ensures that graduates are well-rounded professionals capable of tackling various aspects of the petroleum industry.
Moreover, the program also emphasizes the importance of soft skills training. In today's professional world, technical knowledge alone is not sufficient; effective communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and leadership abilities are equally crucial. By providing soft skill training, UPES ensures that its students are not only technically competent but also possess the interpersonal skills necessary to thrive in a dynamic work environment.
Program Highlights
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
offers a 2-year program with 80 credits, covering core petroleum engineering subjects in the first year and industry-focused internships and dissertations in the second year.
Students gain access to in-house state-of-the-art labs equipped with industry software, providing practical hands-on experience for mastering essential tools and technologies.
The M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program includes programming training and exposure to the latest digital technologies, preparing students to excel in the evolving landscape of the petroleum industry.
UPES career services, in collaboration with faculty members, guide and groom students for placements, opening up opportunities with reputed oil and gas companies.
Students have the chance to participate in the placement process with renowned companies, with several alumni already successfully placed in reputed organizations.
The second-year internships and dissertations ensure a strong blend of academic knowledge with real-world industry experience, making graduates industry-ready and highly sought after in the job market.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program is promising and crucial for the energy industry. Hydrocarbons remain a dependable and efficient energy source, and the sector's success relies on optimizing and automating industrial processes in oil and gas exploration and production. The ongoing discovery of new reserves and cost-effective production methods ensures the industry's longevity. To stay ahead, the implementation of cutting-edge technologies through collaboration between industry and academia, especially in the realms of IoT and AI, is essential to revolutionize legacy processes. However, training the workforce in these new tools poses a challenge for the oil and gas sector. Furthermore, a focus on minimizing carbon emissions and promoting biofuels is expected in the years to come. In the post-COVID era, retaining top talent and nurturing them as energy pioneers also presents a challenge for the industry. Pursuing an M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering will equip students to contribute significantly to this dynamic and evolving field.
Career Opportunities
Upon completing the
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program from UPES School of Advanced Engineering, graduates will have access to a diverse range of career opportunities in the petroleum industry. They can pursue roles in the Exploration and Production (E&P) sector, working with renowned companies like Shell, Reliance, Adani, KS Drilling, Deep Industries Ltd., and Total Energy. Alternatively, they can explore opportunities in service companies such as Schlumberger, Weatherford, Halliburton, Baker Hughes, and Quest Global. Graduates may also choose to venture into the realm of consultancy services with esteemed firms like Market & Market, KPMG, Mckinsey & Company, Deloitte, and Ernst & Young. For those interested in data processing, opportunities exist with companies like Petrolink, Global Data, Infosys, Wipro, and Cognizant. Moreover, academic and research roles can be pursued, including faculty positions at various universities and IITs, allowing for a fulfilling and multifaceted career in the field of petroleum engineering.
Placements
Completing an
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program from UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens an array of promising opportunities for graduates in the oil and gas industry. With a strong focus on practical skills and cutting-edge knowledge, UPES equips its students to thrive in the competitive job market. The university's robust industry connections and dedicated placement cell ensure that graduates have access to top-tier companies both nationally and internationally. The alumni network further enhances career prospects, providing invaluable support and guidance. Graduates can expect lucrative roles in drilling engineering, reservoir management, and exploration, propelling them towards a successful and fulfilling career in the dynamic world of petroleum engineering.

Eligibility Criteria
Interested students must meet the minimum eligibility criteria for M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering as stated below:
Minimum 60% marks at Higher and Senior Secondary level (10th and 12th).
B.Tech/B.E. in Petroleum/ Geo-Science/ Gas Engineering/ Petrochemical Refining/ Petrochemical Engineering/ Mechanical/ Chemical or M.Sc. in Geology or equivalent with minimum 60% marks. GATE /NET Qualified will be preferred.

Program Details
The
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to address the imminent talent shortage in the oil and gas industry, known as the "Great Crew Change." As many experienced energy workers approach retirement, there is a pressing need for skilled professionals to take their place. The program aims to equip students with the necessary knowledge and expertise to handle the challenges of the industry efficiently and safely.
The curriculum of the program covers a wide range of subjects related to petroleum engineering. Students will delve into areas such as petroleum geology, drilling, formation evaluation, production, reservoir engineering, reservoir geomechanics, reservoir modeling and simulation, economics and risk management, enhanced oil recovery, HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment) issues in the oil and gas industry, and programming in petroleum engineering. This comprehensive approach ensures that graduates are well-rounded professionals capable of tackling various aspects of the petroleum industry.
Moreover, the program also emphasizes the importance of soft skills training. In today's professional world, technical knowledge alone is not sufficient; effective communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and leadership abilities are equally crucial. By providing soft skill training, UPES ensures that its students are not only technically competent but also possess the interpersonal skills necessary to thrive in a dynamic work environment.
Program Highlights
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
offers a 2-year program with 80 credits, covering core petroleum engineering subjects in the first year and industry-focused internships and dissertations in the second year.
Students gain access to in-house state-of-the-art labs equipped with industry software, providing practical hands-on experience for mastering essential tools and technologies.
The M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program includes programming training and exposure to the latest digital technologies, preparing students to excel in the evolving landscape of the petroleum industry.
UPES career services, in collaboration with faculty members, guide and groom students for placements, opening up opportunities with reputed oil and gas companies.
Students have the chance to participate in the placement process with renowned companies, with several alumni already successfully placed in reputed organizations.
The second-year internships and dissertations ensure a strong blend of academic knowledge with real-world industry experience, making graduates industry-ready and highly sought after in the job market.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program is promising and crucial for the energy industry. Hydrocarbons remain a dependable and efficient energy source, and the sector's success relies on optimizing and automating industrial processes in oil and gas exploration and production. The ongoing discovery of new reserves and cost-effective production methods ensures the industry's longevity. To stay ahead, the implementation of cutting-edge technologies through collaboration between industry and academia, especially in the realms of IoT and AI, is essential to revolutionize legacy processes. However, training the workforce in these new tools poses a challenge for the oil and gas sector. Furthermore, a focus on minimizing carbon emissions and promoting biofuels is expected in the years to come. In the post-COVID era, retaining top talent and nurturing them as energy pioneers also presents a challenge for the industry. Pursuing an M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering will equip students to contribute significantly to this dynamic and evolving field.
Career Opportunities
Upon completing the
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program from UPES School of Advanced Engineering, graduates will have access to a diverse range of career opportunities in the petroleum industry. They can pursue roles in the Exploration and Production (E&P) sector, working with renowned companies like Shell, Reliance, Adani, KS Drilling, Deep Industries Ltd., and Total Energy. Alternatively, they can explore opportunities in service companies such as Schlumberger, Weatherford, Halliburton, Baker Hughes, and Quest Global. Graduates may also choose to venture into the realm of consultancy services with esteemed firms like Market & Market, KPMG, Mckinsey & Company, Deloitte, and Ernst & Young. For those interested in data processing, opportunities exist with companies like Petrolink, Global Data, Infosys, Wipro, and Cognizant. Moreover, academic and research roles can be pursued, including faculty positions at various universities and IITs, allowing for a fulfilling and multifaceted career in the field of petroleum engineering.
Placements
Completing an
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program from UPES School of Advanced Engineering opens an array of promising opportunities for graduates in the oil and gas industry. With a strong focus on practical skills and cutting-edge knowledge, UPES equips its students to thrive in the competitive job market. The university's robust industry connections and dedicated placement cell ensure that graduates have access to top-tier companies both nationally and internationally. The alumni network further enhances career prospects, providing invaluable support and guidance. Graduates can expect lucrative roles in drilling engineering, reservoir management, and exploration, propelling them towards a successful and fulfilling career in the dynamic world of petroleum engineering.

The
M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program offered by UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to address the imminent talent shortage in the oil and gas industry, known as the "Great Crew Change." As many experienced energy workers approach retirement, there is a pressing need for skilled professionals to take their place. The program aims to equip students with the necessary knowledge and expertise to handle the challenges of the industry efficiently and safely.

The curriculum of the program covers a wide range of subjects related to petroleum engineering. Students will delve into areas such as petroleum geology, drilling, formation evaluation, production, reservoir engineering, reservoir geomechanics, reservoir modeling and simulation, economics and risk management, enhanced oil recovery, HSE (Health, Safety, and Environment) issues in the oil and gas industry, and programming in petroleum engineering. This comprehensive approach ensures that graduates are well-rounded professionals capable of tackling various aspects of the petroleum industry.

Moreover, the program also emphasizes the importance of soft skills training. In today's professional world, technical knowledge alone is not sufficient; effective communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and leadership abilities are equally crucial. By providing soft skill training, UPES ensures that its students are not only technically competent but also possess the interpersonal skills necessary to thrive in a dynamic work environment.

Career Opportunities

M. Tech. in Petroleum Engineering
program from UPES School of Advanced Engineering, graduates will have access to a diverse range of career opportunities in the petroleum industry.
