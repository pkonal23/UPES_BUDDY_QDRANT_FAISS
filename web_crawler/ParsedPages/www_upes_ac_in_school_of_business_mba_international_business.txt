Program Details
The
MBA in International Business
program offered by UPES School of Business is a comprehensive and specialized educational program designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the international business arena. By combining business management principles with a global perspective, this program enables students to navigate the complexities of conducting business across borders.
The curriculum of the
MBA in International Business
covers a wide range of subjects, including international trade and analytics, international business, international marketing, global finance, cross-cultural management, and strategic management. Through engaging in real-world case studies, simulations, and practical projects, students gain hands-on experience in tackling the challenges and opportunities of international business.
A key focus of this program is the development of essential skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, negotiation, and leadership. These skills are crucial for making informed decisions in a global context. Through interactive classroom discussions and collaborative group work, students learn to navigate cultural differences, adapt to diverse business practices, and formulate innovative strategies to gain a competitive edge in the international marketplace.
Program Highlights
Students undertake major projects and dissertations, along with summer internships and live projects with companies.
Encouragement for student and faculty exchange, research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships.
Students are exposed to real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts.
Students of
MBA in International Business
program have the chance to familiarize themselves with the operations and intricacies of export houses, crucial in international trade.
Students gain hands-on experience with software and technology like Power-BI, Gretl, advanced Excel, and analytics tools.
Practical experience through simulations and hands-on projects that address the challenges and opportunities of international business, fostering adaptability and strategy development.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The
MBA in International Business
program offers promising prospects in a globalized world. The demand for professionals skilled in international business is increasing, leading to abundant career opportunities in multinational corporations, consulting firms, and global organizations. Expertise in navigating international trade regulations, managing multicultural teams, and developing innovative strategies is essential as businesses expand into emerging markets and embrace technological advancements. Additionally, the digital transformation and focus on sustainability create avenues for international business professionals to contribute to sustainable development and global economic growth. The scope of the program goes beyond traditional boundaries and encompasses emerging trends such as e-commerce, digital marketing, supply chain management, and sustainability. Graduates of this program can expect to play a crucial role in shaping the global business landscape, making the future of
MBA in International Business
full of immense potential.
Career Opportunities
The UPES School of Business offers an MBA program in International Business that opens up numerous career avenues for its graduates. This program equips students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the dynamic field of international business. Graduates have a wealth of career opportunities across diverse industries, spanning manufacturing, service, and consulting sectors. They can pursue rewarding placements in these three distinct business verticals, showcasing their expertise in various functions. The
MBA in International Business
program at UPES School of Business paves the way for a successful and fulfilling career in the global business arena.
Placements
The
MBA in International Business
program offered by UPES School of Business provides an exceptional platform for students to excel in the global business landscape. With a strong focus on imparting comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in international trade, cross-cultural management, and global marketing, the program equips students with a competitive edge in the international business domain. Through industry-oriented curriculum, guest lectures by seasoned professionals, and immersive learning experiences, UPES School of Business ensures that graduates are well-prepared to take up challenging roles in multinational corporations, international consulting firms, global trade organizations, and entrepreneurial ventures across the world. The program's strong industry connections and dedicated placement support further enhance the career prospects of
MBA in International Business
graduates, positioning them for success in the ever-expanding global marketplace.

Eligibility
Interested students must meet the following minimum eligibility criteria for the
MBA in International Business
program: Minimum 50% marks in Class X, XII and Graduation. Graduation From a recognized University in any stream.
Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for students who wish to pursue the
MBA in International Business
program offered by UPES rely on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams/ CUET followed by Personal Interview.
Non-Examination Pathways:
Students having a minimum eligible MAT /CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will only be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Valid 50 percentile and above score of CAT 2024/ XAT 2025 A score of minimum 400 or above for GMAT, a score of 120 and above in NMAT 2024 / 25 scores will also be accepted.

Program Details
The
MBA in International Business
program offered by UPES School of Business is a comprehensive and specialized educational program designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the international business arena. By combining business management principles with a global perspective, this program enables students to navigate the complexities of conducting business across borders.
The curriculum of the
MBA in International Business
covers a wide range of subjects, including international trade and analytics, international business, international marketing, global finance, cross-cultural management, and strategic management. Through engaging in real-world case studies, simulations, and practical projects, students gain hands-on experience in tackling the challenges and opportunities of international business.
A key focus of this program is the development of essential skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, negotiation, and leadership. These skills are crucial for making informed decisions in a global context. Through interactive classroom discussions and collaborative group work, students learn to navigate cultural differences, adapt to diverse business practices, and formulate innovative strategies to gain a competitive edge in the international marketplace.
Program Highlights
Students undertake major projects and dissertations, along with summer internships and live projects with companies.
Encouragement for student and faculty exchange, research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships.
Students are exposed to real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures from industry experts.
Students of
MBA in International Business
program have the chance to familiarize themselves with the operations and intricacies of export houses, crucial in international trade.
Students gain hands-on experience with software and technology like Power-BI, Gretl, advanced Excel, and analytics tools.
Practical experience through simulations and hands-on projects that address the challenges and opportunities of international business, fostering adaptability and strategy development.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The
MBA in International Business
program offers promising prospects in a globalized world. The demand for professionals skilled in international business is increasing, leading to abundant career opportunities in multinational corporations, consulting firms, and global organizations. Expertise in navigating international trade regulations, managing multicultural teams, and developing innovative strategies is essential as businesses expand into emerging markets and embrace technological advancements. Additionally, the digital transformation and focus on sustainability create avenues for international business professionals to contribute to sustainable development and global economic growth. The scope of the program goes beyond traditional boundaries and encompasses emerging trends such as e-commerce, digital marketing, supply chain management, and sustainability. Graduates of this program can expect to play a crucial role in shaping the global business landscape, making the future of
MBA in International Business
full of immense potential.
Career Opportunities
The UPES School of Business offers an MBA program in International Business that opens up numerous career avenues for its graduates. This program equips students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the dynamic field of international business. Graduates have a wealth of career opportunities across diverse industries, spanning manufacturing, service, and consulting sectors. They can pursue rewarding placements in these three distinct business verticals, showcasing their expertise in various functions. The
MBA in International Business
program at UPES School of Business paves the way for a successful and fulfilling career in the global business arena.
Placements
The
MBA in International Business
program offered by UPES School of Business provides an exceptional platform for students to excel in the global business landscape. With a strong focus on imparting comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in international trade, cross-cultural management, and global marketing, the program equips students with a competitive edge in the international business domain. Through industry-oriented curriculum, guest lectures by seasoned professionals, and immersive learning experiences, UPES School of Business ensures that graduates are well-prepared to take up challenging roles in multinational corporations, international consulting firms, global trade organizations, and entrepreneurial ventures across the world. The program's strong industry connections and dedicated placement support further enhance the career prospects of
MBA in International Business
graduates, positioning them for success in the ever-expanding global marketplace.

Program Details
The
MBA in International Business
program offered by UPES School of Business is a comprehensive and specialized educational program designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in the international business arena. By combining business management principles with a global perspective, this program enables students to navigate the complexities of conducting business across borders.
The curriculum of the
MBA in International Business
covers a wide range of subjects, including international trade and analytics, international business, international marketing, global finance, cross-cultural management, and strategic management. Through engaging in real-world case studies, simulations, and practical projects, students gain hands-on experience in tackling the challenges and opportunities of international business.
A key focus of this program is the development of essential skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, negotiation, and leadership. These skills are crucial for making informed decisions in a global context. Through interactive classroom discussions and collaborative group work, students learn to navigate cultural differences, adapt to diverse business practices, and formulate innovative strategies to gain a competitive edge in the international marketplace.
