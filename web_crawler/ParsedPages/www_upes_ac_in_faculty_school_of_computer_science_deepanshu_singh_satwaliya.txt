Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Mr. D.S.Satwaliya is a distinguished academician who has completed B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from F.G.I.E.T, Raebareli(Govt. College) under A.K.T.U. Then, he has completed M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from NIT Hamirpur. He has qualified the GATE Examination and possess over 3 Years of teaching experience. He is widely known for her self-motivated and dedicated approach towards academic excellence.
Work Experience
Mr. D.S.Satwaliya has been associated with UPES since March 2024. Before joining UPES, Mr. D.S.Satwaliya was an Assistant Professor at the Jain(Deemed-to-be University), Bangalore and acted as a high-end faculty member in one of the most opulent courses at the Jain(Deemed-to-be University).
Research Interests
Artificial Intelligence I Machine Learning
Teaching Philosophy
Central to Mr. D.S.Satwaliya's teaching philosophy is the belief that every student is unique, possessing their own set of talents, experiences, and learning styles. He views education as a lifelong journey and strives to be a lifelong learner himself.  Through dedication, passion, and a genuine love for teaching, he aspires to make a positive impact on the lives of his students and inspire them to become lifelong learners and responsible citizens of the world.
Courses Taught
Mr. D.S.Satwaliya teaches Computer Organization and Architecture, Digital Logic Design, Operating System, Data Structure and Algorithm, C, Python, Data Warehousing and Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Computer Networking.
Awards and Grants
He qualified Graduate Aptitude Test (GATE) conducted in 2017, 2018, 2020 and 2024.
Scholarly Activities
He has published/presented the Research Papers, the Patents and the Book on the National/International Platforms.
Contact
deepanshus.satwaliya@ddn.upes.ac.in
