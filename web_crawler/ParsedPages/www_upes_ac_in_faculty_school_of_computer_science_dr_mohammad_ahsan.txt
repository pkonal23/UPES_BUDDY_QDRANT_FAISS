Dr. Mohammad Ahsan

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Dr. Mohammad Ahsan completed his Ph.D. in 2021 from National Institute of Technology Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh, India. His research interests include Social Media Mining, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, Information Diffusion Analysis at online social networks, and Healthcare.
Work Experience
Currently working as Assistant Professor in Informatics Cluster, School of Computer Science, UPES. Prior to joining UPES, worked as faculty in the Computer Science and Engineering Department of National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh and National Institute of Technology Andhra Pradesh.
Research Interests
Machine Learning | Deep Learning | Natural Language Processing | Social Media Mining I Healthcare.
Teaching Philosophy
Teaching philosophy of Dr. Mohammad Ahsan revolves around student-centered learning, practical application, conceptual understanding, and ethical & social responsibility. By instilling a sense of ethical and social responsibility, he aims to develop students who are not only competent professionals but also responsible global citizens.
Courses Taught
Dr. Mohammad Ahsan handled these courses:
Data Science
Data structure
Design and Analysis of Algorithms,
Data Mining
Natural Language Processing
Advanced Computer Architecture
Computer Programming
Computer Networks
Distributed Computing
Scholarly Activities
Dr. Mohammad Ahsan supervise students in the field of social media mining, machine learning, natural language processing, automous vehicle, and healthcare.
He has published/presented many research papers in Internationational Conferences and International Journals.
Dr. Mohammad Ahsan qualified GATE in 2012, 2013, and 2021.
He also qualified UGC-NET in the year 2015 and 2017.
Contact
md.ahsan@ddn.upes.ac.in
