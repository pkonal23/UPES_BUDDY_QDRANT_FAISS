Program Details
The
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program at UPES is designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of chemical processes and equipment, making them versatile professionals in the industry. The curriculum focuses on fundamental courses such as Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics, Mass Transfer, Fluid Mechanics, Reaction Engineering, and Heat Transfer, supported by robust laboratory experiences to enhance conceptual understanding. Advanced courses in areas like Advanced Transport Phenomena, Carbon Capture Sequestration and Utilization, Advanced Separation Process, and Circular Economy in Chemical Industries expose students to cutting-edge technologies and help them develop solutions for emerging environmental concerns.
A distinctive feature of the program is its three specialisations: Energy Systems and Storage, Refining and Petrochemicals, and Digitalization of Process Technology. The Energy Systems and Storage specialisation addresses the global demand for clean energy professionals, aligning with UN Sustainable Development Goals. Refining and Petrochemicals focus on refining technology and petrochemical processing, providing students with skills essential for the booming petrochemical business in India and worldwide. The Digitalization of Process Technology specialisation prepares graduates for the evolving industry landscape by integrating data analytics, IoT applications, and automation skills, ensuring they are well-equipped for the technological advancements of the future.
To foster an interdisciplinary approach, the program includes capstone projects and programming courses that enable students to apply their knowledge to real-world problems. The department's faculty, with their research expertise, opens avenues for fully funded postgraduate and Ph.D. programs at top global universities. Graduates of the program are sought after by major industry players such as Exxon Mobil, Shell, Haliburton, Cairn Energy, Schlumberger, and Reliance Industries Limited. Overall, the
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program at UPES stands out for its blend of theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and specialized focus areas, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for the diverse challenges and opportunities in the field.
Program Highlights
UPES is the sole university in India integrating AI-ML and digitalization into Chemical Engineering education, providing students with a unique skill set aligned with the latest industry trends.
Boasting a 100% placement rate, graduates secure lucrative opportunities, with the highest package reaching 20 lakhs, ensuring a strong return on investment for students.
The
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program molds professionals for pivotal roles in automated chemical, petrochemical, and pharmaceutical industries, emphasizing process design, technology development, and the creation of digital twins for chemical processes and energy systems.
UPES has forged an international academic partnership with the University of Queensland, Australia, enriching the program with global perspectives and enhancing students' exposure to diverse academic environments.
The
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program is enriched by faculty members from renowned institutions globally, including the University of Missouri, Salford University, IITs, IISCs, NTU Singapore, and Stanford, with 2% of faculty listed in the prestigious Stanford researcher list.
Housed in the Centre of Excellence for Energy Systems, the program benefits from state-of-the-art interdisciplinary infrastructure, providing students with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies and research in the field of Chemical Engineering.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of a
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program is promising and diverse, aligning with global trends in sustainability, energy innovation, digitization, and gas engineering. Chemical engineers are pivotal in shaping industries and driving advancements, particularly in the energy sector, where they lead the development of sustainable solutions and efficient energy storage systems. The program's integration of digitization empowers chemical engineers to utilize cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning, enabling them to optimize processes, enhance productivity, and innovate on unprecedented scales. Furthermore, the field of gas engineering within the program provides opportunities to work with natural gas, petroleum, and other gaseous substances, where chemical engineers play a crucial role in extracting, refining, and responsibly utilizing these resources. Overall, a
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program opens doors to an exciting career in a dynamic and evolving landscape, positioning graduates at the forefront of global initiatives for a greener and technologically advanced future.
Career Opportunities
The
B. Tech. Chemical Engineering
program, with its specialised tracks, equips students for diverse roles across various industries, including chemicals, refining, petrochemicals, gas, fertilizers, energy solutions, pharmaceuticals, specialty chemical, and engineering sectors. Graduates can explore roles such as Production Engineer, Design Engineer, Petroleum Engineer, Refining Engineer, Process Engineer, Research Engineer, Process Automation Engineering, Smart Manufacturer, and Data Analysts. The curriculum, developed in collaboration with industry, academia, alumni, and parents, ensures that students are well-prepared for industry demands upon graduation.
Placements
The
B. Tech. Chemical Engineering
program at UPES boasts an impressive track record of placements with leading industry giants. Graduates have secured coveted positions at renowned organizations such as ExxonMobil, Cairn India, and Reliance Industries Limited, where they have made substantial contributions to the field. The program's success extends beyond traditional chemical engineering roles, as evidenced by placements at prestigious consulting firms like McKinsey & Company and Bain & Company. The global impact of the program is further exemplified by alumni placements at international companies like Samsung Heavy Industries. Additionally, graduates have excelled in diverse sectors, from technology consulting with ZS Associates to environmental engineering with SUEZ India. The program's versatility is evident in placements at Epsilon Carbon and Jubilant Ingrevia, showcasing its ability to prepare students for a wide array of roles in the chemical engineering domain.

Program Details
The
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program at UPES is designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of chemical processes and equipment, making them versatile professionals in the industry. The curriculum focuses on fundamental courses such as Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics, Mass Transfer, Fluid Mechanics, Reaction Engineering, and Heat Transfer, supported by robust laboratory experiences to enhance conceptual understanding. Advanced courses in areas like Advanced Transport Phenomena, Carbon Capture Sequestration and Utilization, Advanced Separation Process, and Circular Economy in Chemical Industries expose students to cutting-edge technologies and help them develop solutions for emerging environmental concerns.
A distinctive feature of the program is its three specialisations: Energy Systems and Storage, Refining and Petrochemicals, and Digitalization of Process Technology. The Energy Systems and Storage specialisation addresses the global demand for clean energy professionals, aligning with UN Sustainable Development Goals. Refining and Petrochemicals focus on refining technology and petrochemical processing, providing students with skills essential for the booming petrochemical business in India and worldwide. The Digitalization of Process Technology specialisation prepares graduates for the evolving industry landscape by integrating data analytics, IoT applications, and automation skills, ensuring they are well-equipped for the technological advancements of the future.
To foster an interdisciplinary approach, the program includes capstone projects and programming courses that enable students to apply their knowledge to real-world problems. The department's faculty, with their research expertise, opens avenues for fully funded postgraduate and Ph.D. programs at top global universities. Graduates of the program are sought after by major industry players such as Exxon Mobil, Shell, Haliburton, Cairn Energy, Schlumberger, and Reliance Industries Limited. Overall, the
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program at UPES stands out for its blend of theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and specialized focus areas, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for the diverse challenges and opportunities in the field.
Program Highlights
UPES is the sole university in India integrating AI-ML and digitalization into Chemical Engineering education, providing students with a unique skill set aligned with the latest industry trends.
Boasting a 100% placement rate, graduates secure lucrative opportunities, with the highest package reaching 20 lakhs, ensuring a strong return on investment for students.
The
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program molds professionals for pivotal roles in automated chemical, petrochemical, and pharmaceutical industries, emphasizing process design, technology development, and the creation of digital twins for chemical processes and energy systems.
UPES has forged an international academic partnership with the University of Queensland, Australia, enriching the program with global perspectives and enhancing students' exposure to diverse academic environments.
The
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program is enriched by faculty members from renowned institutions globally, including the University of Missouri, Salford University, IITs, IISCs, NTU Singapore, and Stanford, with 2% of faculty listed in the prestigious Stanford researcher list.
Housed in the Centre of Excellence for Energy Systems, the program benefits from state-of-the-art interdisciplinary infrastructure, providing students with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies and research in the field of Chemical Engineering.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of a
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program is promising and diverse, aligning with global trends in sustainability, energy innovation, digitization, and gas engineering. Chemical engineers are pivotal in shaping industries and driving advancements, particularly in the energy sector, where they lead the development of sustainable solutions and efficient energy storage systems. The program's integration of digitization empowers chemical engineers to utilize cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning, enabling them to optimize processes, enhance productivity, and innovate on unprecedented scales. Furthermore, the field of gas engineering within the program provides opportunities to work with natural gas, petroleum, and other gaseous substances, where chemical engineers play a crucial role in extracting, refining, and responsibly utilizing these resources. Overall, a
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program opens doors to an exciting career in a dynamic and evolving landscape, positioning graduates at the forefront of global initiatives for a greener and technologically advanced future.
Career Opportunities
The
B. Tech. Chemical Engineering
program, with its specialised tracks, equips students for diverse roles across various industries, including chemicals, refining, petrochemicals, gas, fertilizers, energy solutions, pharmaceuticals, specialty chemical, and engineering sectors. Graduates can explore roles such as Production Engineer, Design Engineer, Petroleum Engineer, Refining Engineer, Process Engineer, Research Engineer, Process Automation Engineering, Smart Manufacturer, and Data Analysts. The curriculum, developed in collaboration with industry, academia, alumni, and parents, ensures that students are well-prepared for industry demands upon graduation.
Placements
The
B. Tech. Chemical Engineering
program at UPES boasts an impressive track record of placements with leading industry giants. Graduates have secured coveted positions at renowned organizations such as ExxonMobil, Cairn India, and Reliance Industries Limited, where they have made substantial contributions to the field. The program's success extends beyond traditional chemical engineering roles, as evidenced by placements at prestigious consulting firms like McKinsey & Company and Bain & Company. The global impact of the program is further exemplified by alumni placements at international companies like Samsung Heavy Industries. Additionally, graduates have excelled in diverse sectors, from technology consulting with ZS Associates to environmental engineering with SUEZ India. The program's versatility is evident in placements at Epsilon Carbon and Jubilant Ingrevia, showcasing its ability to prepare students for a wide array of roles in the chemical engineering domain.

Program Details
The
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program at UPES is designed to equip students with a comprehensive understanding of chemical processes and equipment, making them versatile professionals in the industry. The curriculum focuses on fundamental courses such as Chemical Engineering Thermodynamics, Mass Transfer, Fluid Mechanics, Reaction Engineering, and Heat Transfer, supported by robust laboratory experiences to enhance conceptual understanding. Advanced courses in areas like Advanced Transport Phenomena, Carbon Capture Sequestration and Utilization, Advanced Separation Process, and Circular Economy in Chemical Industries expose students to cutting-edge technologies and help them develop solutions for emerging environmental concerns.
A distinctive feature of the program is its three specialisations: Energy Systems and Storage, Refining and Petrochemicals, and Digitalization of Process Technology. The Energy Systems and Storage specialisation addresses the global demand for clean energy professionals, aligning with UN Sustainable Development Goals. Refining and Petrochemicals focus on refining technology and petrochemical processing, providing students with skills essential for the booming petrochemical business in India and worldwide. The Digitalization of Process Technology specialisation prepares graduates for the evolving industry landscape by integrating data analytics, IoT applications, and automation skills, ensuring they are well-equipped for the technological advancements of the future.
To foster an interdisciplinary approach, the program includes capstone projects and programming courses that enable students to apply their knowledge to real-world problems. The department's faculty, with their research expertise, opens avenues for fully funded postgraduate and Ph.D. programs at top global universities. Graduates of the program are sought after by major industry players such as Exxon Mobil, Shell, Haliburton, Cairn Energy, Schlumberger, and Reliance Industries Limited. Overall, the
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program at UPES stands out for its blend of theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and specialized focus areas, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for the diverse challenges and opportunities in the field.
Program Highlights
UPES is the sole university in India integrating AI-ML and digitalization into Chemical Engineering education, providing students with a unique skill set aligned with the latest industry trends.
Boasting a 100% placement rate, graduates secure lucrative opportunities, with the highest package reaching 20 lakhs, ensuring a strong return on investment for students.
The
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program molds professionals for pivotal roles in automated chemical, petrochemical, and pharmaceutical industries, emphasizing process design, technology development, and the creation of digital twins for chemical processes and energy systems.
UPES has forged an international academic partnership with the University of Queensland, Australia, enriching the program with global perspectives and enhancing students' exposure to diverse academic environments.
The
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program is enriched by faculty members from renowned institutions globally, including the University of Missouri, Salford University, IITs, IISCs, NTU Singapore, and Stanford, with 2% of faculty listed in the prestigious Stanford researcher list.
Housed in the Centre of Excellence for Energy Systems, the program benefits from state-of-the-art interdisciplinary infrastructure, providing students with hands-on experience in cutting-edge technologies and research in the field of Chemical Engineering.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of a
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program is promising and diverse, aligning with global trends in sustainability, energy innovation, digitization, and gas engineering. Chemical engineers are pivotal in shaping industries and driving advancements, particularly in the energy sector, where they lead the development of sustainable solutions and efficient energy storage systems. The program's integration of digitization empowers chemical engineers to utilize cutting-edge technologies such as artificial intelligence and machine learning, enabling them to optimize processes, enhance productivity, and innovate on unprecedented scales. Furthermore, the field of gas engineering within the program provides opportunities to work with natural gas, petroleum, and other gaseous substances, where chemical engineers play a crucial role in extracting, refining, and responsibly utilizing these resources. Overall, a
B. Tech Chemical Engineering
program opens doors to an exciting career in a dynamic and evolving landscape, positioning graduates at the forefront of global initiatives for a greener and technologically advanced future.
Career Opportunities
The
B. Tech. Chemical Engineering
program, with its specialised tracks, equips students for diverse roles across various industries, including chemicals, refining, petrochemicals, gas, fertilizers, energy solutions, pharmaceuticals, specialty chemical, and engineering sectors. Graduates can explore roles such as Production Engineer, Design Engineer, Petroleum Engineer, Refining Engineer, Process Engineer, Research Engineer, Process Automation Engineering, Smart Manufacturer, and Data Analysts. The curriculum, developed in collaboration with industry, academia, alumni, and parents, ensures that students are well-prepared for industry demands upon graduation.
