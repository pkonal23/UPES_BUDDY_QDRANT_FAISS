Program details
The MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) program offered by UPES School of Business is a dynamic and comprehensive two-year course designed to prepare graduates for leadership roles in the evolving power and renewable energy sectors. The curriculum strikes a balance between core business disciplines and specialised knowledge in power and renewable energy areas. This program equips students with the essential leadership, strategic thinking, and decision-making skills required for success in the power industry.
The program's unique features include an in-depth analysis of the Indian and global power scenario, enabling students to develop optimization strategies and project-specific marketing and financial plans using organizational resources efficiently. Emphasis is placed on the renewable sector, exposing students to various technologies and current market trends. The curriculum is shaped with insights from industry experts and alumni working in esteemed organizations like Accenture, Cognizant, Ernst & Young, PWC, Tata Power, Adani Power, Crisil, Sterlite Energy, among others.
One of the program's key strengths lies in its specialisation in Power Business and Regulation. This specialisation focuses on efficient power and energy resource management, covering aspects such as power systems, regulations, project financing, energy conservation, and policy. Students delve into the challenges faced by the energy industry, including climate change and increasing energy demands. Moreover, the curriculum integrates data-driven decision-making, teaching students data mining, predictive analytics, machine learning, and data visualization, empowering them to use data to solve real-world business problems. The MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) program further enhances students' learning through summer internships, live projects, industrial tours, dissertations, and sessions by industry experts from India and abroad, creating a holistic and immersive learning experience for aspiring power and renewable energy professionals.
Program Highlights
•    The MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) program emphasizes hands-on learning through major projects, summer internships, and live projects with companies, providing students with practical insights into power business and regulations.
•    With a flexible curriculum, students can choose to specialise in manufacturing, service, or consulting within the power sector, enhancing their employability and career options.
•    The MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) program promotes internationalization through opportunities like student and faculty exchange, research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering a global perspective in power management.
•    Students benefit from exposure to real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by industry experts, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the challenges and dynamics within the power sector.
•    The curriculum integrates cutting-edge software and technology training, including advanced tools like Excel, SPSS, Python, TEMOA, PyPsa, and analytics tools such as R-studio, equipping students with essential skills for the evolving power management landscape.
•    The management school at UPES provides a conducive learning environment with modern smart classrooms, a well-equipped Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and advanced analytical tools, ensuring a contemporary and technology-driven educational experience for students.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) program is promising and expansive. Geared towards preparing students for mid and senior management roles in power consulting, financing, business development, and research within India and globally, the program addresses a significant shortage of skilled power professionals in the industry. Emphasizing hands-on learning, the curriculum includes internships and on-the-job training, providing practical experience to apply classroom knowledge to real-world scenarios. Covering industry practices, challenges, and emerging trends such as Hydrogen, Energy storage, electric vehicles, carbon trading, and digitalization, the program equips students with a deep understanding of the sector. Exposure to software and technology like Python, R-Studio, TEMOA, and PyPSA enhances their employability. With India's ambitious targets in renewable energy and green hydrogen production, coupled with global growth in the green energy segment and digital innovations in the power sector, the demand for Power Management graduates, especially Energy Analysts, is on the rise, promising a bright and prospective future for them.
Career Opportunities
Graduates of the MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) program at UPES School of Business have a diverse array of career opportunities awaiting them. The curriculum equips students to thrive in the dynamic energy sector, offering roles such as Power and Green Energy regulatory and financial consultant, Energy Efficiency Consultant, and Business Development professional, among others. Through immersive experiences like summer internships and live projects, students engage with industry leaders, paving the way for potential placements in renowned organizations. The program's focus on Power Business and Regulations ensures that graduates are well-prepared for roles spanning ESG specialization, sustainability consultancy, electricity network design, and more, making them sought-after professionals in the evolving landscape of power management.
Placements
The MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) program at UPES School of Business has witnessed remarkable placement outcomes in recent years. Graduates from this program have secured enticing positions in esteemed companies such as Accenture Strategy and Consulting, Infosys, Wipro, CRISIL, Ernst and Young, PWC, Deloitte, ABPS Infra advisory, Mercados Energy, Adani Power, BCG, and others. Notably, the demand for these professionals extends to mid-sized start-ups seeking specialized expertise in the power sector. Fresh graduates typically enjoy starting salaries ranging from INR 7 to 30 lakhs per annum, contingent on their individual skills, knowledge base, and exposure to the dynamic field of power management. This program, with its industry-aligned curriculum, has proven instrumental in preparing students for successful careers in the power industry.

Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for students aspiring to pursue an MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) at UPES School of Business rely on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams / followed by Personal Interview.
Non-Examination Pathways:
Students having a minimum eligible MAT /CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will only be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Valid 50 percentile and above score of CAT 2024/ XAT 2025 A score of minimum 400 or above for GMAT, a score of 120 and above in NMAT 2024 / 25 scores will also be accepted.

Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for students aspiring to pursue an MBA in Power Management (Power Business and Regulations) at UPES School of Business rely on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams / followed by Personal Interview.
Non-Examination Pathways:
Students having a minimum eligible MAT /CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will only be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Valid 50 percentile and above score of CAT 2024/ XAT 2025 A score of minimum 400 or above for GMAT, a score of 120 and above in NMAT 2024 / 25 scores will also be accepted.
