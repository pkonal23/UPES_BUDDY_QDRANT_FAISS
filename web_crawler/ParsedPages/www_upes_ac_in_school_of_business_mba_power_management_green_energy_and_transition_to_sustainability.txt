Program details
The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program offered by UPES School of Business is a cutting-edge curriculum designed to equip graduates with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in leadership roles within the dynamic power and renewable energy sectors. The program seamlessly integrates core business principles with specialized expertise in power and renewable energy, providing students with a holistic understanding of the industry. Emphasizing leadership, strategy, and decision-making, the program prepares aspiring professionals to analyze both the Indian and global power landscapes. Students develop optimization strategies, project-specific marketing plans, and financial acumen, making efficient use of organizational resources to address the evolving market trends and norms.
As a rapidly growing field, an MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) from UPES School of Business offers a comprehensive learning experience to propel students into successful careers within the Power and Renewable energy industry. The full-time two-year program includes practical components such as summer internships, live projects with companies, industrial tours, and a dissertation (major project). Furthermore, students benefit from sessions conducted by industry experts from India and abroad, gaining valuable insights into the field. The curriculum structure has been meticulously crafted with feedback and inputs from industry experts and alumni employed in esteemed organizations such as Accenture, Cognizant, Ernst & Young, PWC, Tata Power, Adani Power, Crisil, Sterlite Energy, among others.
The specialization in Green Energy and transition to Sustainability is a focal point of the program. This specialized track delves into sustainable business practices, emphasizing the role of businesses in addressing environmental challenges. Students engage with sustainability frameworks, corporate social responsibility, and sustainable development principles. The specialization covers the latest trends in the Green energy sector, including Solar, Wind, Hydrogen Energy, Energy Storage and Batteries, Electric Vehicles, and Green Highways, encompassing commercial, regulatory, and technical aspects. Graduates emerge from this program well-equipped to navigate the complexities of the evolving energy landscape while contributing to sustainable and environmentally responsible business practices.
Program Highlights
•    The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program emphasizes a major project (dissertation) allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios, fostering practical skills crucial for the power management industry.
•    With summer internships, live projects, and exposure to manufacturing, service, or consulting sectors, students gain versatile expertise, preparing them for diverse roles within the power management domain.
•    The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program actively encourages student and faculty exchange, research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering a globally oriented mindset and collaborative learning experiences.
•    Through real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by industry experts, students acquire insights into the challenges and opportunities in the power management sector, enhancing their decision-making abilities.
•    Students are equipped with essential software and technology skills, including advanced Excel, SPSS, Python, TEMOA, PyPsa, and analytics tools like R-studio, ensuring they are adept in the latest technological advancements.
•    The management school provides state-of-the-art facilities, including modern smart classrooms, a well-equipped Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and advanced analytical tools, creating an optimal learning environment for aspiring power management professionals.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program is highly promising, offering students opportunities for mid and senior management roles in various aspects of the power and green energy industries, both within India and globally. The industry faces a significant shortage of skilled professionals capable of effectively managing power and green energy businesses. The program emphasizes hands-on learning, providing students with practical experience through internships and on-the-job training, giving them a competitive edge in the job market. The curriculum is designed to impart a deep understanding of the industry, covering emerging trends such as Hydrogen, Energy storage, battery storage, electric vehicles, carbon trading, and digitalization. Students are exposed to cutting-edge software and technology, enhancing their employability. With India's ambitious targets for renewable energy and green hydrogen production, coupled with the global growth in the green energy sector and digital innovations in the power industry, the demand for specialised Energy Analysts in Power Management is on the rise. The future for Power Management graduates appears bright and promising, aligning with the evolving landscape of sustainable energy and digital advancements in the power sector.
Career Opportunities
The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program offered by UPES School of Business opens up a diverse range of career opportunities for its graduates. Students have the chance to engage with reputed organizations, gaining valuable experience in the corporate world through summer internships, live projects, and placements. With a focus on the energy sector, potential career paths include roles such as Power and Green Energy regulatory and financial consultant, Energy Efficiency Consultant, Sustainability consultant, Business Analyst, and more. The program equips students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in various facets of the power industry, making them valuable contributors in areas like electricity generation, carbon trading, and green energy project management.
Placements
The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program at UPES School of Business has witnessed remarkable placement success in recent years. Graduates have secured coveted positions in esteemed organizations such as Accenture Strategy and Consulting, Infosys, Wipro, Ernst and Young, PWC, Deloitte, CRISIL, ABPS Infra advisory, Mercados Energy, Adani Power, BCG, among others. The program also opens doors to opportunities in burgeoning mid-sized start-ups eager to tap into the specialized skills of MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) graduates. With starting salaries ranging from INR 7-30 lakh per annum, the compensation reflects the individual's expertise, knowledge, and exposure in the dynamic field of power management. This success underscores the program's commitment to producing skilled professionals sought after by industry leaders.

Eligibility
Students who are interested in pursuing an MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) must satisfy the designated minimum eligibility requirements: Minimum 50% marks in Class X, XII and Graduation. Graduation From a recognized University in any stream.
Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for individuals aspiring to pursue an MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) at UPES School of Business rely on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams / followed by Personal Interview.
Non-Examination Pathways:
Students having a minimum eligible MAT /CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will only be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Valid 50 percentile and above score of CAT 2024/ XAT 2025 A score of minimum 400 or above for GMAT, a score of 120 and above in NMAT 2024 / 25 scores will also be accepted.

Program details
The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program offered by UPES School of Business is a cutting-edge curriculum designed to equip graduates with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in leadership roles within the dynamic power and renewable energy sectors. The program seamlessly integrates core business principles with specialized expertise in power and renewable energy, providing students with a holistic understanding of the industry. Emphasizing leadership, strategy, and decision-making, the program prepares aspiring professionals to analyze both the Indian and global power landscapes. Students develop optimization strategies, project-specific marketing plans, and financial acumen, making efficient use of organizational resources to address the evolving market trends and norms.
As a rapidly growing field, an MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) from UPES School of Business offers a comprehensive learning experience to propel students into successful careers within the Power and Renewable energy industry. The full-time two-year program includes practical components such as summer internships, live projects with companies, industrial tours, and a dissertation (major project). Furthermore, students benefit from sessions conducted by industry experts from India and abroad, gaining valuable insights into the field. The curriculum structure has been meticulously crafted with feedback and inputs from industry experts and alumni employed in esteemed organizations such as Accenture, Cognizant, Ernst & Young, PWC, Tata Power, Adani Power, Crisil, Sterlite Energy, among others.
The specialization in Green Energy and transition to Sustainability is a focal point of the program. This specialized track delves into sustainable business practices, emphasizing the role of businesses in addressing environmental challenges. Students engage with sustainability frameworks, corporate social responsibility, and sustainable development principles. The specialization covers the latest trends in the Green energy sector, including Solar, Wind, Hydrogen Energy, Energy Storage and Batteries, Electric Vehicles, and Green Highways, encompassing commercial, regulatory, and technical aspects. Graduates emerge from this program well-equipped to navigate the complexities of the evolving energy landscape while contributing to sustainable and environmentally responsible business practices.
Program Highlights
•    The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program emphasizes a major project (dissertation) allowing students to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world scenarios, fostering practical skills crucial for the power management industry.
•    With summer internships, live projects, and exposure to manufacturing, service, or consulting sectors, students gain versatile expertise, preparing them for diverse roles within the power management domain.
•    The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program actively encourages student and faculty exchange, research projects, seminars, conferences, and internships, fostering a globally oriented mindset and collaborative learning experiences.
•    Through real-world case studies, industry visits, and guest lectures by industry experts, students acquire insights into the challenges and opportunities in the power management sector, enhancing their decision-making abilities.
•    Students are equipped with essential software and technology skills, including advanced Excel, SPSS, Python, TEMOA, PyPsa, and analytics tools like R-studio, ensuring they are adept in the latest technological advancements.
•    The management school provides state-of-the-art facilities, including modern smart classrooms, a well-equipped Analytics Lab, a comprehensive library, and advanced analytical tools, creating an optimal learning environment for aspiring power management professionals.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program is highly promising, offering students opportunities for mid and senior management roles in various aspects of the power and green energy industries, both within India and globally. The industry faces a significant shortage of skilled professionals capable of effectively managing power and green energy businesses. The program emphasizes hands-on learning, providing students with practical experience through internships and on-the-job training, giving them a competitive edge in the job market. The curriculum is designed to impart a deep understanding of the industry, covering emerging trends such as Hydrogen, Energy storage, battery storage, electric vehicles, carbon trading, and digitalization. Students are exposed to cutting-edge software and technology, enhancing their employability. With India's ambitious targets for renewable energy and green hydrogen production, coupled with the global growth in the green energy sector and digital innovations in the power industry, the demand for specialised Energy Analysts in Power Management is on the rise. The future for Power Management graduates appears bright and promising, aligning with the evolving landscape of sustainable energy and digital advancements in the power sector.
Career Opportunities
The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program offered by UPES School of Business opens up a diverse range of career opportunities for its graduates. Students have the chance to engage with reputed organizations, gaining valuable experience in the corporate world through summer internships, live projects, and placements. With a focus on the energy sector, potential career paths include roles such as Power and Green Energy regulatory and financial consultant, Energy Efficiency Consultant, Sustainability consultant, Business Analyst, and more. The program equips students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in various facets of the power industry, making them valuable contributors in areas like electricity generation, carbon trading, and green energy project management.
Placements
The MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) program at UPES School of Business has witnessed remarkable placement success in recent years. Graduates have secured coveted positions in esteemed organizations such as Accenture Strategy and Consulting, Infosys, Wipro, Ernst and Young, PWC, Deloitte, CRISIL, ABPS Infra advisory, Mercados Energy, Adani Power, BCG, among others. The program also opens doors to opportunities in burgeoning mid-sized start-ups eager to tap into the specialized skills of MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) graduates. With starting salaries ranging from INR 7-30 lakh per annum, the compensation reflects the individual's expertise, knowledge, and exposure in the dynamic field of power management. This success underscores the program's commitment to producing skilled professionals sought after by industry leaders.

The program also opens doors to opportunities in burgeoning mid-sized start-ups eager to tap into the specialized skills of MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) graduates. With starting salaries ranging from INR 7-30 lakh per annum, the compensation reflects the individual's expertise, knowledge, and exposure in the dynamic field of power management. This success underscores the program's commitment to producing skilled professionals sought after by industry leaders.

Selection Criteria
The selection criteria for individuals aspiring to pursue an MBA in Power Management (Green Energy and transition to Sustainability) at UPES School of Business rely on the individual's performance in UPESMET / National Level Exams / followed by Personal Interview.
Non-Examination Pathways:
Students having a minimum eligible MAT /CMAT 2025 score of 70 percentile will be exempted from the UPES Management Entrance Test and will only be called for Group Discussion and Interview, if qualified. Valid 50 percentile and above score of CAT 2024/ XAT 2025 A score of minimum 400 or above for GMAT, a score of 120 and above in NMAT 2024 / 25 scores will also be accepted.
