Program details
The Ph.D. in Economics program offered by UPES School of Business is designed to cater to the aspirations of individuals seeking an in-depth exploration of economic theory, policy, and empirical research. This program distinguishes itself by placing a strong emphasis on the application of economic principles within the dynamic and constantly evolving energy sector. Through a carefully curated curriculum, students will engage in advanced coursework that encompasses quantitative methods and specialised research projects, aimed at fostering expertise in areas such as energy economics and environmental economics.
What sets this program apart is its focused approach to addressing the challenges and opportunities prevalent in the energy sector, offering students a unique perspective on the critical issues that define this industry. The comprehensive curriculum is structured to ensure that participants not only acquire a solid foundation in economic theories but also gain practical insights into the intricate dynamics of the energy domain. The integration of specialised research projects allows students to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios, preparing them for impactful contributions to both academia and the industry.
Throughout the course of the program, students will be mentored by a distinguished faculty comprising experts in the field of economics. This mentorship, combined with exposure to cutting-edge research methodologies, positions graduates of the Ph.D. in Economics program from UPES School of Business as well-rounded professionals capable of making significant contributions to the broader economic landscape. Whether aspiring to pursue academic careers or to make a mark in the energy sector, graduates emerge with a well-rounded skill set and a deep understanding of the economic intricacies that shape our world.
Program Highlights
The Ph.D. in Economics program promotes interdisciplinary research, emphasizing the interconnectedness of economics, management, energy, and transportation, providing scholars with a holistic perspective on contemporary challenges.
Collaborations with industry partners and experts ensure that research is academically rigorous and directly applicable to current business challenges, enhancing the practical impact of scholarly work.
UPES offers state-of-the-art research facilities, including dedicated labs and extensive databases, empowering scholars to conduct cutting-edge research at the forefront of economic analysis and policymaking.
International collaborations, conferences, and exchange programs provide scholars with opportunities to gain global perspectives and insights, enriching their academic experience and preparing them for diverse career paths.
Distinguished faculty members, accomplished in both research and practice, serve as mentors, guiding Ph.D. candidates through their academic journey and ensuring a high standard of scholarly output.
We actively support scholars in publishing their research in reputable journals, with a focus on ABDC A*, ABDC A, and Scopus Q1 publications. Presenting findings at conferences enhances the visibility and impact of their work, contributing to the global academic discourse and career advancement.
PEO / PSO
The Ph.D. in Economics program at UPES School of Advanced Engineering is designed to cultivate scholars who exhibit excellence in research and contribute significantly to the field of economics. The program's education objectives encompass the development of advanced analytical and methodological skills, enabling students to engage in cutting-edge research and analysis. Emphasizing a multidisciplinary approach, the program aims to foster a deep understanding of economic principles and their practical applications. Graduates are expected to demonstrate the ability to critically assess and address complex economic issues, make valuable contributions to academia, industry, and policymaking, and ultimately become thought leaders in the realm of economics. The program prioritizes intellectual independence, ethical research practices, and effective communication skills, preparing students for impactful roles in academia, research institutions, and the public or private sectors.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
A Ph.D. in Economics offers a diverse and promising future, presenting various career paths. Academically, individuals can secure tenure-track positions as professors, researchers, or academic administrators. In policy analysis, opportunities lie in governmental organizations, think tanks, or research institutions, involving the formulation and evaluation of economic policies. Economic consulting firms seek Ph.D. economists for market analysis, forecasting, and litigation support. The financial sector offers roles in banks and investment firms, focusing on economic indicators and market trends. Non-profit organizations address global economic issues, poverty alleviation, and sustainability. Ph.D. economists can engage in data analysis and research for research firms or as consultants, applying economic models to market research and impact studies. Entrepreneurship and startups provide avenues to apply economic principles to business ventures. Specialisations in environmental economics focus on sustainability, climate change, and resource management policies. Overall, the future for Ph.D. Economists is dynamic, with prospects shaped by specialisation, expertise, and evolving industry needs, ensuring a broad array of opportunities across sectors.
Career Opportunities
A Ph.D. in Economics from UPES School of Business equips graduates with a profound understanding of economic theories and methodologies, preparing them for diverse career opportunities. Graduates can pursue academic roles as esteemed professors, researchers, and economists, contributing valuable insights to the field. Additionally, the program nurtures analytical and critical thinking skills, enabling graduates to excel in policymaking, consulting, and advisory roles within government bodies, international organizations, and private sectors. The emphasis on interdisciplinary approaches enhances versatility, making graduates adept at addressing complex economic challenges across industries. The program's industry-relevant curriculum and research focus empower candidates to explore careers in financial institutions, think tanks, and corporate sectors, where their expertise is highly sought after. With a Ph.D. in Economics from UPES, individuals emerge as thought leaders, ready to make impactful contributions to the dynamic landscape of global economics.
