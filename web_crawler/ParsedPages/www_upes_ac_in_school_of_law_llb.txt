LL.B. - Course Overview
The LL.B. (Hons.) 3 year program offered by UPES School of Law provides students with a comprehensive and rigorous education in the field of law. The program is designed to equip students with the necessary knowledge and skills to pursue a successful career in various legal professions. Throughout the three-year duration, students are exposed to a wide range of subjects, including constitutional law, criminal law, corporate law, international law, and legal research and writing. The curriculum is carefully crafted to balance theoretical understanding and practical application, ensuring that students develop a strong foundation in legal principles while gaining hands-on experience through moot court competitions, internships, and legal clinics.
UPES School of Law places great emphasises on experiential learning, and the LL.B. 3 years program reflects this commitment. Students have the opportunity to participate in various legal research projects, seminars, and workshops conducted by leading legal experts and practitioners. The faculty consists of experienced and renowned professors who bring their expertise and real-world insights into the classroom, fostering a dynamic learning environment. Additionally, the school has strong ties with the legal industry, providing students with networking opportunities and access to internships and placements in top law firms, corporate legal departments, and public sector organisations. By the end of the program, graduates are well-prepared to tackle the challenges of the legal profession and contribute meaningfully to the justice system.

Scope and Highlights
The LL.B. program offered by UPES School of Law encompasses a comprehensive and rigorous curriculum designed to equip students with a strong foundation in the field of law. The program's scope extends across various legal domains, including constitutional law, criminal law, corporate law, and intellectual property rights. Through a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical exposure, students gain a deep understanding of legal principles, processes, and practices. The program's highlights lie in its emphasis on experiential learning, with moot court competitions, internships, and industry interactions enabling students to develop essential legal skills and gain real-world insights. Additionally, the program promotes interdisciplinary learning, fostering critical thinking, research capabilities, and ethical decision-making. Graduates of the LL.B. program from UPES School of Law are well-prepared to embark on successful careers in the legal profession, equipped with the knowledge and skills necessary to navigate complex legal challenges and contribute meaningfully to society.
Career Opportunities
LL.B. is a professional integrated degree that opens up many career opportunities for graduates. Graduates of this program are equipped with a comprehensive understanding of various aspects of law, including constitutional law, criminal law, corporate law, and more. They are trained to analyse complex legal issues, provide sound legal advice, and effectively advocate for their clients. With UPES's emphasis on practical learning and experiential training, students gain valuable skills through moot court competitions, internships, and legal clinics, preparing them for a successful career in law. Graduates can pursue diverse career paths such as litigation, corporate law, legal consulting, public service, academia, and judicial services in India and internationally. The strong industry connections and placement support offered by UPES further enhance the career prospects of LLB graduates, making them sought-after legal professionals in the ever-evolving legal landscape.
Why study the LLB Program at UPES School of Law?
The programs offered by the university have a range of features that set it apart from other law programs. The university has received numerous accolades, including a QS 5-star rating for Employability, University of the Year (North India) awarded by ASSOCHAM, and an Accredited Grade 'A' by NAAC.
As per the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) 2022, the Ministry of Education, India, School of Law, UPES has been ranked 21 in Law and among the Top 10 Private Law colleges in India by India Today.
We have a vision to provide quality legal education in this new age of digital transformation. The institute is committed to exploring and implementing an outcome-based education. We focus on applying the new-age learning technologies to competency-based, adaptive and collaborative education. This will help us create a learning space that meets every student's unique needs.
In the coming years, we aim to focus on digital learning through a balanced combination of technology, digital content, live instruction and experiential learning. Our partnerships with global institutions would provide our students with the much needed ‘international exposure at home’. We have the privilege of becoming the first Indian university to partner with the UK-based University of Law. School of Law, UPES, and UK-based The University of Law (ULaw), one of the most reputed universities for legal studies globally, have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) that aims to provide opportunities to students such as –
Scholarships for postgraduate programs
Faculty and student exchange
Online modules
Summer school program
Research collaboration
Collaborative seminars, moot courts, conferences and student competitions.
We also have MoUs with renowned institutions such as Maurer School of Law and Nottingham Trent University to provide progression opportunities for higher education. The School of Law, UPES has taken another step towards bridging the gap between industry and academia. In line with the guidelines released by the University Grants Commission, we have appointed top legal practitioners as Professors of Practice to inculcate practical skills among students through their vast industry experience. Finally, the program offers unmatched global opportunities, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the international legal market.

What are the career opportunities after doing the LL.B. program?
Completing the LL.B. program offered by UPES School of Law opens up a wide range of promising career opportunities for graduates. With a strong foundation in legal principles and practical skills, graduates can pursue a rewarding career in various fields. Many graduates choose to practise law as advocates, solicitors, or legal consultants, representing clients and providing expert advice. Others explore opportunities in the corporate sector, working as legal advisors or compliance officers for companies. Additionally, opportunities abound in the government sector, where graduates can work as public prosecutors and legal officers or join the judiciary. Furthermore, careers in academia, legal research, and legal journalism also offer avenues for those passionate about contributing to the legal profession through teaching, scholarship, or media. The comprehensive LL.B.  program from UPES School of Law equips students with the necessary knowledge and skills to excel in these diverse career paths and significantly impact the legal arena.
