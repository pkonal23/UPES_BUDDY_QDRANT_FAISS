Program details
The
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program offered by UPES is a four-year undergraduate program that aims to equip students with knowledge and skills in
cloud computing and virtualization technology
. The program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and engineering, followed by specialized courses in
cloud computing and virtualization technology
. The curriculum of the program includes courses in cloud architecture, virtualization technology, cloud storage, cloud security, and more. The program also focuses on practical learning through hands-on exercises, assignments, and projects, enabling students to apply the concepts they have learned in real-world scenarios.
Furthermore, the program provides students with opportunities for internships and industrial visits, which help them gain industry exposure and practical experience. Students also have access to cutting-edge technology and tools, including cloud-based computing platforms like AWS, Azure, GCP, and Git, virtualization software, and more. Overall, the program aims to prepare students for a career in the rapidly evolving field of cloud computing and virtualization technology, where they can apply their knowledge and skills to solve real-world problems.
In conclusion, the
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program offered by UPES provides students with a comprehensive understanding of cloud computing and virtualization technology. The program focuses on practical learning, enabling students to apply the concepts they have learned in real-world situations. The program also provides opportunities for internships and industry exposure, preparing students for a successful career in the field.
Program Highlights
Four exclusive labs dedicated to cloud specialization providing hands-on experience in all cloud-based subjects using high-end systems.
Students get benefits of academic alliance with IBM for syllabus setting, syllabus upgradation, and training to students in cloud-based tools and subjects.
The program offers student exchange programs, study tours, summer schools, and international conferences to gain exposure to different cultures and education systems.
A wide range of tools and technologies used in cloud computing and virtualization technology, including AWS, Azure, GCP, OpenStack, VMware Workstation, VMware Hyper-V, VirtualBox, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins and more.
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology program provides opportunities for students to work on industry projects, internships, and participate in hackathons and coding competitions through industry collaborations.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The global market for
cloud computing and virtualization technology
is rapidly growing, with a projected market size of USD 832.1 billion by 2025, and increasing demand for professionals skilled in
cloud computing and virtualization technology
.
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program prepares students for various career paths in the industry. Graduates can pursue further studies in related fields or opt for certification programs offered by cloud service providers to enhance their skills and employability. The future paths that graduates can explore include multi or hybrid cloud, edge computing, IoT, enhanced data storage capacity, AI, serverless computing, Kubernetes, cloud orchestration and optimization, SASE adoption, data privacy and cloud mitigation, service mesh, and disaster recovery. Cloud computing offers benefits such as flexibility, scalability, and reliability, but its most significant advantage is disaster recovery, making it a crucial aspect for business continuity.
Career Opportunities
Completing a B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology offers a wide array of career opportunities. The program emphasizes practical credits, providing students with hands-on experience in various cloud computing aspects. This approach helps students gain a comprehensive understanding of intracellular and intercellular interactions within molecules by applying physical principles.
Some of the popular career paths that graduates of B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology program can pursue are:
Cloud Solutions Architect
Cloud Support Engineer
Cloud Security Specialist
Project Engineer
Cloud Service Developer
Cloud System Administrator
Placements
Completing
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program opens up job opportunities for students in some of the leading tech companies, such as IBM, Oracle, Apple, Aditya Birla Group, Microsoft, Michael Page, Dell, Amazon, and VM Ware. The program provides practical credits that impart hands-on capabilities on various aspects of the cloud. Graduates of this program can expect an average CTC of Rs. 10.25 lakhs per annum, which is higher than the industry average for entry-level cloud computing jobs in India. The highest CTC offered is Rs. 25 lakhs per annum, indicating significant growth potential and career advancement for program graduates. Cloud Computing is highly sought after in the careers field, making the
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program an excellent option for students interested in pursuing this lucrative career path.

Eligibility
The minimum eligibility criteria for
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
to be fulfilled by interested students is as follows: Minimum 50% marks in Class X and XII with 50% in PCM in Class XII.
Selection Criteria
The process of selection criteria for students interested in pursuing
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
offered by UPES is based on the individual's performance in UPESEAT / JEE Mains / Board Merit / SAT/ CUET.

Program details
The
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program offered by UPES is a four-year undergraduate program that aims to equip students with knowledge and skills in
cloud computing and virtualization technology
. The program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and engineering, followed by specialized courses in
cloud computing and virtualization technology
. The curriculum of the program includes courses in cloud architecture, virtualization technology, cloud storage, cloud security, and more. The program also focuses on practical learning through hands-on exercises, assignments, and projects, enabling students to apply the concepts they have learned in real-world scenarios.
Furthermore, the program provides students with opportunities for internships and industrial visits, which help them gain industry exposure and practical experience. Students also have access to cutting-edge technology and tools, including cloud-based computing platforms like AWS, Azure, GCP, and Git, virtualization software, and more. Overall, the program aims to prepare students for a career in the rapidly evolving field of cloud computing and virtualization technology, where they can apply their knowledge and skills to solve real-world problems.
In conclusion, the
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program offered by UPES provides students with a comprehensive understanding of cloud computing and virtualization technology. The program focuses on practical learning, enabling students to apply the concepts they have learned in real-world situations. The program also provides opportunities for internships and industry exposure, preparing students for a successful career in the field.
Program Highlights
Four exclusive labs dedicated to cloud specialization providing hands-on experience in all cloud-based subjects using high-end systems.
Students get benefits of academic alliance with IBM for syllabus setting, syllabus upgradation, and training to students in cloud-based tools and subjects.
The program offers student exchange programs, study tours, summer schools, and international conferences to gain exposure to different cultures and education systems.
A wide range of tools and technologies used in cloud computing and virtualization technology, including AWS, Azure, GCP, OpenStack, VMware Workstation, VMware Hyper-V, VirtualBox, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins and more.
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology program provides opportunities for students to work on industry projects, internships, and participate in hackathons and coding competitions through industry collaborations.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The global market for
cloud computing and virtualization technology
is rapidly growing, with a projected market size of USD 832.1 billion by 2025, and increasing demand for professionals skilled in
cloud computing and virtualization technology
.
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program prepares students for various career paths in the industry. Graduates can pursue further studies in related fields or opt for certification programs offered by cloud service providers to enhance their skills and employability. The future paths that graduates can explore include multi or hybrid cloud, edge computing, IoT, enhanced data storage capacity, AI, serverless computing, Kubernetes, cloud orchestration and optimization, SASE adoption, data privacy and cloud mitigation, service mesh, and disaster recovery. Cloud computing offers benefits such as flexibility, scalability, and reliability, but its most significant advantage is disaster recovery, making it a crucial aspect for business continuity.
Career Opportunities
Completing a B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology offers a wide array of career opportunities. The program emphasizes practical credits, providing students with hands-on experience in various cloud computing aspects. This approach helps students gain a comprehensive understanding of intracellular and intercellular interactions within molecules by applying physical principles.
Some of the popular career paths that graduates of B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology program can pursue are:
Cloud Solutions Architect
Cloud Support Engineer
Cloud Security Specialist
Project Engineer
Cloud Service Developer
Cloud System Administrator
Placements
Completing
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program opens up job opportunities for students in some of the leading tech companies, such as IBM, Oracle, Apple, Aditya Birla Group, Microsoft, Michael Page, Dell, Amazon, and VM Ware. The program provides practical credits that impart hands-on capabilities on various aspects of the cloud. Graduates of this program can expect an average CTC of Rs. 10.25 lakhs per annum, which is higher than the industry average for entry-level cloud computing jobs in India. The highest CTC offered is Rs. 25 lakhs per annum, indicating significant growth potential and career advancement for program graduates. Cloud Computing is highly sought after in the careers field, making the
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program an excellent option for students interested in pursuing this lucrative career path.

Eligibility
The minimum eligibility criteria for
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
to be fulfilled by interested students is as follows: Minimum 50% marks in Class X and XII with 50% in PCM in Class XII.
Selection Criteria
The process of selection criteria for students interested in pursuing
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
offered by UPES is based on the individual's performance in UPESEAT / JEE Mains / Board Merit / SAT/ CUET.

Program details
The
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program offered by UPES is a four-year undergraduate program that aims to equip students with knowledge and skills in
cloud computing and virtualization technology
. The program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computer science and engineering, followed by specialized courses in
cloud computing and virtualization technology
. The curriculum of the program includes courses in cloud architecture, virtualization technology, cloud storage, cloud security, and more. The program also focuses on practical learning through hands-on exercises, assignments, and projects, enabling students to apply the concepts they have learned in real-world scenarios.
Furthermore, the program provides students with opportunities for internships and industrial visits, which help them gain industry exposure and practical experience. Students also have access to cutting-edge technology and tools, including cloud-based computing platforms like AWS, Azure, GCP, and Git, virtualization software, and more. Overall, the program aims to prepare students for a career in the rapidly evolving field of cloud computing and virtualization technology, where they can apply their knowledge and skills to solve real-world problems.
In conclusion, the
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program offered by UPES provides students with a comprehensive understanding of cloud computing and virtualization technology. The program focuses on practical learning, enabling students to apply the concepts they have learned in real-world situations. The program also provides opportunities for internships and industry exposure, preparing students for a successful career in the field.
Program Highlights
Four exclusive labs dedicated to cloud specialization providing hands-on experience in all cloud-based subjects using high-end systems.
Students get benefits of academic alliance with IBM for syllabus setting, syllabus upgradation, and training to students in cloud-based tools and subjects.
The program offers student exchange programs, study tours, summer schools, and international conferences to gain exposure to different cultures and education systems.
A wide range of tools and technologies used in cloud computing and virtualization technology, including AWS, Azure, GCP, OpenStack, VMware Workstation, VMware Hyper-V, VirtualBox, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins and more.
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology program provides opportunities for students to work on industry projects, internships, and participate in hackathons and coding competitions through industry collaborations.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The global market for
cloud computing and virtualization technology
is rapidly growing, with a projected market size of USD 832.1 billion by 2025, and increasing demand for professionals skilled in
cloud computing and virtualization technology
.
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program prepares students for various career paths in the industry. Graduates can pursue further studies in related fields or opt for certification programs offered by cloud service providers to enhance their skills and employability. The future paths that graduates can explore include multi or hybrid cloud, edge computing, IoT, enhanced data storage capacity, AI, serverless computing, Kubernetes, cloud orchestration and optimization, SASE adoption, data privacy and cloud mitigation, service mesh, and disaster recovery. Cloud computing offers benefits such as flexibility, scalability, and reliability, but its most significant advantage is disaster recovery, making it a crucial aspect for business continuity.
Career Opportunities
Completing a B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology offers a wide array of career opportunities. The program emphasizes practical credits, providing students with hands-on experience in various cloud computing aspects. This approach helps students gain a comprehensive understanding of intracellular and intercellular interactions within molecules by applying physical principles.
Some of the popular career paths that graduates of B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology program can pursue are:
Cloud Solutions Architect
Cloud Support Engineer
Cloud Security Specialist
Project Engineer
Cloud Service Developer
Cloud System Administrator
Placements
Completing
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program opens up job opportunities for students in some of the leading tech companies, such as IBM, Oracle, Apple, Aditya Birla Group, Microsoft, Michael Page, Dell, Amazon, and VM Ware. The program provides practical credits that impart hands-on capabilities on various aspects of the cloud. Graduates of this program can expect an average CTC of Rs. 10.25 lakhs per annum, which is higher than the industry average for entry-level cloud computing jobs in India. The highest CTC offered is Rs. 25 lakhs per annum, indicating significant growth potential and career advancement for program graduates. Cloud Computing is highly sought after in the careers field, making the
B. Tech in Cloud Computing and Virtualization Technology
program an excellent option for students interested in pursuing this lucrative career path.
