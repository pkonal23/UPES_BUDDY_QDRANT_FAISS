M.Sc. - Course Overview

The School of Advanced Engineering offers an M.Sc. in Applied Sciences and an M.Sc. in Applied Geology. The duration of the program is 2 years. Each program offers a range of specialisations, allowing students to tailor their education to their interests and career goals. M.Sc. allows students to specialise in a particular field of science and gain in-depth knowledge and skills required for their careers. The course includes theoretical classes, practical sessions, and research work. Graduates of this program are equipped with the advanced knowledge and skills required to pursue higher education or a career in their chosen field.

Scope and Highlights
Studying M.Sc. at UPES School of Advanced Engineering provides students with a comprehensive education, industry exposure, and practical training, making them well-equipped. The M.Sc. program at UPES provides students with an advanced education, practical experience, and excellent employment opportunities. The programs are highly specialised and designed to meet the needs of the industry.
Some of the highlights of M.Sc. programs include a strong foundation in the subject area, hands-on experience through laboratory work and projects, exposure to research, and opportunities to explore various career paths. The program also provides opportunities for personal and professional development through extracurricular activities, internships, and industry collaborations.
Career Opportunities
M.Sc. graduates in India have a wide range of career opportunities available to them. The career opportunities depend on the individual's field of study, experience, and skill set. However, M.Sc. graduates are in high demand in various fields and can make significantly contribute to their chosen industries. There are numerous job roles that you can take:
Research Scientist
Research Analyst
Software Developer
Scientists
Astronomers
Astrophysicist
Science Teachers
Academic Research Editor
These are just a few examples of the positions that post-graduates can apply for after completing a M.Sc. degree. The specific positions available will depend on the individual graduate's interests, skills, and experience.
Why study M.Sc. at UPES School of Advanced Engineering?
Studying M.Sc. at the UPES School of Advanced Engineering provides students with a high-quality education, practical experience, and excellent employment opportunities. There are several reasons one may consider studying M.Sc. at the reputed UPES School of Advanced Engineering:
Excellent Faculty:
The School of Advanced Engineering at UPES has highly experienced and qualified faculty members who deeply understand their respective fields. The faculty members are actively involved in research and bring their expertise and knowledge into the classroom to provide students with a well-rounded education.
Specialised Courses:
The School of Advanced Engineering at UPES offers a range of specialized M.Sc. courses in various fields, including Applied Geology, Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. The courses are designed to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the subject matter and prepare them for careers in their respective fields.
Industry-Relevant Curriculum:
The curriculum of M.Sc. courses at UPES is designed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure that the students receive an education relevant to the industry's needs. The curriculum includes practical sessions, project work, and internships, which provide students with hands-on experience and help them develop practical skills.
State-of-the-Art Infrastructure:
The School of Advanced Engineering at UPES has state-of-the-art infrastructure, including well-equipped laboratories, research facilities, and a modern library. The infrastructure provides students with access to the latest tools and equipment required for their research and projects.
Placement Opportunities:
UPES has a dedicated placement cell that assists students in finding employment opportunities after completing their M.Sc. The placement cell has strong ties with the industry and works closely with companies to identify student job opportunities.
Innovative Teaching and Learning
We at the department believe in experiential teaching and learning and strive to imbibe problem-solving skills and critical thinking in students. The following practices are in place to improve the quality of teaching-learning and student experience.
Design and review of individual course plans at the beginning of session
Course Completion Report (CCR)
Academic planning and monitoring
Real-world/Project-based assignments
Quality laboratory experience
Encouraging advanced learner
Slow learners support
ICT-enabled classroom (sound system, mic, and projector)
Guest lectures from industry experts
Industry-led courses
FDP/Industry Attachment Program for Faculty members (Abhigyat)
Professional Software Training (PST) and Certification
NPTEL lectures
Prototype development
Use of virtual labs
Participation in competitive events (in-house/national/international)
Participation in conferences/seminars/workshops (national/international)
Semester Exchange Program
Student Testimonials
Collection of feedback and reviews from our former students, providing valuable insights into the course's quality, faculty, infrastructure, and career prospects.

FAQs
What is M.Sc. Program?
M.Sc. stands for Master of Science and is a postgraduate academic degree typically requiring 2 years of full-time study. The program is designed to provide students with advanced knowledge and practical skills in a specific field of science. M.Sc. programs are offered in various fields, such as Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and many more.
What is the eligibility criteria for a M.Sc. program?
The eligibility criteria for the M.Sc. program is 50% in 10th & 12th (PCM/B) and 50% in graduation in the relevant subject.
What are the different specializations I can opt for in M.Sc. Program?

What are the career opportunities after doing M.Sc. Program?
Completing an M.Sc. program opens up several career opportunities for graduates. The specific opportunities available depend on the field of study and the individual's interests and skillset. Some popular career paths for M.Sc. graduates include:
Research and Development:
M.Sc. graduates can pursue a career in research and development in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and chemical industries. They can work in research laboratories, government research organisations, and private research and development companies.
Academia:
M.Sc. graduates can choose to become teachers, professors, or lecturers in universities and colleges. They can also contribute to research and development in academia by joining research teams or pursuing a Ph.D.
Healthcare:
M.Sc. graduates in healthcare fields like microbiology, biotechnology, and pharmacology can work in hospitals, clinics, and pharmaceutical companies. They can develop new treatments and therapies, research, and manage clinical trials.
IT and Data Science:
M.Sc. graduates can work in IT and data science fields such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics. They can work in technology companies or startups, developing new products and technologies.
Environmental Science:
M.Sc. graduates in environmental science can work in government organisations, NGOs, or private companies, contributing to environmental protection, conservation, and sustainability.
Government Services:
M.Sc. graduates can work in various government organisations like the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA), Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), Department of Atomic Energy (DAE), and other government agencies.
What is the average salary after a M.Sc. Program?
The average salary for M.Sc. graduates depends on various factors, including the field of study, the industry, the job position, and the location. Generally, M.Sc. graduates are considered to be highly skilled professionals with advanced knowledge in their respective fields, which can translate into higher salaries. The average salary ranges from Rs. 4 LPA to Rs. 12 LPA, however, this can vary widely depending on the specific field of study and the industry.
