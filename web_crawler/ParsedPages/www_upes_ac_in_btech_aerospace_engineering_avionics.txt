**Program Overview**

The **B.Tech. Aerospace Engineering (Avionics)** program at **University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)** provides students with a specialized education in designing and analyzing electronic and communication systems for the aerospace industry. The curriculum covers essential subjects such as Radar Systems, Fly-By-Wire Flight Control System, Signal and Embedded Electronics, and Avionics System Design. Additionally, it incorporates fundamental aerospace engineering topics like Aerodynamics, Propulsion, and Structures.

**Program Highlights**

* Cutting-edge coursework covering avionics systems, aircraft communication, navigation, and advanced electronics
* Strong industry partnerships and hands-on training opportunities
* Encouragement for research and innovation in avionics
* Highly qualified faculty with extensive industry experience
* Access to modern laboratories, simulation tools, and advanced equipment
* Dedicated career services, placement assistance, and networking opportunities

**Future Scope and Industry Trends**

The future of the **B.Tech. Aerospace Engineering (Avionics)** program holds immense potential as avionics technology is set for revolutionary advancements. These developments will significantly enhance aircraft systems, navigation, communication, and flight control capabilities. The integration of cutting-edge technologies like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and data analytics will play a crucial role in ensuring increased flight safety, fuel efficiency, and the realization of autonomous operations. Graduates of this program will be well-equipped to be at the forefront of this exciting transformation.

**Career Opportunities**

Completing the **B.Tech. Aerospace Engineering (Avionics)** program opens up a world of exciting career opportunities in the aerospace industry. Graduates can pursue roles in avionics systems design, development, and maintenance. They can excel as avionics engineers, working on cutting-edge technologies for aircraft navigation, communication, and control systems. Additionally, graduates can explore opportunities with leading aerospace companies, airlines, and research organizations.

**Placements**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies's **B.Tech. Aerospace Engineering (Avionics)** program offers exceptional placement opportunities. With a strong emphasis on practical skills and theoretical knowledge, students are equipped with the expertise needed to thrive in the aerospace industry. The dedicated placement cell collaborates with renowned aviation companies, leading airlines, and top-notch research organizations. Graduates have consistently secured promising roles in avionics, aircraft design, navigation systems, and related domains.

**Eligibility and Selection Criteria**

Interested students must fulfill the minimum eligibility criteria for **B.Tech. Aerospace Engineering (Avionics)**:

* Minimum 50% marks in Classes X and XII
* 50% in PCM (Physics/Chemistry and Mathematics) in Class XII

The selection criteria for students who wish to pursue **B.Tech. Aerospace Engineering (Avionics)** at **University of Petroleum and Energy Studies** depend on their individual performance in **UPESEAT / JEE / Board Merit / SAT/ CUET**.
