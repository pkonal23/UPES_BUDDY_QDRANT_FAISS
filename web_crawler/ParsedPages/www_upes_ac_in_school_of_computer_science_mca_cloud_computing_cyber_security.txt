**Master of Computer Application (MCA) - Cloud Computing and Cybersecurity**

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Computer Science offers a Master of Computer Application (MCA) program with a specialization in Cybersecurity and Cloud Computing. This advanced program delves into the cutting-edge domains of cybersecurity and cloud computing, catering to aspiring professionals seeking to excel in these dynamic fields.

**Program Details**

The Master of Computer Application (MCA) program at The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) emphasizes practical experience and industry alignment. Students engage in hands-on projects and internships that provide valuable experiential learning opportunities. The curriculum is meticulously designed to equip graduates with the knowledge and skills required to navigate the evolving cybersecurity and cloud computing landscape.

**Program Highlights**

* Stay abreast of emerging trends in Cybersecurity and Cloud Computing.
* Gain proficiency in both Cybersecurity and Cloud Computing, enabling diverse career pathways.
* Participate in hands-on project development and implementation throughout the program.
* Benefit from an industry-oriented curriculum that aligns with real-world demands.
* Solidify your learning through an applied industrial internship during the fourth semester.
* Graduate with a robust skill set encompassing technical expertise and a deep understanding of ethical, legal, and social aspects of cybersecurity and cloud computing.

**Future Scope and Industry Trends**

The MCA program at The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) positions graduates for promising career opportunities in the rapidly expanding fields of cybersecurity and cloud computing. **Eligibility**

Prospective students must meet the following minimum eligibility criteria:

* Minimum 50% marks in 10th and 12th standard.
* Minimum 50% aggregate marks in BCA or B.Sc. (Computer Science) or B.Sc. (Information Technology) or any graduation with Computer Science / IT as a subject for three years.
* Students with Mathematics, Statistics, Business Math's, Business Statistics, or QT as subjects at Graduation or 10+2 level are also eligible.

**Selection Criteria**

Admission to the Master of Computer Application (MCA) program is based on the candidate's performance in a personal interview.
