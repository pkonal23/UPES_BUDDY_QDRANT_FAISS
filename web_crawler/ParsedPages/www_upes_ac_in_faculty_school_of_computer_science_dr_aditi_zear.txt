Dr. Aditi Zear

Assistant Professor

Profile Summary
Dr Aditi Zear has completed Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Engineering, National Institute of Technology (NIT), Kurukshetra, Haryana. She was also associated with DST-SERB project entitled “Network partition recovery in wireless sensor networks with the integration of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles”. She has more than 5 years of research and 2.5 years of teaching experience. She has obtained M. Tech degree in Computer Science Engineering from Jaypee University of Information Technology (JUIT) Waknaghat, Solan, Himachal Pradesh and B. Tech degree in Information Technology from University Institute of Information Technology (UIIT), Shimla, Himachal Pradesh. Her research interests include Data Hiding techniques, Wireless Sensor Networks and Unmanned Aerial Vehicles.
Work Experience
Before joining UPES, Dr Aditi was working as Assistant Professor in COER University, Roorkee, Uttarakhand. She has also worked as Lecturer in National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh.
Research Interests
Dr Aditi is working in the field of wireless sensor networks, unmanned aerial vehicles and data hiding techniques.
Teaching Philosophy
Dr Aditi Zear aims to create supportive and encouraging atmosphere to guide students on their educational journey. She emphasizes on constructive assessment and feedback to help students to understand their progress and identify areas for improvement.
Courses Taught
Database management systems, Advance database management systems, Design and analysis of algorithms
Contact
aditi.zear@ddn.upes.ac.in
