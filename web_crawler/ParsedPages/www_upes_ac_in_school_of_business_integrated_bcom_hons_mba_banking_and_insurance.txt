Program details
The
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program offered by UPES is a comprehensive course that combines undergraduate and postgraduate studies to provide students with a strong foundation in business management and specialized knowledge in the banking and insurance sectors. The program offers a wide range of subjects, covering core business disciplines such as accounting, finance, marketing, human resource management, and strategic management. Additionally, there is a specific focus on banking and insurance courses, including principles of banking, insurance operations, risk management, financial planning, regulatory frameworks, and emerging trends in the industry.
The program aims to equip students with industry-relevant skills necessary for successful careers in banking and insurance. Through case studies, projects, presentations, and group exercises, students develop leadership, communication, problem-solving, and decision-making skills. They also have opportunities to gain practical experience through internships and industry training, allowing them to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world settings, understand industry practices, and build a professional network. The program's faculty members, who have expertise in banking, insurance, finance, and related fields, provide practical insights and keep the curriculum updated with the latest industry trends and regulations.
To enhance students' overall personality and employability, the program emphasizes the development of soft skills, including communication, leadership, teamwork, and interpersonal skills. Students are encouraged to participate in co-curricular activities, presentations, debates, and group discussions. The program also provides career guidance services and placement support, assisting students in exploring career paths, improving their interview skills, and connecting with potential employers. Graduates of the program are prepared for a wide range of roles in the banking and insurance industry and are equipped to navigate its complexities and contribute to organizational success.
Program Highlights
The program covers various aspects of banking and insurance, including principles, operations, products, services, risk management, and regulatory frameworks. Students gain in-depth knowledge of these sectors and their key concepts and practices.
Emphasis is placed on developing practical skills required for banking and insurance careers. Students acquire skills in financial analysis, risk assessment, underwriting, claims management, financial planning, and compliance.
The
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program is taught by experienced faculty members who have expertise in banking, insurance, finance, and related fields. Their industry experience enables them to provide practical insights, real-world examples, and valuable guidance to students.
Students have the opportunity to undertake internships with banks, insurance companies, financial institutions, and other relevant organizations. These internships provide valuable industry exposure, practical experience, and a chance to apply knowledge in real-world settings.
The program may have collaborations with banks, insurance companies, and financial institutions. These partnerships offer guest lectures, workshops, seminars, and industry-specific training programs.
Students can pursue professional certifications in areas such as banking, insurance, risk management, financial planning, and compliance.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program is promising, with numerous opportunities emerging from industry dynamics, technology advancements, regulatory changes, and customer preferences. Graduates of this program will be well-positioned to navigate the digital transformation of the banking and insurance sectors, leveraging expertise in both fields and technology integration. There will be a growing demand for professionals skilled in digital tools, analytics, AI, and blockchain to enhance operational efficiency, customer experiences, and develop innovative financial products. Risk management will remain crucial, with graduates skilled in risk assessment, compliance, and cybersecurity in high demand. The integration of technology and finance (fintech) and technology and insurance (insurtech) is reshaping the industry, providing opportunities in startups and innovation-driven departments. Sustainability and ESG factors are gaining importance, creating opportunities in sustainable finance and green products. Graduates with a global perspective will find opportunities in multinational banks and international finance institutions. Regulatory compliance and customer-centricity will drive demand for professionals ensuring adherence to regulations and designing personalized experiences. Leadership roles, entrepreneurship, and consulting also offer potential avenues. Lifelong learning and staying updated with industry trends and technologies will be crucial for career growth. Overall, graduates with a strong foundation in business management and specialized knowledge in banking and insurance will thrive in this evolving industry.
Career Opportunities
Completing the
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking & Insurance
program offered by UPES School of Business opens up a plethora of exciting career opportunities in the financial sector. Graduates of this program possess a strong foundation in both commerce and management, along with specialized knowledge in banking and insurance. They are well-equipped to pursue rewarding careers as banking managers, investment analysts, risk managers, insurance underwriters, financial consultants, or compliance officers in renowned banks, financial institutions, insurance companies, and consulting firms. The comprehensive curriculum, industry exposure, and practical training provided by UPES School of Business prepare students to excel in this dynamic and competitive field, ensuring a promising future in the world of banking and insurance.
Placements
In recent years, the placement scenario for students pursuing
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
has significantly improved. Renowned companies such as HDFC, Axis Bank, ICICI, IDFC First, HDFC Mutual Fund, Geojit Financials, Mahindra & Mahindra, Amazon, Byjus, and Infosys present lucrative employment opportunities for these students. Moreover, numerous mid-sized start-ups show a keen interest in hiring graduates with an
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) – MBA in Banking and Insurance
background. Freshers in this field can typically expect a starting salary ranging from INR 10-12 lakh per annum. The salary offered depends on the candidate's skills, knowledge, and exposure.

Program details
The
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program offered by UPES is a comprehensive course that combines undergraduate and postgraduate studies to provide students with a strong foundation in business management and specialized knowledge in the banking and insurance sectors. The program offers a wide range of subjects, covering core business disciplines such as accounting, finance, marketing, human resource management, and strategic management. Additionally, there is a specific focus on banking and insurance courses, including principles of banking, insurance operations, risk management, financial planning, regulatory frameworks, and emerging trends in the industry.
The program aims to equip students with industry-relevant skills necessary for successful careers in banking and insurance. Through case studies, projects, presentations, and group exercises, students develop leadership, communication, problem-solving, and decision-making skills. They also have opportunities to gain practical experience through internships and industry training, allowing them to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world settings, understand industry practices, and build a professional network. The program's faculty members, who have expertise in banking, insurance, finance, and related fields, provide practical insights and keep the curriculum updated with the latest industry trends and regulations.
To enhance students' overall personality and employability, the program emphasizes the development of soft skills, including communication, leadership, teamwork, and interpersonal skills. Students are encouraged to participate in co-curricular activities, presentations, debates, and group discussions. The program also provides career guidance services and placement support, assisting students in exploring career paths, improving their interview skills, and connecting with potential employers. Graduates of the program are prepared for a wide range of roles in the banking and insurance industry and are equipped to navigate its complexities and contribute to organizational success.
Program Highlights
The program covers various aspects of banking and insurance, including principles, operations, products, services, risk management, and regulatory frameworks. Students gain in-depth knowledge of these sectors and their key concepts and practices.
Emphasis is placed on developing practical skills required for banking and insurance careers. Students acquire skills in financial analysis, risk assessment, underwriting, claims management, financial planning, and compliance.
The
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program is taught by experienced faculty members who have expertise in banking, insurance, finance, and related fields. Their industry experience enables them to provide practical insights, real-world examples, and valuable guidance to students.
Students have the opportunity to undertake internships with banks, insurance companies, financial institutions, and other relevant organizations. These internships provide valuable industry exposure, practical experience, and a chance to apply knowledge in real-world settings.
The program may have collaborations with banks, insurance companies, and financial institutions. These partnerships offer guest lectures, workshops, seminars, and industry-specific training programs.
Students can pursue professional certifications in areas such as banking, insurance, risk management, financial planning, and compliance.
Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program is promising, with numerous opportunities emerging from industry dynamics, technology advancements, regulatory changes, and customer preferences. Graduates of this program will be well-positioned to navigate the digital transformation of the banking and insurance sectors, leveraging expertise in both fields and technology integration. There will be a growing demand for professionals skilled in digital tools, analytics, AI, and blockchain to enhance operational efficiency, customer experiences, and develop innovative financial products. Risk management will remain crucial, with graduates skilled in risk assessment, compliance, and cybersecurity in high demand. The integration of technology and finance (fintech) and technology and insurance (insurtech) is reshaping the industry, providing opportunities in startups and innovation-driven departments. Sustainability and ESG factors are gaining importance, creating opportunities in sustainable finance and green products. Graduates with a global perspective will find opportunities in multinational banks and international finance institutions. Regulatory compliance and customer-centricity will drive demand for professionals ensuring adherence to regulations and designing personalized experiences. Leadership roles, entrepreneurship, and consulting also offer potential avenues. Lifelong learning and staying updated with industry trends and technologies will be crucial for career growth. Overall, graduates with a strong foundation in business management and specialized knowledge in banking and insurance will thrive in this evolving industry.
Career Opportunities
Completing the
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking & Insurance
program offered by UPES School of Business opens up a plethora of exciting career opportunities in the financial sector. Graduates of this program possess a strong foundation in both commerce and management, along with specialized knowledge in banking and insurance. They are well-equipped to pursue rewarding careers as banking managers, investment analysts, risk managers, insurance underwriters, financial consultants, or compliance officers in renowned banks, financial institutions, insurance companies, and consulting firms. The comprehensive curriculum, industry exposure, and practical training provided by UPES School of Business prepare students to excel in this dynamic and competitive field, ensuring a promising future in the world of banking and insurance.
Placements
In recent years, the placement scenario for students pursuing
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
has significantly improved. Renowned companies such as HDFC, Axis Bank, ICICI, IDFC First, HDFC Mutual Fund, Geojit Financials, Mahindra & Mahindra, Amazon, Byjus, and Infosys present lucrative employment opportunities for these students. Moreover, numerous mid-sized start-ups show a keen interest in hiring graduates with an
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) – MBA in Banking and Insurance
background. Freshers in this field can typically expect a starting salary ranging from INR 10-12 lakh per annum. The salary offered depends on the candidate's skills, knowledge, and exposure.

Program details
The
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program offered by UPES is a comprehensive course that combines undergraduate and postgraduate studies to provide students with a strong foundation in business management and specialized knowledge in the banking and insurance sectors. The program offers a wide range of subjects, covering core business disciplines such as accounting, finance, marketing, human resource management, and strategic management. Additionally, there is a specific focus on banking and insurance courses, including principles of banking, insurance operations, risk management, financial planning, regulatory frameworks, and emerging trends in the industry.
The program aims to equip students with industry-relevant skills necessary for successful careers in banking and insurance. Through case studies, projects, presentations, and group exercises, students develop leadership, communication, problem-solving, and decision-making skills. They also have opportunities to gain practical experience through internships and industry training, allowing them to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world settings, understand industry practices, and build a professional network. The program's faculty members, who have expertise in banking, insurance, finance, and related fields, provide practical insights and keep the curriculum updated with the latest industry trends and regulations.
To enhance students' overall personality and employability, the program emphasizes the development of soft skills, including communication, leadership, teamwork, and interpersonal skills. Students are encouraged to participate in co-curricular activities, presentations, debates, and group discussions. The program also provides career guidance services and placement support, assisting students in exploring career paths, improving their interview skills, and connecting with potential employers. Graduates of the program are prepared for a wide range of roles in the banking and insurance industry and are equipped to navigate its complexities and contribute to organizational success.
Program Highlights
The program covers various aspects of banking and insurance, including principles, operations, products, services, risk management, and regulatory frameworks. Students gain in-depth knowledge of these sectors and their key concepts and practices.
Emphasis is placed on developing practical skills required for banking and insurance careers. Students acquire skills in financial analysis, risk assessment, underwriting, claims management, financial planning, and compliance.
The
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program is taught by experienced faculty members who have expertise in banking, insurance, finance, and related fields. Their industry experience enables them to provide practical insights, real-world examples, and valuable guidance to students.
Students have the opportunity to undertake internships with banks, insurance companies, financial institutions, and other relevant organizations. These internships provide valuable industry exposure, practical experience, and a chance to apply knowledge in real-world settings.
The program may have collaborations with banks, insurance companies, and financial institutions. These partnerships offer guest lectures, workshops, seminars, and industry-specific training programs.
Students can pursue professional certifications in areas such as banking, insurance, risk management, financial planning, and compliance.

Program details
The
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program offered by UPES is a comprehensive course that combines undergraduate and postgraduate studies to provide students with a strong foundation in business management and specialized knowledge in the banking and insurance sectors. The program offers a wide range of subjects, covering core business disciplines such as accounting, finance, marketing, human resource management, and strategic management. Additionally, there is a specific focus on banking and insurance courses, including principles of banking, insurance operations, risk management, financial planning, regulatory frameworks, and emerging trends in the industry.
The program aims to equip students with industry-relevant skills necessary for successful careers in banking and insurance. Through case studies, projects, presentations, and group exercises, students develop leadership, communication, problem-solving, and decision-making skills. They also have opportunities to gain practical experience through internships and industry training, allowing them to apply theoretical knowledge in real-world settings, understand industry practices, and build a professional network. The program's faculty members, who have expertise in banking, insurance, finance, and related fields, provide practical insights and keep the curriculum updated with the latest industry trends and regulations.
To enhance students' overall personality and employability, the program emphasizes the development of soft skills, including communication, leadership, teamwork, and interpersonal skills. Students are encouraged to participate in co-curricular activities, presentations, debates, and group discussions. The program also provides career guidance services and placement support, assisting students in exploring career paths, improving their interview skills, and connecting with potential employers. Graduates of the program are prepared for a wide range of roles in the banking and insurance industry and are equipped to navigate its complexities and contribute to organizational success.

Program Highlights
The program covers various aspects of banking and insurance, including principles, operations, products, services, risk management, and regulatory frameworks. Students gain in-depth knowledge of these sectors and their key concepts and practices.
Emphasis is placed on developing practical skills required for banking and insurance careers. Students acquire skills in financial analysis, risk assessment, underwriting, claims management, financial planning, and compliance.
The
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program is taught by experienced faculty members who have expertise in banking, insurance, finance, and related fields. Their industry experience enables them to provide practical insights, real-world examples, and valuable guidance to students.
Students have the opportunity to undertake internships with banks, insurance companies, financial institutions, and other relevant organizations. These internships provide valuable industry exposure, practical experience, and a chance to apply knowledge in real-world settings.
The program may have collaborations with banks, insurance companies, and financial institutions. These partnerships offer guest lectures, workshops, seminars, and industry-specific training programs.
Students can pursue professional certifications in areas such as banking, insurance, risk management, financial planning, and compliance.

Future Scope / Industry Trends
The future scope of the
Integrated B.Com. (Hons.) - MBA in Banking and Insurance
program is promising, with numerous opportunities emerging from industry dynamics, technology advancements, regulatory changes, and customer preferences. Graduates of this program will be well-positioned to navigate the digital transformation of the banking and insurance sectors, leveraging expertise in both fields and technology integration. There will be a growing demand for professionals skilled in digital tools, analytics, AI, and blockchain to enhance operational efficiency, customer experiences, and develop innovative financial products. Risk management will remain crucial, with graduates skilled in risk assessment, compliance, and cybersecurity in high demand. The integration of technology and finance (fintech) and technology and insurance (insurtech) is reshaping the industry, providing opportunities in startups and innovation-driven departments. Sustainability and ESG factors are gaining importance, creating opportunities in sustainable finance and green products. Graduates with a global perspective will find opportunities in multinational banks and international finance institutions. Regulatory compliance and customer-centricity will drive demand for professionals ensuring adherence to regulations and designing personalized experiences. Leadership roles, entrepreneurship, and consulting also offer potential avenues. Lifelong learning and staying updated with industry trends and technologies will be crucial for career growth. Overall, graduates with a strong foundation in business management and specialized knowledge in banking and insurance will thrive in this evolving industry.
