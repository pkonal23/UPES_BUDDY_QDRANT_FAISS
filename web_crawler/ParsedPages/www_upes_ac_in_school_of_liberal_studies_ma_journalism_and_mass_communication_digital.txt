**University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES)**

**School of Liberal Studies**

**Master of Arts (MA) in Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital)**

**Program Overview**

The MA in Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital) program at University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Liberal Studies is a two-year, four-semester program that provides students with a comprehensive understanding of mass media, with a focus on digital media and communication. The program emphasizes industry-relevant skills, bridging the gap between theory and practice through engagement with industry experts.

**Program Highlights**

* Emphasis on internships, on-the-job training, and major projects for practical experience.
* Career opportunities in various fields of journalism, media, and mass communication.
* Mentorship and training by leading media professionals.
* Ultramodern studio and lab infrastructure for hands-on experience in digital production.
* Curriculum designed for proficiency in the digital media landscape.

**Future Scope and Industry Trends**

The MA in Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital) program addresses the growing demand for skilled digital journalists who can create compelling content for diverse digital channels.

**Career Opportunities**

Graduates of the program are qualified for various roles in digital media, including:

* Digital journalists
* Content creators
* Social media strategists
* Digital editors
* Media analysts
* Communication specialists

**Placements**

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) School of Liberal Studies has a strong track record of placements for MA in Journalism and Mass Communication (Digital) graduates. Partnerships with industry leaders provide internship opportunities and facilitate placements in renowned media organizations.

**Eligibility Criteria**

* Minimum 50% aggregate in X, XII, and undergraduate degree in any stream.

**Selection Criteria**
